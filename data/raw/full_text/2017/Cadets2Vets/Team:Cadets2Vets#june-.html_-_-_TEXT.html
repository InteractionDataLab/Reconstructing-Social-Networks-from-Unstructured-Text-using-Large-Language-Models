The remaining cultures after the initial concentration were 50/50 in terms of E. coli growth. When checked for fluorescence all samples below 100uM showed possible signal from GFP. However further analysis showed an odd mitigating variable, being that the culture media has an auto-fluorescence within the same spectra as GFP. To prove GFP signal each culture was pelleted out via centrifugation, washed with PBS twice and suspended to eliminate any chance of background signal. The results showed little to no GFP signal. However due to culture variability, this will be replicated. Glycerol stocks have also been created of the stock circuit.       Moving Forward: Due to the results above due to many mitigating factors the cell may not be biologically competent. However, because of the variability between replicates of the varying culture concentrations that will need to be repeated.
