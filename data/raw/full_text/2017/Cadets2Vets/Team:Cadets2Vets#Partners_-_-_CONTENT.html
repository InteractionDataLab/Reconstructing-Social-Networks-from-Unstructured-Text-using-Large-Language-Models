Our Project Partners

United states military academy

The United States Military Academy's mission is to educate, train, and inspire the Corps of Cadets so that each graduate is a commissioned leader of character committed to the values of duty, honor, and country, and prepared for a career of professional excellence and service to the nation as an officer in the United States Army.

United states military academy

Cadet Mitchell and Cadet Buehrer are sophomores at West Point. During the summer of their freshman year, they traveled to Tacoma, WA to work with members of the Cadets2Vets team to design the project that will be presented at the iGEM Jamboree in November of 2017.

United states army Edgewood Chemical and Biological center (ECBC)

​ ECBC is currently developing better ways to remotely detect chemical and biological materials before the warfighter or first responder ever enters the threat zone. ECBC is also developing a new generation of technologies to counter everything from homemade explosives to biological aerosols and non-traditional chemical hazards.

United states army Edgewood Chemical and Biological center (ECBC)

ECBC possesses an unrivaled chemical and biological research and development infrastructure with scientists, engineers, technicians, and specialists located at four different sites in the Unites States: the Edgewood Area of Aberdeen Proving Ground, Maryland; Pine Bluff, Arkansas; Rock Island, Illinois; and Dugway Proving Ground, Utah.

United states army Edgewood Chemical and Biological center (ECBC)

ECBC is the primary Department of Defense technical organization for non-medical chemical and biological defense, and is a subordinate command of the United States Army Research, Development, and Engineering Command.

READINESS ACCELERATION AND INNOVATION NETWORK (RAIN) Incubator

The Readiness Acceleration & Innovation Network is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization based out of Tacoma, Washington. Working to unlock local innovation in the biotech and biomedical fields, RAIN was founded as a collaboration between the University of Washington Tacoma, MultiCare Health System, and Madigan Army Medical Center.

Readiness acceleration and innovation network

Thinking many years ahead to what we are working to achieve, we envision our city and region as a major industrial and commercial player in the Pacific Northwest life-science sector with a reputation for being the best in the world at what we do. By helping to build a local innovation-commercialization pipeline, RAIN is inclusive, empowering, and inspiring to our community, friends, and neighbors.

READINESS ACCELERATION AND INNOVATION NETWORK

Together, we provide startups with significant support for ideation, research and development, prototyping, business development, and launch. In addition, the people and City of Tacoma are core partners in our efforts as we work together to create prosperity and well-being in our community.

Center for Urban Waters

The Center for Urban Waters brings together environmental scientists, analysts, engineers, and policymakers who are developing creative and sustainable solutions to restore and protect Puget Sound. It is the result of years of work by community leaders who dreamed of a world-class research center on Commencement Bay dedicated to finding solutions for urban bay communities.

Center for Urban Waters

The City of Tacoma, the University of Washington Tacoma, and the Puget Sound Partnership - our founding partners - are committed to providing a collaborative environment where the best-available science forms the basis for policy development and implementation.

Charles Wright Academy

Charles Wright Academy is a private school in the greater Tacoma area that serves students K-12. The school features small classes, a safe campus, and a preparation center for college and life skills.

Charles Wright Academy

​The Cadets2Vets team is honored to have four of the Charles Wright Academy's best and brightest serve as Interns on their lab team.

Bellarmine Preparatory School

The Bellarmine Preparatory School was founded under the leadership of the Jesuits. The school serves as a Catholic college preparatory school, and is the only Catholic high school in the Tacoma area.​

Bellarmine Preparatory School

The Cadets2Vets team is privileged to have Josh Grace as a Lab Member on their team.

University of Washington Tacoma

UW Tacoma is an urban-serving university providing access to students in a way that transforms families and communities. We impact and inform economic development through community-engaged students and faculty. We conduct research that is of direct use to our community and region. Most importantly, we seek to be connected to our community's needs and aspirations.​

University of Washington Tacoma

A special thanks to UW Tacoma faculty and staff for allowing the Cadets2Vets team the opportunity to utilize their lab, equipment, and facilities.

Stadium High School

Stadium High School is located in downtown Tacoma and has a strong emphasis on preparing students for the future. All students take at least one college-level or college-prep class before graduation and there are programs for Career and Technical Education, Visual and Performing Arts, and Athletics.

Stadium High School

The Cadets2Vets team is privileged to have Ian Gutierrez as a Lab Member on their team.

Tacoma Community College

Tacoma Community College’s mission is to “create meaningful and relevant learning, inspire greater equity, and celebrate success in our lives and our communities.” TCC serves Pierce County and has Bridge Programs with Evergreen State College and UWT. TCC offers a wide variety of degrees to allow its students to choose any career path.

Tacoma Community College

The Cadets2Vets team is privileged to have Kristina Ilyovska as a Lab Member on their team.​
