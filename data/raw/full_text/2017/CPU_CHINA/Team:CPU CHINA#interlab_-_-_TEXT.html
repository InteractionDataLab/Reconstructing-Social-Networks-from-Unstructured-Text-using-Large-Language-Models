One of the big challenges in synthetic biology measurement is that fluorescence data usually cannot be compared because it is reported in different units or because different groups process data in different ways. We aim to improve the measurement tools available to both the iGEM community and the synthetic biology community as a whole. Teams were given twelve test devices of green fluorescent protein (GFP) under kit plate 6 and 7, as well as a positive and a negative control（their location listed in figure 1）. We were asked to measure the fluorescent output of these samples using equipment in our lab. Figure 1. The locations of devices The CPU_CHINA team measured these samples on our Multimode Reader and reported the results to iGEM HQ for further analysis and comparison to other teams' results before deadline（figure 2）.
