    • Achievement

           This year our team is very honored to get a Silver medal in Giant Jamboree. Thanks to all the people who have helped us physically and mentally. All of us have been making lots of efforts during the last eight months. Laughter and tears have witnessed our growth. We're looking forward to taking part in iGEM 2018 and coming out with more fascinated ideas to make the world better. What we've achieved is listed in our opinion as follows.

      Requirements for a Bronze Medal(All completed)
      Register the team
      Meet all deliverables

      Clearly designate work done by team members and others in Attribution

      Participate in the Interlab Measurement Study

      Improve the characterization of an existing BioBrick Part and enter this information on that part’s Main Page in the Registry.Part:BBa_K2462007 ,BBa_K2462006

      Additional Requirements for a Silver Medal(All completed)

      Design at least one new BioBricks: BBa_K2462000, BBa_K2462003, BBa_K2462001that is central to our project and submit samples of these new parts to the iGEM Parts Registry.

      Significantly work with any other registered iGEM team in a meaningful way in Collaborations.

      Think carefully and creatively about whether our work is safe, responsible and good for the word in HP_Silver.

      Additional Requirements for a Gold Medal(At least two completed)

      Expand silver medal activity by demonstrating how we have integrated the investigated issues into the design in Gold_Integrated.

      Improve the function of an existing BioBrick Part:BBa_J61051,BBa_B0030.

      Our project’s implementation is based on insight gained from modeling in Model

      Show our system works under real world conditions
