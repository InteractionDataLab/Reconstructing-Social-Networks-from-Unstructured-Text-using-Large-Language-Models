    Photocatalyst System

    Here, we proved that the cysteine desulfhydrase can generate S2- ions from cysteine. More importantly, we confirmed that this enzyme can precipitate a large amount of CdS sediments on the surface on the bacteria. Lastly, we also ensured the function of CdS nanoparticles--increasing the cathode current and electrons transfer efficiency.
