    Hyunjun Jared Park

    Student Leader

     

    Jared is a senior at our international high school who currently studies biology and physics. He is the student leader of team this year after having participated in the competition in 2016. His primary work was to manage all the departments by having all the members from each department work cooperatively rather focusing solely on their main work. Additionally, he coded the entire wiki. While recognizing the fact that the team is composed of high school students, he hopes that the project could provide a small benefit to developing countries for a better world.
