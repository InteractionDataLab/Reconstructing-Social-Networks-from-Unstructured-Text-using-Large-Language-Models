We added fermentation broth of FY-07 and FY-07 ABC to the diesel in the two tubes respectively. Shook the test tube, and then stood for six hours. After that, we can clearly observe that the diesel fuel in the test tube, which added fermentation broth FY-07 ABC, was emulsified. Experimental results show that the FY-07 ABC, which produce rhamnolipid, can be a good emulsion of oil.
