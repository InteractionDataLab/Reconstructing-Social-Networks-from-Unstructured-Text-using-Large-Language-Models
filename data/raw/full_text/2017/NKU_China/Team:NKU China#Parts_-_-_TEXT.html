Lyase is an enzyme that catalyzes the breaking of various chemical bonds by means other than hydrolysis and oxidation. When the lyase gene expresses, the bacterium will die, which will avoid the diffusion of bacterium to the environment. The function of the lyase is incredible, as our experiment shows. We introduced pBC-ara-lyase into the E.coli S17. After applying arabinose to the culture medium, the lyase gene began to express and the bacterium died.
