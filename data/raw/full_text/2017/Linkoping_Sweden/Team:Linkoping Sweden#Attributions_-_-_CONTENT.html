Attributions

The members of this years team have all provided to the final work of this years project, including but not limited to laboratory work (including preperations) and human practices work.

The people, websites and companies mentioned below have, in different ways, been able to provide different qualities or sponsorships for the students of the LiU iGEM team 2017 in their work during this year’s project. A special thank you is required for all the help and support, making this project possible.

  • Our Principal Investigators Professor Per Hammarström and Senior Lecturer Lars-Göran Mårtensson have supported the team with great knowledge, troubleshooting and advice when it comes to laboratory work and administration work.
  • Alexander Sandberg for helping us with validation of a BioBrick.
  • Samuel Svensson (CBD Solutions) for help and feedback when looking for sponsors.
  • Linköping University of Technology has helped the team with the registration fee, equipment, laboratory and other facilities.
  • The Faculty of Medicine and Health Sciences at Linköpings University has provided with financial support.
  • The Department of Physics, Chemistry and Biology (IFM) at Linköping University for its warm welcome and generosity.
  • Codecademy for teaching us how to code HTML, CSS and JavaScript.
  • W3schools for teaching us how to code and providing examples for the Dropdown, Navigation Bar and Responsive Topnav.
  • FoldEco and its creators for providing the open source Wolfram Mathematica code that has been used to create a MATLAB version for modeling.
  • GATC Biotech for sequencing.
