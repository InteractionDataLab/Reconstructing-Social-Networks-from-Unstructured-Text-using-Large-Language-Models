It will be capable of generating complete fluorescence spectra (via a low-cost monochrometer) and OD600 measurements. Every design-process decision that contributes to our final product will be made with a cost versus performance ratio in mind to ensure that our machine is affordable, reliable, and accurate. Our machine will be one that students can assemble from detailed schematics using cost-effective hardware, rapid prototyping technologies, and open-source software.
