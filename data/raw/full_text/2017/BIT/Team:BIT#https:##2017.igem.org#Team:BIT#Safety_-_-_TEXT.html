Safety of experimental materials 1. Engineering bacteria: the use of the project for the lysine-deficient Escherichia coli, in the absence of lysine environment can not grow, it will not affect the environment; 2.Part: all bio-brick used in the line from the iGEM official plasmid plate provided in the plasmid; 3. Chemical reagents: All chemical reagents used in the project are laboratory reagents, there is no toxicity than the laboratory prescribed reagents, there is no flammable and explosive reagents; 4. Equipment: all the damage to the instruments are clearly classified by a dedicated person in charge of a unified recovery, sharp items can be put into the recycling container after packaging; 5. Waste: whether it is solid or liquid reagents, have a special waste liquid tank recycling; 6. Personnel: Each trial has at least two students in the laboratory to ensure the safety of the experimenter; 7. Laboratory: the last students to leave the laboratory responsible for the lock door, off the window, off the water, air conditioning and so on.
