    Random interviews in China science and technology museum

    Time:2017.8.20 Place: China Science and Technology Museum In order to get more feedback we went to China Science and Technology Museum which is the only national class from 2013, the China Science and Technology Museum is the only national comprehensive science and Technology Museum in China. We did a brief introduction speech of synthetic biology and our project to public. During this activity, Our teammates made a specific explanation synthetic biology and our project to 300 people. In the all nearly 1000 people had notice our posters and folding paper. We also randomly interviewed a few visitors in the hall, we ask about their views towards JACOB and self-health care.
