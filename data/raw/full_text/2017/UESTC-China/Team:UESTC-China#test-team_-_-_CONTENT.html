    • This summer, we collaborated with three 2016 teams and a 2015 team. We have been mentoring a new team, TMMU-China team, which we are the first one introducing iGEM to TMMU. We helped Jilin-China construct a part that optimizing expression and helped UESTC-China-software complete part of the validation experiments. Besides, we got in touch with the W&M team 2015 to get the “curriculum booklet” and had a lesson with 15 families, then communicated the improvements with W&M team. Finally, in the course of our human practices of popularizing iGEM , a high school, Chengdu NO.7 High School, is interested in it. With our help, it’s likely that they will establish an iGEM team next year.
