Library Exhibition In China, many people of synthetic biology (GM) has a very one-sided understanding and prejudice, their understanding is still limited to the potential risk of genetically modified food, but did not see the benefits of synthetic biology. So the four teams together, held the "synthetic biology and human" is the theme of the exhibition at the Chengdu city library, answered the questions from the citizens of GM, changed their view for it. But for a small number of people, they still believe that we are preaching a dangerous, non proven field, Changing their one-sided ideas is what our propaganda means.
