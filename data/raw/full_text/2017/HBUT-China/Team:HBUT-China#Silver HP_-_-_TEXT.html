Team:HBUT-China/Silver HP Silver HP This year, our team HBUT-China mainly focused on developing a biological approach to detect nickel ions as heavy metal contaminant in the natural environment. Thus it is important for us to know the public opinion about nickel pollution. We also take the responsibility to advocate the idea of environmental protection and educate the students about synthetic biology. Therefore, we think we are qualified for the Human Practices Silver Medal. Exhibition at Hubei University of Technology On OCT 12 and OCT 13,we made an exhibition at Hubei University of Technology. We introduced our project and the iGEM competition to people who came and wanted to know about our project. We talked with them about Nickel pollution and told them to protect the environment. We also created a QQ group for iGEM competition communication and we put the group number on the exhibition so that people interested in our project and iGEM competition can join in the group and discuss questions together. Member Recruitment OCT 13 2016 was not as any ordinary day, iGEM HBUT-China had successfully accomplished our first mission together, the member recruitment. At that time, our team consisted of 16 members from different faculties, such as the School of Biological Engineering And Food Science, the School of Art And Design, the School of Scientists etc, but we realized that it was not enough for our team. We showed them our project and iGEM competition through PPT and our explanation. As a result, we searched for more people with hidden potential to join us after the recruitment. This formed our team which has a greater diversity of cross-disciplinary professions. WeChat Official Account We created a WeChat official account to spread knowledges about synthetic biology and the iGEM competition and share moments of our work throughout out our project. Up to now，we have got hundreds of subscribers. So we think we have fullfilled our goal of popularizing synthetic biology and iGEM competition and introducing our project to arise people’s awareness of reducing pollution and protecting the environment. Questionnaires We have done a questionnaire about heavy metal pollution to find out people’s opinion about it. And there are 140 person take part in the questionnaire. Through this questionnaire,we found that people’s awareness to Nickel pollution is not that much. And only a few people know about biological methods to detect mental pollution.
