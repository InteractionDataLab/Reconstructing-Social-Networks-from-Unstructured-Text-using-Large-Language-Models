    iGEM Mini-Jamboree (Public Engagement)
    We held an iGEM Mini-Jamboree on September 21th, 2017, which attracted a proportion of freshmen and sophomore from the Department of Life Science and as well as the Department of Automation in Tsinghua.
    Our member introducing iGEM
    The audience listening to our introduction
    We gave an introduction of synthetic biology first.
    Introduction of synthetic biology
    After that, we gave an introduction to iGEM,including its history, contents and some interesting projects from previous years.
    The introduction of iGEM
    Furthormore, we introduced our project this year.
    The genetic circuits in our project
    Our member describing our project to the audience
    Our member introducing the course of TIP
    Apart from the introduction, we divided the audience into several groups and encouraged them to brainstorm and come up with creative ideas on iGEM projects. They were also inspired to exchange their thoughts or ask questions about iGEM or synthetic biology.
    Our members exchanging ideas and brainstorming with audience
    Eventually, the representatives of each group shared their thoughts with all the people attending this Mini-Jamboree. They impressed us with their imaginative designs. The representatives were awarded with small souvenirs for their good exhibition.
