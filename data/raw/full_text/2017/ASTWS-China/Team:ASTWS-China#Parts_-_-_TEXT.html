The same holds true for synthetic biology, which has also been called engineering biology. However, the ability to repeat measurements in different labs has been difficult. This year is fourth InterLab study that organized by the iGEM Organization Measurement  Committee. It aims to establish a GFP measurement protocol based on engineering principles  that anyone with a plate reader can use in their lab and to improve measurement  tools and methods in laboratory work.
