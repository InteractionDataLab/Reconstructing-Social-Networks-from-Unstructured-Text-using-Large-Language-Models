M CaCI2 CaCL2 0.1M + 15% glycerol Sterile Falcon tubes Steps Inoculate an isolated colony of an Escherichia coli strain in a tube containing 10 mL of LB medium and incubate overnight at 37°C and 260 rpm. Inoculate 100 mL of LB broth with 1 mL of the previous culture. Incubate for 3 hours at 37 °C and 260 rpm until it reaches a 0.6 O.D. Place on ice for 10 minutes. Divide the culture in 15 mL Falcon tubes and centrifuge at 5000 rpm for 5 minutes. Remove the supernatant, resuspend the pellets in 10 mL of cold 0.1 M CaCl2 and allow to incubate on ice for 10 minutes. Centrifuge at 4000 rpm for 10 minutes. Remove the supernatant, resuspend the pellet in 2 mL of CaCl2 0.1M + 15% glycerol solution and allow to incubate on ice for 10 min. Prepare 200 μl aliquots and store at -80 °C.
