Golden Medal

1.「HP」

We established Synthetic Biology club in SHSBNU school.

We went to Navy General Hospital and communicated with Hao, the director of stool detection to discuss the current situation about non-invasive measurement in China and her suggestion about our project.

We handed out questionnaires to public and asked for which factor they care most about our project.

2.「Parts」

We divided an existed BioBrick BBa_K895007 into two parts—BBa_K2507002 and BBa_K2507003

3.「Demonstrate」

After doing the survey, we have developed various methods to solve the current problem and meet the public’s concern. We mainly focused on three aspects: safety, sensitivity, and price and response time.

Curry are used to simulate human’s stool to test whether our project is possible to be observe.

4.「Model」

In the modeling, we’ve verified that the florescence–concentration of ths/ttr can be fitted in Hill Equation. Also, we have counted Hill coefficient and concentration of thiosulfate at half-maximum response. Additionally, we’ve made the contrast between different conditions
