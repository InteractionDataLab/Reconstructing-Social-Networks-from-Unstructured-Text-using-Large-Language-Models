Dilute AMPs by PB buffer. The conc. gradients of diluted AMPs as follow: LL-37: 80ug/ml, 40ug/ml, 20ug/ml, 10ug/ml, 5ug/ml, 1ug/ml, 0.5ug/ml; Grammistin-Pp1: 40ug/ml, 20ug/ml, 10ug/ml, 6ug/ml, 3ug/ml, 1ug/ml, 0.5ug/ml GF-17: 20ug/ml, 10ug/ml, 6ug/ml, 3ug/ml, 2ug/ml, 1ug/ml, 0.5ug/ml After each dilution, vortex 1-3min for each of concentration of AMPs Staphylococcus aureus dilution Measure OD600 value of original Staphylococcus aureus solution. Dilute Staphylococcus aureus solution 500 times and 1000 times respectively in different EP tube by PB buffer; After dilution by PB buffer, vortex each conc. gradients of solution 1-3min. AMPs and Staphylococcus aureus interaction Pipette 50μl diluted AMPs solution and 50μl diluted Staphylococcus aureus solution (1/500 and 1/1000 diluted) into one EP tube. Vortex each EP tube for 1-3 min. Incubate 3.5 hours at 37℃, 180rpm condition. Plate 30μl mixed solution into the LB agar medium. Overnight culture at 37℃ condition.
