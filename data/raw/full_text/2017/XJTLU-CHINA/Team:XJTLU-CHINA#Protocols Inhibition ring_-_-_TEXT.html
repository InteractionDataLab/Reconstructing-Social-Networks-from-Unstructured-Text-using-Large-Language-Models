Add 170μl of LL-37, Grammistin-Pp1, GF-17 solutions to form a 1:1:1 LL-37+Grammistin-Pp1+GF-17 solution. Measure OD600 value of Staphylococcus aureus after 9 hours’ culture. Inoculate 100μl diluted S. aureus (OD600≈0.4) on each medium. Pipette 20μl each peptide solution on each paper disc (LL-37, Grammistin-Pp1, GF-17, and LL-37+Grammistin-Pp1+GF-17). Dry all discs. Place 2 paper discs with peptide, 1 paper disc with positive control (chloramphenicol) and 1 negative control (buffer: PBS+BSA+HAC) for each plate. Overnight culture at 37℃ condition.
