Dilute 5-fold with fresh medium. Aliquot 200μl culture into each well of 96-well plate. Grow at 30℃ in plate reader with a regular test every 30 minutes until stationary phase (OD600≥3). Induce with 0.5-5 ng/ml nisin (usually 1 ng/ml). Continue to observe the growth curve with a regular OD600 test every 1 hour.
