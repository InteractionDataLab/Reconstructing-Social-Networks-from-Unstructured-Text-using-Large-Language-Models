Use the Electroporator with following adjustments: 2500 V (12.5 KV/cm) 25 μF 200 Ω - Pulse (normal reading is 4.5-5 msec) Add 1 ml G/L-M17B + 20 mM MgCl2 + 2 mM CaCl2. Keep the cuvette for 5 min on ice and incubate 1-1.5 h at 30 °C. Plate 10 µl, 100 µl, 900 µl on M17agar with glucose or lactose and antibiotics (depends on plasmid) - Incubate 1-2 days at 30 °C. Tips: Lactococcus lactis grows very slowly in G/L-SGM17B. Leaving out the sucrose is possible but can lower the transformation efficiency. The medium for cell recovery must contain MgCl2 and CaCl2. The sterilization temperature can be set at 108 degrees Celsius in case of carbonization.
