Add equimolar digested fragment. Add 1μl T4 DNA ligase buffer. Add 0.5μl T4 DNA ligase. Add ddH2O to 10ul volume. Ligate at 16℃ for 30 minutes. Heat kill at 80℃ for 20 minutes. Transform 1-2μl of the product.
