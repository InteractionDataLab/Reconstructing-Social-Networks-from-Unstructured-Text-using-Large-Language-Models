Procedure: Prepare an ice box, put every material on ice. Add 0.02-0.5 pmols DNA fragment (which has been amplified by PCR using primer 1 and primer 2—see at PCR protocol) into PCR tube. (The mass of DNA can be calculated by NEB-calculator) Add 1/3 amount of DNA inserts PstI-digested pNZ8148 plasmid in the tube. Add 2X Gibson Assembly Master Mix (produced by NEB) to the tube. Fill the tube with dH2O to a total volume of 20 μl. Incubate the samples in a thermocycler at 50℃ for 15 minutes. (can be longer) Store samples on ice or at -20℃ for subsequent examination or Transformation.
