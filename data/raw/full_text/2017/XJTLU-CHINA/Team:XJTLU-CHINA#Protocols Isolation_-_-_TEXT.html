Centrifuge at 12,000×g for 1 minute to pellet the E. coli. Decant or pipette off as much of the supernatant as possible. Re-suspend the bacterial pellet in 250 μl of Buffer S1 by vortex. Please be sure that the bacteria are completely re-suspended before proceeding. Add 250 µl of Buffer S2, and mix by gently inverting the tube 4-6 times. Add 350 µl of Buffer S3, and mix by gently inverting 6-8 times. Centrifuge at 12,000×g for 10 minutes to clarify the lysate. Insert the required AxyPrep Plasmid Miniprep column into the fittings on the 2 ml Microfuge tube. Transfer the clarified supernatant from Step 4 into the Miniprep column. Add 500 µl of Buffer W1 to Miniprep column. Centrifuge at 12,000×g for 1 minute. Pipette 700 µl of Buffer W2 along the wall of the column to remove residual salt. Centrifuge at 12,000×g for 1 minute. Repeat this wash step with a second 700 µl aliquot of Buffer W2. Centrifuge at 12,000×g for 1 minute to purge residual Buffer W2 from the binding membrane. Transfer the Miniprep column into a clean 1.5 ml Microfuge tube. To elute the purified plasmid DNA, add 60~80 µl of Eluent buffer to the center of the membrane. Let it stand for 1 min at room temperature. Centrifuge at 12,000×g for 1 minute.
