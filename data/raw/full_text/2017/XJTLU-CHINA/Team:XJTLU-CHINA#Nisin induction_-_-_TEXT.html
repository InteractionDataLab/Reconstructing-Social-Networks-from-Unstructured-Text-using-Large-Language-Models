Dilute 1/25 in 2 x 10 ml fresh medium (30 °C). Grow until the OD600 ≈ 0.4. Induce one 10 ml culture with 0.5 - 5 ng/ml nisin (typically 1 ng/ml is used) and keep the other 10 ml culture as negative control. Incubate 2 - 3 hours/ Aliquot both control group and experiment group into 96-well plate. Test the fluorescence under the set of 485 nm excitation and 510 nm emission. Autolysis test (Transformant with pNZ8148 harboring BBa_2309004): Grow 5 ml successfully transformed bacteria culture overnight, 30 °C. Dilute 5-fold with fresh medium. Aliquot 200μl culture into each well of 96-well plate. Grow at 30℃ in plate reader with a regular test every 30 minutes until stationary phase (OD600≥3). Induce with 0.5-5 ng/ml nisin (usually 1 ng/ml). Continue to observe the growth curve with a regular OD600 test every 1 hour.
