The theme is open source, and you can use it for any purpose, personal or commercial. This theme features stock photos by Gratisography along with a custom Google Maps skin courtesy of Snazzy Maps . Grayscale includes full HTML, CSS, and custom JavaScript files along with LESS files for easy customization.
