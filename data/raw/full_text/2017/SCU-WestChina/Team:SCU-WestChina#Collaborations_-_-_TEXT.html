In addition, we consider seriously and extensively whether our project is safe, responsible and good for the world. Therefore, we designed and carried out numerous human practices around our project, and we integrate a lot of human practices considerations into every part of our project. Besides, we also conduct public engagement and education activities to have conversations with participates while inviting public input to shape the direction of our work and spreading synthetic biology and iGEM. During this year, we collaborated with so many teams in project design, experiments, human practices and other parts. We got a lot of help, thanks all those amazing teams so much! SCU_China As two teams in the same school, we had deep and close cooperation with SCU_China. RiboJ At the beginning of our project, SCU_China’s team leader Zhu Junyan privately pointed out to us that to better measure the promoters, we could use the Insulator RiboJ to get different proteins in approximately the same expression level, and her suggestion was adopted. Sleep center The project of SCU_China this year is about melatonin and sleep. In order to get first-hand clinical data and advice from doctors about their project, we helped them get into contact with doctors working in Center of sleep, West China Hospital, Sichuan University. iSWU 2017 SCU_China attended the China iGEM Southwest Union(iSWU) this year. We discussed our projects together, shared different opinions and gave each other suggestions to improve our projects. ACMA 2017 We co-hosted ACMA 2017 with SCU_China. In the academic salon, we presented to the participants the project of Vilnius - Lithuania 2016 and the project of Groningen 2012, answered questions about these two projects and offered some skills in project design. Our team leader showed up as a judge of the preliminary contest, grading, reviewing and asking questions about the participating projects.
