Gold Integrated Human Practices Model your project Our work in wet lab, modeling, and community engagement culminated in a policy proposal that advocates for reassessment of current Cr(VI) regulations and promotes the implementation of our engineered microbe. We developed a model of a wastewater treatment process to see how our engineered bacterial system would perform in a real-world application.
