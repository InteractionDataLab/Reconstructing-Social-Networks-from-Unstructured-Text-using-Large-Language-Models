After introduction of the sponge transgene, sponge RNAs are expressed at a high level and attract miR-21, inhibiting the expression of eGFP reporter. The expression of miR-21 should be identified by their eGFP reporter expression. Model We created a model which can measure the expression of miRNA in cells by using miRNA sponges. This model is very novel because it is the first time of using miRNA sponges to monitor the expression levels of miRNA.
