    SPONSORS

    We would like to thank all of our sponsors for their generous support.

    First and foremost, the Fondation Bettencourt Shueller, who has been a very generous supporter of our team since its inception in 2007.
    We would also like to thank the CRI and the INSERM that provided the lab and the equipment that we needed to finish our project as well as a wide network of scientists.
    Secondly, the Universities that put their trust in our project: Université Paris Diderot-Paris 7 and Université Paris Descartes, as well as the USPC.
    The French Embassy to the US for their yearly trust in the Paris Bettencourt team.
    Finally, we would like to thank all the various sponsors who provided materials or discounts that made our project possible on our budget.
