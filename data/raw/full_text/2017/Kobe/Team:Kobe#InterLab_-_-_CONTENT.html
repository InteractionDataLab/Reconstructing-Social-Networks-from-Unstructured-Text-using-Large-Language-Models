INTERLAB STUDY

We participated in the Fourth International InterLab Measurement Study. They asked us to measure the fluorescence of GFP in different devices in an attempt to explore the question, “How close can the numbers be when fluorescence is measured all around the world? “. We followed the same protocols as directed in the iGEM protocol page.

Materials

E.coli DH5-alpha: Competent cells were purchased from TOYOBO Plate reader: SH-9000Lab
