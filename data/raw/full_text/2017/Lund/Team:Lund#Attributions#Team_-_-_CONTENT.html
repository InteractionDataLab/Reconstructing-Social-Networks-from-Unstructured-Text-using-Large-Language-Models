    Team

    Here we briefly discuss and give a general indication of what each team member contributed with during the iGEM project.

    Albert Anis
    Responsible for the human practices, integrated practices, outreach and related wiki content.
    Sofia Classon
    Responsible for the human practices, integrated practices, outreach and related wiki content.
    Julia Dobrich
    Responsible for the wet-lab and acquiring sponsorship for the project. Contributed to the wiki content, human practices and outreach.
    Isabelle Hallgren
    Founder and team leader of the first iGEM team in Lund. Handled recruitment, structure of team organization, administration, team meetings and provided guidance. Acquired the majority of the sponsorship. Presented the project at the NiC.
    Naomi Hedman
    Contributed to the human practices and outreach.
    Malin Jönsson
    Responsible for the social media activity, contributed to the design of biobricks, protocols and experiments as well as assisted in the wet-lab. Contributed to the wiki content.
    Rebeka Kovačič
    Designed genetic sequences, protocols and experiments. Contributed to the wet-lab and was responsible for the social media activity. Contributed to the wiki content.
    Marcus Rettig
    Responsible for the wiki-design, artwork and animations.
    Frida Rosman
    Responsible for the wet-lab and all related activities. Designed genetic sequences, protocols and experiments. Responsible for project design and acquiring genetic material. Contributed to the wiki-content.
    Adham “agar-agar” Sakhnini
    Responsible for the modeling and project design. Contributed to the wet-lab, wiki-design and wiki content. Responsible for the scientific collaboration with Chennai. Keeping Peter in check.
    Emma Samuelsson
    The founder of the first iGEM team in Lund together with Isabelle. Responsible for the finances during the project. Handled the recruitment, administration and assisted in the wet-lab. Contributed to art and design of wiki.
    Peter Cornelis Skog
    Responsible for the project design and wiki content. Contributed to the wet-lab, modeling, design of experiments and practices. Responsible for the scientific collaboration with Chennai. Coaching Adham in life.
