    iGEM's interlab study aims to establish a GFP measurement protocol based on engineering principles that anyone with a plate reader can use in their lab. This means teams have to use the same exact protocol around the world to produce common, comparable units for measuring GFP with different plate readers. For more information on the protocol, check out iGEM's official Interlab Study webpage. Below is the data that we collected for the Interlab Study:
