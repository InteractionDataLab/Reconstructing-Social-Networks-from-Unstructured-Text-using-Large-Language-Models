Who Are We?

Welcome to our team page! The inaugural RPI (Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute) iGEM team comprises 11 undergraduates. Our team has benefited from the guidance of two professors in addition to other advisors. We are the first official team from RPI to successfully participate in iGEM, and we are excited and grateful for the opportunity to compete. Our team plans to realize the iGEM vision of advancing the state of the art in synthetic biology to improve health and generate knowledge. Our proposed work is highly interdisciplinary in that our project will feature several fields of inquiry including genetics, synthesis and identification of molecules, assessment of biological properties through cell based assays, and metabolic pathway engineering. Following a summer and fall of interdisciplinary collaboration to carry out an innovative synthetic biology project, we plan to present our work at iGEM. Our team members come from many different areas of science and engineering and have diverse skill sets in cellular biology, bioinformatics, analytical chemistry, and metabolic/enzyme engineering. We've been doing our best thus far, and look forward to meeting the other teams at the Giant Jamboree in November!
