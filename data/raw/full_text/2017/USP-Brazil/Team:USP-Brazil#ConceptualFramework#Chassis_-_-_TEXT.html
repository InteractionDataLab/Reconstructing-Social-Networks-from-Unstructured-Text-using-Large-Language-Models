And there are a lot of good options, ready to be tinkered with by the iGEM community! An ideal chassis for controlling population of parasites should be: Easy to cultivate and modify Naturally adapted to your targeted insect species Non pathogenic for humans, animals or plants Transmissible vertically and horizontally in your targeted population Here we provide a list of possible chassis characterized for future synthetic biology work: Table of chassis Ideal organismus Pantoea agglomerans Serratia sp.
