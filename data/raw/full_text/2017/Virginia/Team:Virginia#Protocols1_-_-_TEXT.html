Do not vortex. Place the mixture on ice for 30 minutes. Do not mix. Heat shock at exactly 42°C for exactly 30 seconds. Do not mix. Place on ice for 5 minutes. Do not mix. Pipette 950 µl of room temperature SOC or LB media into the mixture. Incubate at 37°C and 200-250 rpm for 60 minutes. Mix the cells thoroughly by flicking the tube and inverting. Spread: For ligation reaction DNA: 100µl of each transformation reaction onto a selection plate. For the rest of 900 µL: Pellet cells at 8000rpm for 3 minutes Remove and dispense 600 µL of supernatant Re-suspend cells by light vortexing Plate resuspended cells as above For known plasmid: 10 & 100 µL of each transformation reaction onto a selection plate. For the rest of 890 µL: Pellet cells at 8000rpm for 3 minutes Remove and dispense 600 µL of supernatant Re-suspend cells by light vortexing Plate resuspended cells as above Incubate overnight at 37°C with plates upside down.
