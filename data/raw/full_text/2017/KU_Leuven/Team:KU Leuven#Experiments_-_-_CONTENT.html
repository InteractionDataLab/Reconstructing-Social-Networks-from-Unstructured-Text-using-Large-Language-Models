    Cell Culture

    Medium of HEK T293 cells

    • DMEM (Gibco N° 41965-039) 500 ml
    • 50 ml (10 %) FCS = (Hyclone)
    • 10 ml Penicillin/Streptomycin (Gibco N° 15070-071) = 100-100U/ml
    • 10 ml Glutamax (Gibco N° 35050-038) = 10 mM
    • 5 ml Non-Essential-Amino-Acids (Gibco N° 1140-035) = 10x
    Growing Conditions
    • Incubate at 37°C and 10% CO2
    • Split cells around 70-90% confluency
    • Change medium twice a week
    Standard procedure for Trypsinization
    1. Wash the cells with Versene (Gibco N° 15040-033, ± 2 min)
    2. Remove versene and add Trypsin (Gibco N° 25300-096)
    3. Incubate at 37°C (around 3-5 min)
    4. Stop the trypsinization by adding 5-10 times volume of medium
    5. Count cells
    6. Centrifuge for 5 minutes at 1200 rpm
    7. Aspirate supernatant and resuspend the cells in medium
    8. Plate cells for experiments or culture
