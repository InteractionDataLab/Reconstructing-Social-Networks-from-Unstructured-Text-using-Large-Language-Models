Therefore, we discussed our project with people that would actually come into contact with our product: Doctors and patients dealing with various diseases. Furthermore, we paid a lot of attention on sharing our enthusiasm for science with the world, and spreading information about synthetic biology. Interested in what else we did? Then continue reading!
