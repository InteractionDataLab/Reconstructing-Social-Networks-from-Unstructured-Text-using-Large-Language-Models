    • Interlab

    iGEM's interlab study aims to establish a baseline and robust protocol for fluorescent measurements. Teams that participate in the interlab study will follow a standardized procedure that guides them through the transformation, inoculation and measurement processes. For more information on the protocol, check out iGEM's official Interlab Study webpage. Feel free to also refer to our Lab Notebook, which also lays out what we did for the Interlab Study. Below is the data that we collected for the Interlab Study:

    OD600 Reference Point

    Several measurements of LUDOX-S40 and distilled water were taken with a spectrophotometer. The resulting data was then analyzed to set an OD600 Reference point and calculate the correction factor, which was applied to the final set of absorbance data in the transformed E. coli DH5 alpha cells
