Signal transmission system from bacteria to human cells ~ Integration of systems derived from bacteria and human cells ~ In our system, the transcription level is controlled by integrating quorum sensing (bacterial cell-to-cell communication) and NF-kB, transcription factor in mammalian cell. We used this system the signal transmission from bacteria to human cells. To validate the system, we conducted the assays shown below.
