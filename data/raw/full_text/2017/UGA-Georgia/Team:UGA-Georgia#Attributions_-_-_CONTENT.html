Attributions

We would like to thank everyone who has made our project and participation in the iGEM competition possible.

The University of Georgia iGEM Team was responsible for nearly all aspects of the project from designing the project, creating the appropriate protocols, and conducting all of the lab work.

We would like to give a special thanks to:

  • Dr. Brian Kvitko; Plant Pathology DepartmentFor allowing our team to utilize his lab space as the primary PI for our project. Dr. Kvitko was critical in helping with troubleshooting and providing lab materials for the project.
  • Amy Smith; Plant Pathology Lab TechnicianFor helping our team organize our space in the lab and helping purchase lab materials
  • Dr. William B. Whitman; Microbiology DepartmentFor helping our team find sponsors within the university to support our project
  • Dr. Jia-Sheng Wang; Department of Environmental Health ScienceFor providing the appropriate environment for safe usage of Aflatoxin B1. Additionally, Dr. Wang as critical in providing the Aflatoxin B1 that was used to test our project
  • Logan JahnkeFor designing and creating our iGEM wiki page

Sponsors
