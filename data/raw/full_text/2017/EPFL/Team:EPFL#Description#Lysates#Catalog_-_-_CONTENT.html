Lysate catalog

In the following we list the characteristics of the cell lysates we produced in our labaratory for the purposes of improving cell lysates characterization and finding the most suited lysates for our detection tool.

For supporting information, click here.

BL21(DE3) Lysate
  • Organism: E. coli
  • Strain: BL21(DE3)
  • Bacterial strain supplier: Novagen1
  • Genotype: F-ompT hsdSB(rB-mB-) gal dcm (DE3)
  • Polymerase(s):
  • E.coli RNA polymerase
  • T7 RNA polymerase (gene under control of the lacUV5 promoter induced by IPTG)
  • Antibiotic Resistances:
  • None
  • Comments:
  • Engineered for transformation and high protein expression
    • Deficient in Ion and ompT protease
    • T7 RNA polymerase expression when induced with IPTG
    M15 Lysate

    • Strain: E. coli M15 DZ291
    • Bacterial strain supplier: Qiagen2
    • Genotype: F- Φ80ΔlacM15 thi lac- mtl- recA+KmR
    • Polymerase(s):
    • E. coli RNA polymerase
  • Antibiotic Resistances:
  • Kanamycin
  • Comments:
  • Deficiency in lacZα
  • blue-white screening or complementation of β-galactosidase

  • Antibiotic Resistance:
  • Ampicillin

  • Comments:
  • T7 RNA polymerase expression when induced with IPTG
