InterLab Study is a measurement of GFP fluorescence among different lab organized by the iGEM community, aiming to produce common, comparable units for measuring GFP with different plate readers. This year (2017), 6 different RBS devices are tested. Our Protocol Transformation: please refer here for the protocol. Calibration and Cell measurement : We were following the protocol provided except that we have measured the Abs595 instead of Abs600 due to the limitation of the plate reader in HKUST.
