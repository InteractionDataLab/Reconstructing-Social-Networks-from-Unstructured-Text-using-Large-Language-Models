There are several designs we show promise to the application: Genetically modified cyanobacteria: Companies can use large ponds or closed systems to harvest biofuel simply by growing these more efficient microalgae. (See more detail: Pigments - Conclusion ) Monitoring device for nitrogen starvation: Growers in open pond can use our device to instantly monitor algae growth and use our genetically modified E. coli for nitrogen starvation accordingly. (See more detail: Device ) Environmentally friendly: We have successfully included suicide mechanism in our nitrogen starvation E. coli, by adding lactose to the pond, the E. coli can be lysed. (See more detail: Nitrogen Starvation ) Valuable pigment side product: Our genetically modified cyanobacteria can also be used to produce valuable pigments like zeaxanthin. (See more detail: Pigments - Zeaxanthin, Lycopene )
