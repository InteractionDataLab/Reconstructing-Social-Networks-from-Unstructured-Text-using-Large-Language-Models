Pichia pastoris complete module with reporter gene : Odr-10 diacetyl receptor (BBa_K1072010), pFUS1 (BBa_K1072023) This part includes Odr-10 receptor under the control of pGAP, a constitutive yeast promoter. When diacetyl binds to Odr-10 a cascade of activation of Ste proteins (endogenous to P. pastoris ) will lead to the binding of Ste12 on pFUS1 promoter, and the expression of RFP should be activated. This system allowed to produce RFP in response to diacetyl. This construction was cloned in pPICZalpha yeast vector. To learn more: see BBa_K1072010, BBa_K1072023 and our results .
