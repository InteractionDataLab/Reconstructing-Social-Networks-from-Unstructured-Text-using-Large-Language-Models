BBa_K2216002 BBa_K2216002 is a basic part consisted of Malonate-semialdehyde dehydrogenase from Erwinia pyrifoliae DSM 12163 BBa_K2216003 BBa_K2216003 is a composite part consisted of Promoter+RBS+ald A+RBS+ Malonate-semialdehyde dehydrogenase+Terminator. The composite part is the whole device that degrade MDA to acetyl-CoA, CO2, NADH, and NADPH. When synthesized by Genscript, it was reported that the sequence is unstable to put on a high copy plasmid, possibly due to the length. The concentration of DNA became very low after plasmid extraction, restriction digestion, and gel extraction.
