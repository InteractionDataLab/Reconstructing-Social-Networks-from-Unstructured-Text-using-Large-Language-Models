Scientific Festival was an integrated survey of scientific and technological education achievements, as well as a stage for students to exhibit their talents on science. We introduced iGEM competition, synthetic biology and its application, and other biological technologies to the senior high school students. The speaker used the words easy to understand and an interesting video to capture audiences’ attentions deeply. Meanwhile, we also gave a lecture for junior high school students. On June 5, 2017, we held a presentation at junior high school of Shenzhen Foreign Languages School.   SFLS.
