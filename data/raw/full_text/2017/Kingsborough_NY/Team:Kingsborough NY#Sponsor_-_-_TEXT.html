Integrated DNA technologies generously provided all of the DNA synthesized for our project. This included gBlock fragments of the different genes involved, primers, and even whole plasmids. Obtaining our target construct (BBa_K2268004) would probably not have been possible without their help. We received a complementary supply of buffers we had difficulty preparing on our own. QIAgen's support made it much easier to purify DNA from our bacteria. A special thanks to Kingsborough Community College - in particular, the Department of Biological Sciences and the Office of College Advancement.
