After the foundation of the school in 1915, it has undergone several changes in the name of the school. Not until the year 2000 did the school finally get the name it now adopts, National Taichung First Senior High School. There are now 75 classes, including 63 general classes, 3 classes of high achievement in math and science, 3 class of high achievement in language, and 3 art-oriented classes.
