Team:UConn/InterLab UConn IGEM 2017 - Interlab Study Team Project ▼ Background Notebook Protocols Results Parts Interlab Safety Attributions Interlab iGEM’s 4th International InterLab Measurement Study aims to develop a “robust measurement procedure for green fluorescent protein”. By standardizing protocols, the InterLab study can test for reliability in results. Our team was one of many teams across the world that measured the fluorescence of GFP to see how precisely the protocol could be followed.
