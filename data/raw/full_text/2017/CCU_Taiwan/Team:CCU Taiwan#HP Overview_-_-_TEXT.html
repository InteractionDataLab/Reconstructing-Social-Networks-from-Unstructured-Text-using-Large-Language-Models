Therefore, we hope to focus on the question that how to make our products in this national policy, our product still have attract and influence with the community . So, we have done a lot of product surveys, Oral status and condition survey、PMR(Potential Market Research)、Online product form survey, in which we also easy to analyze about foreign market. On the other hand, in order to understand what our product condition in market, we base on our products to do a product analysis, competitors analysis. Hoping to analysis pros and cons that compare with existing products, Based on these data, we have building a business model, compared to an innovative start-up company's business model.
