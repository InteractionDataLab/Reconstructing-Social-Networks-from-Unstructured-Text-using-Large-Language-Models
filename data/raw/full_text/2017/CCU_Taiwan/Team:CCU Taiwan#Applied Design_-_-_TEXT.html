In the worst situation, your tooth decay became so serious, that a tooth implantation is required, which is a painful and costly procedure. But with Carindex, the things will change. Carindex is a device designed to detect customer's dental caries risk by simply spitting your saliva into a drawer and put it into Carindex; it will tell you your risk of caries.
