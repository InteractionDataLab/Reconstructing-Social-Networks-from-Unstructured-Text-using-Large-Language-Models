So we designed 2 surveys, one online and the other one face-to-face, in order to know what our fellow citizens are thinking of pollution, science and scientific opportunities in Chile. The surveys were very short and asked 3 important details: 1) Is pollution one of the Chile's main problems? 2) Who is mainly responsible for the pollution? 3) Is Greenhardtii Project useful? Hoping it would be representative, the surveys were done for a month (reaching aprox 700 people). We got answers mainly from young people (15-25 years old), because of the social media.
