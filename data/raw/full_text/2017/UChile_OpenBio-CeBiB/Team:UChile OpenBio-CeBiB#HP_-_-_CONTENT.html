Human Practices Overview

Relating with the community is challenge that every iGEM team must experience, our team was very concerned of how life quality is diminished when air pollution is present and the destructive power of climate change on our geography and life style.

Chile's electric energy generation till depends highly on fossil fuels and mainly on coal and gas thermoelectric plants. Being Quintero’s bay, commune of Puchuncaví, one of the places that concentrate a high density of power plants in the central region. So we decided to help neighbors to improve their life quality together with fighting carbon emissions.
