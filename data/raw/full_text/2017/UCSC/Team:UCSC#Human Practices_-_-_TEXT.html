Integrating human practices into our work is essential, because it allows us to build and shape a relevant potential solution around a real problem. To develop our idea, we first needed to ensure our solution would be beneficial to our intended recipients, while being specific enough to apply our knowledge of synthetic biology. We sought out widespread, real-world problems in order to take the first steps towards creating a healthier world.
