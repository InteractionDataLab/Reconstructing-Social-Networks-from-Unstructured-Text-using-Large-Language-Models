    • Medal Criteria

    Bronze Medal Achievements

    • We completed the team registration, had a great summer and everyone is looking forward to attend the Giant Jamboree.
    • We created this WIKI to document our project and to attribute all the work to the corresponding persons.
    • We participated in this year's InterLab Study.
    • We met all deliverables as listed on the Competition Deliverables page in time.

    Silver Medal Achievements

    • We collaborated with iGEM teams from different countries.
    • We contributed 14 new parts to the registry and validated their functionality.
    • We reached out to experts and engaged with students across Europe to relay what we've learned during our project.

    Gold Medal Achievements

    • We integrated the findings of our Human Practices investigations into our project design.
    • We created mathematical models to guide and complement our wet lab experiments.
