    Situated in the heart of Europe, yet forging connections all over the world, ETH Zurich is pioneering solutions to the global challenges of today and tomorrow. ETH Zurich is a science, technology, engineering and mathematics university in the city of Zurich, Switzerland. It has more than 19,000 students from over 120 different countries. Twenty-one Nobel Prizes have been awarded to students or professors of the Institute in the past, the most famous of whom was Albert Einstein.

    The Department of Biosystems Science and Engineering (D-BSSE) unites biologists, engineers, computer scientists, and mathematicians to work towards a quantitative understanding and purposeful engineering of complex biological. The integration of experiments, theory, and development of novel systems is what sets D-BSSE apart from other departments and makes it an attractive research environment.

    Amgen is one of the world’s leading biotechnology companies and harnesses the powerful tools of cellular and molecular biology and medicinal chemistry to discover, develop and commercialize naturally occurring proteins, antibodies and small molecules. With your financial support, you facilitated our experiments and the set up of our lab. Thank you!

    First released in 2005, Geneious is one the world’s leading bioinformatics software platforms. It is a very powerful and comprehensive suite of molecular biology tools, which enabled us to design and assemble all our DNA constructs. Thank you for providing each team member with a license!

    Genscript is the world’s leading biotech company providing services and products such as gene synthesis, peptide, protein and preclinical drug development service capabilities. Thank you for awarding us the GenScript Scholarship and the great gene synthesis service!

    Integrated DNA Technologies, Inc. is the world's largest supplier of custom nucleic acids. Thank you for the quick high-quality synthesis of all our gBlocks!

    Lucerna-Chem aims at the provision of high quality diagnostic and research reagents for cell biology, molecular biology, microbiology, biochemistry, immunology, histology and neuroscience. Thank you for offering your products at a reduced price!

    Microsynth provides services related to DNA/RNA synthesis, various kinds of DNA/RNA analyses and molecular biology project outsourcing. Thank you for the extremely fast and reliable sequencing of all our constructs!

    The National Centre of Competence in Research (NCCR) Molecular Systems Engineering strives to design and engineer molecular factories to be used in industrial production and in health care. Thank you for the financial support of CATE!

    The Swiss Industry Science Fund (SISF) promotes scientific excellence in research and education in Switzerland and provides financial support for selected initiatives. The fund kick-started our lab. Thank you!

    The Children’s Hospital Zurich is the largest university children’s hospital in Switzerland and one of the leading centers of paediatrics in Europe. We would like to thank Beat Werner from the Center for Magnetic Resonance Research for advising us on our first MRI and focused ultrasound experiments and models, giving us a tour around the MR facilities and giving us insights into the imaging process for patients.
