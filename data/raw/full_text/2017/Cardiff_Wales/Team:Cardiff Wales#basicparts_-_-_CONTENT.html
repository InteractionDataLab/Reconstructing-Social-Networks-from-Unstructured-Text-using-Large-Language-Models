Basic Parts used in our Project

Our team used several basic parts to create our gene constructs. Here is an overview of all the individual parts that our team attempted to work with, each with a description of the part, its function, and what we did with them.Unfortunately we did not add part information to our Judging Form, but our contributions are outlined below.
