    October 21, 2017Columbia iGEM Team We presented our project to the Columbia iGEM team at Columbia and vice versa. We collaborated by giving feedback to each other’s presentation and project.

    October 16, 2017Kingsborough Community College iGEM The Kingsborough Community College team filled out our questionnaire for assessing the risks of our project, in addition to the ethical, health and environmental consequences of it.

    October 4, 2017Greece iGEM Team We collaborated with the Greece iGEM team and answered two of their questionnaires to take part in their risk assessment and large-scale human practices campaign. In exchange, they assessed the risks of our project and the ethical, health and environmental consequences of it by filling out our survey. In return, we confirmed their data by analyzing the DNA samples they provided using a nanodrop machine.
