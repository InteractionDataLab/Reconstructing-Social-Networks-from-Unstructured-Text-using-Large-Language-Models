INSTRUCTOR

Dr. Sujata Roy

Dr. Sujata Roy is an Assistant Professor of Bioinformatics and Computational Biology. She mainly works on In-silico drug designing. She is working to find out the molecular mechanism of main constituent of different natural products. Recently, she studied actions of Withaferin A and Curcumin. Designing of drugs for Cardiovascular disease and Alzheimer's disease based on the binding mode and pharmacophoric features of these compounds is the main goal of her research. Another part of her research is on prediction of ADME and toxicity of compounds using Machine Learning Algorithms. You can reach her at sujataroy1@gmail.com.
