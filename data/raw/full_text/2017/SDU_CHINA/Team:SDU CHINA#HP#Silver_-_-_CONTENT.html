    Giving publicity to citizens in community and science museum

    We went to a lot of communities to show the synthetic biology concept and the experimental design of our team in Jinan; And we displayed booth with the JNFLS high school team in the Science and Technology Museum of Shandong Province. We made different propaganda according to the audience age.

    Volunteering in Cancer Parents' Association and Mutual Organization Meeting

    We participated as volunteers in the 5th China Cancer Parents' Association and Mutual Organization Meeting on July 22-23. The meeting invited international and domestic top experts, and they gave some lectures for the status of long-term rehabilitation of leukemia, the progress of treatment programs, pain management and other aspects of knowledge and experience. We learned a lot about cancer, and understood some treatment of cancer.
