    • Public Education

    In order to expand the influence of synthetic biology and iGEM, we publicized our project, synthetic biology, as well as iGEM to the freshman in our university and to the high school students in Hebei Baoding.

    Freshman propaganda in Shandong University

    In early September, the beginning of a new semester, we mainly publicized to the freshmen in Medical School, Shandong University. We made some posters, videos, and two molecular biology lectures for the freshman. What's more, we held a regular academic salon to discuss the current progress of synthetic biology.

    Teaching and propaganda to high school students in a poverty-stricken areas

    In order to bring our propaganda and advanced ideas to the poverty-stricken areas, in early July, we went to Hebei Baoding Tangjiabing Middle School to teach the middle school students about synthetic biology and we promised to open our lab to them in the next holiday, opening the morden technology world for the children.
