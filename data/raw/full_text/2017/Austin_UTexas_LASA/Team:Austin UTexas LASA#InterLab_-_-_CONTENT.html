    Bronze Medal Criterion #4

    Standard Tracks: Participate in the Interlab Measurement Study and/or improve the characterization of an existing BioBrick Part or Device and enter this information on that part's Main Page in the Registry. The part that you are characterizing must NOT be from a 2017 part number range. For teams participating in the InterLab study, all work must be shown on this page.
