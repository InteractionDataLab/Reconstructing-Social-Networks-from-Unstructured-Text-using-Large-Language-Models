Signal transduction and amplification Expression of downstream genes When our engineered Trichoderma atroviride meets phytopathogens, (take Phytophthora nicotianae as an example), some of report genes will be activated and give warning to our device. How we prove it? ☑︎Cloned the ech42 promoter (the promoter can be elicited when Trichoderma atroviride meets phytopathogens) from Trichoderma atroviride and performed confrontational coculture to test its phytopathogen sensitivity. Once our Trichoderma atroviride has received the signal, the downstream gene will be expressed. How to prove it? ☑︎ We managed to express a special Serine protases in Saccharomyces cerevisiae and tested its activity.
