    This page is designed to help you better understand our model and make your own explorations. It is powered by our software, Nεtro, which enables you to design and explore your own gene transcripton network. In this page, we will illustrate four essential parts of our modelling work. The first two correspond to the Theoretical Basis part, and the last two the Network Modelling part. (Note that all the parameters bellow are shown in natural logarithm.)
