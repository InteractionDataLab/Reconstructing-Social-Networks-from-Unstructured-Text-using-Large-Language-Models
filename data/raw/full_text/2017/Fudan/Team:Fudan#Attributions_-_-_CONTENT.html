    Attribution

    Project Design

    Zijie Yang and Professor Cai contributed the initial idea of this project. All team members participated in the brainstorm and put forward creative ideas. The discussion with Ziyan Yu brought us fresh and excellent thoughts.

    Experiment

    Yijie Pan and Zijie Yang designed most of our genetic circuit. Yijie Pan, Zijie Yang and Tong Fu were responsible for most molecular cloning. Yijie Pan created the method to assemble multiple repeats of transcription factor (TF) response element (RE) based on 3A assembly. The assembly of DNA fragments with highly repetitive sequence is difficult even for companies. Thanks to his contribution, we can successfully build our Part collection.

    Yiming Cai and Yan Liu were responsible for most cell culturing and flow cytometry experiments. Yiming Cai established stable cell lines for the experiments and did most of the transient transfection before cell sorting. Jie Wang and Yefan Hu gave massive help on confocal microscopy, offered direct hand-on help of the related figures of our project. Professor Cai and Jing Fu gave us a lot valuable suggestions and critical comments of the results.

    Interlab

    Ao Feng finish most of the Interlab. iGEM 2017 Fudan_China and iGEM 2017 NYU_Shanghai also contributed to our experiments.

    Model & Software

    Yiheng Wang did all the outstanding works for these two parts.

    Silver & integrated HP

    Ao Feng and Tong Fu were in charge of HP. All team members participated in HP activities. By the way, Ao Feng and Tong Fu were our contacts with the hospitals.

    Education and Public Engagement

Blupha Inc. helped us with public promotion in the field of education in Beijing.
