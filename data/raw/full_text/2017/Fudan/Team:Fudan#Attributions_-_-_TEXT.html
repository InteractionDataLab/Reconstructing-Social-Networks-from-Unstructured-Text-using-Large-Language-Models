Experiment Yijie Pan and Zijie Yang designed most of our genetic circuit. Yijie Pan, Zijie Yang and Tong Fu were responsible for most molecular cloning. Yijie Pan created the method to assemble multiple repeats of transcription factor (TF) response element (RE) based on 3A assembly. The assembly of DNA fragments with highly repetitive sequence is difficult even for companies. Thanks to his contribution, we can successfully build our Part collection. Yiming Cai and Yan Liu were responsible for most cell culturing and flow cytometry experiments. Yiming Cai established stable cell lines for the experiments and did most of the transient transfection before cell sorting. Jie Wang and Yefan Hu gave massive help on confocal microscopy, offered direct hand-on help of the related figures of our project. Professor Cai and Jing Fu gave us a lot valuable suggestions and critical comments of the results.
