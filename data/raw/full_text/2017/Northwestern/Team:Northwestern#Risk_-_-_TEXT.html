To decrease the toxicity of LPS, and render OMVs a safe delivery method, we would need to ensure that OMV administration is non-hazardous. Different approaches have shown promise in reducing the presence of LPS. Detergent-based approaches, although costly and laborious, have proven effective for vaccine administration. More elegant approaches based on genetic modification have recently been explored. For example, the mutation from a hexa to a penta-acylated lipid A leads to the formation of less toxic OMVs, while retaining their key structural features. These considerations were key when we were evaluating the feasibility of this treatment and evaluating the risks.
