The InterLab experiment aims to improve the measurement tools available to both the iGEM community and the synthetic biology community as a whole by comparing GFP fluorescence in different labs using the same protocol, as established by iGEM headquarters. Our results were formally submitted and are summarized in our notebook below. Results and Conclusions As anticipated, our fluorescein standard curve was approximately linear (Figure 1).
