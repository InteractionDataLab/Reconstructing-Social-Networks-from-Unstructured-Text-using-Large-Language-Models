Team Instructor

Prof. Dr. Heribert Warzecha Research: Plant Biotechnology and Metabolic Engineering Prof. Dr. Heribert Warzecha is professor for Plant Biotechnology and Metabolic Engineering at the TU Darmstadt. He initiated the iGEM project at our university in 2012 and supported us in all our efforts since then. His research focuses on the elucidation of natural compound biosynthesis in plants as well as on the production of novel structures by pathway engineering.
