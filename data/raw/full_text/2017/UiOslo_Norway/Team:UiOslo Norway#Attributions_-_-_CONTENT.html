    Throughout this year, everyone on the team has done their fair share of work, as documented in the team members-page and the notebook on the wiki. However, we would never have pulled through had it not been for all the people who assisted and supported us throughout the project. These following people have been of great help for the project, and have our wholehearted gratitude for spending time and energy on helping us pull through.

    Dirk Linke

    Professor at the Department of Bioscience, University of Oslo

    Main supervisor for the team, and has spent large amounts of time and energy on guiding us through the project, as well as helping with registrations and general labwork.

    Eric de Muinck

    Postdoctoral Fellow at the Department of Bioscience, University of Oslo>

    Like Dirk, Eric has been one of the main supervisors for the team. He has given useful advice throughout the project, and has helped on labwork, design of primers and parts, and more.

    Athanasios Saragliadis

    Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the Department of Bioscience, University of Oslo

    Has assisted greatly with general labwork, as well as primer- and parts design.
