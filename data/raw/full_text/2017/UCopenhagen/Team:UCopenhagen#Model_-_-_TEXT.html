The modelling was meant to serve 3 main tasks: 1)As an in silico representation of the combined host-endosymbiont system which we couldn’t physically create in the wet lab given the available amount of time and resources at our disposal. 2)As a way to predict parameter dependencies and feasibility of the real system and thus give inspiration to the design of wet lab experiments and future usages. 3)As a very simple software toolbox to be used in practical implementations of future synthetic endosymbiosis setups.
