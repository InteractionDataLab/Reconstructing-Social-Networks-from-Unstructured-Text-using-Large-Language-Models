We cooperated with the Japanese company GURUNAVI Ink. for research on fermented foods of Tokyo Institute of Technology. This project analysis data of the microorganisms involved in the information and food related to traditional fermented food of Japan, food functionality and nutrition of data, such as by matching the statistical data, scientifically elucidate the food culture of Japan around , Trying to find new value in food. We are researching traditional fermented food of Shiga prefecture called Sushi Sushi, so I was asked to investigate it. We investigated the shops that make Fnazushi for over 100 years and have the history and method of making. From the method of making Funazushi, We got a hint of our research. We were surprised to see that the techniques that have been handed down since ancestors still remain the same as in the past, being performed politely. Through this article We think that it would be good if many people have the opportunity to know the depth of the sushi history and the goodness as a fermented food. http://comp.bio.titech.ac.jp/gnavi/ Crowd funding May 19〜July16 We raised funds to many people through the Internet in a way called cloud funding to acquire research expenses.
