Acknowledgement

We'd like to thank all those who have helped us over the competition. Without their sincere support, our project this year would have not been achieved.

General Support

Prof. OUYANG Qi, Prof. LOU Chunbo, Dr. ZHANG Haoqian, ZHANG Yihao, DONG Yiming and other teachers helped us during our brainstorming and gave us useful suggestions. Prof. OUYANG Qi kindly provided the laboratory to us. ZHANG Yihao gave us meticulous guidance and great help, from the beginning to the end.

Materials Support

Dr. ZHANG Haoqian provided us with an expression plasmid Repressor Generator. Wang Xuan provided us with the original plasmid for terminator characterization. Prof. Johan Paulsson provided us with the plasmids used in repressilator, including pLPT107, pLPT25, pLPT41, and the E. coli ΔclpXP strain to make sure repressilator oscillating stably and synchronously. DNONG Yiming provided us with a few RBSs gotten through random mutation.

Experimental Equipment Support

Prof. OUYANG Qi allowed us to use his lab to do some basic molecular cloning experiments for recombinase characterization. Prof. OUYANG Qi allowed us to use fluorescence microscope, flow cytometry, and microplate reader in his lab. And Prof. LUO Chunxiong and DONG Yiming gave us great help about how to use the equipment. Prof. LUO Chunxiong allowed us to use the lab for microfluidic chips processing and provided us with the relative experimental materials and a lot of instructions and guidance. LI Xiao, LI Lingjun and WANG Shujing provided us with guidance and help on the design, manufacture, and usage about microfluidic chips. Peking University Library provided us with the 3D-printing service for the hardware “Autosnap” manufacture. Core facilities at sequencing platform, School of Life Sciences, Peking University, assisted us with parts sequencing. Dr. DONG Zhi provided us with the relative experimental skills.

Theoretical Guidance

ZHANG Yihao provided us with useful suggestions and help for wiki building and Synbio Wiki construction. Bluepha provided us with some help in Synbio Wiki. Prof. OUYANG Qi, Prof. LOU Chunbo, Prof. WEI Ping, Prof. LIN Yihan, Dr. ZHANG Haoqian and ZHANG Yihao coached us for the presentation and gave us a lot of suggestions in our project . Prof. CHEN Jiang provided us with great suggestions about the design of project and presentation. DONG Yiming provided us with helpful suggestions about our presentation slides.
