2017 International workshop Innovation corner – progress through microbiology 18.05.2017

The event was organized by the Medical university of Sofia and reviewed the newest and most refined technology for genetic alterations in microorganisms and the big role it plays in different aspects of the future technologies – medicine, biology, informatics, production, renewable energy sources etc. IGEM Bulgaria 2017 too took part in the workshop with a presentation devoted to genetic engineering in prokaryotes as well as explaining what the iGEM competition is about and evoking their interest by showing them some of the most interesting projects that were taken by various iGEM teams.
