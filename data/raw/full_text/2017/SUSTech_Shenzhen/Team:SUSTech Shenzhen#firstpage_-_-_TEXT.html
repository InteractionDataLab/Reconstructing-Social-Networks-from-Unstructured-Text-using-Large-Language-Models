Theme of the seminar: Discussion of the project of design, implementation and support of technology for blended learning at NSU within the framework of the program of competitiveness improvement of the national universities. The seminar presented a draft technology for blended learning at NSU, prepared for the competitiveness improvement program. The goal of the project is to create and implement the blended learning model (platform) at NSU. The project is designed to bridge the gap between NSU and the world's top universities in the use of e-learning. It has covered the directions of mechanisms establishment and technical support of blended learning, including customer support service implementation and that of e-learning and learning management, information and methodological support for the educational content development, CPD training programs on the use of technology of blended learning in NSU.
