Awards and Medals

Bronze Medal Requirements

We qualify for the bronze medal because we have met all of the requirements: 1) We are registered for and will be attending the iGEM Jamboree2) We have met all of the deliverables from the iGEM HQ Requirements page, which include this project wiki, a poster presentation at the Jamboree, a 20 minute presentation at the Jamboree, a project attributions page, Registry parts page, sample submissions, the safety form, and the judging form.3) We participated in the Interlab Study, and improved upon an existing part.

Silver Medal Requirements

We qualify for the silver medal because we have met all of the requirements: 1) We submitted a validated part to the registry.2) We completed collaboration with the the BU Wetlab iGEM Team and the Groningen iGEM Team.3) Completed a Human Practices project through Public Engagement and Education.

Gold Medal Requirements

We qualify for the gold medal because we have met the following requirements: 1) We completed integrated human practices by integrating the advice and words of experts into our project2) We improved upon an existing part.3) We developed and utilized a modelling system for choosing guides sequences in our system.
