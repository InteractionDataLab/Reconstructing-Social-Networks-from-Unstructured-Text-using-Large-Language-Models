    • Summary

    This year is the first time ever for that our team from Shenzhen Institute of Technology to participate in iGEM competition. As a polytechnic, students from our school have acquired hand-on operational skills through a wider range of curriculum, including microbiological techniques, pharmaceutical techniques, basic medical course, biochemistry, cell biology, etc. These courses provide us with a solid foundation for grasping new technical skills and engaging in product development. The skills we developed in the past couple of years provide our a good platform that help transferring our laboratory experience to real life applications.

    Training and Experience

    Although most of the students from our school have solid operational skills, we are relatively weak on theoretical studies, especially in molecular biology and professional English. However, we determined to make a difference in the game by putting more efforts. We spent one whole year preparing the project. We had our PI organized an interest group in December last year to start gathering potential members from different departments, and spent the entire winter holiday studying basic knowledge in molecular biology. Our members of the teams were selected through a number of tests, both the skills and attitude were important to the formation of our team. At the beginning of March, we had many rounds of theoretical studies provided by our PI Shuqiao Yu, instructor Zhaoying Guan, adviser Wei zhao Chen, and training management center from our school on topics such as molecular biology, synthetic biology, project management, English and laboratory safety. As the project progresses throughout the year, not only we started comprehending more in depth on the concepts of molecular biology and synthetic biology, but also acquired a number of useful skills like how to build a wiki, design a poster, give a presentation in English, etc.

    Instructors & Adviser

    For a team that is the first time participating in iGEM competition, we could not have done so without the helps from our instructors and advisers. Shuqiao Yu as the "Primary PI" provides training and guidance to us throughout the year. She supports us on molecular biology techniques, such as light control gene expression system construction, verification, pesticide degradation enzyme studies, as well as English training and proofreading. She built a foundation for our project to progress upon. Peng Ying as the ‘Secondary PI’ who provides lots of help on project management, fund allocation, laboratory resource allocation, etc. Zhaoying Guan as the "Instructor"shares her project experience of environmental Engineering with us, and gives us lots of advice on how to design a device. Weizhao Chen as the adviser from Shenzhen University iGEM team, overlooks the project build-up and progress, he is a safe-guard to our project, makes sure that we are always on the right track. Zhanfan Lin as another adviser helps us to develop software, like App, and provides other technology-related support.

    Individuals

    Our team members are from a variety of background and specialties. Most of us who worked in web lab are from Applied Biology Department, and are mostly from 2nd and 3rd year; there are two members who come from department of Information Technology are responsible for wiki building and App design. We also have one member from the School of Design who works primarily on device design for the product .
