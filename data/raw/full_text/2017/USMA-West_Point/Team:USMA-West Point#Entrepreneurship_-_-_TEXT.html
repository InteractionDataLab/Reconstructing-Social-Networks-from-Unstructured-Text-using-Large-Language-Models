One of the Cadets took an Entrepreneurial Management course where he wrote an executive summary, a business model, and marketing strategy to design a competitive advantage given analyzed financial factors. To assist with the development of their plan, the team met and presented their idea to a panel of consultants from Bain and Company in New York City, NY where they were critiqued on their style and knowledge. After working with the consultants to improve their business model, the team presented at the 2017 Mid-Hudson Regional Business Plan Competition where they took First place in the Advanced Technology section.
