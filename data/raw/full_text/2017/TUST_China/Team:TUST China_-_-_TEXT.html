In nature, there are many cellulose originated from different source, such as plant cellulose, seaweed cellulose and bacterial cellulose. In between, bacterial cellulose is polymerized from pure D-glucose with high purity and doesn’t contain other polysaccharides. As the advancement of molecular biology and deeper understanding in the nature of bacterial cellulose along with its synthesis mechanism in recent year, bacterial cellulose has applied in food, medicine, chemical industry and worsted fabrics. food face blood tube wound healing drug However , low yield and high costs of bacterial cellulose limit its wider application.
