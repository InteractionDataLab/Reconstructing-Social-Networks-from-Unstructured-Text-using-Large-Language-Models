Team:Tartu TUIT/Project/LabDiary Home Team Project InterLab Studies Parts Safety Human Practices Awards Judging Form Attributions and Sponsors Project: Yeasthylene The demand on ethylene has only been increasing during the last decade. It is used as an essential building block in many chemical compounds.The main aim of our project is to find an alternative and biological way of producing ethylene. That is why we have decided to genetically engineer yeast cells to produce ethylene from sucrose.
