Safety Health risks to lab researchers are minimal because the organisms used are all Biosafety level 1─our project does not involve any genes from human pathogens or plant pathogens. All precautions with respect to recombinant DNA are observed: All waste is autoclaved before being thrown away. Researchers practice aseptic technique, and frequent hand washing. Bench surfaces are disinfected with ethanol.
