ArtScienceBangalore consists of ten undergraduate art and design students in their 2nd or 3rd years of study at the Srishti School of Art, Design and Technology. We have been assisted, guided and mentored by Yashas Shetty, an artist-in-residence at the Centre for Experimental Media Arts (CEMA) and the National Centre for Biological Sciences (NCBS) along with Navneet Rai,a graduate student and Dr. Mukund Thattai,Assistant Professor also from NCBS.
