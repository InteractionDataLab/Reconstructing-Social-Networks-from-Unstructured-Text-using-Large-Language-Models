The ligation was carried out in 37°C for 1h and then inactivated for 15min. at 65°C. A 200ul batch of chemocompetent bacteria was transformed with 3ul of ligation mix and incubated on petri dishes containing LB medium supplemented with ampicilin, X-gal and IPTG.
