The mgtc promoter digest mix was prepared as follows: 10μl purified promoter 2μl Tango buffer (Fermentas) 1μl SpeI enzyme 1μl XbaI enzyme The solution was topped up with H2O to the final volume of 20μl. The digest was carried out in 37°C for 3h. and then inactivated in 80°C for 20min. The digested plasmid was separated on 1% agarose gel. The plasmid backbone was extracted from agarose gel using the GelOut kit (A&A Biotechnology) accrding to the manufacturers protocol (in the total volume of 50μl). The ligation mix was prepared as follows: 50μl purified backbone 20μl digested promoter 8μl T4 ligase buffer (Fermentas) 2μl T4 ligase (Fermentas) The ligation was carried out in 18°C overnight.
