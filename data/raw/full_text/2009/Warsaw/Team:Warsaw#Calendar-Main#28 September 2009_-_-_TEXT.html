Equal concentrations of both oligonucleotides were mixed together, heated to 95°C for 15 min. and left to cool. The pSB1A3 plasmid was digested with XbaI and PstI and the resulting backbone was isolated (all as described previously). The ligation was carried out in 18°C for 4h.
