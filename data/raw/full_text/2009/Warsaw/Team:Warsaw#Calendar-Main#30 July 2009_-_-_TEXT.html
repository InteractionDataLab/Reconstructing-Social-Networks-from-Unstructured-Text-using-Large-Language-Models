The liquid cultures were incubated for 6h at 37°C with aeration. The plasmids were isolated according to the protocol (see at the end of the page). The plasmids were digested with EcoRI and PstI enzymes. The results were visualised with electrophoresis on 1% agarose gel. NOTE: Steps 3 to 5 were not carried out by me and I cannot take credit for them.
