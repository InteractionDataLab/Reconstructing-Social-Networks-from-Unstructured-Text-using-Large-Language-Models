The digest mix was prepared as follows: 20μl purified plasmid 3μl Orange Buffer (Fermentas) 1μl PstI Enzyme (Fermentas) 1μl EcoRI Enzyme (Fermentas) 5μl H2O The digest was carried out in 37°C for 4h. The results were visualized on 2% agarose gel.
