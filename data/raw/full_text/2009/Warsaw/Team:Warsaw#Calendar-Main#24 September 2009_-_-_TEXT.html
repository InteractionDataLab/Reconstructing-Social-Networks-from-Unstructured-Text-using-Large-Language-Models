Task 1: DNA digest to create following biobricks: BBa_K177037 BBa_K177044 Methods: Constructs to digest: BBa_K177035 - PstI, SpeI BBa_K177035 - PstI, XbaI BBa_K177036 - PstI, SpeI BBa_K177044 - PstI, XbaI Reaction mixture composition: 15 μl purified plasmid DNA product 1 μl enzyme 1 (Fermentas) 1 μl enzyme 2 (Fermentas) 5 μl Buffer Tango (Fermentas) 28 μl MQ water Reactions were carried out 7 hour and subsequently they were inactivated via heating.
