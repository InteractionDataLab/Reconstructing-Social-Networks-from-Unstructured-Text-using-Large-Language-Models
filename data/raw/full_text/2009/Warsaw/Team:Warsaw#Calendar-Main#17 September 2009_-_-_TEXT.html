Cloning of the cro-box into the pSB1A3 plasmid Kamil Tasks: Ligation to the plasmid Bacteria transformation Methods: The ligation mix was prepared as follows: 20μl purified plasmid backbone 10μl Cro-Box 4μl LIgase Buffer (Fermentas) 5μl 30% PEG 1μl T4 Ligase (Fermentas) The ligation was carried out in 18°C for 4h. A fresh batch of chemocompetent bacteria was transformed according to the protocol.
