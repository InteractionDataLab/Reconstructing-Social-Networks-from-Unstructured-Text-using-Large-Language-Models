Plasmid isolation Plasmid isolation was performed on the cultures of E.coli TOP10 containing the above mentioned plasmids with the "Sygma-Aldrich™ GenElute™ Plasmid Miniprep Kit". From each tube 4mL of culture (8mL for pNL29) was collected in a 2.0mL cup (tubes from pArsR-GVP, GVP and pNL29 were combined), and the cells were pelleted by centrifugation for 1 min. at max. speed and the supernatant discarded.
