O/n @ 37dg Dry Jasper finished the GlpF model by fitting a Michaelis-Menten equation to the raw data (using the method of Goudar1999 ). Mathematica proved to be the best tool for this, as it is one of the few programs that has the Lambert W function built-in (as ProductLog). Furthermore, an interactive model was added to the Wiki demonstrating the results. There is a specific interaction between the ArsA and ArsB proteins. The expression of the ArsB gene is required for membrane association of the ArsA protein. arsB expression is not dependent on gene dosage, so that both arsenical extrusion and resistance are limited by the amount of ArsB protein in the membrane. Tisa1990 . There are two As(III) binding sites on the arsR Summers2009 .
