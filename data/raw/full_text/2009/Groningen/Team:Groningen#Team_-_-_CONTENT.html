Our Team At A Glance

Our team!

Welcome to the website of the iGEM Groningen team! We are an interdisciplinary team of 11 enthusiastic students from the University of Groningen situated in the not-too-big city of Groningen in the north of the Netherlands. You can contact us by mail.

Our team consists of the following student-members:

  • Jolanda Witteveen (Biomedical Technology): Chair, Project Manager
  • Sven Jurgens (Molecular Biology): Treasurer
  • Jasper van de Gronde (Computational Science and Visualization): Configuration Manager, Modeller
  • Michael Verhoeven (Chemistry): PR Officer
  • Nienke Kuipers (Molecular Biology): Minutes secretary and Lab manager
  • Steven Jelle Meijer (Molecular Biology): Facility Manager Haren
  • Wilfred Poppinga (Medical Pharmaceutical Sciences): Vice Chair, Treasurer
  • Paul Schavemaker (Molecular Life Sciences): Minutes secretary
  • Frans Bianchi (Molecular Biology): Modeller
  • Klaas Bernd Over (Applied Physics): Modeller
  • Annelies van Keulen (Molecular Biology/Psychology): Modeller

Our advisors

  • prof. dr. Oscar Kuipers: Molecular Genetics (Head)
  • prof. dr. Jan Kok: Molecular Genetics
  • prof. dr. Bert Poolman: Biochemistry; Centre for Synthetic Biology (Director)
  • prof. dr. Roel Bovenberg: Synthetic biology and Cell engineering; Corporate Scientist Biotechnology, DSM
  • dr. Dirk Slotboom: Enzymology
  • iGEM Groningen 2008. Especially Auke van Heel & Martijn Herber
