Dephosphorylation of the vector via CAP. Do not (preferably) clean up PCR product from gel, but use ~1uL for checking on gel and clean the rest with a PCR cleanup kit. Use conventional restriction enzymes for digestion of the insert and vector, as these can be heat-inactivated. Increase the amount of insert from 1:3 to 1:6 or 1:10. Digest insert with XbaI and SpeI, these give compatible overhangs but these sites are closer to the gene (and have longer spacer).
