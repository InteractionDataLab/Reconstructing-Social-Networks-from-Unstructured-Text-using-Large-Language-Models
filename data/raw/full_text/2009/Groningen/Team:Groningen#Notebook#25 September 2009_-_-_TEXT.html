Harvest E.coli WT (pSB1A2 + pLac), E.coli + pArsR-RFP and E.coli + pLac-fMT by spinning down @ 6000rpm for 20min in Sorvall centrifuge. Wash 2x with TB74S buffer. Resuspend upto OD=10. Dry 1ml sample for 2hrs @ 65°C. Do uptake experiments with the different samples (1gr cells @ 22mg/ml concentration) At As(III) concentrations for 1hr: 0, 10, 20, 50 and 100uM As(III). And at time points with 10uM As(III) at: 0, 10, 20, 40 and (60)min. For fMT only the concentration samples were taken! Spin down, wash with TB74S and resuspend in 10ml ddH2O. Dry for 2days @ 65°C. The determined dry weight was 5x lower than expected, so the sample which should have contained at least 1gr dry cells only contained ~100-200mg dry cells.
