The Management Team of the Kluyver Centre has decided to offer the opportunity to all Dutch iGEM -teams 2009 to present their idea during the 2nd Programme Day of the Kluyver Centre. This event will take place at the Wageningse Berg on the 24th of September. Every team will have 10 minutes to present in front of the scientific community of the Kluyver Centre.
