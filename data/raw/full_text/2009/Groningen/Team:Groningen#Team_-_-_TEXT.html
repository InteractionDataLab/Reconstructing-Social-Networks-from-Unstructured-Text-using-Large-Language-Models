Welcome to the website of the iGEM Groningen team! We are an interdisciplinary team of 11 enthusiastic students from the University of Groningen situated in the not-too-big city of Groningen in the north of the Netherlands . You can contact us by mail . Our team consists of the following student-members: Jolanda Witteveen (Biomedical Technology): Chair , Project Manager Sven Jurgens (Molecular Biology): Treasurer Jasper van de Gronde (Computational Science and Visualization): Configuration Manager , Modeller Michael Verhoeven (Chemistry): PR Officer Nienke Kuipers (Molecular Biology): Minutes secretary and Lab manager Steven Jelle Meijer (Molecular Biology): Facility Manager Haren Wilfred Poppinga (Medical Pharmaceutical Sciences): Vice Chair , Treasurer Paul Schavemaker (Molecular Life Sciences): Minutes secretary Frans Bianchi (Molecular Biology): Modeller Klaas Bernd Over (Applied Physics): Modeller Annelies van Keulen (Molecular Biology/Psychology): Modeller Our advisors prof. dr.
