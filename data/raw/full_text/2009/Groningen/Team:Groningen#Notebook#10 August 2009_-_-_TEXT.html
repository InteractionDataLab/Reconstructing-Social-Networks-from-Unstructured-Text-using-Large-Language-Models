Colony PCR as done for pSB1AC3-HML+RFP and pSB3K3-HML+RFP though the PCR program was slightly modified: Initial denat 95°C for 5min Touchdown 10x denat 95°C for 30sec anneal 62-50°C for 30sec elong 72°C for 30sec Cycles 20x denat 95°C for 30sec anneal 58°C for 30sec elong 72°C for 30sec Final elong 72°C for 5min Hold 4°C infinite Run on 1%Agarose gel.
