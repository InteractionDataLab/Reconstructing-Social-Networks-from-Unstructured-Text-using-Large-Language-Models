[ edit ] Vanillin Production Restriction digest on EF1, EF2, EF3 and EF4 Gel electrophoresis of digestion, result: EF1 and EF3 appear to have an insert. The insert of EF4 is too short, between 1500 and 2000 bp (might just be ech) and EF2 appears to have been cut by only 1 enzyme Also, restriction digest overnight results in an extra very weak intensity band at 600 and one between 1500 and 2000 bp This result has been seen before on just ech. It might be due to an extra restriction site on the ech gene. The two bands could result from this same band. The addition of both bands would then be the same as the insert.
