DESIGN The final project consisted of a simplified biothermometer design. Using the 32°C ribothermometer linked to either RFP or GFP would produce a system with a visual response to differences in temperature. There were two levels for this: K235034 and K235035 used a lactose promoter and repressor to regulate RFP and GFP respectively; and K235036 and K235037 were directly coupled to RFP or GFP respectively.
