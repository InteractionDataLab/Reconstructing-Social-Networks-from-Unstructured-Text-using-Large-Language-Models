Site map To ligate two biobricks into a base vector. Part 1: 2 µl Part 2: 2 µl Destination Plasmid: 2 µl 10X T4 Ligase Buffer: 2 µl T4 Ligase: 1 µl H2O: 11 µl Incubate at room temperature for 10 minutes and then inactivate the enzyme at 80°C for 20 minutes. Transform 5 μl of the ligation product into 50 μl of competent DH5-alpha E. coli cells.
