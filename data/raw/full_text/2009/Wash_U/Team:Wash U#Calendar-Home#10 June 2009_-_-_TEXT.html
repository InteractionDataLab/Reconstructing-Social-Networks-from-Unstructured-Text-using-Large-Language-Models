Using 5ul of DNA (diluted 1:16 in TE) Allow the DNA and competent cells to sit on ice for 30 minutes Heat shock at 42ºC for 60 sec in water bath. Recover on ice for 5 min. Incubate at 37ºC for 2 hr while the tubes are rotating.
