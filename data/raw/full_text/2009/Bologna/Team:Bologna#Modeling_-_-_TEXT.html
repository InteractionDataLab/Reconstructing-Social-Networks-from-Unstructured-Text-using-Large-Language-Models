Einstein Contents 1 Introduction 2 Mathematical Model 3 Reactions 4 Differential Equations 5 Simulations 5.1 T-REX device Introduction We developed a mathematical model to simulate the response of the testing circuit (Fig. 1). Figure 1 - Genetic Circuit to test CIS and TRANS' mRNA functionality Mathematical Model Transcription and translation processes are considered similar to a second order kinetics like an enzymatic reaction: RNA polymerase and ribosome perform enzymes' role, while gene promoter and RBS sequence act as substrates. The binding between enzyme and substrate leads to the formation of a complex, yielding to the final product: mRNA for the polymerase-promoter complex and protein for the ribosome-RBS complex.
