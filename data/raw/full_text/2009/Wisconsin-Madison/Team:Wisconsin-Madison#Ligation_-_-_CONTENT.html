Ligation

Concentration

  • 1 backbone : 5 insert
  • insert: [100 x (insert size bp/backbone size bp) x 5 / concentration(ng)] μL

Thermocycler

  • users: Dan A: Lig
  • 2 hours 16 C or overnight

Materials

  • 1μL T4 ligase
  • 1 μL T4 ligase buffer
  • backbone + insert =< 8μL (remainder H20)
