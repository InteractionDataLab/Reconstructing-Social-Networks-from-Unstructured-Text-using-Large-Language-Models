These gene configurations (or circuits) have the capability to integrate signals and trigger events after a delay from the initial detection event. As described before, such characteristic is needed in our project; therefore we considered two approaches based on transcriptional cascades. After some preliminary analysis, we chose to develop the approach termed negative synthetic transcriptional cascade. Preliminary approaches Negative transcriptional cascade Based on the work of Hooshangi S et al 2005 [ 4 ] and the project requirements, the scheme was proposed.
