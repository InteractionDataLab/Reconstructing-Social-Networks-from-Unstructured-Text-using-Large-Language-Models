Key messages

Every audience has its own key message, the key messages are differentiated to specific audiences.

Scientists

  • Students from TU Delft are participating the iGEM competition.
  • Your knowledge and assistance is very useful to iGEM teams.
  • iGEM competition is a useful tool to gain the interest of student for Synthetic Biology.

In science interested public

  • Students from TU Delft are participating the iGEM competition.
  • Synthetic Biology has many future potential benefits for the society.
  • Synthetic Biology is not hazardous and it is not (yet) possible to create life.
  • Scientists and students are aware of the public impact of their work and do consider the ethical consequences of their work.

Sponsors and potential sponsors

  • Sponsors are crucial to a iGEM team.
  • We spend sponsored budget carefully.
  • Sponsoring an iGEM team is a good promotion.

Students

  • Participating the iGEM competition is very instructive and fun.
  • Participating iGEM competition is a nice alternative to Design Course, for Life science & Technology students.

Future students

  • Synthetic Biology is a new and interesting field.
  • Bsc and Msc programs in Genetic Engineering are very interesting.
