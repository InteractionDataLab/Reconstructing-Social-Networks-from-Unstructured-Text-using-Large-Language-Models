Daniel Prepared the glycerol stocks for the remaining culture of the 9 biobricks [BBa_E0040 - GFP, BBa_C0040 - TetR, BBa_C0051 - cl, BBa_I12006 - λp, BBa_E1010 - mRFP1 of Delay module and BBa_I714031 - OriT-R, BBa_J23100 - strong promoter, BBa_E0840 - GFP generator and BBa_I13522 - pTet GFP of Conjugation module] for future use. The protocol is as follows: 1. Centrifuge 1ml of culture in eppendorf at 8000rpm for 3 minutes at 15°C. 2. Decant the supernatant. 3. Add 1 ml of culture and centrifuge at 8000rpm for 3 minutes at 15°C. 4. Decant the supernatant. 5. Add 1 ml of culture and centrifuge at 8000rpm for 3 minutes at 15°C. 6. Decant the supernatant. 7. Add 1 ml of culture and centrifuge at 8000rpm for 3 minutes at 15°C. 8. Decant 0.5ml of the supernatant. 9. Resuspend the dense pellet throughly, add 0.5 ml of 10% glycerol and store in -80°C freezer.
