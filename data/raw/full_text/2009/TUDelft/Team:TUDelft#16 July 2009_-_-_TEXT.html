For 20 ml plates just adding 20 µl of any antibiotic would be sufficient. Thus all the kanamycin containing biobricks must be redone. I got colonies in all the tubes except the GFP(E0040). So I need to re-culture them from plates and oomph plates are getting overgrown colonies so was transferred fridge after covering with parafilm.
