    September, 7th

    In the morning we have prepared culture of A2 to stock in glycerol and we have prepared LB medium.

    In the afternoon we have incubated in adequate medium:

    • B9 (10 ml LB+Amp)
    • B10 (10 ml LB+Amp)
    • T9002 (10 ml LB + Amp)

    for a qualitative test of ethanol production,

    • A9pg (5 ml LB + Amp)
    • A16 (5 ml LB + Amp)
    • A8 (5 ml LB + Amp)
    • A2 (5 ml LB + Amp)
    • RBS33 (5 ml LB + Amp)

    to perform an aTc induction test,

    • A15 (25 ml LB + Amp)
    • pSB3K3 (25 ml LB + Kan)
    • A14-1 L (5 ml LB + Amp)

    to miniprep tomorrow.

    These coltures have been incubated at 37Â°C, 220 rpm overnight. Tomorrow, we will miniprep A14-1 L and midiprep A15 and 3K3 (pSB3K3). We perform midiprep on pSB3K3 because it is a low copy number plasmid and on A15 because it is a lysis cassette, so for leackage only "few" cells survive.

    Glycerol stocks of A2-3 have been stored at -80Â°C, named A2-3 and A2-3 bis.The previous stock has been thrown away.

    Preparation of a qualitative test to assay the presence of ethanol

    We have also induced coltures B9 and B10 incubated yesterday morning at 37Â°C 220 rpm in flasks and grown overnight. In the morning the coltures have been diluited to reach the desired OD (0.03), then re-incubated for 4 hours and induced with 3OC6-HSL 10nM. The induced coltures have been incubated at 18.00 o'clock and will stay overnight. Flasks have been ermetically closed, to allow fermentation. This qualitative test is performed to assay the presence oh ethanol.

    Tomorrow we will use the "Ethanol assay kit" to test the presence of ethanol!!

    September, 8th

    A first test with ethanol assay kit has been made using coltures B9 and B10 induced the evening before and using as blank the reaction mix of Kit without ethanol. In the evening, we have also made a measure of blank made by LB + mix. The test have been performed on the supernatant of the coltures, obtained by centrifugation for 20 minutes 13000rpm.

    • A14 L-1 have been miniprepped, with a yield of 33ng/ul
    • pSB3K3 and A15 have been midi-prepped and tomorrow will be digested E-P and ligated

    aTc induction test:

    • one of the coltures incubated the day before has been "accidentally" centrifuged, and has shown no growth in the next 3 hours, so the coltures have been re-inoculated, as the day before:
    • A9pg (5 ml LB + Amp)
    • A16 (5 ml LB + Amp)
    • A8 (5 ml LB + Amp)
    • A2 (5 ml LB + Amp)
    • RBS33 (5 ml LB + Amp)

    to perform a new aTc induction test, tomorrow.
