    December, 16th

    Official presentation of the project "Engineering E. coli to multiplex and demultiplex signals" by UNIPV-Pavia iGEM 2008 team to the main academic authorities:

    • Rector of 'Università Degli Studi di Pavia', Prof. Angiolino Stella
    • Engineering Dean of 'Università Degli Studi di Pavia', Prof. Carlo Ciaponi
    • Director of Department of Biomedical Engineering, Prog. Giovanni Magenes

    During the conference, the president of 'Fondazione Costa' awarded Lorenzo and Mattia, 2008 iGEM students, for the excellent results achieved in the previous competition. All the studentes interested in took part in the 2009 iGEM team were present.
