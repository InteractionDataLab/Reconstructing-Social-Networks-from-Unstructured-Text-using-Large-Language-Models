The DTU iGEM '09 team was formed on the initiative of assistant professor, Kiran Raosaheb Patil. The six of us quickly came together, and with inspiration from our four advisors, a project was formulated. The organisation of the team is based on the bottom-up approach, and from day one we have been very self-suffient, spearheading fundraising, laboratory work, and all other practical details ourselves.
