BioBricks

The USU iGEM team has successfully constructed 49 BioBrick parts and devices. Of these, we have had time to demonstrate the functionality of 8 BioBricks, and had 41 of them sequenced. Check out our BioBrick Registry page to see all of these parts and to find more detailed information about them.
