Team:NYMU-Taipei/PCR

From 2009.igem.org

Usage:

  1. initial denature time
  2. cycle denature time
  3. cycle primer-stick time
  4. cycle assembly time
  5. assembly time

Extra params:

  • Mix:
  • polName=change polymerase name
  • pol=change polymerase amount
  • ddH20=change water amount
  • fp=forward primer name
  • rp=reverse primer name
  • Protocol:
  • cycles=change amount of cycles
  • a0=change the initial denature temperature
  • a1=change the cycle denature temperature
  • b1=change the cycle primer-stick temperature
  • c1=change the cycle assembly temperature
  • c0=change the last assembly temperature
