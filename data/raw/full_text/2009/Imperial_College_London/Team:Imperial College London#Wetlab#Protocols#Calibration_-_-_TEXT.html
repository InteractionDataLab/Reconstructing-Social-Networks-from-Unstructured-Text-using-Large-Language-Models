The relation of absorbance reading to number of cells varies with different cell strains. We are therefore doing one for Top-10. CA2:External GFP fluorescence External fluorescence * To relate how the fluorescence observed can be correlated with number of purified GFP molecules CA3:Internal GFP fluorescence Intracellular fluorescence * To relate how various intracellular concentrations of GFP leads to different observed fluorescences.
