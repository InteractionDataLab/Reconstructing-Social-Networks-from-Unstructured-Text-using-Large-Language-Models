Bacteria

Chemotropes which reduce Fe3:

  • S. Oneidensis
  • R. ferireducines

Phototropes which Oxidize Fe2+

  • R. palustris TIE-1 strain – hopefully transformed!

Genetic manipulation

  • P. aeruginosa- source of OprB gene[1] (membrane porin sequence).
  • pJQ200SK plasmid suicide vector for R. palustris.
  • E. coli- for plasmid production/ purification.

Primers for isolating and manipulating genes

Pseudomonas aeruginosa OprB primer 

  Forward: 5' ATGTACAAGAACAAGAAAACC 3'
  Reverse: 5' TCAGAACACCGTCTGGATC 3'

Mutegen primer to change GCTGCA to GCAGCA in OprB gene so it can be a biobrick 

  Forward: 5' CTGTACCAGCCGCaGCAGGACACCGAG 3'
  Reverse: 5' CTCGGTGTCCTGCtGCGGCTGGTACAG 3'

R. palustris OprB primer -R. palustris might be upregulated 

  Forward: 5' ATGCTGCCGTCGCGCTGGCT 3'
  Reverse: 5' TCAGAACTGCGTGCGCAGTCG 3'
