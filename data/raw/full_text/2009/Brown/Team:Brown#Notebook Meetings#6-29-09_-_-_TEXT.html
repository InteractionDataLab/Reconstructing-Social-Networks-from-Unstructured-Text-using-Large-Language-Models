Gary suggests looking at the original paper for a sequence that it minimally required for the promoter. “It may just be the nature of the beast” – Gary. Adrian has an answer: Ecocyc.org is a giant database for promoters. 9:31: Team 1 update. They’ve been working on getting EV131 out of pBlueScript. But when they ran the gels they didn’t see 2 bands. Look at the pNoTat sequence – look at where restriction site is relative to EV131 codon.
