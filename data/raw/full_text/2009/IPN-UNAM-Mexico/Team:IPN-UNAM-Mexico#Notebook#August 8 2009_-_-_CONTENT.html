August 8th, 2009

6 Biobricks were ligated (see 06/08/09) and they grew in the following plates:

  • 15: 3 lig2 Amp/Kan
  • 2:3 lig1 Amp
  • 2:3 lig2

 

Falcon tubes were inoculated with colonies picked up from the ones that grew in the plates. We took 2 from 15:13 and 2 from 2:3 with a total of 4 Falcon tubes with their respective antibiotic resistances.
