The Stanford iGEM team consist of ten undergraduate students, ten graduate student mentors and four faculty advisors. This summer, students worked full time throughout to engineer a synthetic biological system. The results from experimentation will be presented in the form of posters and a 20 minute presentation at the Jamboree. This is the first time that our team is competing.
