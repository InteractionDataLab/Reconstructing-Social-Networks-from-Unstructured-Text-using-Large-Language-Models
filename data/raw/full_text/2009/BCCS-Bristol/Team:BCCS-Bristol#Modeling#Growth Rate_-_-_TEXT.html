Click here for a link to the paper. This means that the growth of the bacterium can be divided into 3 phases. Phase 1 From time zero, T_0, to the time when formation of the septum begins, T_c. Phase 2 From the time when formation of the septum begins, T_c, to the time T_2. Phase 3 From the time T_2 to the generation time, T_g. At each phase there is an individual linear growth rate, or gradient, a. Values for the parameters are shown in the table below: Parameter * Mean S.D. a_1 0.067 0.027 a_2 0.122 0.035 a_3 0.174 0.049 T_c 11.42 6.78 T_2 21.15 6.02 T_g 25.08 7.97 * Times are in minutes and growth rates are in micrometres/minute.
