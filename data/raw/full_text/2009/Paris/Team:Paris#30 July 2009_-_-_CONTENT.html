    New competent DH5α by RbCl

    • Competent bacteria
    • 1/50 ON culture of New DH5α
    • OD600=0,6
  • Transformation with puc19 (10pg/µl)
  • 250µl of RbCl competent DH5α
  • 12,5µl puc19
  • Leave 20 min in ice
  • 45s at 42°C
  • Leave 2 min in ice
  • Add 1ml of hot LB (42°C)
  • 1h at 37 under agitation
  • Put on plate (LB agar + Amp)
