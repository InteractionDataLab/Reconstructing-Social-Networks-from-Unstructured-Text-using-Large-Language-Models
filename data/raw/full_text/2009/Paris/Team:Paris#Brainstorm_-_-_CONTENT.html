    In this section, every single thought and train of thoughts will be unveiled !

    We offer you the opportunity to find the answer you are looking for, or to discover new questions and reflexions. In order to satisfy your demand we choose to proceed by ideas. Furthermore you will follow the team step by step in his reflexion, we would also be very grateful if you wanted to share some thoughts with us.
