The idea In the fun application part of our brainstorming, we thought that if we could have bacteria that could be responsive to radio wave such as the wifi frequencies, we could build a device to give the strength of the signal. 2. How to Is there any protein or ARN that could change its conformation under a radio wave signal ? If yes, with a touch of fluorescent protein we could give a graduate response to the signal. 3.
