The sinequanone conditions : we tried to achieve this aim without sacrifying important proprieties of our message : specific, repeatable , multidirectional. It seems that we have two possible ways : the ABC transporters or the two component systems. ABC transporters and two component systems are natural transport systems (export or import) of nutriments or toxines, and even information but the mecanism is mostly unknown so the DNA-containing OMVs would have been a useful mean of information transport if it would not be so unknown.
