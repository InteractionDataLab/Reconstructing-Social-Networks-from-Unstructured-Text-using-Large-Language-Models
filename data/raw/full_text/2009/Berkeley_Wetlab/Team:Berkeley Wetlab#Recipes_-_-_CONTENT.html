10X TBST

31.4 g TRIS HCl 80g NaCl 10ml Tween-20 bring volume to 1000ml pH 7.5

1X TBS

605 mg Tris 876 mg NaCl 80 ml H2O pH 7.4 bring volume to 100 ml

Artificial Seawater

500 ml ddH2O 12.2 g NaCl 2.6 g MgCl2 .57 g CaCl2

Tris Buffer + 2% SDS

50 mM Tris 150 mM NaCl 2% SDS pH 7

Precipitation Buffer

2 mL of acetic acid Add concentrated NaOH until the pH is 5 Add water to reach 10 mL total volume volume 0.2 g ZnCl Add 40 mL ethanol
