EMILY The Learning Commences Today we met in our lab for the first time. We took care of some more logistical questions and started to learn about our project. We started off with the basics, going back over some powerpoints on transcription, translation, DNA, RNA, etc. I also started looking at team Wikis from 2007 and 2008 to start getting some ideas for our 2009 Wiki. FAHD First day of work, May 4th 2009 Today was my first day of work. Today we learned the basic principles of synthetic biology such was a DNA and RNA. We also did some team bonding stuff such introducing ourselves to other teammates and learning about their backgrounds and interests in synthetic biology and iGEM. IMAN Work place assigned in the lab Great news is that we have our little Membrane Computing Headquarters setup over at main campus on ICT 7th floor. We have the computers setup, on the network, have our account created. Today was also more on getting familiar with the setup and getting know the guys on this side. We also started reading papers provided to us by our mentors on synthetic biology and watched few iGEM youtube videos. Cool beans! JEREMY Start of a great group Today was the first day of work, where I had the opportunity to finally get to know every member of our team. We discussed our team goals and administrative issues. We also started our journey into molecular biology as our supervisors started teaching us some of the basics in the field. KATIE Enter Second Life I was able to set up my Second Life account along with the rest of the Second Life team. I explored Help Island and learned to move my avatar as well as teleport to different places by searching for the specific names of places. I went through an area called Caledon, where I read through all of the posters displayed throughout the island and learned about: How to manage my inventory Good areas to start out if you are new to Second Life, which I may have a look at in my free time How to modify my avatar, although I have not had much success with this so far KEVIN Wiki-seeing Although there were not many teams that have updated their wiki's, including our's, I began looking into some wiki's to see what other teams are upto. MANDY Second Life Exploration Got my Second Life account today :) Ran through a tutorial on a pirate themed island. So far, I learned how to move around, touch things, change my camera angles, and fly. The inventory controls are still confusing to me so I still need to work on that.
