    EMILY
    Campus Fair
    This morning I spent some time at our Campus Fair booth. I helped set up in the morning and then I was in charge of preparing extra 'Bacteria kits' for our craft as well as helping some of the younger kids with the glue and scissors. We taught kids a little bit about bacteria, explaining what DNA, genes and flagella were, etc. I also talked briefly with some of the parents/ older siblings about our project. It was a lot of fun and it was great to see the kids so engaged!
