I found quite a few of the protein structures but unfortunately couldn't find the picture of protein in their phosphorylated form . Therefore I decided just to place an image of a phosphate beside the protein to signify phosphorylation. I also wondered if credits should be given for the images and how to go about doing it best . I couldn't find a 3D image of AI2 so I decided it was best to use a 2D image. EMILY BHSC Research Symposium Tonight Mandy and I presented our project at the BHSC Research Symposium. Mandy presented the Second Life video while I presented Intro, Lab, Modeling and Human Practices. After the oral presentation, we also participated in the Poster session with two of our posters. We set up a demo of Second Life on on one of our computers and let people look around at our Synthetic Kingdom. This symposium was really good because it gave us the chance to show our work to many of our peers in Health Sciences. This was also more practice presenting, answering questions and explaining about our poster, which is important as the iGEM Jamboree is just around the corner. FAHD BHSC SYMPOSIUM Our University of Calgary iGEM team was part of tonight's BHSC Symposium held at the University of Calgary Health Sciences Centre. I attended the Symposium.
