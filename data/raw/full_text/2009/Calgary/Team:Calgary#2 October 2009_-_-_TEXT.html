Plans for next meeting : Work on the wiki both for Modelling and Notebook EMILY BHSC Symposium and Restreaks for Mutant/Reporter Testing Today we had our weekly team meeting. We discussed the BHSC Research symposium and how we can improve on our performance and organization there. We also talked about T-Shirts, finalizing our design and color choice. We planned some more fundraising initiatives (read: bake sales!), After the meeting we did restreaks of all of our plates that we're using for the Mutant/ Reporter testing and I made overnight cultres of B0034-LuxOD47E, B0034-K082003 and R0040-I13502. Here is the summary of what we need to work on during the time left: 1. Describe the qualitative model and what it gets at (i.e.,When you change concentrations of LuxPQ, how does expression of Luciferase change? Explain the science behind it.) 2. Explain all the elements of the model. Explain MC approach., formalisms, compartmentalization. Gillespie, why we used Mathematica (and why not Mathlab, etc.), etc. 3. Tutorial of how to use the tool (i.e., what steps do you need to take to find something out). Iman, you were going to make narrated video. In addition, we need to edit the paper we are working on by the competition time. JAMIE Descriptive Title of What You're Doing WIKI CODING HERE JEREMY Mutant Testing and Discussion Today we discussed the BHSc research symposium and how we must conserve our team unity throughout the school year even though it may be difficult because we are all in separate courses.
