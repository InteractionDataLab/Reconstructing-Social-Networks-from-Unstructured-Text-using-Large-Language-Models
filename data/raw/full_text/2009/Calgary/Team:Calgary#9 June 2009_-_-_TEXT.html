CHINMOYEE CLASS EMILY Isolating Plamid, Nanodrop and Preparations for DNA Sequencing Isolated plasmid from LuxOD47E overnight cultures using Sigma miniprep kit, lot # 035K6068, eluting in ddH2O. Learned how to use Nanodrop Spectrophotometer to determine the concentration of the DNA. Prepared DNA for DNA sequecning. The optimal concentration of DNA for DNA sequencing is 100 ng/ 1 kb. Our gene is 1362 base pairs and the concentration is 292.3 ng/uL, so we sent down 1.8 uL of DNA with 7.2 uL ddH2O.
