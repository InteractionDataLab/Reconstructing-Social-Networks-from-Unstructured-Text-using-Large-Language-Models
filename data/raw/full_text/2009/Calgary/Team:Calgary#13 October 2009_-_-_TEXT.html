High School tomorrow. This brochure introduces the iGEM competition, this year's project, applications of Synthetic biology, the goals of iGEM and ways for high school students to get involed. It also highlights some of the benefits of conducting such research projects with other undergraduates (develop leadership skills, become part of a team, building your own project, etc).
