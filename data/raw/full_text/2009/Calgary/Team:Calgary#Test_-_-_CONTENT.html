    SPONSOR OF THE WEEK
    BioAlberta

    BioAlberta is a private, not-for-profit industry association, representing Alberta's growing bioindustry. They support, promote, and proactively facilitate the growth of Alberta's biotechnology industry. BioAlberta focuses its activities on government advocacy, industry promotion, and increasing industry access to financing. BioAlberta also focuses on facilitating access to human resources, business development, and networking opportunities.
