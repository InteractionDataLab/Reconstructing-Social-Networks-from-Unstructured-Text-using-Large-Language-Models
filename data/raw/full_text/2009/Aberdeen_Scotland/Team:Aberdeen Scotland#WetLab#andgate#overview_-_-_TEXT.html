It was designed so that production of our target protein (in our experiments we used GFP) would only commence in the presence of two imputs, hence being an AND gate (only when imput A AND B are present does 0 become 1). In terms of biology, this module was composed as in Figure 1, consisting of a pLux-Lac hybrid promoter [1], lambda cI, GFP and a double terminator. The imput signals are IPTG which will diffuse into the pipe upon a breach, this will remove the LacI protein inhibiting transcription.
