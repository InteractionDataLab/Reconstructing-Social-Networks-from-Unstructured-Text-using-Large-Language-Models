The first of these sub-projects is the AND gate of which Calum and Zuzana assumed charge of. In addition to this they also carried out the motility assays. The second sub-project was the LacI-Latching mechanism as well as the Lysis function. This part of the project was mainly covered by Daniel and Risat.
