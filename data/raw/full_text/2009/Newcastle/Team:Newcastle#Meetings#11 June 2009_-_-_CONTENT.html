    Mintes of the meeting

    • Students present: Goksel, James, Jane, Jess, Matt
    • Students absent: Hanny, Arun, Craig
    • Instructors present: Jen, Phil Aldridge, Dan
    • Chair: Goksel
    • Minute taker: James
    • The group introduced themselves to Phil Aldridge, whose lab we will be working in.
    • The group gave a brief overview of the project, up to the exam period, ran through action points.
    • Promoter library - Hanny in Matlab - prices and problems.
    • Sigma (70) - Phil asked what we were looking for a change of response to, but we clarified that it was the 'strength' of the promoters.
    • Clarified the use of JSim, as a tool for running CellML models, then parsing the output data.
    • Why do we want to clean up Cadmium from the environment? Showed the prepared draft document - "All" wording to be changed to "many" - airborne distribution - batteries in rubbish dumps - what industries use Cd - how big of a problem is it? - university staff located in the Devonshire building may be able to help.
  • Need DNA design before Neil gets back.
  • Push back lab work, due to trip, holidays and exams.
  • Edinburgh - tickets are booked for those who have confirmed that they are going - staying in student accommodation.
  • Lab introduction/tutorial set for Wednesday 17 June 2pm in the Leech building - Students are to meet outside of Medical Building at 1:45pm - L2.4, Offices - Dan M2046, Phil M2107.
  • Ran though what some of the other teams were doing, and had a small discussion about some of them.
  • Lab work to start on Monday 29 June, as there will be free space, as 2 people who normally work there are off. During the 1st week we should not expect to get much work done, as we will be learning - there will be plenty of help if needed, eg 2 PHD students are available until the end of July.
  • Lab refresher (Neil?) - Making media, plasmids, streak strains, ect.
  • We may get together with other teams later on and work with them.
  • Hanny, Goksel and Jen are to work on the promoter library, rest are to work on BioBricks, could use Sequentia.
