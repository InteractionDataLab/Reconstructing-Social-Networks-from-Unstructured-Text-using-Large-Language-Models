The iGEM2009 Newcastle team participated at an ethics brainstorming exercise at the iGEM-UK meeting at Edinburgh on the 24th of June 2009. Is synthetic life still life? What is life? Do synthetic organisms have rights? Should you patent synthetic life? Is it ok to make a synthetic species extinct? How synthetic is synthetic? Who is responsible? Who has the right to benefit from the advantages of synthetic organisms? Are we prepared to take responsibility?
