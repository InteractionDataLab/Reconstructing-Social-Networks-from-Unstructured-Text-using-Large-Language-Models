However the stochastic switch team had time to set up some overnight cultures to redo the minipreps and restriction digests for the biobricks from last week. These will be carried out tomorrow. We mixed the colonies in 3 ml of LB+Amp and left them overnight in shaking incubator. To prepare LB + Amp solution we used 5ml of LB + 250ul of Amp. 12 cultures were left overnight.
