They are midi-prepped plasmid DNA samples extracted using Sigma-Aldrich's GenElute Midi-prep kit , however the ethanol present in Wash Solution 2 hadn't been completely removed so ethanol precipitation was necessary. With the ethanol removed, it should be analyse the samples through DNA gel electrophoresis.
