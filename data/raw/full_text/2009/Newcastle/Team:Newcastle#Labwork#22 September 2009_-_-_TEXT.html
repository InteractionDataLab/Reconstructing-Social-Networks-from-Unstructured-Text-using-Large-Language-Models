We used the following controls: Tube 1: Backbone + insert + ligase (proper ligation) Tube 2: Backbone + ligase (control for background ligation level) Tube 3: Backbone + insert (control for ligase effectiveness) These were left in the incubator for an hour and then plated out (2 plates per tube 200ul and 50ul) onto Amp + Tet LB plated and put in the 37C incubator overnight.
