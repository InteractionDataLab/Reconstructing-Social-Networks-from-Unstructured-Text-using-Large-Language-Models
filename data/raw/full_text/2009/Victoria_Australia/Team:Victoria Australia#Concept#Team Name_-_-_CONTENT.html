    TEAM MASCOT

    The Koala in the Lab coat: "Fluoro Lou"

    Although also short for Louis, our mascot has been called Lou as a play on words for the two letters: Lu

    These letters form the beginning of the Latin words Lumen, Lux and Luz all used as names for Light.

    Lou is a Koala, one of many distinct Australian animals, he sports a lab coat which shows he's ensuring his personal protection in the laboratory whilst creating a fluorescent bioligical lighting system that he has used to decorate the Eucalyptus leaves he is grasping.
