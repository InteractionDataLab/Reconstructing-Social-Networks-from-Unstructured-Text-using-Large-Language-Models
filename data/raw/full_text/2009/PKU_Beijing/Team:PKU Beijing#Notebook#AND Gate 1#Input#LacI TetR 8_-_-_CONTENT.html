Every plate (P+L1/L2) is very well: more than 100 clones But many colonies are become green under the blue light, which means that the expression of LacI can not fully repressed the promoter lacP.

Result & Discussion

I successfully constructed clone: Promoter-Pcat-2M-lacI-term-lacP-GFP . However, I disappointed to find that these clones are not work very well, because the GFP express significantly even on the plate (without induce)!!! The expression of lacI is still not enough to repress the lacP.
