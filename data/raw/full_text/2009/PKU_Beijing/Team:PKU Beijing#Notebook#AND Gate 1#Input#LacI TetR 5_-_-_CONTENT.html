Delete LVA tail from reverse tetR-tetP-GFP

Resource: Reverse tetR-tetP-GFP: from Lin Min, plasmid. Renamed as TPG Reverse tetR-tetP*2, (not very confirm): from Lin Min, plasmid, Renamed as TP1, TP2 Vector: a plasmid with Kan resistant. From Lin Min Primer: Delete LVA primer, with complement to 20 last bps of tetR coding sequence and a TAA+Xba1 tail. And it can be use with one of standard primers (Rev), to amplify any sequence between the end of tetR and standard suffix. 5’-GCTCTAGATTAGGACCCACTTTCACATTTAA-3’ Designed by myself.

Result

I successfully deleted the LVA from the Reverse tetR-tetP-GFP. Yet, the result is not very promising, because the colonies became green on the plate, without inducing. It needs more quantitative data, but it is obviously that this cloning does not work very well. ^Top
