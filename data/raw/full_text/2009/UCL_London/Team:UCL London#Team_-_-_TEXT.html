The name the stress busters came under the influence of the film “Ghost busters”. As men on a mission, their role was to seek out the bad ghosts and arrest them. However, the three musketeers at UCL are on a mission to detect any processing related stress levels that the E-coli cells are undergoing. As you can see, our team of 3 is quite small so roles like designing/updating the wikipages,working in the labs and researching are shared between us. The Stress Busters are a tight knit of Biochemical Engineers and Biochemists.
