The mms6 enzyme from Magnetospirillum magneticum has been shown to produce uniform Magnetite nanoparticles when in the presence of an Iron Chloride based solution. The coding region has been equipped with an IPTG inducible promoter, strong RBS and double terminator. Thank you to the University of Alberta Team for collaborating with us on this part.
