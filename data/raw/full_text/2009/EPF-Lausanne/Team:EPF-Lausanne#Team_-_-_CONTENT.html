    Who we are

    We are a team of undergraduates with an interdisciplinary background. Most of us are from the School of Life Sciences but some are from microengineering, mechanical engineering and chemistry.

    Where we are from

    We are all studying at EPFL aka the Swiss Institute of Technology of Lausanne. The campus is located near the shore of lake Léman in the surroundings of the city of Lausanne, 50 km away from Geneva.
