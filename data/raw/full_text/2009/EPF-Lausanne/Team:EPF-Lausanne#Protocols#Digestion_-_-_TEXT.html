If the DNA preps are not concentrated enough, you can add more DNA and increase the volume of the reaction, respecting the buffers concentration (10x). Complete with dH2O if needed. Reaction Mix DNA 7 µl (700 ng of 100 ng/µl prep) NEB buffer 10x 1 µl BSA 10x 1 µl Enzyme 1 0.5 µl Enzyme 2 0.5 µl dH2O 0 µl Total 10 µl Dephosphorylation Add 1 µl of artic phosphatase to the vector to prevent ligation of the vector on itself.
