Do the following mixing: dNTP 1 μl 5x Buffer + MgCl2 10 μl Primer Mix 1 μl template 0.5 μl HiFi Plus DNAp 0.5 μl dH2O 36.5 μl Final Volume 50 μl 2. Make it run 10 cycles: 1             94°C 4:00 2             94°C 0:30 3             55°C 1:00 4             72°C 2:00 5             Cycle 2-4 10 times 6             72°C 7:00 7             4°C ∞ 3.
