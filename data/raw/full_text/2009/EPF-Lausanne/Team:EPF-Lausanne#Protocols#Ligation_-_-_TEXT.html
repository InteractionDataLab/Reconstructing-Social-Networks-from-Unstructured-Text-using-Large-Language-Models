Complete with dH2O up to 10 µl. Molar ratio Notes In general, we can take 1 µl of vector for 3 µl of insert. But you can change the molar ratio in case the sizes of the insert and the vector are really different. To control ligation specificity, you need to have a control with the vector only. Incubation 1h at Room Temperature.
