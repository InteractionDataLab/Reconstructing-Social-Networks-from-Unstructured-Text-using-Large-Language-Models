What you will need: Anchor oligos (complementary) Terminator oligos (complementary) Procedure: To make a 10 μM Anchor solution with an A(B) overhang one must anneal Anchor_A(Anchor_B) and Anchor_Comp. To make a 10 μM Terminator solution with an A(B) overhang one must anneal Term_A(Term_B) and Term_Comp. Combine 10 μL of Anchor/Term_(A or B) and Anchor/Term_Comp 100 μM stock solutions into an eppendorf tube. Add 80 μL water or TE, mix. Heat solution to 95C for 2 minutes. Let cool to room temperature slowly.
