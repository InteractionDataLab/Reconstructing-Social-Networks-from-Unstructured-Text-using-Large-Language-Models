Put on face shield and gloves. Turn on transilluminator and quickly make four slices; one on each side of the band you want to cut out and turn transilluminator off. Keep exposure to a minimum. Pry out the gel ‘cube’ and place it in an eppendorf tube. Follow instructions in kit manual.
