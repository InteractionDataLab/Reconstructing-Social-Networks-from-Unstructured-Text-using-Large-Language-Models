The loxP site could be rapidly and efficiently integrated in the genome by lambda Red and large deletions of both 117- and 165-kbp regions could be generated in 100% efficiency by Cre/loxP. Comparative genomic hybridization microarray experiments of deletion strains indicated that deletions were generated only in expected regions of the genome.
