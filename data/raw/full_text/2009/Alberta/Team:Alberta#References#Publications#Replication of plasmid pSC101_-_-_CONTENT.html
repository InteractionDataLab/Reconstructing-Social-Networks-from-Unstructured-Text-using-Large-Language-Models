From 2009.igem.org

D. Manen and L. Caro

Mol Microbiol. 1991 Feb;5(2):233-7

Abstract: The origin of replication of plasmid pSC101 presents features reminiscent of those found in a number of plasmids. As for those plasmids, many details about the way it initiates its replication are beginning to be known, but the regulation of this process will not be easily understood.
