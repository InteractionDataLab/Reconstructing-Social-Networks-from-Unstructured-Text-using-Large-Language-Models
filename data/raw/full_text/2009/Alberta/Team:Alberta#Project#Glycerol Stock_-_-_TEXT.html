Add 350 uL of overnight culture to each tube. Pipet up and down to gently mix. Flash freeze in liquid N2 or dry ice/methanol bath Place in -80 C freezer when frozen. Notes If we make extra,then we have them if we need to send them out or want to store in several locations.
