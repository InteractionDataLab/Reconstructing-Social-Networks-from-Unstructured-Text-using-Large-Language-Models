Control restriction of vector with His-tag on N-term. containing EWAND or TetShack showed that none of this inserts was present in vector. Ligation of construct LL37_ELST_GCN_ELST (front insert) and P1/P2 (front vector) was set. Vectors containing constructs ELST-ELST-ELST-mNGF, p53, GyrB-p53, foldon-p53, p53-APH, APH-p53, BCR-p53 and p53-APH1 were isolated. Control restriction was performed on vectors supposedly containing construct ELST-ELST-ELST-mNGF. Insert without His -tag was ligated in vector. October 2nd, 2009 Ligation of Construct no. 51 (elst_AEA) was transformed into DH5a. Transformation of constructs LL37_ELST_GCN_ELST_P1/P2 into DH5a. Vector containing p53-BCR was isolated from mini-preps. Control restriction was performed on vectors supposedly containing construct LL37-ELST-GCN-ELST-mNGF. RADA-mNGF and p53-APH peptides were produced. October 3rd, 2009 Vector containing LL37-ELST-GCN-ELST-mNGF was isolated from mini-preps. October 4th, 2009 Isolation and control restriction of constructs LL37_ELST_GCN_ELST_P1/P2. Western blot was performed to confirm the presence of RADA-mNGF and p53-APH. Control restriction was made on several clones of vector that is not supposed to contain His-tag. Bands on gel were not visible. October 5th, 2009 Electrophoresis of PCR product. Isolation from gel. Constructs LL37_ELST_GCN_ELST_P1/P2 and KSI_DP_P2/33_LL37_ELST_GCN_ELST were sent to be sequenced. Control restriction was made on several clones of vector that is not supposed to contain His-tag.
