We then ran a gel to make sure that we had the right DNA fragments. Our results corresponded to our predictions. June 17 We are completing a digestion of MicA, OmpA, and OmpF. Following the digestion we will incubate with SAP and then run a gel to verify the digestion. We will then extract the DNA for the sRNA target sequence and sRNA gene.
