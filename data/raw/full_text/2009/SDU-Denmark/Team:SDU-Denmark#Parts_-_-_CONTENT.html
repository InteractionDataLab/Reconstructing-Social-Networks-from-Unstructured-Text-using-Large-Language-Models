Our submitted parts

Our Parts on the registry

  • RIP - - The heart of our project. This is the minimal sequence coding for RIP. We have submitted a ligation mixture instead of a miniprep, due to temporal issues. PCR of the ligation mixture indicates that RIP has been correctly inserted.
  • sRIP - - RIP with an OmpA leader sequence, designed to let RIP leave the cytoplasm. We have submitted a ligation mixture instead of a miniprep, due to temporal issues. PCR of the ligation mixture indicates that sRIP has been correctly inserted.
  • ompA (Theoretical brick) - - The leader sequence used for sRIP, which should allow for peptide exporting from E. coli.
  • RIP-Term - - RIP combined with the double terminator
  • Constitutive Promoter-RBS-RIP- Term - - Constitutive RIP generator.

Characterizations of these parts

As mentioned above, we haven't had time to obtain a sequence for the RIP and sRIP.

For both RIP-Term and our RIP generator we have obtained sequencing data, confirming the parts. Sequencing data can be accessed through the parts registry. Much more info about the parts can be found on our results page.
