Protocol for Primer PCR

dNTP mix (10mM)

10ul 100mM dGTP

10ul 100mM dATP

10ul 100mM dTTP

10ul 100mM dCTP

60ul H2O

(Total V=100ul)

PCR reaction

39ul H2O

5.0ul Pfx-buffer (÷MgSO4)

1.0ul 50mM MgSO4

1.5ul 10mM dNTP mix

1.5ul forward primer

1.5ul reverse primer

0.5ul Pfx enzyme (add ju1st before PCR run)

PCR program

1. Start 94˚C 2min
2. Denaturing 94˚C 1min
3. Annealing 52˚C 1min
4. Elongering 68˚C 1min
5. GOTO 2 rep. 29x
6. Slut 68˚C 3min
7. Hold 4˚C
