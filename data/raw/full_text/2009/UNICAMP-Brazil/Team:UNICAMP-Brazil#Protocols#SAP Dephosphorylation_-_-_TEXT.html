Resuspend 1 pmol of DNA ends (about 1 μg of a 3 kb plasmid) in nuclease-free water. 2. Prepare reaction mix in a 20 μl volume according to the following: - DNA - 1 μl - 10X SAP Reaction Buffer - 2 μl - Water, Nuclease-Free - up to 19 μl - Shrimp Alkaline Phosphatase (1 unit/μl) - 1 μl 3. Incubate at 37°C for 30-60 min. 4. Stop reaction by heating at 65°C for 15 min. This completely inactivates SAP.
