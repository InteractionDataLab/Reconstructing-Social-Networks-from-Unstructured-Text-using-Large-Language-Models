Our advisors suggested us that, since we digested our plasmid vector with XbaI and SpeI (X sticky ends can come together with S sticky ends), it recircularized without the introduction of our inserts. Therefore, we must perform the dephosphorylation of our digested vector. That may prevent it from recircularizing without the insert introduction.
