We performed a colony-pcr to confirm that this colony has our plasmid. We used 2 pairs of primers - VF/VR and PY primers - and analyzed the results of our PCR in an agarose gel: The expected size for PY1 + RFP + BBa_B0015 amplified with VF/VR primers is 1279 bp. The expected size for PY1 + RFP + BBa_B0015 amplified with PY primers is 133 bp. As we can observe in the gel photo, the colony presented bands compatible with the size expected for both pairs of primers!!!! =) Fabi and Léo YeastGuard Yeast experiments We did miniprep of 10 inocula of pDLD+YFP and 10 inocula of ADH1+YFP.
