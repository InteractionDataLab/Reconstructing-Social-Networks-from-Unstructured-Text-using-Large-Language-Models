In our team, there are four instructors, thirteen students and two advisors. We are attending the iGEM2009 from Suzukakedai Tokyo. The Tokyo-Tech iGEM team are planning to Terraform Mars with bacteria. We hope you to check it out in more detail. Introduction Terra forming Our project goal is Terraforming of Mars . To achieve this goal, we propose 4 approaches . Blackened E.coli for controlling Mars temperature. Iron-oxidizing bacteria to get energy. Anti freeze protein against freezing stress . Consortium among multiple bacteria. For more detail, please go to Terraforming of Mars .
