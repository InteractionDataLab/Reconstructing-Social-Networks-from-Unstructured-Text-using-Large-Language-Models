  • ENTREPRENEURSHIP
    • Spun a fiber of cellulose acetate (with a little bit of "cheating" though)
    • 20% w/v cellulose acetate dope
    • loaded into a 10 mL plastic syringe with a 27.5 gauge needle (luer lock attached)
    • extruded at a rate of 6 uL/min into water
    • upon extrusion, the dope would blob up, so I had to use tweezers to gently pull the mass away from the needle tip. Upon doing so, a thin fiber would follow
    • this fiber was not uniform at all, as it depended on how fast I was pulling the rest of the cellulose acetate away from the needle tip.
  • a fiber was also formed when extruded at a rate of 1 uL/min. I had to do the same pulling to get the fiber to form.
