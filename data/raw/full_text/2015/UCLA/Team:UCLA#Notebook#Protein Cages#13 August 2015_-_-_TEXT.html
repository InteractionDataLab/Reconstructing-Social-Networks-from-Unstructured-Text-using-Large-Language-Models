For today, the glycerol stock will be used to streak on a plate. In addition, sequencing primers will be designed. The previous sequencing primers look like they should work for PCquad prime 3.0 and Mutant 10, so no primers were made. Streaking the plate: The glycerol stocks were obtained and put on ice. The ampicillin plate was obtained and warmed in the incubator for 15 minutes. Using asceptic technique, a wire loop was flamed, and cooled on the gel. It was then dipped into the glycerol stock, and then plated in a zig-zag fashion. The wire loop was flamed and cooled again before running another zig-zag pattern across the end of the previous streak. This was done a total of three times. The plate was then put in the 37C incubator at 5:30PM.
