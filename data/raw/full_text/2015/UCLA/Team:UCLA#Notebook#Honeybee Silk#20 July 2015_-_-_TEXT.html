We suspect that either the plates that we used to grow the cells on or the bl21 cells are bad. We are making new plates (Tristan) and I will run a test to see if a colony of the Bl21 cells grows in plain LB. Redo of Transformation with pET+silk We suspect there might be something wrong with the kanamycin plates we grew our colonies on, so today I will redo the transformation with the new plates. Thaw cells (~80uL) on ice for 10 minutes. Add 2uL of ligated product (diluted) Place on ice for 5 minutes. Rescue in 320uL of SOC, incubate and shake for 1 hour. Warm kanamycin plates at 37C. Spin down bacteria to pellet, discard supernatant. Add 110 uL of SOC, take 10uL and dilute 1:10. Plate 100uL of bacteria (1:1 and 1:10) on each plate. Spread with beads, incubate at 37C.
