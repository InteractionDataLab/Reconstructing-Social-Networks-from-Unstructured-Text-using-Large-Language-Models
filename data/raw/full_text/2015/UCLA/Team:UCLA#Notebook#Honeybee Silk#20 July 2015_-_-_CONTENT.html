    Redo of Transformation with pET+silk

    We suspect there might be something wrong with the kanamycin plates we grew our colonies on, so today I will redo the transformation with the new plates.

    1. Thaw cells (~80uL) on ice for 10 minutes.
    2. Add 2uL of ligated product (diluted)
    3. Place on ice for 5 minutes.
    4. Rescue in 320uL of SOC, incubate and shake for 1 hour.
    5. Warm kanamycin plates at 37C.
    6. Spin down bacteria to pellet, discard supernatant.
    7. Add 110 uL of SOC, take 10uL and dilute 1:10.
    8. Plate 100uL of bacteria (1:1 and 1:10) on each plate.
    9. Spread with beads, incubate at 37C.
