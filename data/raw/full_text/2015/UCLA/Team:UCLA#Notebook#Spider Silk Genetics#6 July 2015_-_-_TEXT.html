We will test ligation of a 3-mer using these beads to see if we get a different result compared to using Magnabind Streptavidin beads. Ligations were performed in 10 uL reactions using T7 ligase (Enzymatics), 50 ng of the relevant DNA, and 1 uL of the relevant 5 uM capping oligo. 5 uL of beads were used. Washed twice using 2x BW buffer. Resuspend washed beads in 5 uL 2x BW buffer, 1 uL of 5 uM initiator, and 4 uL ddH2O. Rotate at RT for 45 minutes. Wash twice using 0.5x BW buffer. Add ligation mix 5 uL 2x T7 ligase buffer 50 ng 2AB 0.5 uL TL ligase ddH2O to 10 uL Repeat ligations and washes as needed. Final wash using ddH2O. Elute in 15 uL 0.01% Tween-20 at 95 C for 3 min.
