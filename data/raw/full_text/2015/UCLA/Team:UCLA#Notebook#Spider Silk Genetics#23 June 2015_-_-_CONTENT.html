    Streptavidin Binding Test

    • Today we are testing if the streptavidin beads are binding properly to the biotinylated DNA.
    1. Prepared 10 uL of magnetic beads with 1 uL of 5 uM initiator.
    2. Incubated 35 min.
    3. Washed 2x with 100 uL 0.5x BW buffer
    4. Prepare contructs A-B and C-Term (C-T) separately,
    5. Prepared each in 10 uL reactions with 0.5 uL T7 ligase.
    6. Incubate each at 25 C for 10 minutes.
    7. Combined A-B and C-T to form A-B-C-T
    8. Incubate 25 C for 10 minutes.
    9. Mix A-B-C-T onto the prepared beads.
    10. Incubate at 25 C for 10 minutes with rotation.
    11. Saved the supernatant, then added 1 uL of 5 uM initiator to it.
    12. Incubate RT for 10 minutes.
    13. After bead ligation, eluted using 0.01% Tween-20 at 95 C for 5 minutes with occasional mixing.
    14. Ran 25 uL PCR using 1 uL of eluate as template.
    15. same protocol as 6/19/2015.
    16. Saved all samples in -20 C.
