Prepared 10 uL of magnetic beads with 1 uL of 5 uM initiator. Incubated 35 min. Washed 2x with 100 uL 0.5x BW buffer Prepare contructs A-B and C-Term (C-T) separately, Prepared each in 10 uL reactions with 0.5 uL T7 ligase. Incubate each at 25 C for 10 minutes. Combined A-B and C-T to form A-B-C-T Incubate 25 C for 10 minutes. Mix A-B-C-T onto the prepared beads. Incubate at 25 C for 10 minutes with rotation. Saved the supernatant, then added 1 uL of 5 uM initiator to it. Incubate RT for 10 minutes. After bead ligation, eluted using 0.01% Tween-20 at 95 C for 5 minutes with occasional mixing. Ran 25 uL PCR using 1 uL of eluate as template. same protocol as 6/19/2015 . Saved all samples in -20 C.
