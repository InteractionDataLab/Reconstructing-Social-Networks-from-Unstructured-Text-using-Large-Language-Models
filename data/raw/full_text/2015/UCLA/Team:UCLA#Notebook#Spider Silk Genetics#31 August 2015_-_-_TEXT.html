Starter Cultures Analysis Starter cultures grown at 30 C are overgrown. Cultures at 20 C show nothing. Miniprep for M1-SeqAB2 Used qiagen kit to prep all 11 mL of culture. 419.90 ng/uL A: 1.88 BsaI Digestion for M1-SeqAB2 Digest in 2x 50 uL reactions 5 ug of plasmid using 4 uL of BsaI. Digest at 50 C for 2 hrs, heat kill 65 C for 20 min. Visualize on 1% TAE gel, with 2 uL of NEB 50 bp ladder. Fig. 1 BsaI digestion of M1-SeqAB2. The expected size is 102 bp. Excised the 102 bp band, and extracted. ~25 ng/uL Liquid Culture Picked 3 colonies from each plate: M1-12(1C3)/(T7), M1/2[1:2]-12(1C3)/(T7) into 5 mL liquid culture with chloramphenicol. Grew overnight at 37 C.
