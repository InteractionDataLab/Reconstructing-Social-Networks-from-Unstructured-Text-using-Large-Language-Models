Sequencing of Silk Biobricks We sent in both of our constructs to Genewiz for sequencing. For sequencing primers, we used the iGEM VF2 and VR primers that bind to the psb1c3 backbone, as well as the sequencing primer that we designed to bind to the silk sequence (p13) see https://benchling.com/s/p7bClpzQ/edit The sequencing results can back and checked out for sample 1 of construct 1 (silk coding region) and samples 1 and 2 for construct 2 (regulartory elements+silk) We have our first honeybee biobricks!
