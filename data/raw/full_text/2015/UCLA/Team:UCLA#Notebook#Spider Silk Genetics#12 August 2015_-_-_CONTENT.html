    Colony PCR for M1 SwqAB 1 & 2 and M1-9(T7)

    2X Taq MM 6.25 uL
    VF Primer 0.625 uL
    VR Primer 0.625 uL
    Template 1 uL
    ddH2O 4 uL
    95C 3 min
    95C 30 sec
    72C 25 sec (M1SeqAB)/ 1min 40 sec (M1-9)
    x30
    72C 5 min
    12C Hold
    Lane 3-6: M1-9(T7) Colony 1-4. Lane 7-10: M1SeqAB1 Colony 1-4. Lane 11-14: M1SeqAB2 Colony 1-4

    Grew liquid culture for

    • all 4 colonies of M1-9 (T7); they all showed band close to the expected size ~1200-1300bp but they are slightly different so decided to sequence all of them.
    • colonies 1-3 for M1SeqAB1
    • none for M1SeqAB2 since we probably do not need this seq construct
