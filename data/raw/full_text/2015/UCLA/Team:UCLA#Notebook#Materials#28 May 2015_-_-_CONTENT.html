  • ENTREPRENEURSHIP
    • added 330 uL of water to the concentrated sample to dilute it down to 15% w/v
    • a small note: after refrigeration (4 C), the concentrated sample will look like it has gelled. However, if you let it "thaw" for a while, it will become liquid again.

    Spinning

    • we used an 18 gauge needle to draw 0.8mL of the silk into a 1 mL syringe
    • then replaced the needle with 10cm of the PEEK tubing
    • interestingly, no air bubbles formed when drawing up the plunger. (drawn up very slowly)
  • extruded the dope at 6 uL/min, into 3 different solvent
  • Ammonium Sulfate
  • the dope just coagulated into a blob. Similar to cellulose acetate spinning into water
  • Ethanol (70%)
  • the dope came out as a fiber
  • however, sometimes it seemed like multiple fibers were extruding from the tube at once
  • upon using tweezers to pull away the mass at the tip of the fiber, it sometimes started extruding as a single strand
  • Isopropanol (70%)
  • it was very similar to the ethanol spin
  • sometimes, multiple strands came out of the PEEK tubing at once
  • sometimes, a single strand comes out
    • the fibers that came out settled on the bottom of the beaker in a tangled mass
    • all fibers were left to soak in the coagulation bath overnight
