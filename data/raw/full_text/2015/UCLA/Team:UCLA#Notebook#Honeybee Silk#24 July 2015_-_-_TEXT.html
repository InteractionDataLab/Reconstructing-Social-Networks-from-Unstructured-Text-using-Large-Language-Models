Add 1uL of ligated product. Place on ice for 5 minutes. Rescue in 320uL of SOC, incubate and shake for 1 hour. Warm chloramphenicol plates at 37C. Spin down bacteria to pellet, discard supernatant. Add 111 uL of SOC, take 11uL and dilute 1:10 (11uL into 99uL). Plate 100uL of bacteria (1:1 and 1:10) on each plate. Spread with beads, incubate at 37C.
