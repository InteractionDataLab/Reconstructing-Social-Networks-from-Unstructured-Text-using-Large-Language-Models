    Miniprep for M1-15(1C3)/(T7) and Tamura(1C3)

    • Used qiagen kit.
    • Elute in 30 uL.

    Restriction Digest Test

    • Digest with X,P to see insert size.
    • Digest 1 ug of plasmid in a 50 uL reaction with 1 uL of each enzyme.
    • Digest at 37 C for 1 hr 15 min, heat kill at 80 C for 15 min.
    • Visualize on 1% TAE gel with 2 uL of 1 kb ladder.
    Fig. 1 X,P digest of M1-15(1C3), M1-15(T7), and Tamura(1C3) samples prior to sequencing. The expected insert size for all three constructs are 1.6 kb. None of the M1-15 colonies have the right insert. Both Tamura(1C3) colonies have the correct sized insert.
    • M1-15 colonies are wrong; don't send for sequencing.
    • Send Tamura(1C3) for sequencing with VF, VR primers.
    • Also send old Tamura(1A3) for sequencing as well.

    Colony PCR for M1-15(T7)

    • Since no colonies are left on M1-15(1C3), will focus on M1-15(T7).
    • Picked 8 colonies from M1-15(T7) plate, resuspended in 50 uL LB.
    • Colony PCR with 1 uL of resuspended colony as template.
    Volume (uL)
    2x Taq Red Master Mix 6.25
    10 uM For (VF) 0.625
    10 uM Rev (VR) 0.625
    Colony 1
    ddH2O 4
    Total 12.5
    95 C 3 min
    95 C 25 sec
    56 C 30 sec
    72 C 1.5 min
    repeat from step 2 30x
    72 C 5 min
    12 C hold
    • Visualize colony PCR results on 1% TAE gel with 2 uL of 1 kb ladder.
    Fig. 2 Colony PCR of M1-15(T7) using VF/R. The expected band size is 1.9 kb.
    • Colony PCR shows that the 8 colonies picked are incorrect. Will pick more.
