Eluted in 12 uL. Digestion for M1-15 Digest 5 uL of the PCR product with E,P and 5 uL of PCR product with X,P. Digest in a 50 uL reaction with buffer 2.1 and 1 uL of each relevant enzyme. Digest at 37 C for 1.5 hrs, heat kill at 65 C for 20 min. PCR purified the digestion product. Ligation for M1-15 Ligate E,P digested products into pSB1C3. Ligate X,P digested products into BBa_K525998(S,P) Ligate at a 1:3 molar vector to insert ratio. Ligate at 25 C for 1 hr, heat kill at 65 C for 20 min.
