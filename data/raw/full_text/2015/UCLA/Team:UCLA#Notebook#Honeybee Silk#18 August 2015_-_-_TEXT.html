Using the new BL21 cells that I made last week Thaw cells (50uL) on ice for 10 minutes. Add 1uL of ligated product. Place on ice for 5 minutes. Rescue in 200uL of SOC, incubate and shake for 1 hour. Warm chloramphenicol plates at 37C. Spin down bacteria to pellet, discard supernatant. Make 2 serial dilutions (1:10 and 1:100) Plate 100uL of bacteria (1:1, 1:10, 1:100) on each plate. Spread with beads, incubate at 37C.
