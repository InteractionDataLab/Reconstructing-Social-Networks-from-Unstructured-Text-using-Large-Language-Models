Used NEBuffer 2.1 Digest at 37 C for 1.5 hours, heat kill at 65 C for 20 min. Run 15 uL of digest on 1% TAE gel, with 2 uL of NEB 1 kb ladder. Fig. 1 Size Analysis of Putative M1-12(1C3). The expected size for a digested 12-mer is 1324 bp. The expected size for a digested 9-mer is 1018 bp. The expected size for digested pSB1C3 is 2029 bp. The slight size difference ins sequence sample 4 is due to the inclusion of the T7 promoter-RBS sequence from BBa_K525998. Results indicate that what we believed to be M1-12(1C3) is in reality M1-9(1C3). This results is most likely due to contamination during the transformation process, possibly from the glass beads used to spread bacteria.
