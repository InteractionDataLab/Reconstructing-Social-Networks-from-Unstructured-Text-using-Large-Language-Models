Procedures: PCR amplification with primers for pET22b: Using the NEB Tm calculator, the annealing temperatures chosen were 64C, 68C, and 72C. The following reaction components were combined in a mastermix, and 24uL were aliquotted to 3 reaction tubes. 5x Q5 buffer, 5uL 2mM dNTP, 7.5uL 20uM primer for pET22b forward, 1.875uL 20uM primer for pET22b reverse, 1.875uL 1ng/uL template, 0.75uL Q5 polymerase, 0.75uL ddH2O, 45.75uL The thermocycler conditions were the same as on 07/24/15. The PCR product was then ran on a 1% agarose gel for 20 minutes. Results: Media:2015-07-27 PCR PCquad amplification pET22b 64, 68, 72.JPG 68C and 72C are the preferred annealing temperatures. It seems as though the desired product is present, but it is smeared. The non-specific band appears lower than 250bp, so it may be different than the non-specific bands previously. The 68C, ad 72C bands were cut out from the gel, and kept in the -20C.
