Amplification of MaSp2 2CA We scaled up to 8x 50 uL reactions to amplify MaSp using the VF/R primer pair for conducting digestion and ligation testing. For the template, we used 2CA diluted 1:1000 for a final concentration of 219 pg/uL. We used the protocol below: Volume (uL) 5x Q5 Buffer 81 10 mM dNTPs 8.1 10 uM For 22 10 uM Rev 22 Template 8.1 5x GC buffer 81 Q5 Polymerase 4 ddH2O 4.95 Total 410 98 C 30 sec 98 C 10 sec 66 C 15 sec 72 C 15 sec repeat from step 2 18x 72 C 2 min 12 C hold The reaction was set up as a master-mix without polymerase, which was added after distribution into the strips. 2.
