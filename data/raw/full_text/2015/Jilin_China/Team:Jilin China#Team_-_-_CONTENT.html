    Shuwen Guan, Ph.D

    Scientific research field

    Our research interest is focused on understanding cellular and molecular mechanisms about aging and aging related disease. In particular, we are investigating how the signaling pathway of TOR (Target Of Rapamycin) works in aging in response to environmental influences such as heat or oxidative stress.

    This research will employ genetically engineered cell lines, transgenic worms (Caenorhabditis elegans), RNA interference, Gene transfection, and other state of the art molecular techniques.
