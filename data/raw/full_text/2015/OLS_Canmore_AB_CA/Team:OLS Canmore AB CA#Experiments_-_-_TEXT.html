Here is a link to access the guidebook:   https://static.igem.org/mediawiki/igem.org/7/71/AITF_iGEMHS_Guidebook_Final.pdf     Moving forward from this year, we plan to complete a qualitative assay and a quantitative assay for our parts. The first of these would simply determine if the Keratinase is effectively breaking down keratin by comparing it against a control flask containing keratin, and non-Keratinase producing E.coli K12. The second, and quantitative assay will provide us with information on speed of keratin breakdown due to Keratinase activity. This will help determine the effectiveness of our construct and its viability as an alternative to current keratin waste treatment methods.
