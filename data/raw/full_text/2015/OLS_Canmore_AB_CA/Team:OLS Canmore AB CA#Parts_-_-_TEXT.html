The basic parts include the protein coding sequences for Keratinase A or KERA (BBa_K1717171) and Keratinase US or KERUS (BBa_K1717172). The composite parts are made up of an RBS and IPTG-inducible promoter (BBa_J04500), as well as the gene for KeratinaseA (BBa_K1717000) or KeratinaseUS (BBa_K1717173).
