Place the following solutions in a 50 mL falcon tube:      Mix:      - 4.2 mL dH2O      - 2.5 mL TRIS 4X Buffer      - 3.3 mL Polyacrylamide Solution. Mix and invert six times. Add 33.3 μl of APS ( Ammonium Persulfate ) into the falcon tube. Mix and invert six times. Add 6.7 μl of TEMED to the falcon tube.      NOTE: Must be done in a fumehood. TEMED is toxic. Mix and invert six times. Solution will harden in roughly 15 minutes. Add solution in between glass plates using a pipette aid roughly 3/4 the way up the front plate. Fill remaining space with ethanol to ensure an even, level gel. Ethanol also gets rid of SDS bubbles. Once gel is solidified, remove the ethanol using a Kim Wipe ensuring the area in between the plates is now dry. Stacking Gel: Place the following solutions in a 50ml falcon tube:      Mix:      - 3.05 mL dH2O      - 1.25 mL Stacking Buffer Solution      - 650 µL Polyacrylamide Solution Mix and invert six times. Add 25 μl of APS ( Ammonium Persulfate ) into the falcon tube. Mix and invert six times. Add 5 μl of TEMED to the falcon tube.      NOTE: Must be done in a fumehood. TEMED is toxic. Mix and invert six times. Fill in the remaining area in between the glass plates with the stacking solution and place comb inside. After gel is solidified, remove from casting frame and place in plastic wrap. Store in the fridge. READY FOR USE! SDS-PAGE Sodium Dodecyl Sulphate Poly-Acrylamide Gel Electrophoresis Prepare samples that will be ran in the gel electrophoresis. Mix 15μl of each sample with 5μl of sample buffer solution. A 3 to 1 ratio is used. A maximum of 30μl can be inserted into each well. Place glass plates in the electrode assembly and into the tank cell. Fill tank with 1X electro buffer solution. Using a loading pipette and tips, load desired samples into wells along with a molecular weight ladder sample. Connect the lid to the tank and place the correct cables onto the tank lid. Run gel at 120 volts for 60-90 minutes or until desired bands are about the length of the gel. Silver Stain Plus Protocol Once SDS-PAGE is complete, remove gels from the glass plates and remove the stacking gel layer using a paper towel. Place gel in a glass container, wash with 200ml distilled water for 5 minutes. Repeat the wash step 4X. (shake gel back and forth in shaker) Decant water into waste container. Mix a solution with 90ml of 10% acetic acid and 90ml of 10% methanol in a clean beaker. Add 10ml of fixative enhancer solution into the beaker and pour over the gel. Let sit for 20 minutes on shaker, or up to a maximum of overnight, depending on the desired sensitivity of the gel. Decant solution into a proper waste container. Wash gel once again with 200ml of distilled water for 5 minutes on shaker. Repeat 4X. Decant water into waste container. Mix both parts of stain solution into two separate falcon tubes. Mix 1 2.5 mL of Silver Complex Solution 2.5 mL of Reduction Moderator Solution 2.5 mL of Image Development Reagent 17.5 mL of DH20 Mix 2 1.25 g of Development Accelerator 25mL of DH20 Mix both solutions together until each are dissolved, pour over gel. Let sit for up to 20 minutes or until desired bands show. Decant solution into proper waste container. Prepare a stop solution containing 90ml of 10% acetic acid and 90ml of 10% methanol. Pour over gel and shake for 15- 20 minutes. Decant solution into proper waste container. Wash gel with 200ml of DH20 for 5 minutes on shaker. Repeat 2X. GELS ARE STAINED! Ordering with IDT Ordering with IDT was tricky because we could not order sequences that were highly repetitive. Unfortunately, the unique characteristics we found in the colour producing proteins in literature involved high concentrations of specific amino acids. To overcome this limitation, we had to insert amino acids that we believed had no colour in between the amino acid of choice.
