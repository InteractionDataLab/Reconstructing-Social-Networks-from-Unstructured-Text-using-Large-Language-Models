We put the biobrick under T7lac promoter with strong RBS, and double terminator. We designed our Rhamncolipid to express reporter protein when producing rhamnolipid. We tried to find best reporter for our system that has fast turnover and doesn’t have significant fitness cost to the bacteria. We tested two reporters, RFP and aeBlue, with each reporter hasdifferent tags, either not tagged, LAA-tagged, or LVA-tagged. The LAA and LVA tags have the shortest half-life in 37oC (our cultivation temperature) in RFP and GFP, respectively. We also designed our control device which encodes LacI under different promoter strength, strong, medium, or even without the control.
