Guangdong Experimental High School began in 1872 when the predecessor of the Qing government established the "mission students in English Preparatory School", dating back over 140 years. Students of all generations adhered to the motto of "patriotism, unity, truth-seeking, innovative" and the educational philosophy of "people-oriented and quality legislates school ", forming a ”experimental, innovative and exemplary "school characteristics.
