    Attribution

    Chang Gung University is participating for the first time in the International Genetically Engineered Machines competition. As the founding members of CGU_Taiwan iGEM team, we have been helped and supported by many people, including professors from various disciplines, graduate students, other experienced iGEM teams and many more. We would like to thank every person who involved to the project. Without those helpful contributions, we probably wouldn’t have anything about iGEM.

    Department of Biomedical Sciences

    Dr. Ming-Chih Lai, Assistant Professor

    Recommend useful suggestions on RNA experiments and provide us luciferase assay reagent.

    Dr. Scott C. Schuyler, Assistant Professor

    As one of our instructors, provided his lab space for us to do experiments, guide us greatly on yeast experiments and provide lab materials on yeast experiment.

    Dr. Sebastian D. Fugmann, Professor

    Gave us great advices on team presentation.

    Dr. Shu-Yuan Yang, Assistant Professor

    As one of our instructors, assisted us greatly to maintain our team, guide us on experimental design, laboratory techniques and presentation skills. She is always there to guide us on discussion and encourage us to move forward.

    He was the first one who supports the establishment of CGU_Taiwan Team. As one of our instructors, assisted us greatly to startup our team, provide us lab materials and guide us with all aspects of the project. Without him, there wouldn’t be a CGU_Taiwan team in our university.
