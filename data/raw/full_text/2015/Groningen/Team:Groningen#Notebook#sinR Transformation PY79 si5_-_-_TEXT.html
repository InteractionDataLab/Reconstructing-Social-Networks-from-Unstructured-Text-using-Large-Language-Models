Overexpression of SinR in the Bacillus subtilis strains. This was done to overexpress matrix genes in the strains, which should result in an increased robustness of their biofilm. Transformation failed since cultures were plated to late. Transformation 14:37, 25 August 2015 - 00:00, 25 August 2015 MC was placed in the 37°C incubator. The OD was measured at 14:37, Bacillus subtilis PY79 had not grown enough. OD was measured to be 0.021 after 10x dilution with demiwater. 1 µL of SinR DNA was added to 200 µL of MC with Bacillus subtilis PY79 at 19:55. The cultures were not plated until the following morning.
