Our goal is to overexpress TasA by adding a second copy of the tasA gene under the control of a salt inducible promoter. This overexpression will lead to a stronger biofilm when the biofilm is exposed to salt water. The ligation of the PliaG promoter with tasA was performed. Ligation 10:30, 13 August 2015 - 20:15, 13 August 2015 The samples were ligated overnight at room temperature.
