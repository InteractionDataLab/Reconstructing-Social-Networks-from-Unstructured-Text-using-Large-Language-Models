A new reverse primer was designed because the old one had the suffix the wrong way around. To create a salt inducible promotor. PCR was successful. PCR 00:00, 16 August 2015 - 00:00, 16 August 2015 PCR was performed with the forward and the new reverse primer for Psalt (figure 1) and a annealing temperature of 55°C and extention time of 60 seconds. PCR product was checked and purified on 1% agarose gel with 1:50000 SERVA DNA Stain G. Gel results show a band at approximately 600bp.
