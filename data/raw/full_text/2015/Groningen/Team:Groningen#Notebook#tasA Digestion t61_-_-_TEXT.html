Our goal is to overexpress TasA by adding a second copy of the tasA gene under the control of a salt inducible promoter. This overexpression will lead to a stronger biofilm when the biofilm is exposed to salt water. The digestion of PliaG and T2 was carried out. Restriction 13:22, 6 August 2015 - 18:40, 6 August 2015 PliaG was digested for ligation with T2. Restriction enzymes were used that create a scar. For the digestion the restriction enzymes EcoRI and SpeI were used for the promoter and XbaI and EcoRI for T2.
