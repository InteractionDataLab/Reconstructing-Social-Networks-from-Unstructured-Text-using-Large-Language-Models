Our goal is to overexpress TasA by adding a second copy of the tasA gene under the control of a salt inducible promoter. This overexpression will lead to a stronger biofilm when the biofilm is exposed to salt water. On gel, the band for tasA was hardly visible, but the PCR possibly did work. PCR 00:00, 8 June 2015 - 00:00, 8 June 2015 In this experiment it was attempted to increase the amount of tasA. For this first a Mastermix was made, to which primer and DNA were added.
