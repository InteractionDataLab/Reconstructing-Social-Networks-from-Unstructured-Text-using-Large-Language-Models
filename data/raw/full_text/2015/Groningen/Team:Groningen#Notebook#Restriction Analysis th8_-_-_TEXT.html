To 5 µL from each plasmid, 13 µL MiliQ, 1 µL fast digest EcoRV and 1 µL fast digest buffer was added. The mixtures were incubated for one hour at 37 °C. The correct plasmid would have 2 bands; K823023 (original) or pdg1664 (original) would show one band. Fortunately, all 5 plasmids had two bands.
