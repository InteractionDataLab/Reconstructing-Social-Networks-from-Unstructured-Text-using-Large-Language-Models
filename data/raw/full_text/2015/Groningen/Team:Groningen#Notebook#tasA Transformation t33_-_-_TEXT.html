Our goal is to overexpress TasA by adding a second copy of the tasA gene under the control of a salt inducible promoter. This overexpression will lead to a stronger biofilm when the biofilm is exposed to salt water. In this experiment the heat shock transformation of tasA with BBa_K823023 and BBa_K823024 was performed. The cultures were plated on LB+amp plates and incubated overnight at 37 °C.
