Sample: 20 µL DNA (ferritin). 4 µL 6x buffer. Ladder: 2 µL Thermo Scientific GeneRuler 1kb DNA ladder, ready to use. Gel was run on 100 V for 30 minutes. Gel results show a band of 1500 bp. This corresponds to the size of ferritin. Gel was cut and dissolved in 2:1 binding buffer of the PCR purification kit and purified according to protocol. Sample was stored as: Ferritin dig (7.4 ng/µL, Nanodrop value). Harm Ruesink 2015 iGEM Groningen
