Overexpression of SinR in the Bacillus subtilis strains. This was done to overexpress matrix genes in the strains, which should result in an increased robustness of their biofilm. The transformation had suceeded as colonies were found on the plate. Transformation 10:00, 27 August 2015 - 00:00, 27 August 2015 Bacillus subtilis JH642 was added to LB. Standard competence medium was prepared. 10 µL of phenylalanine was added to Bacillus subtilis JH642 mixture. Bacillus subtilis JH642 was added to MC and the mixture was placed in the 37°C incubator at 13:00. At 18:00 1 µL of SinR plasmid was added to the MC mixture, which was placed back in the 37°C incubator. At 21:00 200µL transformed Bacillus subtilis JH642 was plated on spectinomycin plate. Hanneke Siebe 00:00, 28 August 2015 - 00:00, 28 August 2015 Transformation succeeded, as there were colonies on the plate. Hanneke Siebe 2015 iGEM Groningen
