DNA Isolation B. subtilis 168 and B. subtilis ComI
B. subtilis 168 and B. subtilis ComI were grown on LB and afterwards their DNA was isolated and stored.
To isolate the DNA of B. subtilis 168 and the DNA of B. subtilis ComI.
The DNA of B. subtilis 168 and the DNA of B. subtilis ComI was isolated.
Finibus sapien, nec cursus...
00:00, 06 May 2015 - 00:00, 07 May 2015
Streaked out B. subtilis 168 & B. subtilis ComI on LB agar from -80°C stock on LB agar plates and grown overnight at 37 °C.
Harm Ruesink
00:00, 07 May 2015 - 00:00, 07 May 2015
Stored the plates in the fridge at 4 °C.
Harm Ruesink
00:00, 12 May 2015 - 00:00, 12 May 2015
Picked up a colony from both 168 & ComI plate strain and put in LB medium (3 mL in culture tube). Grown overnight in shaker incubator (37 °C, 220 rpm).
Harm Ruesink
00:00, 13 May 2015 - 00:00, 13 May 2015
DNA isolation (168 & ComI) with GenElute Bacterial Genomic DNA Kit.
Stored in -20 °C as:
168 DNA (50 µL, 21.9 ng/mL DNA)
ComI DNA (50 µL, 24.1 ng/mL DNA)
Harm Ruesink
