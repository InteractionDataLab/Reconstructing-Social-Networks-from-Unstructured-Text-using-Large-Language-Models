Overexpression of SinR in the Bacillus subtilis strains. This was done to overexpress matrix genes in the strains, which should result in an increased robustness of their biofilm. Transformation failed since plating of culture was too late. Transformation 14:37, 25 August 2015 - 00:00, 25 August 2015 MC was placed in the 37°C incubator. The OD was measured at 14:37, Bacillus subtilis 3610 delta comI had grown enough. 1 µL of SinR DNA was added to 200 µL of MC with Bacillus subtilis 3610 delta comI at 19:55. The cultures were not plated until the following morning.
