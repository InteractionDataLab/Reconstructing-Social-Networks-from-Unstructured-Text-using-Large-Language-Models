To create a salt inducible promotor. No fluorescent protein visible. None 00:00, 1 September 2015 - 00:00, 1 September 2015 This was done by growing B. subtilis ComI in LB + chloramphenicol for four hours. Then cells were transferred to Spizizen salts medium (SSM) and grown for three additional hours. Then different salt concentrations were added (0.1M, 0.5M and 1M). This was incubated for 1 hour at 37°C. Afterwards cells were fixated in 1,25% agarose in PBS. Fluorescent microscopy did not show any differences between salt concentrations.
