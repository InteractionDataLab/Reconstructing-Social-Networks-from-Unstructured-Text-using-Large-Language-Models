Our goal is to overexpress TasA by adding a second copy of the tasA gene under the control of a salt inducible promoter. This overexpression will lead to a stronger biofilm when the biofilm is exposed to salt water. The heat shock transformation was performed but no colonies were visible after growing overnight. Heat shock transformation 10:00, 29 July 2015 - 12:00, 29 July 2015 With the ligation product of tasA with two promoters of 28 July, the heat shock transformation was performed and the culture was plated on an LB+cm plate and incubated overnight at 37 °C. Harm Ruesink 00:00, 30 July 2015 - 00:00, 30 July 2015 The transformation of 29 July was checked. No colonies were visible on the LB+cm plate. T2 was send to Macrogen for sequencing.
