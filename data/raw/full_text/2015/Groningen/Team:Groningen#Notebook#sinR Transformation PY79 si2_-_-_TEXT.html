This was done to overexpress matrix genes in the strains, which should result in an increased robustness of their biofilm. Transformation failed since no colonies were found. Transformation 15:48, 24 August 2015 - 24:00, 24 August 2015 Standard competent medium (MC) was placed in the 37°C incubator. The OD was measured at 20:30, but Bacillus subtilis PY79 needed more time to grow. 1 µL of SinR DNA was added to 200 µL of MC with Bacillus subtilis PY79. The culture was plated at 24:00.
