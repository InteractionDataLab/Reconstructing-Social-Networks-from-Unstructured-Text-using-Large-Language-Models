Our goal is to overexpress TasA by adding a second copy of the tasA gene under the control of a salt inducible promoter. This overexpression will lead to a stronger biofilm when the biofilm is exposed to salt water. Sample 1 was not assembled correctly; sample 2 did show a band on gel with the right size.
