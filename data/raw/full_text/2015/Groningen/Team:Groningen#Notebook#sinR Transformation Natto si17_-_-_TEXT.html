Overexpression of SinR in the Bacillus subtilis strains. This was done to overexpress matrix genes in the strains, which should result in an increased robustness of their biofilm. Transformation succeeded, as colonies were found on the plate. Transformation 13:08, 29 August 2015 - 00:00, 29 August 2015 Bacillus subtilis natto was added to LB . Bacillus subtilis natto was added to MC and the mixture was placed in the 37°C incubator. At 18:00 1 µL of SinR plasmid was added to the MC mixture, which was placed back in the 37°C incubator. Plated transformed Bacillus subtilis natto on LB with spectinomycin. Hanneke Siebe 13:08, 30 August 2015 - 00:00, 29 August 2015 Transformation succeeded, as colonies were present on the plate.
