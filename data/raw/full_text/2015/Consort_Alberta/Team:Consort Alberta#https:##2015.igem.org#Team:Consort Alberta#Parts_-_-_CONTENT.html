    • Parts

    ECOS consists of the following parts:

    J23100 - which is a constituent promoter. B0034 - the RBS for our XylR gene. I723017 - the XylR coding region which encodes for the transcriptional regulator XylR protein. B0015 - the double stop codon for this sequence. I723020 - This is the Pu promoter. B0030 - RBS.1 strong.

    We also attached the Reporter AmilCP in the backbone pCB1C3 to give us an output in correspondence to the level of xylene present.
