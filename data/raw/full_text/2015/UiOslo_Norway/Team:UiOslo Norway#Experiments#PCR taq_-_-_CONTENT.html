    • PCR with Taq DNA Polymerase (NEB):

    Back to Protocols

    Using the guidelines of the provided Taq DNA Polymerase with Standard Taq Buffer (M0273) protocol.

    Reaction Setup: 2.5 µl 10X Standard Taq Reaction buffer 2.5 µl dNTPS [2 mM] 0.5 µl Forward Primer [10 µM] 0.5 µl Reverse Primer [10 µM] 1 µl Template DNA 0.125 µl Taq DNA Polymerase 17.875 µl MilliQ Water

    25 µl Total Reaction

  • PCR Program:
    StepTemperatureTime
    Initial Denaturation95 °C30 seconds /5 Minutes for Colony PCR
    35 Cycles95 °C30 seconds
    45 °C – 72 °C60 seconds
    68 °C1 minute / 1 kb
    Final Extension68 °C5 minutes
    Hold4 °Cforever
