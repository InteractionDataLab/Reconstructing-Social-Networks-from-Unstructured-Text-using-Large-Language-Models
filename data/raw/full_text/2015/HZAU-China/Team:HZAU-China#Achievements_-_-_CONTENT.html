    As for the Best Information Processing Project:

    The life systems in the real world and the virtual world are thought to be entirely independent. The gap between them appears to be hard to break. However, we choose an oscillator to stand for the life system of both real and virtual and the light is regarded as the medium to connect them together. This ingenious design leads to the data exchanging between them accessible. This is truly an unprecedented attempt. Till now, we have completed a perfect loop to make the information flowed and have confirmed it is effective.

    As for Best Model:

    In terms of our project, modeling part is performed at the same time with the wet lab, which is different from simple prediction or verification. Through analysis of the biological processes, two oscillators were built in E. coli and computer, respectively. To reach our goal, mixed-reality state, we divided it into three stages, the former two of which have been completed. We have achieved the coupling of virtual and virtual systems, and even the coupling of real and virtual parts through interface devices. It’s an excited moment to witness the coupling simulation results in Video 1 and 2.

    As for the Best Policy &Practices Project:

    This year, we have held and participated activities in various forms. Plenty of meeting up, education activity and novel club! We have joined 3 Meetups and have learned a great deal from others. We volunteered in science and technology museum at the Children's Day which integrated education and propaganda as one. Besides all the above, we focused on the inheritance and the expansion of influence in iGEM and set up a club to share fresh news with everyone concerned about synthetic biology. Moreover, we wrote a paper to show the deep reflection of the influence of our project.
