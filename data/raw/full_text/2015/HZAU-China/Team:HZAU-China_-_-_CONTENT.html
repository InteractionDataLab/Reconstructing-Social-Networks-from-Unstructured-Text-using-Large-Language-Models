    • Project

    With the rapid development of biology and computer technologies, the era of mixed-reality is coming, which has profound influence on the future research. In this project, we are working on creating a new kind of life form, mixed-reality cells (MR. Cell), which are half-real and half-virtual.

    view more

    WetLab

    In wetlab, we constructed a genetic oscillator in E. coli as the real part. In order to connect the real oscillator in E. coli with the virtual oscillator in computer simulation, a light control circuit associated with the oscillator was adopted. Besides, we do a series of verifying and testing work to confirm that the real part can work.

    view more

    Modeling

    Corresponding to the real part, an e-oscillator was simulated in a computer as the virtual part. To validate the coupling process, we simulated the interaction between the two e-oscillators. Besides, mixed-reality state of physical systems was demonstrated to indicate that our MR. Cell could work.

    view more

    Hardware

    Till now, the real part and the virtual part have all been done. But, how can we combine them together? That's what we do at the hardware part. We aim at unifying the two parts to form an inter-reality system, MR. Cell.
