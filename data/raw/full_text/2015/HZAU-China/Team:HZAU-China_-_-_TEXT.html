In this project, we are working on creating a new kind of life form, mixed-reality cells (MR. Cell), which are half-real and half-virtual. view more WetLab In wetlab, we constructed a genetic oscillator in E. coli as the real part. In order to connect the real oscillator in E. coli with the virtual oscillator in computer simulation, a light control circuit associated with the oscillator was adopted. Besides, we do a series of verifying and testing work to confirm that the real part can work. view more Modeling Corresponding to the real part, an e-oscillator was simulated in a computer as the virtual part. To validate the coupling process, we simulated the interaction between the two e-oscillators. Besides, mixed-reality state of physical systems was demonstrated to indicate that our MR. Cell could work. view more Hardware Till now, the real part and the virtual part have all been done. But, how can we combine them together? That's what we do at the hardware part. We aim at unifying the two parts to form an inter-reality system, MR. Cell. view more InterLab Study This year, we are not only in general event but also participate in Measurement Interlab. view more Achievements We have done lots of work to make our project perfect.Not only have we met the standards of gold medal, but we believe that what we did has great significance in future research. view more Team Our team is made up of 17 members with different majors and can be divided into three groups, wet-lab, modeling and connection.
