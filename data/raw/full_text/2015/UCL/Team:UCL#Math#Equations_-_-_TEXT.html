Also where $B(t)$ is a Brownian motion. $X$ is a solution to the above equation, given that X is a function or path, if it satisfies the following $$X(T)=\int_0^T \mu(t,X(t))dt + \int_0^T \sigma(t,X(t))dB(t)$$
