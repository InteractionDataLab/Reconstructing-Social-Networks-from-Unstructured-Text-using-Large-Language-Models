Miniprep of 7/15 Transformation I miniprepped the culture from 7/16 using the Zymo Miniprep Kit. OD: 294.12 ng/uL The sample was sent in for sequencing. Transformation of BL21(DE3) with pET24a+silk Will using the miniprepped sample that came back with two silent mutations (pET+silk4) Thaw cells (~80uL) on ice for 10 minutes. Add 2uL of ligated product (diluted) Place on ice for 5 minutes. Rescue in 320 uL of SOC, incubate and shake for 1 hour. Warm kanamycin plates at 37C. Spin down bacteria to pellet, discard supernatant. Add 101 uL of SOC, take 1uL and dilute 1:10. Plate 100uL of each (1:1 and 1:10) on a plate.
