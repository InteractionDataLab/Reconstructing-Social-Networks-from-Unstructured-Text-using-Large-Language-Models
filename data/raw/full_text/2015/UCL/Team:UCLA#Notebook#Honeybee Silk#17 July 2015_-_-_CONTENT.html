    Transformation of BL21(DE3) with pET24a+silk

    • Will using the miniprepped sample that came back with two silent mutations (pET+silk4)
    1. Thaw cells (~80uL) on ice for 10 minutes.
    2. Add 2uL of ligated product (diluted)
    3. Place on ice for 5 minutes.
    4. Rescue in 320 uL of SOC, incubate and shake for 1 hour.
    5. Warm kanamycin plates at 37C.
    6. Spin down bacteria to pellet, discard supernatant.
    7. Add 101 uL of SOC, take 1uL and dilute 1:10.
    8. Plate 100uL of each (1:1 and 1:10) on a plate.
    9. Spread with beads, incubate at 37C (4 PM)

    Double Digestion of honeybee silk in psb1c3 and BBa_K525998 and Cleanup

    • I will be digesting our previous Biobrick (honeybee silk in psb1c3) and BBa_K525998 in preparation for ligation.
    • Honeybee silk+psb1c3: XbaI and PstI
    • BBa_K525998: SpeI and PstI
    Component Volume (out of 50uL)
    NEB 2.1 Buffer 5uL
    SpeI/XbaI 1uL
    PstI 1uL
    SIlk in psb1c3/BBa_K525998 4uL/3uL
    Nuclease Free Water 39uL/40uL

    Program

    Step Temperature Time
    Incubation 37C 1 hour
    Heat Inactivation 80C 15 min
    Hold 10C Hold

    I ran the digests on a 1% agarose gel against a 1kb ladder. The appropriate band sizes were present.

    Ladder, Backbone, Insert

    The backbone band and the 100bp band from the insert were gel extracted and purified using the Qiagen Gel Extraction Kit.

    OD: Silk Insert: 4.45ng/uL Backbone: 30.89ng/uL

    Ligation

    • Using double digested samples of the Silk Insert and Backbone (3:1 molar ratio)
    Component 3:1 insert:vector) Volume (out of 20uL)
    T4 Ligase Buffer 2 ul
    BBa_K525998 backbone (30.89 ng/ul) 1 ul
    Silk insert (4.45 ng/ul) 9uL
    T4 Ligase 1 ul
    Water 7 uL
    • Incubated overnight at 16C
