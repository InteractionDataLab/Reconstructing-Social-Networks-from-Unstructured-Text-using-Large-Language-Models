In order to reach many people with our topic, we decided to write a press release in English and German for the media. Several newspaper and online magazines reported about our project after the publication of this press release. That confirm us that our chosen topic arouses the public’s interest and has a great impact to deal with. Newspaper Figure 1: Newspaper article about our project. Through both interviews on YouTube and on TV we reached many people in the public and demonstrated how handy a cell-free biosensor would be.
