Heavy metals Here you find some detailed information about the lab work concerning the construction of our heavy metal biosensors in E. coli. You can see the difficulties we ran into and follow our progress in creating our sensor systems. Date rape drugs The detailed process of the construction and characterization of the sensors detecting GBL, GHB and GABA in E. coli, is shown. You find our various methods to obtain the necessary elements from gene synthesis, A. tumifaciens and B. subtilis. CFPS In cell free protein synthesis (CFPS), the extracts of lysed cells, containing all the necessary components for transcription and translation, are used to produce proteins in vitro. Find out how we developed and optimized our own cell extract. PRIA See how we developed this alternative to standard biosensors, which is based on in vitro interaction of immobilized repressor proteins with plasmids containing the operator site corresponding to the repressor.
