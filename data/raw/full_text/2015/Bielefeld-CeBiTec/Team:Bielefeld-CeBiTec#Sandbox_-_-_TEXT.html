Find Out More Project Our team decided to establish cell-free systems ("cell-free protein synthesis" and "plasmid repressor interaction assay"). For demonstrating the extensibility of our systems we worked with biosensors for heavy metals and we construct a new biosensor for date rape drugs. See what we are doing this year. Get Started!
