Lay plates sideways overnight before incubating at 37C Innoculate remaining cells (~0.6mL) from 10^0 to the balch tube with 2mL broth, record OD and incubate overnight at 37C Day 3 (enrichment in broth) Record OD again from the final step in Day 2, inoculate 0.5mL into 2 5mL of selective broth. Place in the anaerobic chamber overnight Dispense media and pressurize to 15psi with N2/CO2 gas Preparation of Sodium Sulfide Add 200mL of diH2O to flask and mark water line. Add 2 pellets of NaOH and 20mL more of ddH2O. Boil the 220mL diH2O while flushing flask with N2 until the water level reaches the original 200mL water line. Let flask cool while flushing with N2, move the flask to the gassing station in the fume hood. Continue flashing with N2. While flask is cooling, weigh our slightly moe than 5g Na2S-9H2O. For the following steps, work in the fume hood and wear gloves. Clean the sodium sulfide crystal by briefly rinsing the crystal with diH2O. To do this, swirl the crystal in a small beaker with the water. Dry the crystal by blotting with a paper towel or kimwipe. Reweigh the crytal to insure that the final weight is 90-110% of the desired weight. Add the clean, weighted sodium sulfide to the cooled flask while flushing with N2 and mix until partially dissolved. Stopper the flask, discontinue flushing, and transfer to the anaerobic chamber Dispense into tubes that have been equilibrated in the chamber for at least for one day to allow the O2 to desorb from the glassware. Use the thick butyl stoppers that are ungreased and have been equilibrated in the chamber for one day. Remove tubes from the chamber and pressure with N2 at 15 psi. Autoclave on gravity cycle (rapid dry) for 20 minutes. Store in anaerobic chamber. Making anaerobic agar plates Add 0.15g agar to each glass plate bottle and place in chamber for at least overnight. Make the full volume of media needed and also place in chamber overnight. Distribute 10mL media to each of the glass plate bottles. Stopper and seal the bottles. Pressurize each bottle to 15psi with N2/CO2 Autoclave on gravity cycle with a 30 minutes sterilization. Remove from autoclave.
