Giuliano Berloffa from the Mansy lab for helping with some good tips on protein purification and for troubleshooting with the anaerobic chamber Microbial Fuel Cell (MFC) The small MFCs and some of the materials used in this work were kindly given to us by Martin Hanczyc (CIBIO, Univ. of Trento) and built by Ioannis Ieropoulos (UWE Bristol).
