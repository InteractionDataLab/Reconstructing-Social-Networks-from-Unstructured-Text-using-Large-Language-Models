We had enjoyable collaboration with team SJTU, SYSU and Tsinghua A. During our initial seek for an idea project, SJTU provided us several plasmids containing the light system because they have used similar proteins before. Apart from sharing similar parts with them, we also invited two students from SJTU to Tsinghua University for a visit when they came to Beijing in August. They said that they had a good time in the campus and looked forward to better cooperation with us. We are also grateful that SYSU later began to collaborate with us after a conference in Taiwan. They kindly shared their plasmids encoding recombinase with us. Besides this, we also held a meet-up in Beijing in August, during which we not only discussed the technical obstacles we encountered before but also shared useful tips for later experiments. As an exchange, we spotted out several wrong description or sequencing errors in their plasmids, which at first might be misleading.
