Place a bottle of sterile water at least for 2 hours on ice. Start a 1.4 mL LB culture with the respective antibiotic, when necessary with 30 μL of the o/n culture and incubate at 30 °C (e.g. with pSC101BADaβγA) or 37 °C with 950 for 2 hours. Put the cells on ice. Keep the cells on ice whenever possible. Spin the cells down at 8,000 rpm for 0.5 minutes in a cooling centrifuge at 2 °C. Discard the supernatant. Resuspend the pellet in 1 mL of ice cold water with a pipette. Spin the cells down at 8,400 rpm for 0.5 minutes at 2 °C. Discard the supernatant. Resuspend the pellet in 1 mL of ice cold water with a pipette. Spin the cells down at 8,800 rpm for 0.5 minutes at 2 °C. Discard as much supernatant as possible. The remaining solution should be about 30 μL, resuspend the cells in it. Use the cells immediately.
