    • About us

    Our team consists of 11 students and 2 supervisors. We are biologists, bioengineers and biochemists. If you want to know a little bit more about us, you can read some information on this site.

    Abhinaya

    Abhinaya Anandamurugan is 26 years old and comes from India. She is currently studying Molecular Bioengineering at the TU Dresden. She finished her Bachelors in Biotechnology in 2010 from Anna University, Chennai, India. She did her Bachelor thesis at M.S. Swaminathan Research foundation, Chennai on analyzing "Zinc Solubilizing Bacteria involved in gluconic acid production". She worked 4 years before starting the Master's and she is currently working at the Bioinnovation Zentrum Dresden.
