    1. The day before start a 1 mL LB culture with the respective antibiotic when necessary and incubate at 30 °C (e.g. with pSC101BADaβγA) or 37 °C with 950 rpm o/n .
    2. Place a bottle of sterile water at least for 2 hours on ice.
    3. Start a 1.4 mL LB culture with the respective antibiotic, when necessary with 30 μL of the o/n culture and incubate at 30 °C (e.g. with pSC101BADaβγA) or 37 °C with 950 for 2 hours.
    4. Put the cells on ice.
    5. Keep the cells on ice whenever possible.
    6. Spin the cells down at 8,000 rpm for 0.5 minutes in a cooling centrifuge at 2 °C.
    7. Discard the supernatant.
    8. Resuspend the pellet in 1 mL of ice cold water with a pipette.
    9. Spin the cells down at 8,400 rpm for 0.5 minutes at 2 °C.
    10. Discard the supernatant.
    11. Resuspend the pellet in 1 mL of ice cold water with a pipette.
    12. Spin the cells down at 8,800 rpm for 0.5 minutes at 2 °C.
    13. Discard as much supernatant as possible.
    14. The remaining solution should be about 30 μL, resuspend the cells in it.
    15. Use the cells immediately.
