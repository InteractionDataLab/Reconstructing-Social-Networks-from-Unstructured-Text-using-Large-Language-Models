    • Transformation into not competent E.coli cells

    • grow overnight culture
    • Dilute1:100
    • grow 10 mL day culture 3h @ 37 °C
    • take 1 mL
    • centrifuge (1min, 4000 rpm, 4 °C)
    • resuspend the pellet in 100 µL of TSS
    • add 1 µL of plasmid (~200 ng)
    • incubate 30-60 min on ice
    • heatshock @ 42 °C for 1 min
    • incubate 15 min on ice
    • add 1 mL LB-broth
    • incubate 1h on thermoshaker (37 °C, 400 rpm)
    • centrifuge (10min, 4000 rpm)
    • resuspend in 50 µL residual media
    • plate on plate
    • incubate @ 37 °C over night
