Mix briefly by vortexing. Application DNA Binding Buffer : Sample Plasmid, genomic DNA (>2 kb) 2:1 PCR product, DNA fragment 5:1 ssDNA 7:1 Transfer mixture to a provided Zymo-Spin™ Column in a Collection Tube. Centrifuge for 30 seconds. Discard the flow-through. Add 200 µl DNA Wash Buffer to the column. Centrifuge for 30 seconds. Repeat the wash step. Add ≥ 6 µl DNA Elution Buffer or water directly to the column matrix and incubate at room temperature for one minute.
