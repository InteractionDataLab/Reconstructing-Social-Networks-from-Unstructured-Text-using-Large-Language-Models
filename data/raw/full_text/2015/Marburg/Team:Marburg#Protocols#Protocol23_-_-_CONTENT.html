    • Yeast Miniprep

    • pellet 10ml of ON yeast culture
    • resuspend in 600 μl water
    • add 100 μl lysis buffer
    • add 1Volume beads
    • 2 min bead beating - 8000rpm
    • use hot needle to make hole into tube to get rid of beads
    • centrifuge (5min, 4000 rpm)
    • add 350 μl neutralisation buffer
    • centrifuge (5min, 4000 rpm)
    • apply supernatant to column
    • centrifuge (5min, 4000 rpm)
    • apply 200 μl Endo wash buffer
    • centrifuge (5min, 4000 rpm)
    • apply 400 μl wash buffer
    • centrifuge (5min, 4000 rpm)
    • dry on heating block for 10min
    • heat water
    • add 20 μl hot water
    • centrifuge (2min, 13000rpm)
