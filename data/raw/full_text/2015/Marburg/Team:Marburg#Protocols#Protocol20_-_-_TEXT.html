Do not allow the lysis reaction to proceed for more than 5 min. If using LyseBlue reagent, the solution will turn blue add 350 μl Buffer N3 and mix immediately and thoroughly by inverting the tube 4–6 times. If using LyseBlue reagent, the solution will turn colorless centrifuge for 10 min at 13,000 rpm (~17,900 x g) in a table-top microcentrifuge. apply the supernatant from step 5 to the QIAprep spin column by decanting or pipetting. S Centrifuge for 30–60 s and discard the flow-through, or z apply vacuum to the manifold to draw the solution through the QIAprep spin column and switch off the vacuum source recommended: Wash the QIAprep spin column by adding 0.5 ml Buffer PB. S Centrifuge for 30–60 s and discard the flow-through, or z apply vacuum to the manifold to draw the solution through the QIAprep spin column and switch off the vacuum source. Note: This step is only required when using endA+ strains or other bacteria strains with high nuclease activity or carbohydrate content wash the QIAprep spin column by adding 0.75 ml Buffer PE. S Centrifuge for 30–60 s and discard the flow-through, or z apply vacuum to the manifold to draw the solution through the QIAprep spin column and switch off the vacuum source. Transfer the QIAprep spin column to the collection tube centrifuge for 1 min to remove residual wash buffer place the QIAprep column in a clean 1.5 ml microcentrifuge tube.
