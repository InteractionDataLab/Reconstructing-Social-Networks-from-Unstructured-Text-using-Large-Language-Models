    FRT problems

    The transformation with the FRT may be run well, but the plasmid with the gene coding for flippase works only for E. coli. We can't use this plasmid because it will be rejected by the yeast. Other transformation with CreLox system is possible. CreLox is a gene which have the same fonction than the FRT, it not cut by the flippase but by the Cre recombinase.

    We design two primers for the new transformation with CreLox system. Primer 5'-3' CreLox + PHO855’-TATCATTATATATACATGGCTACGGTTTTTCGCTGACGGGCTGCGATAACTTCGTATAGCATACATTATACGAAGTTATATAATCATTTGCATCCATACATTTTGATGGC-3’ Primer 3'-5' CreLox + PHO853’-AAGGGATATATAGCGCGGCAAACTGGGCAAACTTGAGCAATACCAATAACTTCGTATAGCATACATTATACGAAGTTATCAGCAGTATAGCGACCAGCATTC-5’ - Homology tail on gene PHO85 - CreLox sequence - Kanamycin resistance binding
