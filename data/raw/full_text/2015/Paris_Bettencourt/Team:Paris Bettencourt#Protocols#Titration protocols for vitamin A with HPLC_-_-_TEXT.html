Add 0.5 to 0.75 mm glass beads and vortex for 3 minutes. Add 2.5 mL of 0.2% (wt/vol) pyrogallol dissolved in methanol and vortex for 3 minutes. Add 1.25 mL of 60% (wt/vol) KOH and vortex for 10 seconds. Incubate for 1 h at 75ºC, vortexing every 15 minutes. Add appropriate amount of hexane. Centrifuge tubes for 5 minutes at 2,800 rpm. Pipette 1 mL of hexane into a cuvette.
