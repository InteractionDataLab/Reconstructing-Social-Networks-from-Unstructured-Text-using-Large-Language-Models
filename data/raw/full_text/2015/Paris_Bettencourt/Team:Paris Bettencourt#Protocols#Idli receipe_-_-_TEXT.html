(for 1.5 volume of rice, put the same volume of water, and 0.5 volume of dall, put the same volume of water). Rinse both, mix it, add 2/3 volume of water of this volume. Blend this mix, and let ferment for 12-16 hours. Then Cook it with idli cooker for 15 minutes. In the case where we add micro-organisms, we have 3 options : 1) Add µorganisms during the soak phase; 2) Add µorganisms after the blending phase. 3) Let soak µorganisms in the same time that rices and dall, and add it for the blending phase. The third was principally used.
