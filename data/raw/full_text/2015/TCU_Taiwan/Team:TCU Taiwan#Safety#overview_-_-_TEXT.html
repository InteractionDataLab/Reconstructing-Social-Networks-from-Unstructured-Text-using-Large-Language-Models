Such as EtBr, chloroform, isopropanol, etc. To prevent their harm, we wear rubber gloves, lab coat and used chemical hood when using them. Risks to the safety and health of the general public: Our antimicrobial peptides, Signferin is from Crinia signifera, and Epinecidin-1 is from Epinephelus coioides. We use E.coli to synthesizes the peptide. The antimicrobial peptides will be applied to open wounds or burns, the small peptides might induce allergic reactions in some individuals. To reduce this risk, allergic test can be preformed prior to the application of the deressing. Risks to the environment: We modified E.coli to produce antimicrobial peptides, if these E.coli are released to environment, they might cause genetic pollution.
