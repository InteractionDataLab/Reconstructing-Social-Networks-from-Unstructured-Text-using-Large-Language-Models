Soil contamination experiment

by Johan and Coralie We use a soil extract which was prepared by suspending 1g of soil in 5mL of steril water. We made 5 dilutions of the supernatant: 10^-2, 10^-3, 10^-4, 10^-5 and 10^-6. We streaked 100µL of each dilution in different plates:

  • LB medium:
  • Without any antibiotic
  • 100ng/µL Spectinomycin
  • 20 ng/µL Chloramphenicol
  • 10ng/µL Tetracycline
  • Mac Conkey medium:
  • Without any antibiotic
  • 100ng/µL Spectinomycin
  • 20 ng/µL Chloramphenicol
  • 10ng/µL Tetracycline

    • Incubation ON, 37°C
