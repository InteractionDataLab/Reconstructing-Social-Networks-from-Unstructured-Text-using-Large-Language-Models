    Soil experiment

    by Coralie and Johan

    Soil

    Observation of J0 plates: there are a lot of colony in each.

    Control (-):

    • MCK + Tet: 40 pink colonies
    • MCK + Cm: 10 pink colonies
    • MCK + Spectino: 1 pink colony
    • MCK: 100 pink colonies and a lot of white colonies

    We can suppose that we have a contamination between our experiment and control. Or maybe we have a MCK problem because we change it during the experiment.

    So, we decide to stop the experiment and do some test:

    • Soil + H2O on new MCK
    • Soil + LB on new MCK
    • LB on new MCK
    • MCK alone

    Incubation over night, 37°C
