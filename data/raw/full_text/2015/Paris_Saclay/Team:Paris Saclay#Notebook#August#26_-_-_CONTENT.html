PCR on colony

by Audrey

  • BBa_K1707037 #1 to #9

PCR mix for each tube:

  • 9,75 µL H2O
  • 5 µL Buffer Taq 10x
  • 1 μL dNTP 10mM
  • 0,5 μL Forward primer iPS43
  • 0,5 μL Reverse primer iPS44
  • 3 μL 25mM MgCl2
  • 0,25 μL Taq Pol

PCR Cycle:

  • 95°C - 6 + 2 min
  • 30 cycles:
  • 95°C - 30 seconds
  • 51,8°C - 30 seconds
  • 72°C - 2 minutes
  • 72°C - 10 minutes
  • 4°C for ever
