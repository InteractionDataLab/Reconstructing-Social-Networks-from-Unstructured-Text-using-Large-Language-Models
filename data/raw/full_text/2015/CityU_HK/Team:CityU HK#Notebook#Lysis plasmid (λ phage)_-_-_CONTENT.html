    Lysis plasmid (λ phage)

    Keys to the table: ori: original gene sequence, without codon optimization co: codon optimized λ Lysis(o_o_o): SλWT(ori)-Rλ(ori)-Rzλ(ori)λLysis(c_c_c): SλWT(co)-Rλ(co)-Rzλ(co) λLysis_Sm(o_c_c): Sλmut(ori)-Rλ(co)-Rzλ(co)λLysis_Sm(c_c_c): Sλmut(co)-Rλ(co)-Rzλ(co) [x/y]: restriction sites digested: E, EcoRI; X, XbaI; S, SpeI; P, PstI Gene name or plasmid name [x/y]: the gene fragment or plasmid flanked with the restriction sites x and y at the ends -A ribosome binding site (RBS) is linked upstream of each gene -Coloring refers to sub-tasks in that particular group -The cells used for transformation were competent JM109 E. coli cells
