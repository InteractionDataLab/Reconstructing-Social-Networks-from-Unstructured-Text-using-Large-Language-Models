The high school students enjoyed the talk and activities organized by our team and expressed their interest in the field of synthetic biology which is presently not covered in the high school curriculum.  Aims Educate high school students about synthetic biology Stimulate interest in synthetic biology through interactive and educational activities Generate discussion about social and ethical concerns surrounding synthetic biology From the outreach program, we realized that high school students are more knowledgeable than we previously thought. They have a basic understanding of synthetic biology and its applications although there is little discussion about synthetic biology by teachers and classmates in schools. Through our interactions with high school students, it is clear that the barrier that lies between synthetic biology and the younger generation is the lack of adequate means for proper discussion.  Gallery - CNEC Christian College Gallery - Henrietta Secondary School Gallery - QESOSA Tong Kwok Wah Secondary School ABOUT US We are a diverse team of CityU undergraduates, working hard to create a better world. read more LOCATION Department of Biology and Chemistry, City University of Hong Kong Tat Chee Avenue, Kowloon, Hong Kong SAR CONTACT US Email: cityu.igem2015@gmail.com Tel: +852 34427654 FOLLOW US ON
