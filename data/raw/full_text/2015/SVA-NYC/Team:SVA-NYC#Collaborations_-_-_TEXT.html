We offered our facilities as a data validation collaboration where we would transform and characterize their systems using various strains across varied conditions but the delivery of the plasmids did not arrive until after the wiki freeze. Genspace Brooklyn, NY The Genspace iGEM team offered to collaborate on characterizing an E. coli Nitrate Biosensor consisting of the YeaR promoter element driving a fluorescent protein and regulated by the NsrR repressor element that inhibits transcription in the absence of nitrates and nitrites. Unfortunately, the assembly of said part was not completed in time for us to test using our strains.
