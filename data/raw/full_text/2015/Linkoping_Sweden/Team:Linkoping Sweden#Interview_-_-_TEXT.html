A young allergic woman, the mother of an allergic child, a childrens allergist and a scientist specialised in child allergies participated in this documentary to share their experiences of living and working with food allergies. The purpose of this documentary is to provide a deeper understanding of any eventual needs for an allergen detector. This is done by gathering opinions from the affected of how they feel about relying on the public to understand their needs. The goal is also to present it to the public and increase their understanding of food allergies and what obsticles people with food allergies might experience in their everyday life.
