    Sponsors

    Our participation at this yearâ€™s competition was made possible by the extremely generous support of our sponsors. We are eternally thankful for everything you have done for us!

    Chalmers University of Technology provided us with lab space, equipment, material and all the chemicals and biomaterials used during our efforts, as well as also financially funding our team in order to send us to the Jamboree in Boston. We are all very thankful for everything you have done for us!

    Chalmers MasterCard. The foundation at Chalmers that gives financial support to student driven projects at the university. We are extremely grateful that they decided to help our team.

    Promega Biotech AB. The company has products for research in genomics, protein analysis and expression, cellular analysis, drug discovery and genetic identity. They are a global leader in both solutions and support for scientists in both the academic and industrial world. They have been generous with their support of our project by providing monetary aid that have helped us in reach our goal of sending our team to the Jamboree in Boston. We appreciate all their help!

    Geneious. They have gracefully provided us with free licenses of their online software in order for us to be able to design our constructs. The software is a great help for scientists in molecular biology and they have our sincerest gratitude.

    QIAGEN has given us licenses of their workbench software, which we are super appreciative for.

    GATC Biotech. They have provided us with free SUPREMERUN DNA sequencing of our constructs. This has helped us a lot in the control of our systems and we are ever so thankful for their help.

    We would like to give a special thanks to those who have helped us by donating money via our crowd funding page! We are very grateful for your support: M.N, Glenn, Neuros Moonshiner, iGEM Stockholm, Felix Richter, Martin Hellgren, Niclas Ekestubbe.
