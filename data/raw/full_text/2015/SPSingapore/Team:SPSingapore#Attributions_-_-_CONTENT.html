    Team Members
    Tan Yi Han was in charge of purchasing supplies, and safety in the lab. She also worked on the pNirB Anaerobic Promoter system and on characterising the Invasin toxin. Wong Chi Yan worked primarily on the pNirB Anaerobic Promoter system. Clarice Hong worked on the esa Quorum sensing (QS) system. Yeo Xin Yi worked on videos and the Invasin + Listerolysin toxins. Adrian Tan Hong Ji designed the tools for our human practices workshop and worked on the esa QS system. Soong Yun Ting worked on the Invasin + Listerolysin toxins. Nguyen Duy was in charge of the website, and confocal microscopy. Hee Yan Ting established the mammalian cell line work in our lab, and also worked on the Invasin + Listerolysin toxins. Kenneth Lim designed tools for our human practices workshop, and characterised the esa QS system.
