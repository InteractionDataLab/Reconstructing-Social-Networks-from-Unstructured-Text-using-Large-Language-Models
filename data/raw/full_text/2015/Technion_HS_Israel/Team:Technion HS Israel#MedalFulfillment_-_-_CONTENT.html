Achievements

Bronze

    Register for iGEM, have an amazing summer, and plan to attend the Giant Jamboree

    Complete the Judging form and all required consent forms

    Create and share a Description of the team's project using the iGEM wiki, and document the team's parts using the Registry of Standard Biological Parts.

    Prepare a poster and a talk at the iGEM Jamboree

    Create a wiki page with clear attribution of each aspect of our project.

    Become a mentee for any iGEM team that has previously participated in the iGEM competition and is attending the 2015 Jamboree

Silver

    Document at least one new standard BioBrick Part or Device central to your project and submit this part to the iGEM Registry

    Document the characterization of an existing part in the 'Main Page' section of that Part's/Device's Registry entry

    Human Practice activity

Gold

    Demonstrate an innovative Human Practices activity that relates to your project

    Experimentally validate that at least one new BioBrick Part or Device of your own design and construction works as expected
