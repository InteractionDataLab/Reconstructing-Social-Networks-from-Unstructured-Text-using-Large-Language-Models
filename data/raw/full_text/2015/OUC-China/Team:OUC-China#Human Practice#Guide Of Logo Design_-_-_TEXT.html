A good logo can be an advantage in communication, judge and presentation. Here we made a guide for new iGEM members to show them how to design their logos. Purpose of Design Attract Attention Show the Image of the Team Symbolize Project Principle of Design Artistry Good-looking is necessary to a logo, iGEM team logo is without exception. Easy identification Make your team logo easy to recognize. Try to add something can stand for your team in your logo. Originality The iGEM logo is often used as a part of many team logos with or without any change. Honestly, it is a good way to show the relationship between the iGEM and the team. And a new image created by team with the meaning of iGEM used in logo will be great, too! Intelligibility The logo with clear symbolic significance will be good. Universality A good iGEM team logo should have universality so that can be used in wiki, poster, banner, name cards, team uniforms, etc. It requires that the logo is good-looking in all these medias. For example, the logo can be recognized in both monochrome and multicolor, can be harmonious in all the size of the background. Taboos of Design We summarized some taboos of design for iGEM teams to avoid unnecessary misunderstandings. The meaning of these cautions are easy to find via internet.
