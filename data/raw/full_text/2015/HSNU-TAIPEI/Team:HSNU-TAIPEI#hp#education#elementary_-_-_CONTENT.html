    Introduction

    With the experienced visits of Tamshui Junior High School and Hua Xing High School, we have confidence to promote our concept to different age. So we visit Xing Long Elementary school, to promote IGEM in elementary schools. By means of the opportunity, we want to introduce the basic knowledge of synthetic biology to the little boys and girls. We want to contribute to the society and make the society progress and influence more and more people to work together.

    flow

    1. 13:30-13:35 introduction ourself
    2. 13:35-13:50 Project presentation
    3. 13:50-14:00 Q&A time
    4. 14:00-14:30 Giving feed back through google form and let them play with phone apps we've made for the project
    5. 14:30-14:35 take pictrues
