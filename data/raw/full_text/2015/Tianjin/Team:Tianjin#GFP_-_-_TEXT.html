Fragment concentration:55.0ng/μL Date: Wednesday,7.15.2015 SOE PCR SOE PCR stands for Splicing by Overlapp Extension PCR. It is a standard overlapp extension procedure, enabling the assembly of genes wihtout performing any cloning, digesting or ligation inbetween. All you need to do ist running PCRs with specific primers. If gene A is the upstream part and gene B has to be assembled downstream of gene A, primer lo of gene A should have an overlapp of around 20 nucleotides complementary to the first 20 nucleotides of gene B. Primer up of gene B should haven a complementary overlapp of 20 nucleotides to the end of gene A. gene of interst：GFP- sJanus primer：Xba1-GFP、sJanus-Pst1 temperature: 53, 55, 56, and 58 centigrade.
