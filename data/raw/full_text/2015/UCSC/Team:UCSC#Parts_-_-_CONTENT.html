    The UCSC iGEM team fully supports open source information and collaborative thinking towards the advancement of scientific research. And as our campus is the home of Human Genome Browser and several other sources of biological data, we are driven to bring the sharing of knowledge into the new century. In order for our research to be the most available it can be, we will be uploading all of the sequences of the engineered gene, primers, or biological tools that we create so that they may be used by researchers across the world for the benefit of mankind.
