Judging Criteria 2.1 Bronze We have registered our team , spent a full summer, and plan to enjoy a feast of knowledge and culture at the Giant Jamboree. We have successfully completed and submitted the iGEM 2015 Judging form. Our story of Synthetic Intelligent Ranger E. coli for Nematodes (SIRENS) are descripted using the iGEM wiki. And we have designed, built and characterized several new functional standard biobricks including: Biobrick: We made a poster and presentation, which is going to be presented at the Giant Jamboree. The attribution of our work is clearly, and we have distinguished the work done by us and work down by others in the wiki. 2.2 Sliver We have designed, built and experimentally validated three new functional biobricks and submitted characterization data to the parts registry page. BBa_K: Three new parts that we designed have been submitted to the iGEM Parts Registry. BBa_K: Safety Lab Safety: All members of laboratory have underwent safety training. Before one leaves the lab, he must fill out the laboratory safety recording form. Environment Safety: First, we have evaluated the project feasibility and its relating security issue. To ensure that Sirens can work safely and effectively in the environment, we constructed a bidirecational light control promoter system to control the release time of toxic protein. In addition, we designed a suicide switch for E.coli colony to avoid E.coli spreading into the environment after attractanting and killing nematodes. Finally, we designed a device to limit the range of E.coli proliferation. Pesticide Use Safety: We made a survey in the Ceyu town, Hebei province for pesticide using. Our survey objects contained government, dealers and farmers. 2.3 Gold Policy&Practice We have characterized a previously existing BioBrick Part——the light senser system( BBa_I15008 , BBa_I15009 , BBa_I15010 and BBa_R0082 ), see the improvement page for more information. We gave a detailed help in modeling and release of membrane protein to Beijing Institute of Technology Release of Membrane Protein: Modeling: We have demonstrated a functional prototype of our project. As well as designed a device by data of experiment and modeling, we put it into practice in the laboratory.
