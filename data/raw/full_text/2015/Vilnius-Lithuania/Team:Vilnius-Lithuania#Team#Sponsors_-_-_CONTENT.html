      Vilnius University

      Vilnius University is the oldest and largest Lithuanian higher education institution. It was established in the 16th century and now is one the most important parts of culture, science and research in Lithuania. The mission of Vilnius University is to create, accumulate and disseminate knowledge by ensuring continuity of authentic university culture, dintinguished by the atmosphere where old traditions and new ideas enrich each other.

      GENERAL SPONSOR

      Institute of Biotechnology

      Institute of Biotechnology is a part of Vilnius University, which focuses on scientific researches and biotechnology. Institute seeks to maintain the high standarts in scientific endevour, research training and technological advance, with its main focus in a broadly defined field of molecular biotechnology, including nucleic acid and protein technologies, bioinformatics, immunodiagnostics, drug design, microfluidics, next generation epigenomics and gene editing technologies.

      Industry sponsors

      Thermo Fisher Scientific

      Thermo Fisher Scientific is the largest company in the world, supplying products, equipment and services for science. They serve customers, including academical and research institutions, diagnostic laboratories and biotechnology companies in more than 50 countries. One their goals is to accelerate life sciences research and increase laboratory productivity.

      GENERAL SPONSOR

      Neurotechnology

      Neurotechnology was founded in 1990 in Vilnius, Lithuania, as the company that brought together neuroinformatics, image processing and pattern recognition. Since the establishment they released more than 80 software development products or algorithms for identification and verification of objects and personal identity. With millions of customer installations worldwide, Neurotechnology's products are used for both civil and forensic applications.

      GENERAL SPONSOR

      Linea libera

      Linea Libera is laboratory equipment supplier, which is focused on new and effective technology, allowing scientists to achieve better results at work. They do not only provide laboratories with the equipment, but they also actively promote new technologies in Lithuania by organizing seminars, demonstrations and collaborating with schools.

      Nanodiagnostika

      Nanodiagnostika is a biotechnological company, which performes sequencing and oligonucleotide synthesis. They also provide reagents supplies, such as DNA, RNA or protein extraction and purification kits.

      Friends

      OMConsulting

      OMConsulting is a company, offering solutions on strategic communication. Their mission is to help people and different organizations to build and retain a good reputation. OMConsulting acts as advisor to managers, helping them to see an organization from a different perspective.

      Enterprise Lithuania

      Enterprise Lithuania is business-promoting agency, which works by providing high quality training, consultancy, by performing market analysis and offering business partner search services. Enterprise Lithuania gives advice to business agencies and operates as mentors for young and ambitious enterpreneurs.

      We love Lithuania

      We love Lithuania is a non-profit organization, which was first established as a photography project to show how beautiful as a country Lithuania is. We love Lithuania released a postcard series of best photography shots of Lithuanian nature, cities, people, food and atmosphere.

      Lithuanian World Community

      The Lithuanian World Community is a non-governmental and non-profit organization established in 1949 that unifies Lithuanian communities abroad. They organize educational, cultural, community, sports and social events and, in addition, assists lithuanians or lithuanian organizations in foreign countries.

      Expo vakarai

      Expo vakarai is the western Lithuanian exhibition and trade fairs organizer. During the 19 years of operation they organized more than one hundred events, from small regional fairs to major international exhibitions and conferences, which took part in more than 7000 companies from Lithuania and foreign countries.
