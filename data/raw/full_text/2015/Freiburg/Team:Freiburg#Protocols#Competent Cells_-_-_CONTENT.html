    Protocol

    • streak out E. coli cell on LB plate without (or with appropriate antibiotics), grow over night at 37°C
    • inocculate 5 ml o/n culture with one colony
    • inocculate 100 ml LB with 1 ml o/n culture, grow for 2-3 h until an OD(600)=0,4 to 0,6
    • transfer bacteria into pre-cooled and sterile centrifuge tube (250 ml) and pellet bacteria 5 min (5000 rpm; 4°C)
    • discard supernatant

    from now on: work in cold room

    • resuspend pellet in 20 ml cold TFB1 with cut tips
    • incubate 5 min on ice
    • centrifuge 5 min (5000 rpm; 4°C)
    • resuspend pellet in 4 ml cold TFB2 with cut tips
    • aliquot 50 ml in screw cap eppis and freeze in liquid nitrogen
    • store at -80°C

    buffer preparation

    TFB1200ml
    30 mM KAc0,589 g
    50 mM MnCl21,6919 g
    100 mM KCl1,482 g
    10 mM CaCl20,294 g
    15 % Glycerol30 g

    * sterile filtrate and store at 4°C

    TFB2200 ml
    10 mM MOPS0,419 g
    10 mM KCl0,149 g
    75 mM CaCl22,205 g
    15 % Glycerol30 g

    * autoclave and store at 4°C
