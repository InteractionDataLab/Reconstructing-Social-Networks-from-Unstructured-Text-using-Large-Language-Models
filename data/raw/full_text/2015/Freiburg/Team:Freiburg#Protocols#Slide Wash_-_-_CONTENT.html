    iRIf slide wash steps

    iRIf slide wash steps after measurement/before new measurementremarks concerning the protocol (author, adapted from etc.)

    material: 0,1% SDS, 10% RBS, diH2O, Kimtechtuch duration: Incubation in SDS o/n + ~10 mins for further washing

    1. Add volume of added chemical (µL) (remarks, tips)
    2. instructions time, speed, temperature (remarks, tips)

    After measuring with iRIf device:

    • Fill slide-box with 0,1% SDS; incubate Slides o/n
    • rub slide surface with 0,1% SDS with Kimtechtuch
    • wash with diH2O from wash bottle
    • dry slides with cleaning wipe/nitrogenpistole
    • incubate in 10%ige RBS (glasbox)for 1-3 minutes; twist slides 1x within this time window
    • rub slide surface with 10% RBS with Kimtechtuch (RBS that is already on the slides is enough)
    • wash properly with diH2O
    • dry with nitrogenpistole
    • when completely dry (change position of the pincette at least one time to get rid of the water that is covered through it) sort slides into slidebox
    • plasma activate slides for 5 min
    • store slides in iRIf slide box
    • Wash pincettes before and after use with aceton (Caution: do not wash them in top of the H2O/Ethanol-waste, seperate waste!)
    • Slide „303“ is not properly cleanable, avoid using it
