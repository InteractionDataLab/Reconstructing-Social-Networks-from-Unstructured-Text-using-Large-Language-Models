    Coomassie microwave staining protocol

    modified from Stefan, AG Roth

    material:aqua dest, Coomassie staining solution, 20% NaCl solution duration: 1h

    staining and destaining

    • stain the gel in staining solution for 10min in an orbital shaker
    • remove staining solution and wash once with water
    • repeat the following steps 2-3 times
    • add water so that gel is covered and heat in microwave (800W ~ 0.5min) until solution starts to boil
    • incubate 5 min on orbital shaker
    • discard water and refill
  • wash the gel in aqua dest for 10 min
  • if bands are sufficiently visible take a picture

    • Possibly the use of 20% NaCl for at least 5 min may further increase the detection limit.

    Original protocol from Simon Wischgoll
