Therefore only the template, which was amplified in E. coli and thus is methylated will be digested, while the unmethylated PCR products will remain intact. material: DpnI, thermoblock, PCR product duration: 10 min preparation, 80 min incubation Add 2 µl DpnI to your PCR products Incubate at 37°C for 60 min (digest) Incubate at 80°C for 20 min (heat inactivation of DpnI) PCR products can be cleaned-up and used e.g. for cloning purposes protocols , cloning
