    blunt end ligation

    material: blunt end vector pjet, 2x ligation buffer, T4 Ligase, autoclaved H2O, DNA fragment of interests duration: 7 min

    • pipet the following:
    quantity/µlingedient
    1ligation fragment (10 ng/µl)
    0.5pJet
    1T4 ligase
    52xbuffer
    2.5H2O
    • incubate for 5 Min @ RT
    • do a Transformation with 5 µl in E. coli TOP10, plate 100 µl
    • keep the rest at 4°C as backup until transformation worked
