    Regeneration of Tris NTA slides

    from Chang AG Piehler

    material: Imidazol, EDTA, HBS-buffer, HCl, NiCl duration: … min

    Always check for pH after using deionized water!
    • Prepare slide holder with 500 mM Imidazol
    • Incubate slide for 15 min
    • Prepare slide holder with 250 mM EDTA pH 7.5
    • Incubate slide for 15 min
    • Dip 3x in HBS buffer in slideholder A
    Also wash tweezers! Best step to store @ -20 °C
    • Prepare 100 mM HCl in HBS
    • Incubate 1 min with slides crossed
    • Dip 3x in HBS buffer in slideholder A
    • Prepare 10 mM NiCl solution
    • Incubate 3 min with slides crossed
    • Dip 3x in HBS buffer in slideholder B
    • Incubate in 500 mM Imidazol for 1 min
    • Dip 3x in HBS buffer in slideholder B
    • Prepare 1/5 HBS buffer dilution in aqua dest. (Control pH!)
    • Wash in diluted HBS buffer
    • Dry slides with wafer gun
