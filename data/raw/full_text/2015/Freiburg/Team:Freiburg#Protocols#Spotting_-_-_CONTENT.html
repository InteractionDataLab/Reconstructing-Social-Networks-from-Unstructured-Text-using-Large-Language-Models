    Spotting+Blocking

    e.g. protocol spotting and Blocking of GOPTS and APTES/PDITC slides

    material: chemicals, used kits, … duration: … min

    • get GOPTS or APTES/PDITC slides
    • pipette 0.5 µl proteinsloution (e.g. GFP) per spot onto the slide (use following schema)
    Spotting model. green: GFP 500 µg/mL, yellow: GFP 1000 µg/mL, red: GFP 1680 µg/mL, violet: 2 µg/ml bBSA, blue: BSA 10 mg/mL
    for spotting with PDMS mask:
    • use 5 µl proteinsolution
    • remove proteinsolution after incubation with pipet
    • block for 5 min with 10mg/ml BSA (in PDMS wells)
    • put slide in blocking solution and remove PDMS mask
    1. incubate for 1 h at RT
    2. transfer slides into blocking solution for 30 min-60 min
    3. blocking solution for GOPTS: 10mg/ml BSA + 0.5% ethanolamine
    4. blocking solution for APTES/PDITC:5mg/ml BSA + 5% ethanolamine
    5. 2x wash slides for 10 min in PBS (shake it)
    6. clean with 1/10 diluted PBS
    7. dry it with nitrogen gun
    8. measure fluorescence at fluorescence microscope
    9. incubate for 45 min in Strep-Cy5 solution (2µg/ml)+ 5-10mg/ml BSA
    10. 2x wash 10 min in PBS (shake it)
    11. clean with H2O form wash bottle and dry it with nitrogen gun
