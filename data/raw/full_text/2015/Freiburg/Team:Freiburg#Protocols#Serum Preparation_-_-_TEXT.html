Inspect serum for turbidity. Turbid samples should be centrifuged and aspirated again to remove remaining insoluble matter. aspirate supernatant (=serum) and aliquot into cryovials and flashfreeze in liquid nitrogen and store at –80 °C, take care not to disturb the cell layer or transfer any cells protocols
