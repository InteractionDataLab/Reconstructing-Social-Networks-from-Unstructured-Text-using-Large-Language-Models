    Below are some of the recipes and protocols we used during our project.

    Recipes

    LB Broth (makes 1 liter)

    1. 10.0 g tryptone
    2. 10.0 g NaCl
    3. 5.0 g yeast extract
    4. Adjust volume to 1.0 L with DI H2O
    5. Adjust pH to 7.5 with NaOH/HCl as necessary.
    6. Sterilize via autoclaving.

    Polyacrylamide Gel (makes 5 milliliters)

    1. 500 µL 10X TBE
    2. 35 µL ammonium persulfate
    3. 375 µL 40% acrylamide solution
    4. 2 µL TEMED
    5. Adjust volume to 5 mL with DI H2O
