    Week 106/01 - 06/05

    Reception and bacterial storage of Biobricks

    1. Resuspension of Biobricks (BBa) DNA in 15 µl of DNAse, RNAse free water: BBa_K808007, BBa_K808010, BBa_K808011, BBa_K808012, BBa_K808013, BBa_K808014, BBa_K936000, BBa_K316003.
    2. Storage at -20°C.
    3. Transformation in DH5-α competent cells purchased from Invitrogen (# 18265-017) was done following the transformation protocol. (BBa_K808007, BBa_K808010, BBa_K808011, BBa_K808012, BBa_K808013, BBa_K808014, BBa_K936000, BBa_K316003).
    4. Cells were plated on LB + chloramphenicol 34 µg/ml (Cm 34).
    5. Incubation overnight at 37°C.
    6. Storage at 4°C.
    7. Plasmid purification following the MiniPrep protocol.
