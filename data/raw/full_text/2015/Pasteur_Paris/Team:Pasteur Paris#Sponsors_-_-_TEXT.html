BlueBird Bio BlueBird Bio is an american company based in Boson (MA) working on gene therapy as a one-time treatment option for patients with severe and rare genetic diseases and cancer. Biomatters - Geneious Based in New Zealand, Biomatters is the software company that developed Geneious, used to study specific DNA sequences. Citech The Center for Innovation and Technological Research (Citech) is a technological hub inside the Pasteur Institute and provides access to high-technology equipment and skills. Eurofins Genomics Located in Eberbsberg, Eurofins is an international provider of genomic services around the core business lines next generation sequencing, genotyping, gene expression, custom DNA sequencing, oligonucleotides, siRNA and gene synthesis. French Embassy in the USA The Office for Science and Technology at the French Embassy in the United States of America provides financial support to French students and scientists to enable them to participate in competitions on the subject of Life Sciences. Institut Pasteur Founded in 1887 and based in France, the Institut Pasteur is an international research institute of excellence with a mission to advance science, medicine and public health. The Institut Pasteur is a private, non-profit foundation with recognized charitable status entrusted with four core missions of public interest: research, education, public health, and valorisation of scientific research. Institut Carnot - Pasteur MI Within the Institut Pasteur, the Institut Carnot - Pasteur MI promotes an ambitious research and partners with companies in human health and biotechnology. New England Biolabs New England Biolabs is a leader in the discovery and development of a large variety of recombinant and native enzymes, used for genomic resaerch. The company is a major supplier of reagents for the life sciences research. Veolia Environnement - Research and Innovation Veolia is a French company specialized in diverse fields going from water delivery to public transportation.
