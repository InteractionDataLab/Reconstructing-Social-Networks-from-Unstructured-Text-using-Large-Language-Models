In addition to being non-pathogenic, the yeast strain has the additional safeguard in that it is an auxotrophic species unable to synthesize tryptophan, uracil, leucine, and histidine. We always wore nitrile gloves when working on the benchtop, and we autoclaved anything biosafety level 1 organisms could have touched before discarding it as directed by the lab biosafety officer, Chris Flynn. We wore gloves whenever we were working with ethidium bromide, We wore lab coats, gloves, and a face mask whenever we had the UV light on to excise DNA from an electrophoresis gel, and we wore gloves while working in a fume hood when we used hexane. For our sequences we chose to use sequences all from Biosafety level 1 organisms.
