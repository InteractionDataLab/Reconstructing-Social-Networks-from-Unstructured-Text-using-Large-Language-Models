    We would like to thank...

    Agilent Technologies

    Agilent graciously donated a SureVector kit for creating yeast shuttle and expression vectors as well as a set of XL1 Blue Supercompetent cells in conjunction with their sponsorship of iGEM.

    Integrated DNA Technologies

    IDT kindly provided us with 20Kb free DNA synthesis in conjunction with their sponsorship of iGEM.

    GenScript

    GenScript kindly provided us with free PCR primer synthesis in conjunction with their sponsorship of iGEM.

    SnapGene

    SnapGene were kind enough to donate free licences for use with their SnapGene DNA visualization and management software.

    UCL Engineering

    UCL engineering generously funded the travel, accomodation, and Jamboree attendance fee to allow two London Biohackspace members to represent us at the 20165 jamboree. University College London hosted a Boot Camp week in which our team members gained valuable assistance and skills to prepare for competing in iGEM.

    SynbiCITE

    SynbiCITE generously funded the iGEM entry fee for the London Biohackspace 2015 iGEM team.

    Genome Compiler

    Genome Compiler were kind enough to donate free licences for use with their online DNA visualization and management software.
