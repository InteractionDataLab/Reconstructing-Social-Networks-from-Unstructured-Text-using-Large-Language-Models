Compared with the free enzyme, the immobilized enzyme can maintain its high specificity and catalytic properties of a particular enzyme. Cellulosome are muti-enzyme complex present in certain prokaryotes by a variety of cellulases, hemicellulases, relying on the specific interaction of cohesion-dockerin.
