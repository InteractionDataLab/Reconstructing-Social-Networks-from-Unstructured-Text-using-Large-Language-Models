He works in the Wente Lab investigating the effects of the Ran gradient on mitochondria in the context of HGPS. As President, Ophir is responsible for overseeing the different divisions of the team, including planning projects, fundraising, managing team resources, and outreach. Stephen Lee Vice President Stephen is a Junior majoring in Biomedical Engineering. He works in the Friedman Lab using CRISPR-Cas9 to study the role of telomere-associated proteins in double-stranded break repair in yeast. As Vice President, Stephen conducts lab work for the team, manages team resources, and assists with organizing team events. Sikandar Raza Treasurer Sikandar is a Sophomore majoring in chemistry and neuroscience. Jarrod Shilts Director of Research Jarrod is a Junior majoring in Neuroscience and Molecular & Cellular Biology.
