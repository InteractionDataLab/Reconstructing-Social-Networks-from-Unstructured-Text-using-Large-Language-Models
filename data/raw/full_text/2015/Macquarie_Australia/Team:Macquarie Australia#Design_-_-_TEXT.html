Photosystem II Through the expression of 5 operons we aim to induce E. coli to build the Photosystem II protein complex which contains the Oxygen Evolving Centre of photosynthesis. Hydrogenase A hydrogenase enzyme can take two protons and two electrons to build H2 (molecular hydrogen). Please see our References page for the list of citations.
