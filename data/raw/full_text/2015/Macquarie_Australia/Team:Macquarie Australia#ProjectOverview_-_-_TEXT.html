Protoporphyrin-IX is present within E. coli and is typically used to make a different tetrapyrrole: heme. Photosystem II Through the expression of 5 operons we aim to induce E. coli to build the Photosystem II protein complex which contains the Oxygen Evolving Centre of photosynthesis. Hydrogenase A hydrogenase enzyme can take two protons and two electrons to build H2 (molecular hydrogen).
