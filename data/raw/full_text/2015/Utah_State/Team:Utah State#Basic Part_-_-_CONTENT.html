    BBa_K1820014

    This part was designed as a green fluorescent protein generator to provide a proof of concept for the ability to transform and express different genes in Lactococcus lactis.

    Intermediate parts (N- to C-terminal)

    K2033220 – Constitutive promoter from 2013 Uppsala team

    B0034 – Ribosome binding site with 1.0 level of efficiency

    K1365020 – Super-folded green fluorescent protein (GFP) from 2014 Groningen team

    B0015 – Composite part of two transcriptional terminators

    BBa_K1820019

    Intermediate parts (N- to C-terminal)

    K1033225 – Constitutive promoter from 2013 Uppsala team

    B0015 - Composite part of two transcriptional terminators

    BBa_K1820020

    This part was constructed as a phage inducible green fluorescent protein generator to detect the presence of phage within a Lactococcus lactis culture. Refer to our project page for background information on phage infection in L. lactis cheese cultures and to our design page on how our system works.

    Intermediate parts (N- to C-terminal)

    K1820001- Composite part of K180000 (phage-inducible promoter) and B0034 (a ribosome binding site with 1.0 level of efficiency)

    K1365020 – Super-folded green fluorescent protein (GFP) from 2014 Groningen team

    B0015 - Composite part of two transcriptional terminators
