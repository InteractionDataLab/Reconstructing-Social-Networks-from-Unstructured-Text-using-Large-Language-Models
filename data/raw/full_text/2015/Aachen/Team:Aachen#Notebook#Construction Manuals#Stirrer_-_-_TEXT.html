The motor is cylindrical, with a diameter just under 25 mm, D shaped output shaft which is 4 mm in diameter and extends 12.5 mm from the face plate of the gear box. This motor unit weighing 95 g, has a speed of 1400 RPM and 150 mA Current rating at 6V. 3D Structure with magnets spacer Magnets and 3D structure Neodymium cubic supermagnets of size 5 mm are fitted into a 3D structure on the motor, to create a magnetic field for the fish inside the bioreactor. The 3D structure is a platform to be fitted on the DC motor, having cubical holes to fit the magnets in. Geared DC Motor Transistor IRLIZ44N It is a single pulse avalanche rated MOSFET in an isolated package. It has a continuous Drain current of maximum 30 A and Drain to Source maximum withstandable voltage of 16V. spacer Circuit The circuit uses a Transistor and a Diode to control the RPM value using a PWM pin from Arduino.
