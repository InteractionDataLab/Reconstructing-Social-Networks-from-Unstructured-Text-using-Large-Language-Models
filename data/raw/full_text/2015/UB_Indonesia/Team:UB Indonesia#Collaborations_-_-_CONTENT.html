    Collaborating with company and health department. Kyushu Medical Co. Ltd., recently open a new branch in Indonesia. We collaborate in some researches and this human practice project together with Health Department of East Java Province and Kediri City. Regularly, we visit the Health Department Entomology Laboratory to learn more about mosquito together with Grandpa Mosquito. He is the only one who can dissect the tiny mosquito and analyze the ovary and salivary gland. Awesome!!!
