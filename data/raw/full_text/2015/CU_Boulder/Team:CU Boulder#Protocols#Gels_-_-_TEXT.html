It should barely cover the gel Running the gel Mix loading dye and water with DNA as needed Vortex and spin briefly Load wells, careful to not let samples out of the well Don't forget the ladder! Plug in and run Smaller gels don't need as high of dosage At a lower voltage, the samples move slower but the separation is more clear Team:CU-Boulder - 2015.igem.org University of Colorado Boulder Home / Sponsors / iGEM 2015 / Contact
