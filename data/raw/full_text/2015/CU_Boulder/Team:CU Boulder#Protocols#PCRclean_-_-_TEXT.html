Mix by pipetting Transfer mixture to a spin column Spin for 1 minute Discard the flow-through Add 750ul of Buffer PE to the column Spin for 1 minute Discard the flow-through Dry spin for 1 minute Remove column from collection tube ant put into a new, labeled 1.7mL tube Add 30ul of elution buffer Let column stand for 1 to 4 minutes Spin for 1 minute Keep the tube; discard the column Protocol modified from Qiagen PCR Purification Kit Team:CU-Boulder - 2015.igem.org University of Colorado Boulder Home / Sponsors / iGEM 2015 / Contact
