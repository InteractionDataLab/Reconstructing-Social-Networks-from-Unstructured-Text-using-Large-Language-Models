Requirements for Gold

  • iGEM projects involve important questions beyond the bench, for example relating to (but not limited to) ethics, sustainability, social justice, safety, security, and intellectual property rights. Expand on your silver medal activity by demonstrating demonstrate an innovative human practices activity that relates to your project.
  • Convince the judges you have helped any registered iGEM team from another university.
  • Improve the function OR characterization of an previously existing BioBrick Part and enter this information in the Registry. Our improved parts are BBa_K1718006.
  • Demonstrate a functional prototype of your project. Your prototype can derive from a previous project (that was not demonstrated to work) by your team or by another team. Show this system working under real-world conditions (biological materials may not be taken outside the lab)
