Protocols:

Interlab Parts

  1. Resuspend DNA from Distribution Kit using iGEM instructions..

    BBa_J23101: 2015 Kit Plate 1, Well 20K. Size: 35 bp.

    BBa_J23106: 2015 Kit Plate 1, Well 22A. Size: 35 bp.

    BBa_J23117: 2015 Kit Plate 1, Well 22K. Size: 35bp.

    BBa_I13504: 2015 Kit Plate 4, Well 21J. Size: 875 bp.

  2. Transform BioBrick parts using Transformation Protocol.
  3. Confirm transformation
  4. Fill culture tubes with 5 mL of LB and appropriate amount and type of antibiotic.
  5. Pluck colonies from plates using 200 μL pipette tip and place in tubes, leave tubes in 37.5 ° shaking incubator on 250 rpm for 12-18 hours.
  6. Retrieve overnight cultures from incubator and miniprep using Qiagen Miniprep Protocol
  7. Digest miniprepped DNA using Spe1 and Pst1 restriction enzymes
  8. Run a gel and image to validate construct.
  • Create construct.
  • Digest BBa_J232101 with SpeI and PstI at 37 °C for 60 minutes.
  • PCR I13504 with appropriate primers and digest using Xba1 and Pst1.
  • Gel extract digested DNA.
  • Ligate both pieces of DNA using gel image and ligation protocol
  • Repeat with BBa_J23106 and BBa_J23117
  • Transform ligation product.
  • Confirm transformation
  • Fill culture tubes with 5 mL of LB and appropriate amount and type of antibiotic.
