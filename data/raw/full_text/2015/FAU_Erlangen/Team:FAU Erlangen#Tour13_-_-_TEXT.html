Giving us our lab spaces and getting the Department of Biology behind our project was an enormous help to us. Regularly, he checks up on us to ask if everything is going all right. Wiki page layout support This Wiki Page layout was in large parts copied from the 2014 Team from SDU Denmark , which itself is also based on the 2013 Team from denmark under the CC license . There have been several styling adjustments to make it optically distinguishable from their wiki, but the core concept remains the same. Initially, we planned to make our own layout, but since no one in our team has ever done any webpage developing, we had to adapt and only make small changes to an already existing layout. Team logo design We are the first team of FAU Erlangen to participate in iGEM, so we didn't have a logo yet. Hence Andreas Feser from team Marburg offered us to design our logo and he did a great job!
