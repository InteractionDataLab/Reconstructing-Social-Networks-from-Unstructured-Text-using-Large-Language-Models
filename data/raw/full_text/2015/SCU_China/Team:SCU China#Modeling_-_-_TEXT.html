In aerobic respiration, one Acetyl-CoA was consumed away and 10 ATP are produced. The electron transport chain was supposed as the main part to produce ATP. But the fact is that the system we designed to fix carbon is derived from anaerobic bacteria and the enzymes with metal active center in it may be oxidated by oxygen. Therefore a model was built to get the solution to make the two system compatible. Fig. 1. CARBON FIXATION SYSTEM. In one cycle, one ATP is cost and one CoA is acetylated to Acetyl-CoA. It is reported that all the reactions is not very fast and the enzyme damaged by oxygen is can be repaired in other way. What the oxygen effects is reversible. Therefore it is believe that some concentration of oxygen can make both two systems work at the same time. What we did At first, all the reactions are assumed as elementary reaction.
