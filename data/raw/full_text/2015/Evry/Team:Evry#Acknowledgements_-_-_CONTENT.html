    A lot of institutions and people are supporting us during our project! We'd like to thank them here.

    Sponsors

    People

    Institute of Systems and Synthetic Biology people

    We would like to thank people from the Institute of Systems and Synthetic Biology for welcoming us in the institute and lending us rooms, machines and stuff to work all the summer long.

    We particularly wish to thank Bernadette Lauret and Sylvie Bobelet, who were more helpful than ever to deal with tackle at every administrative and ordering issues. And for bench work, we would like to thank André Lejeune, Brian Jester, Vincent Libis and Ioana Popescu for giving us interesting advices.

    In vivo&in vitro tests

    We would like to thank people from the Institut Curie, Olivier Lantz, Christine Sedlik, Katarzyna Franciskiewicz, for letting us try our immunotherapy on melanoma bearing mice.

    We are also very grateful to Anne Galy and Maxime Ferrand from the Genethon, for giving us consumables and material needed to perform in vitro tests

    Software

    We would like to thank Rémy Nicolle from the French league against cancer for his insight on our bio-informatics project. He gave us advice regarding target genes identification and tools we could use in our pipeline.

    Human Practices

    We also kindly thank Jean-Jacques Garaud, non-executive Director of the Board of Directors at MedDay, and Fabrice Beauchêne, CEO at My Pharma Company, for accepting the interview on participative funding and health research.
