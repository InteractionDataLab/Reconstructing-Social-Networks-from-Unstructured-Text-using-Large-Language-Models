Introduction From our modeling which objective was to precisely replicate the payoff matrix, we found that there was an unexpected leakage in the promoter, since the results of the modeling we had done beforehand, did not match with the results of the wet lab. After modeling 2 different solutions to overcome the leakage, we found the most efficient solution of tagging the CmR protein with ssrA, which successfully led to expected results in our wet lab and ultimately led to our precise replication of the payoff matrix. We could not obtain positive results in our modeling by increasing the concentration of Cm, which was the other solution, so we confirmed that tagging the CmR gene with ssrA is a better solution. 2.
