Currently, all marketed antibodies are made in vitro, by exploiting mammalian cell's immune system and its ability to produce antibodies against a certain antigen. Increasing demands implies the necessity of quicker methods to drive down the costs of production, both because of economical issues and the expense of animal lives. These are exactly the issues that we have been trying to accommodate. Sounds interesting, right?
