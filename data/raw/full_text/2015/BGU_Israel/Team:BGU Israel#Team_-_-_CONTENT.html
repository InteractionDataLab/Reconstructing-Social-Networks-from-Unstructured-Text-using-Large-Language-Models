    Our team includes 8 students, creating a dynamic and heterogenic group, studying various subjects:
    • Biotechnology Engineering
    • Medicine
    • Economics and politics
    • Biology
    • Brain and Cognition Science
    We are supervised by Prof. Smadar Cohen, Dr. Emil Ruvinov, and Dr. Efrat Forti from the department of Biotechnology Engineering. Our ambition is to contribute to establish Israel and BGU at the forefront of development and innovation in the field of synthetic biology.
