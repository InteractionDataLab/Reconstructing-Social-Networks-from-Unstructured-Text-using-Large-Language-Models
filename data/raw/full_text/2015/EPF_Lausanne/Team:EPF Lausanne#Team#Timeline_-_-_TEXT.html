"Programmable repression and activation of bacterial gene expression using an engineered CRISPR-Cas system". Bikard sent us his strains JEN202, JEN202+PWJ89 and DH5A+PWJ66. Blast 02.04.2015 In our project dCas9 target DNA sequences using gRNAs and acts as an activator or an inhibitor depending on its position. Axel and Cyril created a C++ program to blast random generated gRNA sequences on the E. Coli or Yeast genome. And the winner is... 26.03.2015 ...the new framework for biologic circuits using dCas9.
