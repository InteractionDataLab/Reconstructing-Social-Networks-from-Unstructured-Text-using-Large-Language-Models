Coli Yeast Protocols Team Meet us Timeline Attributions Bio LOGIC Logic Orthogonal gRNA Implemented Circuits Engineering transcriptional logic gates to program cellular behavior remains an important challenge for synthetic biology. Currently, genetic circuits reproducing digital logic are limited in scalability and robustness by output discrepancies and crosstalk between transcriptional pathways. We propose to address these issues using RNA-guided dCas9 fused to a transcription activation domain as a programmable transcription factor.
