Funding & Support

We would like to thank the following for funding and support: The National Science Foundation, The Synthetic Biology Group at Virginia Tech, GenoCAD, The Virginia Bioinformatics Institute, The Virginia Polytechnic Institute and State University

Attributions and Contributions

Non-undergraduate work: Dr. Martha Eborall advised us on DNA assembly and cell transformation, and made a few of our constructs. Dr. Jean Peccoud and Dr. David Ball designed our mathematical model and provided modeling advice, and Laura Adam provided advice on creating attribute grammars and integrating them into GenoCAD (but did not do any of the work).
