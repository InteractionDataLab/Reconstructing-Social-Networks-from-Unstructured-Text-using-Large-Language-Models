    L Broth

    For 1L:
    • 10g Tryptone
    • 5g Yeast extract
    • 5g NaCl

    PYE Broth

    For 1L:
    • 2g Bacto peptone
    • 1g Yeast extract
    • 1mL 1M MgSO4⋅7H2O
    • 1mL 0.5M CaCl2

    M2G Minimal Broth

    1. For 1L of 20x M2 Salts:
    2. 17.4g Na2HPO4
    3. 10.6g KH2PO4
    4. 10g NH4Cl
    5. Autoclave this sol'n.
    6. To make M2G:
    7. Dilute 20x M2 salts sol'n to 1x and autoclave.
    8. Add the following sterile sol'ns:
    9. 1mL 0.5M MgSo4
    10. 10mL 20% Glucose
    11. 1mL 10mM FeSO4, 10mM EDTA (Sigma #F-0518, kept at 4°C)
    12. 500μL 1M CaCl2 (Note: add this last to avoid precipitation)

    Agar Plates

    Add 15g Bacto-agar to 1L of liquid media before autoclaving.

    Freezer Stocks

    To an appropriate tube add:
    • 700μL overnight liquid culture
    • 300μL 50% glycerol sol'n
    Then place in storage at -80°C.
