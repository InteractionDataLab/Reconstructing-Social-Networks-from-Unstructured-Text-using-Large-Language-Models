    L Broth

    For 1L:
    • 10g Tryptone
    • 5g Yeast extract
    • 5g NaCl

    PYE Broth

    For 1L:
    • 2g Bacto peptone
    • 1g Yeast extract
    • 1mL 1M MgSO4⋅7H2O
    • 1mL 0.5M CaCl2

    Agar Plates

    Add 15g Bacto-agar to 1L of liquid media before autoclaving.

    Freezer Stocks

    To an appropriate tube add:
    • 700μL overnight liquid culture
    • 300μL 50% glycerol sol'n
    Then place in storage at -80°C.
