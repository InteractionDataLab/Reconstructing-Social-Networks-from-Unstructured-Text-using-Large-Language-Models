Ethanol is a renewable energy source that can supplement or replace gasoline as a proven source for transportation fuel. They have graciously donated $1,000 to our iGem team. Autodesk, Inc., a world leader in 2D and 3D design, engineering, and entertainment software, has joined as an iGEM sponsoring partner. Autodesk will provide iGEM teams with Autodesk Maya®, and virtually all other Autodesk software, to help animate and simulate in 3D their projects.
