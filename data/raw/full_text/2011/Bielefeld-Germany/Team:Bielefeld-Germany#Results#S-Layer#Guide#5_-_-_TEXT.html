Centrifugation does not remove all of the cell debris, therefore a tangential flow ultrafiltration step with a 300 kDa membrane is performed. The retentate is collected. Instead of this device you can also use a simple dead-end sterile filter (0.22 µm poresize) but it is easier the way described above (the dead-end filter gets clogged after some time...).
