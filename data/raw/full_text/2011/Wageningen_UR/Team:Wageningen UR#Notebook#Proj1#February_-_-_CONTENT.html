    February 2

    Problem with repressilator: signal loses clarity after subsequent rounds. Two approaches to solve this: 1. Use three-gene repressilator together with protease which degrades transcription factors which prevent steady state. 2. Use five-step repressilator to give transcription factors more time to degrade to keep the non- steady state active.
