April 12 The maximum estimated cost of the synthesis of our constructs is about 5.500 euro's. Strain selection has not been done jet: there were some problems with the strains. The Fungus Work Plan is being worked on. April 21 The A. nidulans strain (A1147) we ordered from the FGSC arrived today! April 29 A. niger strains with GFP, RFP and YFP were received on plates. These strains were donated by A. Vinck. We will use these strains to familiarize ourselves with the microscope. The plates were left during the weekend at room temperature.
