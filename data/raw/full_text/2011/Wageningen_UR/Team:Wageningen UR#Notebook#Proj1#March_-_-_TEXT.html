The model is divided into three modules: the quorum sensing/synchronising module, the luciferase regenerating module and the dual quorum sensing/synchronising module. We need to make a gene list for each module, preferably completely biobricked. The design has to be evaluated and modeled. Therefore, more information is needed on the transcription, translation, diffusion and degradation rates.
