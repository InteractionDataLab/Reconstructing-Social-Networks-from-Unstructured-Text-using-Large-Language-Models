Transformation is the introduction of a foreign plasmid into competent cells. We follow the protocol from last year's team: 2010 protocol Additional notes The protocol specifies to ask Henrike for plasmid quantities. Here is her answer: For relatively pure plasmids, 1 ul is enough. For low concentration plasmids, like those originating from a Gibson assembly, use 2 to 3 ul. If you are conducting the heat shock in an eppendorf tube, use 45 seconds instead of 30.
