Coordinator

Veronica Zepeda

This is Veronica's first year working with the UCSF iGEM team! She recently received her PhD in molecular biology/biochemistry and is having a lot of fun learning about the field of Synthetic Biology.

Advisors

Wendell Lim Wendell has had a lab at UCSF for over 15 years and has been an adviser to the UCSF team since it started in 2007. He enjoys working with bright, open-minded young scientists and seeing their ideas develop into scientific projects.
Connie LeeConnie is the Associate Director for the Center for Systems and Synthetic Biology. She is a cell biologist by training and has spent the last 11 years working as a scientific editor, most recently as Deputy Editor at Cell. She enjoys being back in an academic environment and interacting with the iGEM team throughout the summer.
