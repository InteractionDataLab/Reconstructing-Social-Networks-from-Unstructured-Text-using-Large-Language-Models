Sequencing Stanford iGEM's Construct The Stanford / NASA iGEM Team needed to sequence a very large ~10kb cassette for their project. We arranged to have our adviser Russell prepare sequencing libraries for the PacBio sequencer to help them out. Columbia Cooper - Maker Faire Partners We teamed up with the Columbia-Cooper iGEM Team to present Synthetic Biology workshops at the 2011 NYC World Maker Faire. Our group even ended up winning the Make Magazine Editors' Choice Award!
