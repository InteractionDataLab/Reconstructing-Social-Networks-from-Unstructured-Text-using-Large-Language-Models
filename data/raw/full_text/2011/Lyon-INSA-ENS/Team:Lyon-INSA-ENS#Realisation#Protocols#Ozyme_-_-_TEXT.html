Procedure 1. In an eppendorf tube, add the following solutions in any order : -11µL water -10µL DNA -2.5µL NE Buffer 2 -0.5µL BSA X50 2. Add 0.5µL of each one of the desired enzymes. 3. Incubate at 37°C for 10mn 4.
