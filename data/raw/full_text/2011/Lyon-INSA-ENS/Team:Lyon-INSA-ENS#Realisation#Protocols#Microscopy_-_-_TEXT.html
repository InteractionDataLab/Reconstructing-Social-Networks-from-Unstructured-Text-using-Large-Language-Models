Procedure 1. In empty sterile plates, introduce 10mL of M63G medium and 100µL of appropriate antibiotic. 2. Add 100µL of bacteria from a saturated liquid culture. 3. Finally, add 3 or 4 sterile glass slides with a sterile tweezer. 4. Incubate at 30°C overnight. 5. Observe the slides with a fluorescent microscope.
