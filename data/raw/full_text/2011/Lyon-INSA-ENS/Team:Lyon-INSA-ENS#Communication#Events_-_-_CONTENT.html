Visits

Visit of a Nuclear Power Plant

On the 25th of May, the team went to the French Nuclear Power Plant Tricastin. Since we could ask questions and visited the installations of the site, it was for us a great opportunity to understand the context in which our project is inscribed.

Activities around the project

Dragon boat

On the 27th of July, all the team gathers on the Miribel lake (near Lyon) to discover a fun and original activity : the dragon boat. After a short initiation, we face a team of biologists in a race.

Shooting the team presentation video

Organised by our unyielding directors Margaux and Clémence, the whole team gathered for a great moment of fun. After a quick rehearsal, we shot the video. Everyone gave their best to produce a wonderful video ; one of our courageous actors admitted :« I'm sore from climbing the stairs ».

iGEM Team is in the race

The "CROSS de l'INSA de LYON" gathers more than 600 runners in two races (4 and 6 km). What makes this event so original is that most people, registered in teams of 4, run in fancy dress. The craziest costumes are even rewarded. This year, around ten participants have represented the iGEM project among which were the remarkable "CobaltBuster" and "Pili-Pili" teams.
