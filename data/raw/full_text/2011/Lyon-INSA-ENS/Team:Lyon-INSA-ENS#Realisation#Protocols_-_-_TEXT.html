All volumes mentionned are meant for a 100X mother solution. The composition of the different media we have used is the following : LB : 10 g of tryptone, 5 g of yeast extract, and 10 g of NaCl for 1L of liquid LB. Solid LB for plates is made by adding agar to reach a concentration of 1.5%.
