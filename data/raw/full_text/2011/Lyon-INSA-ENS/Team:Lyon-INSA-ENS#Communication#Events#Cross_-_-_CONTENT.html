IGEM Team is in the race

The "CROSS de l'INSA de LYON" gathers more than 600 runners in two races (4 and 6 km). What makes this event so original is that most people, registered in teams of 4, run in fancy dress. The craziest costumes are even rewarded. This year, around ten participants have represented the iGEM project among which were the remarkable "CobaltBuster" and "Pili-Pili" teams.
