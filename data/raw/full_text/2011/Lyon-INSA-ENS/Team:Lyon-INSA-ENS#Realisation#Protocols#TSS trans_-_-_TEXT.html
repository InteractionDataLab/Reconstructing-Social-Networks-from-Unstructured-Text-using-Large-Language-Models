Procedure 1. Monitor the OD600 of a 5mL culture of NM522 cells ( or the strain you want to transform ) in LB medium at 37°C. Proceed to the next step when it reaches 0.3-0.4 ( not higher than 0.6 ), which takes approximately 3 hours. 2. Take 1 mL from the previous culture and centrifuge at 6000rpm for 2mn . 3. Resuspend into 100µL of TSS. Starting from this step, keep the bacteria on ice. 4. Incubate on ice for 5-10mn. 5. Add 5µL of the DNA to be transformed. 6. Incubate on ice for 10mn. 7. Heat shock the bacteria by heating them at 42°C for 50s. 8. Incubate on ice for 2mn. 9. Add 900µL LB and mix by inverting the tube. 10. Incubate for 1h at 37°C. 11. Plate 200µL on LB medium with the appropriate antibiotic.
