Procedure 1. In an eppendorf tube, add the following solutions in any order : -250 ng DNA ( if the concentration is unknown, use 5µL ) -2.5µL (1X) or 5µL (2X) 10X tango buffer ( this depends on the enzymes used ) -water to get a volume of 25µL after addition of the enzymes 2. Add 1µL of each one of the desired enzymes. 3. Incubate at 37°C for 1h30 4. Incubate at 70°C for 10 mn to inactivate the restriction enzymes.
