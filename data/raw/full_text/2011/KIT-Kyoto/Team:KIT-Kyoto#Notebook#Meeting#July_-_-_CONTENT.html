1st July

at laboratory on 3rd floor
<Participants>
Yoshimura, Matsumoto, Matsunami, Nakagawa, Yokoigawa, Nakamura
・Questionnaire in the survey
We talked on the temporally decided questionnaire in the survey again. In the next meeting we will decide the final version of questionnaire in the survey and adopt it.
・About safety
We talked on the answer of safety proposal presented by iGEM in Japanese. We will translate it into English.
・About wiki
Members who are undergraduate students of Department of Architecture and Design presented the design of our team logo. We made a few suggestions to improve it. They will present the revised version of the design and we continue to consider it.

5th July

at 236 meeting room
<Participants>
Yoshimura, Matsumoto, Matsunami, Nakagawa, Yokoigawa, Takeda, Nakamura, Maeda, Kokado
・Questionnaire in the survey
We checked the final version of questionnaire in the survey and decided to adopt it. We will conduct it.
・Project Description
We talked on the project description in Japanese. We will present the revised version of the project description in both Japanese and English on next meeting.

8th July

at laboratory on 3rd floor
<Participants>
Yoshimura, Matsumoto, Matsunami, Nakagawa, Yokoigawa, Takeda, Nakamura
・Project Description
We talked on and checked the revised version of the project description. We decided to adopt it. We will upload it in the team wiki page by July 15th(Fri).
・Open Campus
We talked on the draft of poster. We will present the revised draft of poster in the next meeting.

12th July

at 236 meeting room
<Participants>
Matsumoto, Matsunami, Nakagawa, Yokoigawa, Yamamoto
