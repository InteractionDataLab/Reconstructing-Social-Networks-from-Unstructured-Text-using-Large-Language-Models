PCR条件

10 µM GFP Primer R1.5 µl
BBa_E02401 µl
10 x PCR Buffer for KOD Plus5 µl
dNTPs4 µl
MgSO44 µl
ddH2O32 µl
KOD Plus1 µl
 total 50 µl
Cycle条件
Pre-Denature95°C30sec 
Denature95°C30sec30 Cycle

End4°Ckeep
