Load the sample in the PCR machine and select an appropriate program based on the primer design. Our primers were designed to have an annealing temperature of ~ 65oC. "iGem Flash" = 35-40 cycles: 98oC - 10s 98oC - 5s 63oC - 5s 72oC - 1min 72oC - 3min 4oC - infinite Once the PCR is finished, run 4 μL of each sample on a diagnostic gel to visualize approximate band lengths. Incubate the remaining 36 μL of each sample with 1 μL DPN 1 (enzyme) @ 37oC for 1 hour. Purify each sample using a similar approach to the Gibson DNA purification protocol.
