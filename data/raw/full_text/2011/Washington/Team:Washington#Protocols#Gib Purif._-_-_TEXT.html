Pour/Pipet the mixture into a spin column (pink). Centrifuge the sample for ~ 1 minute. Discard the flow-through. Wash the sample by adding 750 μL buffer PE and centrifuge for ~ 1 minute. Centrifuge the sample for ~ 1 minute. Discard the flow-through. To Elute the DNA, place the spin column in a clean 1.5 μL (labeled) microcentrifuge. Add 30 μL of buffer EB and let Stand for 1 minute!
