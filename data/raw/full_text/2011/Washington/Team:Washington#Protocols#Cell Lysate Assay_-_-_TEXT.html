Triton Lysis Triton Lysis Buffer (10mL, enough for 2 plates) 9mL of 1x PBS 10mg of Lsyozyme (5-20 is fine) 5mg of DNase (2-10 is fine) 1mL of 10% Triton X100 Add 50uL of Triton lysis buffer to each well Shake on High Speed Plate Shaker (Set to 1500rpm, in J562) for 20-60 minutes Add 250uL of 100mM NaOAc pH 4.0 to each well Spin 40 minutes 4000rpm Sonication Lysis Add 300uL of 100mM NHAc pH 4.0 to each well No lysozyme or DNase needed as sonication will break up the cell wall AND the genomic DNA Use Plate Sonicator (Ask Chris) Spin 40 minutes 4000rpm 2.Assay Add 90uL of 5microM substrate (in NaAc pH 4.0 buffer) to each well of a black fluorescent microtiter assay plate Start reaction by adding 10uL Supernatent (try to avoid bubbles and pippette quickly, but accurately) Use the P20 multichannel with a taped cardboard stopper to make sure you don't hit the pellet!
