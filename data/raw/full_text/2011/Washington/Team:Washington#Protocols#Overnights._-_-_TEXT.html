Add ~ 3-5 mLs of an appropriate broth (either LB or TB) Add an appropriate antibiotic if necessary and set this tube safely aside Amp (1:100) Chlor (1:1000) Kan (1:1000) Prick the desired colony off a plate and suspend it in 50 uL PBS. Refer to the PBS stock making protocol! Add 10 uL of the PBS sample and add it to the broth previously set aside. Incubate overnight cultures @ 37oC, overnight with shaking.
