pGA vector Assay

  • Prepare these mixtures on ice
  1. Obtain a 40 uL aliquot of BL21 cells.
  2. Add 120 uL of ice water to the aliquot.
  3. The total volume is now 160 uL; split evenly among four labeled tubes: INS + BCK, INS + BCK, INSctrl, BCKctrl.
  4. INS + BCK tubes (x 2)
  5. add 1 uL of 10X pGA Gibson product (1A3 BCK/INS pLacGFP)
  6. INSctrl tube
  7. add 100 pg of pLacGFP gel extract
  8. BCKctrl
  9. add 100 pg of 1A3 gel extract
  10. Repeat steps 1-3 for the comparison vector pSB using the following instead:
  11. 1 uL of pSB Gibson product (T19-1A3/pLacGFP)
  12. 1 ng INS (pLacGFP- gel extract)
  13. 1 ng BCK (T19-1A3- gel extract)
  14. Once all the samples are ready, begin the transformation.
  15. Rescue each sample in 500 mLs of LB
  16. Incubate all samples @ 37oC for ~45 min.
  17. Plate 50 uL on each sample onto a LB + Chlor + Amp + IPTG plate.
  18. 1 plate for each control in each vector set.
  19. 3 plates for each Gibson product tube in each set. ( 6 plates for each vector set + 2 control plates)
