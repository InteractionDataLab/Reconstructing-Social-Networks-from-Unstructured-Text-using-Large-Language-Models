PR is a light-activated proton pump and generates a pmf which is short for proton motive force. This part, K603000 designed by Danyang Wang, was constructed as below: We replaced the PR's precusor sequence with the leader peptide of human cytochrome oxidase subunit 4 isoform 1, targeting it to mitochondrial inner membrane. Meanwhile, all the codons were re-designed according to codon usage bias for Homo sapiens.         Retrieved from " http://2011.igem.org/Team:UST-Beijing/Parts " Recent changes What links here Related changes Special pages My preferences Printable version Permanent link Privacy policy Disclaimers
