    We did a lot of work today like:

    1. The miniprep of the strains containing the BioBrick rh1AB.

    2. An electrophoresis in agarose gel at 1.5% in wich we observed the plasmidic DNA.

    3. Mean while some of us picked colonies with P+RBS and the GFP+Ter and transferred it to LB medium.

    4. Last attempt to inoculate the E. coli containing the rh1AB mutated from 2/3/11 in LB medium but it didn´t grow again! =(. This time we putted the exact concentration of cloranfenicol (33 ug/ml).

    5. We did all the necessary calculations for the antibiotics concentrations.
