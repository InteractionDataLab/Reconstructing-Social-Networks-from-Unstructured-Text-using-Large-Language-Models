Amplification

PCR to amplify certain parts out of the red light sensor of edinburgh: K322123, K322124, ompR:

PCR-Program:

1) Initial Denaturation: 96°C, 4'

2) Touchdown (8x)

   Denat.: 96°C, 30 s
   Annealing: 65°C -> 61,5°C; -0,5°C/cycle, 20 s
   Extension: 72°C, 4'

3) Constant Temp. (22x)

   Denat.: 96°C, 30 s
   Annealing: 61°C, 20 s
   Extension: 72°C, 4'

4) Final Extension: 72°C, 8'

5) Hold: 4°C, oo
