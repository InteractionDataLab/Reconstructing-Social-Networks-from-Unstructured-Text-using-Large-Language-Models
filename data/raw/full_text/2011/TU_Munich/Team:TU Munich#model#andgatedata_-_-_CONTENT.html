1<a id='magicparlabel-48190' ></a>Model

<a id='magicparlabel-48191' ></a>The model for our AND-Gate is based on the model of the iGEM team PKU Beijing 2009 for their AND-Gate1. We modified the equations such that the change in tRNA and Aa-tRNA does not include the degradation of the mRNA which caused negativity of some concentrations in our model.[<a href='#pkubeijing'>1</a>]
<a id='magicparlabel-48192' ></a>For more details see the paper by Anderson [<a href='#Anderson-2007'>2</a>].
<a id='magicparlabel-48193' ></a>The dye output was an adaption of the model proposed in the paper by Yildirim[<a href='#Yildirim-2004'>3</a>].
