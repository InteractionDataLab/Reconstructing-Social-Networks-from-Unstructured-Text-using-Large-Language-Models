Allows for selection of a any product which needs specific controlled expression either temporally or spatially. For proof of concept coloured carotinoids will be used, however in practice these products could be enzymes in a metabolic pathway that can be selectively induced to create different end products from the same precursor molecule. Strength is considered relative to BBa_B0031, BBa_B0032, BBa_B0033 and BBa_B0034 Beta-carotene BBa_K274200 Biobrick designed by the 2009 Cambridge team. Converts FPP to beta-carotene.
