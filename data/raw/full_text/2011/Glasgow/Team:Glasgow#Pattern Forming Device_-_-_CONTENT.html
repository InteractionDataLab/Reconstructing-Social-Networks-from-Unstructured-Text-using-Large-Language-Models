    Pattern Forming Device Construct

    The pattern forming device essentially causes targeted dispersal of biofilm. To target the dispersal a light dependant promoter is used allowing bio-photo-lithography.

    Blue Light Dependant PromoterBBa_K238013

    Weak blue light dependant promoter. Strength is considered reletive to I20260

    Strong Ribosome Binding SiteBBa_B0030

    Strong RBS based on Ron Weiss thesis. Strength is considered relative to BBa_B0031, BBa_B0032, BBa_B0033 and BBa_B0034

    Colicin E2BBa_K131009

    The Colicin E2 operon without the SOS promoter. Colicin has DNAse activity.
