Thaw a tube of NEB 5-alpha Competent E.coli cells on ice for 10 minutes 2. Mark the location of the Biobrick well (letters from top to bottom, numbers from left to right) 3. Resuspend specific biobrick part (1 μl) with distilled water (20 μl). Aspirate up and down a few times 4. Inoculate with DNA ( 1 μl) to the competent cells 5. Place the mixture on ice for 30 minutes. Do not mix 6. Heat shock at exactly 42°C for exactly 30 seconds. Do not mix 7. Place on ice for 5 minutes. Do not mix 8. Pipette 950 μl of room temperature SOC into the mixture 9. Place at 37°C for 60 minutes. Shake vigorously (250 rpm) or rotate 10. Warm selection plates to 37°C 11. Do serial dilutions (2-3) at 105. Mix cells, flick/invert gently 12. Spread (100 μl) on agar plates of each dilution 13.
