The University of East Anglia and the John Innes Centre are entering the iGEM competition for the first time! We are a team of nine students from a range of disciplines within Biology (as well as one chemist) supported by a long-suffering collaboration of advisors. We're based in the Norwich Research Park, and are using the facilities there to introduce a species of algae and a species of moss to iGEM for use as two new model organisms in the competition. For a full outline of our project and the reasons behind it, visit our "Aims" section. Follow us on Twitter and facebook too!
