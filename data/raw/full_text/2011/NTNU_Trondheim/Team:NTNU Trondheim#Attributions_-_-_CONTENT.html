    Attributions

    Wetlab work

    All biological work, including cloning, measurements, etc. was performed by the students on the team. Clone Manager Suite 6.0 was used for in silico work.

    Oligos used for PCR were synthesized by Sigma-Aldrich. Sequencing was performed by Eurofins DNA, at a generous discount.

    Modelling

    All the modeling has been done by the students on the team, software tools used were Dizzy and Matlab.

    Wiki-design

    All wiki work was performed by the students on the team.

    Poster and presentation

    The poster and presentation was designed by the students on the team using InkScape, with help and tips from our advisor Rahmi Lale.
