The resulting plots shows the distributions of the flouresence for the red end of the spectrum (i.e around the wavelength corresponding to mCherry). First of all there is a remarkable difference between the distributions for M9 and LB. The distribution for M9 is relatively sharp compared to LB which means there is little difference in the level of light a emitted from the individual cells. The distribution for LB is much broader showing a greater variance and it is also shifted to the right.
