For the first time, a Norwegian team is participating! Our team, consisting of students from the Norwegian University of Science and Technology (NTNU) are really excited to be able to compete, and we are very much looking forward to honing our skills in synthetic biology. Our project idea is based on using the alarmone ppGpp to detect stress in E. coli, and basically make it turn red with anger!
