This excess biotin prevented the biotinylated GFP from binding to the chip. Took pictures of previous GFP lysate experiment pictures. Pictures were taken before and after a wash with DI H2O (Deionized water). All pictures were taken in air. After 4 days there was still GFP bound to the chip. During this experiment the biotin was filtered out of the lysate before the lysate was flown through the chip. The binding was concentrated at the inlet port and the first channel of the chip. This suggests that a larger amount of lysate must be flown through to coat the entire chip or that the cells must be modified to express more of the GFP biobrick.
