This way we have obtained expression adaptors on pSB1C3. All adaptors are under BBa_J23102 promoter in order to measure their strength acording to Synthetic Biology standards. The adaptors have following predicted strengths according to RBSDesigner software: Part Name Description Expression level in thousands of absolute RBSDesigner units Expression level as %B0034+YFP BBa_K528015 Expression Adapters s00_45k 45 64,29% BBa_K528016 Expression Adapters s01_24k 24 34,29% BBa_K528017 expression adapters s02_20k 20 28,57% BBa_K528018 Expression Adapters s06_16k 16 22,86% BBa_K528019 Expression Adapters s05_12k 12 17,14% BBa_K528020 expression adapters s03_65k 65 92,86% BBa_K528021 BBa_K528018+YFP measurement device 16 22,86% reference BBa_J23102+B0034+YFP (100% reference construct) 70 100,00% Wet lab results So far we have managed to obtain only BBa_K528021 (BBa_K528018+YFP measurement device) as working part. We have characterized it using fluorimetry in order to see how accurate are RBSCalculator predictions for the BBa_K528018 adaptor.
