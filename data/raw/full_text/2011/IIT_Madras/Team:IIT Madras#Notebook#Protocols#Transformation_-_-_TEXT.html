Heat shock : 90 s for 43 C. Keep on ice for 2 minutes. Add 900 ul of LB broth (sterile) into each microfuge tubes. Incubate in shaker at 37 C for 1 hour. Plate on plates (with antibiotics if necessary) and incubate at 37 C overnight.
