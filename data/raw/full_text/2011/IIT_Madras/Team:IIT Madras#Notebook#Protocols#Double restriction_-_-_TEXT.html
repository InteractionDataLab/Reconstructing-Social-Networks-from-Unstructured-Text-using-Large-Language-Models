The buffers are 10 X. Thus the volume of the buffer added to the digestion must be such that it is 1 X. Usually 0.3-0.5 ul of Enzymes can be used for every 1 ug of DNA – 1 unit of Enzyme is added. In case of sequential digestions, the following techniques can be used: Use enzyme 1 – restrict – purify DNA – Use Enzyme 2. Use enzyme which requires a more dilute buffer first, Then add the concentrated buffer for restricting DNA using 2nd Enzyme. Analytic Restrictions – final volume 10 ul. Preparative Restrictions – 40 – 50 ul. BSA should be added if recommended. 0.1 ul of BSA is usually added for 10 ul reactions. Run the products on a gel and check double digestion.
