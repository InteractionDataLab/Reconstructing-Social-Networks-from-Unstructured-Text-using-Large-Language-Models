A record is a collection of related data treated as a single entity. For example, a hockey trading card could be called a record: it brings together the name, photograph, team, and statistics of one player. An introduction to our web site A collection of records that share the same fields is called a table because this kind of information can easily be presented in table format: each column represents a field and each row represents a record. In fact, the word column is synonymous with the word field, and the word row is synonymous with the word record.       Copyright (C) 2003, your company name. all right reserved Tutorials | Codes | Templates Retrieved from " http://2011.igem.org/Team:IIT_Madras/Sandbox " Recent changes What links here Related changes Special pages My preferences Printable version Permanent link Privacy policy Disclaimers
