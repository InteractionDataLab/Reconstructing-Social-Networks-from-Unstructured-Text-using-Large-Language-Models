Assemble all reaction components on ice and quickly transfer the reactions to a thermocycler preheated to the denaturation temperature (98 degrees C). All components should be mixed and centrifuged prior to use. It is important to add Phusion Master Mix last in order to prevent any primer degredation. For 50 uL reaction: 10 uM forward primer 2.5 uL 10 uM reverse primer 2.5 μL Template DNA 1 uL Nuclease-free water To final volume (including mix) of 50 μL 2X Phusion Master Mix 25 μL Transfer PCR tubes from ice to a PCR machine with the block preheated to 98 degrees C and begin thermocycling.
