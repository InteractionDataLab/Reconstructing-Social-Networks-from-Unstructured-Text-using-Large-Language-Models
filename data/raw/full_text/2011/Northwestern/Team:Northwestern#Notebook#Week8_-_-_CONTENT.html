      Notebook
        Week 8
      Day 34 - Monday, August 1st 2011
      • Miniprepped constitutive promoters, RBSs, and a 3 part ligation
      • Digested 2A (w/ PCR clean-up) and 3A for ligations of RBSs with parts
      • Ran gel of digested 3 part ligation
      • Transformed cells with ligated digestions
      • Found strep tag sequence? Fingers crossed. Worked on primers for adding tag to receptors.
      • Prepared samples for sequencing (lasP+RBS30+RFP, rhlP+RBS34+GFP)
      Day 35 - Tuesday, August 2nd 2011
      • Made glycerol stock of constitutive promoter + RBS34 + rhlR
      • Digested, ligated, and transformed constitutive promoters and RBSs/parts
      • Finished primers for adding tag to receptors.
      • Prepared samples for sequencing.
      • PCRed a bunch of parts and 2L ligations; did ONs of backbones...we’re running out of DNA! =O
      • Received promoter oligos in mail!
      • Used up chlor plates for transformations so we made more plates (chlor and tet)
      • Plated ligations from glycerol stocks
      Day 36 - Wednesday, August 3rd 2011
      • Miniprepped yesterday’s ONs
      • Made glycerol stock of CP2
      • Annealed CP oligos received in mail
      • Digested RBSs+parts and ligated with CP oligo
      • Most of yesterday’s transforms did not work - ONed transforms that did work
      Day 37 - Thursday, August 4th 2011
      • Miniprepped, digested, and ran gels of RBS34+parts and RBS30+parts ligations
      • Miniprepped chlor backbone
      • Transformed oligo ligations
      • Plated RBS30+rhlR from glycerol stock
      • Plated TOP10 cells seedstock in preparation for making more competent cells
      Day 38 - Friday, August 5th 2011
      • Made new sterile MgCl2
      • Made glycerol stocks of what worked on yesterday’s gel
      • Worked on our super awesome iGEM t-shirt design
