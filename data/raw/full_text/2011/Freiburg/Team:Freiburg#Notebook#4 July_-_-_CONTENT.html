    Sequencing

    Investigators: Theo

    PCR of Lys1, Lys2, Lys3, Lys4, Lys5.

    Materials:

    • H2O
    • Seq. Buffer
    • DNA (Lys 1-Lys5)
    • Primer
    • Polymerase

    PCR programm:

    1. 95°C 3 min
    2. 95°C 15 sec
    3. 50°C 15 sec
    4. 60°C 2 min
    5. 4°C stored til needed again

    step 2 to 4 was repeated 40x times.
