    Testdigestion of 2a-assembly

    Investigators: Julia Continuing from 31 of Aug. [1] Before Miniprep of 20 different colonies. Each minipreped DNA-sample (3μl)was digested with EcoRI and PstI. Result:

    the samples

    46b= ho1 with a RBS (BBa_B0034)

    50b= RBS(BBa_B0034)+pcyA+terminator

    64b= Promotor (BBa_J23110)+CcaS

    67b= Promotor (BBa_J23110)+CcaR

    were send on 6.09 to GATC for sequencing

    3A-assembly:Digestion

    Investigators: Sandra

    Digestion of:

    InsertI:

    • 64, CcaR
    • 46, ho1

    with EcorI and SpeI

    InsertII:

    • 67, CcaS

    For this assembly we used the constructs from the 2A-assembly made on 31.08
