Team:Bilkent UNAM Turkey/suffix

From 2011.igem.org

Suffix is a component of the format of Biobrick standards used in the Igem competition. it is found at the end of a sequence and contains some consensus sequences that facilitates the IGEM community to easily modify the nucleic acid sequences. Several standards that are established by labs or IGEM members that are composed of prefixes and suffixes. They bring various advantages in preparing devices and quality of fusion proteins. The protocol and necessities for each Biobrick standards are listed in Biobrick Standard Registry web page.
