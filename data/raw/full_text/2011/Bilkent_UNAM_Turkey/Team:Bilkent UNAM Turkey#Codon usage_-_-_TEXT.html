So, one may not know the actual DNA or RNA sequence from the protein sequence (codon degeneracy). Chlamydomonas reinhardtii has a bias for synonymous codons and does not generally prefer to use the others. If we design our genetic sequence by using those unfavorable genetic codes, we may end up having less transcribed protein or no transcription. Thus, each gene that will be expressed in a different organism than from the host should be checked for codon usage bias. Codon bias tables are created to serve for this purpose.
