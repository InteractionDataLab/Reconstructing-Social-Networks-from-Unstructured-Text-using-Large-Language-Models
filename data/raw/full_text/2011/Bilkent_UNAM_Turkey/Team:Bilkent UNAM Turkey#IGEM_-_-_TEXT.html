Teams around the world are given a date to describe, experiment and explain their project with the kit that is sent. The competition grows rapidly and becomes well known to the press. Over 150 teams around worldwide participate and challenge each other. Gold, Silver, Bronze medals and special prizes are given according to the predetermined achievements of each teams. Example project include that a bacteria that can detect and clean heavy metals, colorful E.coli bacteria, synthetic structures in the body etc. .
