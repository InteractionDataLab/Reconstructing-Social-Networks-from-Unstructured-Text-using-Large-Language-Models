And first successful D. rad PCR Full plate of D. rad amplifications. Gel shows 6 definite successes and 6 'maybe's. PCR pBAD promoter(K206000) for insertion in front of RBS and for assembly in composite promoter-RBS biobrick. Also PCR'ed preexisting composite promoter-RBS biobricks with some succes (K081005 fail, J04500 success). PCR RBS with RFC primers and with Verification primers for composite part. Inoculate large cultures of pSB1C3+RFP as backbone for our screening plasmid. Miniprep large cultures of pSB1C3+RFP. Digest pSB1C3 backbone with E+P. Digest RBS plasmid with E+X to insert pBAD in front of RBS. Digest RBS from RFC and Ver PCRs with X+P as downstream part for assmbly into pSB1C3 with pBAD. Digest pBAD (K20600) with E+S for use as upstream part in RBS plasmid pSB1A2 and in composite part in pSB1C3 backbone.
