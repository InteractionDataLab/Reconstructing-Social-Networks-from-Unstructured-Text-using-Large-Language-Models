    Collaborative Efforts 

    Advisers, iGEM Nevada Club Members, and Financial Supporters
    Major Advisers: Dr. Christie Howard and Dr. David Shintani

    -helped us by organizing weekly meetings -provided feedback on proposed projects -were available over the summer for help in the lab, developing assays, outlining cloning strategies, and other project related help -set up individual meetings with us for help on our project -wrote grants to Nevada INBRE (NIH) to help fund the iGEM registration and for travel -both professors helped in proofreading of posters and wiki pages -Dr. Howard helped out with the News Updates by interviewing us and proofreading our final draftsAdditional Advising: Dr. Patricia Ellison -taught the iGEM assay group how to develop assays for analysis of alcohol -attended meetings in the spring semester and gave feedback on project proposals
