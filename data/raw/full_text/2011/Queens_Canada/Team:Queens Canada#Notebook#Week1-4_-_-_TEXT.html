Administrative tasks like acquiring sponsorship and designing the wiki also consumed a lot of our time. We had our main project (directed chemotaxis through transgenic GCPR’s) defined near the end of week two and we spent the remainder of this period researching and acquiring the genes and genetic templates required for the project.
