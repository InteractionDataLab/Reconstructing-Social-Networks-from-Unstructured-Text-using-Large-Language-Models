Prepare a mastermix that contains T4 ligase buffer and T4 DNA ligase 2. Transfer 5µl of the mastermix to each properly labeled sample tube. 3. Add corresponding insert DNA and vector DNA into the tubes. Top the volume to 20µL using ddH2O. 4. Incubate one hour at 22⁰C (or room temperature) 5. Heat inactivate T4 DNA ligase at 65⁰C for 10 min. 6. Use up to 5 µL of the mixture for transformation of chemically competent cells.
