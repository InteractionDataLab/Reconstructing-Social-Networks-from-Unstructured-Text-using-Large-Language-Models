Remove the digestion buffer and enzymes from the -20⁰C freezer, place on ice. 2. Add 14 uL of water to an eppendorf tube. 3. Thaw the digestion buffer and the enzymes. 4. Add digestion buffer to tube. 5. Add DNA to tube. 6. Add enzymes to tube. 7.
