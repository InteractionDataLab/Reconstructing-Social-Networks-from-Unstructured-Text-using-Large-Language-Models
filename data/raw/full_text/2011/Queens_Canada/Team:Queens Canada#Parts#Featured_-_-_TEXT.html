Length: 3666 back to top OX1R Chemotaxis Construct BBa_K631031 - ODR7:OX1R:UNC-54 This is one of our favourite chemotaxis constructs! It is a composite part designed to place the non-native GPCR, OX1R, into the neurons of the worm using the ODR-7 promoter, acquired from Mus musculus, for expression in the AWA olfactory neurons. This should give our worm the ability to detect cholorobenzoic acid!
