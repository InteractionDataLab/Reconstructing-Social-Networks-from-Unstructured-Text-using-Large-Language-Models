About our Team

Team VIT_Vellore is the first team from VIT University to participate in iGEM. We are in fact the first group from our University to conduct research in Synthetic Biology

Our team consists of 6 highly-motivated and dedicated undergraduate students all pursuing their B.Tech. in Biotechnology from the Vellore Institute of Technology. The team members are as below: 1. Devang Mehta 2. Harikrishnan Nair 3. Khalilullah Abdulkader Ibrahim Dalvi 4. Sukalp Muzumdar 5. Thomas George 6. Y.V.S. Maaruthy Kumaar We were also assisted at every step by our two guides: 1. Dr. A. Sivakumar and 2. Dr. G. Kalaichelvan
