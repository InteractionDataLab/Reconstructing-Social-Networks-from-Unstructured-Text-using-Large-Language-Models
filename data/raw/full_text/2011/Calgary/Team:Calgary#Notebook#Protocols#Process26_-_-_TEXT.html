To make our buffer, we used calcium chloride dissolved in nanopure water the most effective concentration was found to be at 0.1M. Our solution was made according to the following: 22.2g of CaCl2 The buffer should be made fresh for each sample that is to be run, with the total volume equalling 20mL in a 50mL beaker.
