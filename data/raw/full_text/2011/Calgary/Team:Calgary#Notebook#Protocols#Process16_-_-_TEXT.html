Allow gel to cool until flask can be handled comfortably. Place comb in the gel rig. Pour agarose into gel tray. Allow to solidify. While the gel is solidifying prepare the samples. Add your sample and 1 uL 10x Loading Dye, 4 uL of DNA and 5 uL of water Pour 1X TAE over gel so that gel is covered by a 3-5mm buffer Load samples into lane (Don't forget to load a 1kb+ ladder into one of the lanes) Hook electrodes to gel apparatus.
