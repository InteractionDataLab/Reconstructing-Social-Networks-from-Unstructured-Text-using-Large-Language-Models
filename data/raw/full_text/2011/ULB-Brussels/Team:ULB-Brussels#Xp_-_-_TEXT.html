In addition to the pINDEL construction, we rebuild a functional biobrick containing the FRT’-CmR-FRT’ cassette (BBa_K551000).  The biobrick is functional since it confers Cm resistance.  Moreover, we were able to show that the FRT’ sites of the intermediate construct (TOPO®-FRT’-Cm-FRT’) are functional since the Cm resistance phenotype is lost upon FLP induction, although at low frequency. iGEM ULB Brussels Team - Contact us Retrieved from " http://2011.igem.org/Team:ULB-Brussels/Xp " Recent changes What links here Related changes Special pages My preferences Printable version Permanent link Privacy policy Disclaimers
