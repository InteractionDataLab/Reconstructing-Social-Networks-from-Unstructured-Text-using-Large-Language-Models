Experiments: they were done by the wetlab team (Sébastien Gérard, François Delvin, Fanny Franchini, Thibaut Hallaert, Fanny Philippart, Esther Brihaye, Arnaud Selvais) with some help from the modelling team (Maxime Lucas, Pierre Corvilain, Mitia Duerinckx, François Thilmany, Jonathan Frish). The host lab contributed with the work area and the advisors and instructor gave technical advice. Data analyses: this was done by the wetlab team. Modelling: The modelling was done by the modelling team with inputs from the wetlab team for the biological relevance of the model and some data. Human Practice: Virginie Jacobs, Morgane Préat, Mathieu Peiffer were responsible of the human practice part of the project; they interacted with the wetlab team to define the societal problems that could be relevant to the project. Wiki: The wiki was the work of Sébastien Gérard, Maxime Lucas and Pierre Corvilain.
