How about light responsive bacteria? Have you ever wondered how living things process information? Well, perhaps this community will surprise you. Not only it can see light, but it can interpret it. There is no trick, no magic, only pure synthetic biology. If you think this is an interesting concept, why not reading a bit more? Meet HuBac, our S.C.I.E.N.C.E. project for this iGEM 2011 here . About us iGEM-UANL is the representative team from Universidad Autonoma de Nuevo León, at Monterrey, México. This team is composed of ten students who spent their summer in the lab, having fun with transformations, constructions and plasmidic DNA extractions. This wiki is all about the work we made.
