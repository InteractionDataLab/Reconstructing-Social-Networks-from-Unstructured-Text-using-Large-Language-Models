Summary of Lab Work We ligated araC and pBAD into the iGEM plasmid backbone. However, after transforming them parts and then miniprepping them we found that the ligation was not successful. We re-suspended a number of parts from the iGEM distribution kit, including LacI, GFP and pSB3K3 plasmid backbone. These parts were transformed, as were some other genes we had synthesized. We also transformed more of the pSB1C3 plasmid backbone. Minipreps were done of the transformations of GFP, lacI, Plac/ara, GlnAp2. Nanodrop of the final concentrations: GFP = 46.6ng/ul LacI = 40.8ng/ul Plac/ara = 81.6ng/ul GlnAp2 = 54.4ng/ul 3ml culture of the pSB1C3 transformations was prepared, followed by miniprep.
