Home made quick saliva DNA extraction protocol: Rinse the mouth 5 times with clean water, the last rinse must be deposited in a plastic glass Prepare a solution with soap and water in a beaker Add the soap solution to the last rinse Add frozen (0°C) etanol to the last rinse Observe how a DNA thread forms, take it and place it in an eppendorf tube.
