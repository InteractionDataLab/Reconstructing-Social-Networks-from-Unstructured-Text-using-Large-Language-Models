Single digestion

- 1 uL BamHI

- 10 uL plasmid DNA

- 3 uL buffer 4

Plasmids

- INP-pMCS5BB

- INP-GFP-pMCS5BB

- INP-Sil-pMCS5BB

Incubate at 37 degrees overnight

Results

- INP pMCS5BB: band at ~5.5 kb

- INP-GFP: band at ~6.2 kb

- INP-Sil: ~6.5 kb
