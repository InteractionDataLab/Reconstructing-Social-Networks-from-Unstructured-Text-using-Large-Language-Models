    Extensive primary literature review was performed by Jeff Gralnick, Claudia Schmidt-Dannert, Sung Bong Lee, Sukdong Lee, Nathan Davis, Lauren Cole, Jessica Dent, Honglin Li, Danielle Young, Brian Woolums, Reginald Sudarta, Nathanael Sahli, Kenneth Mayer, Viki Ding, Poonam Srivastava, David Babson, Sarah Bloch, Maureen Quin, Tanhia Gonzalez, Ian Windsor, Swati Choudhary, Nagendra Palani, and Grayson Wawrzyn.

    Cloning Boot-Camp (Cloning 101)

    Project designed and conducted by Jeff Gralnick, Claudia Schmidt-Dannert, Poonam Srivastava, David Babson, Sarah Bloch, Maureen Quin, Tanhia Gonzalez, Ian Windsor, Swati Choudhary, Nagendra Palani, and Grayson Wawrzyn.

    Project designed by Jeff Gralnick, Claudia Schmidt-Dannert, Maureen Quin, and Poonam Srivastava.

    Cloning done by Lauren Cole, Nathan Davis, Sung Bong Lee, and Reginald Sudarta.

    Project designed by David Babson, Claudia Schmidt-Dannert, Sara Bloch, Tahnia Gonzalez, and Jeff Gralnick.

    Team biography picture editing by Viki Ding.
