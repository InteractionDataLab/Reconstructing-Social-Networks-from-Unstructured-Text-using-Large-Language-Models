SiL & GFP PCT

Project: Surface Display

Date: Tue, 30 Aug 2011 18:38:12 GMT

Author: Viki Ding (dingx055)

Access: Public

Revision History:

  • Mon, 26 Sep 2011 18:47:02 GMT (dingx055): entry created in project'Surface Display' by dingx055 (id=9)

New vent polymerase ordered.

Objective: obtain SiL and GFP fragments

Silicatein (~1 kb)

- 1 uL vent pol.

- 3 uL dNTP

- 5 uL vent buffer

- 5 uL forward primer

- 5 uL reverse primer

- 2 uL SiL DNA

- 29 uL H2O

Taq mix

- 5 uL forward primer

- 5 uL reverse primer

- 2 uL SiL DNA

- 12 uL taq green mix

PCR conditions

95 degrees >> 4 min

95 degrees >> 30 sec

63 degrees >> 30 sec

72 degrees >> 1 min

34 cycles

72 degrees >> 10 min

GFP (less than 1 kb, ~950 bp)

- 1 uL vent pol.

- 3 uL dNTP

- 5 uL vent buffer

- 5 uL forward primer

- 5 uL reverse primer

- 2 uL GFP DNA

- 29 uL H2O

Taq mix

- 5 uL forward primer

- 5 uL reverse primer

- 2 uL GFP DNA

- 12 uL taq green mix

PCR conditions

95 degrees >> 4 min

95 degrees >> 30 sec

60 degrees >> 30 sec

72 degrees >> 1 min

34 cycles

72 degrees >> 10 min

Results

- Sil taq: thick bright band below 500 bp

- Sil vent: thick bright band at ~ 1 kb

- GFP taq: thick bright band at ~ 950 bp

- GFP vent: think bright band at ~ 950 bp
