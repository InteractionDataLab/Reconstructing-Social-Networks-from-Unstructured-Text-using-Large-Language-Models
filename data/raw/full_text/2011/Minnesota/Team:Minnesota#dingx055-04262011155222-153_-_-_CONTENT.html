PCR set up for INPNC & GFP.

50 uL Reaction

1 µl vent polymerase

2 µl dNTP

2 µl DNA template

5 µl vent buffer

5 µl forward primer

5 µl reverse primer

30 µl nuclease free water

PCR conditions

95 degrees >> 10 min

95 degrees >> 30 sec

60 degrees >> 1 min

72 degrees >> 1 min

72 degrees >> 10 min

35 cycles

Results: multitple bands, not the correct size
