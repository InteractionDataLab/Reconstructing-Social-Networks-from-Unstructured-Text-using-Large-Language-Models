PCR of ompR and silicatein

Project: Regulatory

Date: Tue, 30 Aug 2011 13:29:13 GMT

Author: Nathan Davis (davis950)

Access: Public

Revision History:

  • Tue, 30 Aug 2011 13:33:57 GMT (davis950): entry created in project'Regulatory' by davis950 (id=2)

Set up a PCR reaction to amplify ompR promoter from distribution plates (BBa_R0082). Fifty ul reaction volume contained:

  • 1 ul template DNA
  • 1 ul forward primer
  • 1 ul reverse primer
  • 5 ul thermopol buffer
  • 0.5 ul 20 mM dNTPs
  • 2.5 ul 10 mM MgSO4
  • 0.5 ul Vent polymerase
  • 38.5 ul of H2O

Silicatein alpha amplified from TOPO-Silicatein using the same reaction mixture as above.
