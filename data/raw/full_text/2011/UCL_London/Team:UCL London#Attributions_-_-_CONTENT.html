Attributions

All work has been conducted by the team except for assistance from the people and organisations below:

Supervisors, Darren Nesbeth and Eli Keshavarz-Moore - requested funding from UCL Biochemical Engineering Department, sought permission for lab access.

Instructors, Annie Wei and Oriana Losito - assisted in primer design and debug protocol

Prof. Anthony Maxwell of JIC - advised on Supercoiliology project and provided research materials

Prof. Mike Hoare - provided advice regarding manufacturing process

Prof. Yuen Kah Hay - provided sponsorship for project

Chris Morris - helped us with manufacturing bioprocess design and cost analysis

Haroon Khan - provided advise regarding manufacturing plan and public engagement

Dr. Steve Branston - helped us with bioreactor setup and provided fermentation advice

Dr. Yuhong Zhou - provided advice regarding manufacturing process

A-Level intern student, Tom Dean - assisted with basic lab techniques

WMG Operon - provision of primer synthesis

UCL Cancer DNA sequencing Institute & Wolfson Institute of Biomedical Research - DNA sequencing

Kong Khai Jien - helped us to make the gyrase protein models.
