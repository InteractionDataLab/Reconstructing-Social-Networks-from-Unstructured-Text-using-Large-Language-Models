Acknowledment

iGEM UT-Tokyo team would like to thank the following organizations for their understanding of our activity generous support.

Laboratory

We are grateful to Nureki Laboratory, Graduate School of Science, University of Tokyo, for providing us with lab space, equipments and valuable advice.

We are also grateful to Takuya Ueda, Graduate School of Frontier Science, University of Tokyo, for providing the precious opportunity of presenting our ideas at annual meeting of The Society for Biotechnology, Japan.

Support from Our University

We have received financial support from Divison of International Affairs, University of Tokyo as the International Academic Exchange Grant.

Sponsor Companies

We received financial and/or material supports from the following companies:
Microtec Co., Ltd. for lending us their luminometer
REVOX Inc. for providing financial support and their optic products
NIPPON GENE Co., Ltd. for free supply of reagents
The MathWorks Inc. for providing licenses for their software products (MatLab, SimBiology)
Berthold Japan Co., Ltd. for free supply of luciferin and loan of luminometer
Scientific Education Group Co., Ltd. for their financial support
COSMO BIO Co., Ltd. for their financial support
OPTY Co., Ltd. for providing our custom T-shirt at reduced price
