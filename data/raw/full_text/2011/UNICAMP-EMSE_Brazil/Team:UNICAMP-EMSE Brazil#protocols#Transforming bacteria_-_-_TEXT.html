Add the mixture to the tube containing competent bacteria and pipette up and down. Place the tube on ice for 30 min. Leave the tube for 10 min in room temperature. Add 1ml of LB-Media 1 hour at 37˚C. Plate aliquotes of the transformed bacteria on LB-agar containing the appropriate antibiotic. Incubate overnight at 37˚C.
