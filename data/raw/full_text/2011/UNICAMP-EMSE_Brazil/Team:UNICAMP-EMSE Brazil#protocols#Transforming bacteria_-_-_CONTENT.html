Transforming bacteria:

Thermocompetent Transformation

  • In the flux, add between 5 and 10 μl of ligation or purified pDNA to 15 μl of transformation buffer.
  • Add the mixture to the tube containing competent bacteria and pipette up and down.
  • Place the tube on ice for 30 min.
  • Leave the tube for 10 min in room temperature.
  • Add 1ml of LB-Media
  • 1 hour at 37˚C.

Plate aliquotes of the transformed bacteria on LB-agar containing the appropriate antibiotic.

Incubate overnight at 37˚C.
