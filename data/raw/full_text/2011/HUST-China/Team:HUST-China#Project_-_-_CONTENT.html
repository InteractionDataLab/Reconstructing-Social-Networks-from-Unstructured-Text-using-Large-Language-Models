    Overview---Background & abstract

          Because of the growing popularity of iGEM, an increasing number of people tend to be a member of the big family of iGEM. Ideas for this competition are come up with mostly in the following ways(figure).       To solve those problems,we design the Super E.coli Architect (SEA) software.The Super E.coli Architect (SEA) is a kind of design software for engineering bacteria and offers a convenient and efficient design platform, having capability to integrate scattered simple ideas into a complete solution.

    Tool--a brief view to SEA

    The existing models of SEA software:Idea Cloud

          This model is Idea Cloud, which provides customers brianstorm support. When a project has not yet formed an idea, it is inefficient to find idea without purpose. what is more, the results of the idea would be tendentious and uneffective.
          Our Idea Cloud model is based on summarizing most ideas that former teams concluded. After reorganizing, refining, we conclude these strong functional elements, which can provide outstanding effects to people who are looking for an excellent idea.[>>more]
