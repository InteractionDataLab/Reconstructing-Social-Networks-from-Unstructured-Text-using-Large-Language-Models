    Idea Cloud
          Our Idea Cloud has a vairiety of functional elements, spining and rotating around. Once clicking on a element you are interested in，elements that are related to that particular element will gather around the element you are interested in.       The reason why it is called Cloud is that we have considered all the relationships betweeen each elements, which are linear instead of reticular.       Through our rotate idea cloud, we could only only gain deep understanding of former teams' ideas, but also could combine and intergrate all the functional elements during designing our process, resulting a brand new idea.       Tools required: Flash, Papervision 3D, Php, My SQL
