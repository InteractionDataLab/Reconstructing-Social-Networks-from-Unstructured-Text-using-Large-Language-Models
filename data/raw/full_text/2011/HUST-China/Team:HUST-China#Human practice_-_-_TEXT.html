Meanwhile, we introduced to them the origination and development of iGEM. Results below are based on sample size of 100 students. Question one: Are you familiar with synthetic biology? a. Never heard about it b. I know it, but not too much c. I am familiar with it d. I am just doing it Question two: What do you think synthetic biology focus on? a. Nucleotide/Protein b. Bioinformatics b. Engineering c. Chemistry d. Genetics Question four: How do you think about the future of synthetic biology? a. It will result in the next technological revolution and bring benefit to the human race for a long time b. It does not grasp the essence of life and will ultimately fail c. It goes against the order of Nature and will probably play the same role with the nuclear weapon d. I do not know Question five: Which of the human dreams below do you think synthetic biology could most probably realize? a. To be spider man b.
