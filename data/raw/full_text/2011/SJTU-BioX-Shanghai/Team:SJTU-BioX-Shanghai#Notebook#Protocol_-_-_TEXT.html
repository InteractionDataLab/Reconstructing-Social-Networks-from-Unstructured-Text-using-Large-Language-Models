Luciferase Assay -Remove growth medium from cultured cells. -Re-suspend cell precipitate in 0.85% NaCl. -Prepare of bacterial cell lysate using Ultrasonication. Sonics ON 3 seconds, OFF 3 seconds, total ultrasonication time 3minutes. -Mix 50μl of cell lysate with 50μl of Luciferase Assay Reagent(Luciferase Reporter Gene Assay Kit of Beyotime) and measure the light produced with luminometer.
