Puppeteer DB -> schema I now have my work cut out for me for the next few weeks. Me, Swapnil, and Viktor had a juicy discussion about how to deal with the plate allocation issue. How does Puppeteer/ResourceManager figure out what plates are least needed and make room on the deck when the deck clutter's up with plates. We agreed to execute a 3 pass method to do this, the first pass calculates resource requirements and initializes the deck, the second pass figures out what plates are accessed at what time, and during the third pass we actually passes instructions to the robot. By this point, the resource manager knows how to make room on the deck and put what plates in the hotel depending on the access time for each plate.
