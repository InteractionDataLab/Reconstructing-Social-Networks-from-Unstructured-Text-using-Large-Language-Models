August 19

WET LAB

Inoculation of transformed bacteria to grow them

Materials Falcon tubes of 50mL Solid LB Stock of chloranphenicol BBa_K410000, pSB1C3 BBa_K328003, pSB1C3 BBa_K328001, pSB1C3 Six small petri dishes

To fill each petri dish we used the following calculation to obtain the desired concentration: 20mL de LB × (33μg Chloramphenicol)/(mL de LB) × mL/(34mg Chloramphenicol)=19,4μL

1. We heated solid LB to make it liquid. 2. We placed it in an incubator to keep it warm. 3. We placed 20mL of LB in a 50 mL falcon tube and cooled it a bit but avoiding solidification. 4. We added 19.4 µL of chloramphenicol to the falcon tube. 5. We poured about 3.5 ml to each Petri dish. 6. We tooke an aliquot with a handgrip and spread it on a plate with LB and Chloramphenicol. 7. We repeated the above procedure for each sample containing different BioBrick using a different handgrip to spread.
