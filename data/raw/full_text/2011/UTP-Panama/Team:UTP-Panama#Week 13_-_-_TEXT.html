September 2 WET LAB Proposal for Experiments with BBa_K381001 1- Proposal # 3 was done The 3 tubes contained: 10mL of LB 20µL KNO3 1M solution 9,7µL chloramphenicol 34mg/mL A tube as negative control was prepared, without KNO3 Inoculated tubes with e. coli transformed with BBa_381001 Tubes were set with different temperatures: Negative control at 37°C 37°C 15-20°C 20-30°C 2- Calculations for the preparations of the solutions utilized during the experience: Preparation of 10mL of KNO3 1M stock solution: MMKNO_3=101,1g/mol
(1mol KNO_3)/1L×(101,1gKNO_3 )/(1molKNO_3 )×1L/1000mL×10mL=1,011gKNO_3 Expected Results The functionality of the Biobrick is to detect presence/absence of nitrates, in this case was KNO3 0.20% in LB, in the tubes set at the indicated temperatures. It’s expected that all tubes, except in the negative control, was observed a green fluorescence under UV light.
