I.III ALUMNI iGEM AlumniGEM is a new platform for connecting past, present and future members of iGEM. It will unite the iGEM community, resurrect old ties, and bring forward inspiring stories from past alumni. It has a forum where users can connect with each other and share every kind of information, especially about SynBio.
