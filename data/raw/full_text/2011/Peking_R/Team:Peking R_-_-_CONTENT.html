Team:Peking R

From 2011.igem.org

In our project, we are aiming at establishing an extensible and versatile methodology for the softcoding of genetic program in bacteria, composed of genetic rheostat and RBS calculator. The genetic rheostats are truly modular ligand-responsive riboswitches, while the RBS calculator is software for both translation of ligand concentration into quantified translation strength and automated design of synthetic ribosome binding sites (RBS) with customized strength. To learn more, please click here.

 

Peking_R team consists of 11 undergraduate students who are enjoying synthetic biology.

To know more about us and our work, please visit our Team page and our Notebook.

We've submitted basic reliable genetic rheostats. Besides, we applied them to construct various functional devices. Click here to learn more.

Our human practice concerns on how the development of synthetic biology affects public health and the process of horizontal gene transfer among prokaryotes. To learn more, please clickhere.

Here we describe measures taken to guarentee biosafety in our project, answering frequently asked questions concerning about environment and public health. To get more information, please click here.

It is an unforgettable summer when we fought for our dream and the honor of Peking iGEM team.

Photos recorded every foot-print of everyone. Please click here to join us together!
