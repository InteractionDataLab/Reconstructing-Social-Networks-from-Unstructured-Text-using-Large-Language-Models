Tetracycline induces chiX transcription. 2. chiX base-pair and sequester the lacZ mRNA RBS, which inhibits translation of the mRNA. 3. Arabinose induces chiXR transcription. 4. chiXR base-pair to chiX , which promote degradation of the sRNA. 5. LacZ repression is relieved. In a strain that constutively expresses chiX but is lacking chiXR, induction with chitobiose only has the effect of inducing transcription from the promoter, and does not remove chiX downregulation. In a strain deficient in chiX induction with chitobiose has little effect since the chiP RBS is already unregulated. Data For Our Favorite New Parts: Main Page PchiP(original):lacZ, BBa_K564012 : Promoter of the chitoporin chiP gene fused with lacZ (from BBa_I732005 ) encoding β-galactosidase. Is induced by chitobiose and is regulated by chiX Main Page PchiP(mutated):lacZ, BBa_K564013: Mutated promoter of the chitoporin chiP gene fused with lacZ (from BBa_I732005 ) encoding β-galactosidase. Is induced by chitobiose, but not regulated by chiX Data For Pre-existing Parts: Experience lacZ (encoding beta-galactosidase, full-length), BBa_I732005: This part controlled by the chiP promoter gave blue colonies when grown on chitobiose. Experience Inducible pBad/araC promoter, BBa_I746908: We generated an synthetic promoter library (SPL) based on this part. This allows for the generation of a vast number of arabinose inducible promoters of various strengths. The arabinose promoter was improved both by rationally changing the -10 and the -35 part of the promoter, and randomly by generating an SPL from the wild-type promoter.
