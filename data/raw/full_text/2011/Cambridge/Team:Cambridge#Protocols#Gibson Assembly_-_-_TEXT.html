This protocol is adapted from RFC57 by the 2010 Cambridge iGEM team. Practice Master Mix for Gibson Assembly Reagent Volume (µl) Taq ligase (40u/µl) 50 5x isothermal buffer 100 T5 exonuclease (1u/µl) 2 Phusion polymerase (2u/µl) 6.25 Nuclease-free water 216.75 Total 375 Master Mix is 1.33x concentrated DNA and Gibson Master Mix should be combined with a volumetric ration of 1:3 in a PCR tube. The total volume can be 20-50µl. Set thermocycler containing the PCR tubes to 50 degrees C for 1 hour. Safety No bacteria are used during the reaction there is therefore little or no biological hazard. Nevertheless, it is important to observe correct laboratory procedure and wear appropriate clothing and gloves; nucleases are present on human skin.
