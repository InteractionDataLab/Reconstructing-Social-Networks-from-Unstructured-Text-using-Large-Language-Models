Brainstorming
Our project didn't just appear out of nowhere, it was rather a result of a long and tedious process. At the very beginning of it all, we did some brainstorming. This resulted in a list of E. coli with preposterous and even ludicrous capabilities - but fortunately some of the ideas turned out to be feasible :)

  • Blood clotting bacteria
  • Stain removing bacteria
