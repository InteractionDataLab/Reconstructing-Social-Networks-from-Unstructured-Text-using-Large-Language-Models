Remove competent cells from -80, let thaw for 10 min on ice and aliquot in 50 ul amounts. 2. add 2-5 ul of vector, usually in H2O, to 50 ul cells, no mixing by pipet due to shear induction. 3. keep on ice for 20 minutes (vector spreading through volume) 4. heat shock (42°C) for 45 seconds 5. keep on ice for 2 minutes 6. add 200 ul SOC, put on 37°C for 1 hour or longer with agitation. 7. plate out 250 ul on appropriate antibiotics.
