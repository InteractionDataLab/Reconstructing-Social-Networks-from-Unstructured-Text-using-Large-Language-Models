    May 16

    Results of PCR amplification of OxyS, SoxS, KatG, and HemH promoters.

    Amplified OxyS, SoxS, KatG, and HemH promoters and cleaned up the products with ethanol.

    May 17

    Restriction digested the products from the previous day.

    May 18

    Froze down bacterial strains: pGLOW, pPLAT, and pPLAT w/ GFP. Ligated above promoters into pPLAT w/ GFP and transformed them into DH5a E. Coli.

    May 19

    Prepared colony PCR of above transformed bacteria to ensure the ligation and transformation worked.
