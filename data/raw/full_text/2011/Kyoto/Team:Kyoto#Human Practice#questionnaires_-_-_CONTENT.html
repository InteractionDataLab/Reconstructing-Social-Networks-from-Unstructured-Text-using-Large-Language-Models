    • Human Practice Questionnaires

    We carried out two kinds of questionnaires. Students answered about their opinions for synthetic biology in one questionnaire. Another questionnaire asked them of their impressions and comments on our lecture. These files are results and discussions.

    Q1.How did you know this lecture?Q2.Do you know iGEM?Q3.Do you like Biology?Q4.What about our lecture left an impression on you?Q5.What is your impression of iGEM?Q6.Please rate the difficulty of this lecture.Q7.If given a chance, what would you like to do in iGEM?Q8.Please tell us what you want to know more.
