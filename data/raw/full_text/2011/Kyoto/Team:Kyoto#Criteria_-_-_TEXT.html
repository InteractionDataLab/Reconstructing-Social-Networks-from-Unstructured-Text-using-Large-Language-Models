Page name: Team:Kyoto/HumanPractice iGEM Prizes Best Human Practice Advance Best Experimental Measurement Best Model Best human practice:We conducted the final way to arose interest in students and give then appropriate knowledge based on their own experiences, introduce and attract them iGEM HS division. This is a new and powerful approach to make iGEM and synthetic biology more popular. In order to assist future iGEMer, we founded the department which is for iGEM HS division in September. Komatsu and Nobeyama became point man of this project, then we started to accept question about iGEM HS Division, synthetic biology, how to experiment, and so on Best Experimental Measurement: We devised a new way to calculate RPU and the equation is expressed as follows. RPUφ = [mRNAφss]/[mRNA j23101 ss]. It is a lot of troubles to calculate RPU using GFP fluorescence , but this way we can save these troubles. Using our new method you can characterize the promoters' activities regulated by the substances which could be consumed by cells, while using the previous method, you can't.
