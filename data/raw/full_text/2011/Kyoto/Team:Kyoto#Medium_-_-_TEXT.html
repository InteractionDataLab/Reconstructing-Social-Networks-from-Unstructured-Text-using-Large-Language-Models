Then, autoclave it. Stir corn flour 45g and glucose 50g with the remaining water. Stir 1 and 2, then autoclave it again. after autoclave, add propionic acid 1.5mL and 10 % p-hydroxybenzoate in 70 % Eternol 5mL into it. Standard medium for drosophila M9 medium Stir Na2HPO4 6 g, KH2PO4 3 g, NaCl 0.5 g and NH4Cl 1 g with water 1 l. After autoclave, add 10 ml filter sterilized 100 mM MgSO4, 20 % glucose, 10 mM CaCl2, 100 mM thiamine-HCl. If you need, add 10 ml filter sterilized 20 % casamino acid. LB medium Stir Tryptone 20 g, Yeast extract 10 g and NaCl 10 g with water 200ml. If you make LB plates, add agar 10 g. Autoclave. SOB medium Stir Tryptone 20g and Yeast extract 5g with water. Add 2 ml 5 M NaCl and 840 ul 3 M KCl and add water up to 1 l. After autoclave, add 10 ml filter sterilized 1 M MgSO4 and 1 M MgCl2. SOC medium Add 2 M glucose 1 ml to 100 ml SOB. Buffer TB Stir PIPES and CaCl2・2H2O with 100 ml water. Add 8.315 ml 3 M KCl. Add KOH and adjust pH 6.8. Add MnCl2・4H2O. Add water up to 200 ml.
