    • Ubbit repository

    The Ubbit repository is a web database developed to facilitate the implementation of the Ubbit standard. The repository is in a very early stage of its development, so we are launching it today in a pre-alpha release.

    The main functionalities of the database are:

    • Add your own wires, and logical cores. Let's make the repository grow!
    • Search over other researchers' resources.
    • A friendly interface, more atractive and easier to use than other biological databases.

    Over the following days you will be able to see it in action in Ubbit.org or here.

    The future of Ubbit.com

    We will continue developing the repository, adding new features, like the module section and user management. If you think of a new feature for the repository, don't hesitate to contact us, you have the forum below. Or you can just send us an email to contacto@arcanumproject.com , follow us in Twitter or look for us in Facebook
