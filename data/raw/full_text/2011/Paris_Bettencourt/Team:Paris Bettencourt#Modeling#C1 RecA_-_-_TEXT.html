LacI repression can be cancelled by IPTG. This way we can induce production of RFP and T7' by puttig IPTG on the cells. Inactivated LacI can not repress the pLAC promoter anymore. Note that we consider that the reaction between IPTG and LacI fires without any delay. This assumption is justified by the fact that this reaction is much faster than any other in our gene network.
