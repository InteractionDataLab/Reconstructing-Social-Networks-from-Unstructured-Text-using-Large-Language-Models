Our project is about light sensing systems. But our college does little research on this filed. Luckily, we got a lot help from our instructors and other iGEM teams. Here we would like to give our sincere thanks to all of you. Thank you very much and wish you iGEM teams get good results. Instructors During this summer, our two instructors gave us a lot of help. They did not help us do experiments. But without their good suggestions, good instructions and also the laboratory's supporting, we could not carry out our projects. iGEM_11 Team-Edinburgh They sent us materials of green light sensing and reporting parts. PhoA promoter with lacZ'alpha and mCherry reporter genes, in pSB1C3. 4. CcaRS DNA, sent by Mexico - it appears that this is a PCR product, not a plasmid. iGEM_11 Team-HUST-China They gave us the BBa_I15010, as ours was identified as void-vector. iGEM_11 Team-UNAM-Genomics_Mexico They sent us materials of blue light sensing and reporting parts.
