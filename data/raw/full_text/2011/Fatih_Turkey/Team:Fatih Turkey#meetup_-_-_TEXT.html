Yasama Sanatı Meet Up SYNTHETIC BIOLOGY TURKEY MEETING Synthetic Biology Turkey meetings 1.0 of iGEM Turkey teams were organized by Association of Synthetic Biology and METU-Ankara Team in September 10, 2011 and we invited to the meeting organization, we were be glad to being a part of this meet up. The organization’s aim was to arrange meeting with all Turkey iGEM team members and researchers and related companies in order to discuss the Synthetic biology in Turkey and the iGEM competition potential on ongoing studies. In this case, all of iGEM teams and Bio- entrepreneurship company owners presented their presentations also some foreign academics talked about synthetic biology and gave some helpful advices to listeners with helping the teleconferences. It was very informed for us also very enjoyed. We presented a succesful presentation which is about our project and played our Canwas Town Game with other iGEM Turkey teams. Also one of the important news informants of Turkey which is Anadolu Agency came to this meet up and talked with Turkey teams’ members and took some photos.
