LB Broth Preparation - Add 200 mL of dH2O to a graduated cyclindar. - Transfer dH2O into glass bottle. - Add 4 gr of LB powder - Autoclave the bottle. - After cooling, add 200 uL antibiotic (The LB agar solution should be cool enough not to damage to antibiotic)  Transformation - Aseptic conditions prepared (70% EtOH, Bunsen burner etc.) - Place 500 uL LB in epp into heat block(42˚C). - Thaw 50 uL competent cells on ice. - Add 1 uL plasmid into the competent cell epp and spin for few sec. - Incubate for 45 min on ice. - Incubate at 42˚C for 80 sec in heat block.    - Incubate for 5 min on ice. - Complete to 500 uL with pre-heated LB (42˚C). -.Epp s adhered with tape to horizontal on shaker. - Incubate at 37 C for 1 h at 240 rpm. - Spread 125 uL from each tube on agar plates with suitable antibiotic. - Incubate plates at 37˚C not longer than 12-14 h.
