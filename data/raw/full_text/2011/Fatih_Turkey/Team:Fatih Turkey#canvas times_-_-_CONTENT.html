    Canvas Times

    CANVAS TIMES We have prepared a journal which is named “Canvas Times”. It gives you the actual news of iGEM competition, our lab and team as well as other iGEMers and their projects. Also, you can learn about our project and read our columnist, too. During our lab working, we could find some time to have fun and talk with other people about our project, synthetic biology and iGEM. All of our activities and human practices are existed in our journal such as “Fatih Turkey at Kindergarten” and “International Medical Students Conference. Therewithal, you will find some information of our university and faculty. You can find all of the stores. This journal is an easy way to reach out more people that has been very useful during our human practices.
