    After electrophoresis results; we furthermore performed sequencing and imaging tests to characterize our BioBricks. For BBa_541505 and BBa_541506; we added a GFP sequence and transformated into E.coli. This has characterized our main proteins. cDNA sequencing for these parts was also done successfully. In addition, BBa_541800 is transformated into B.subtilis and successfully imaged red that shows RFP region and therefore plasmid backbone works properly. We characterized BBa_K541545, BBa_K541515 and BBa_K541915 via sequencing.
