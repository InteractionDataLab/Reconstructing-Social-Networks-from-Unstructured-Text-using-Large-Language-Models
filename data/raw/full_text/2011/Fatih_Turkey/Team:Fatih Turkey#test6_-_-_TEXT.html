Sterilization period of Supernatant We have deduced that supernatant of B.subtilis that produces LALF sterilize the surfaces that LALF had applied on. Thus, we wanted to test how long this sterilization exists. To do this, supernatants with and without LALF waited in +4 ⁰C for 24 and 36 hours. Then, we tested their ability to sterilize. Equal amount of E.coli with RFP sequence were spread into the fresh plates Then, 100 ul supernatant with and without was added on plates. Note that no colony grew on where supernatant that contents LALF was added after both 24 and 36 hours. (the plates on the right) To understand better how much effective our protein is, we measured the diameter of the no growth zone. In 24 hour waited plate, diameter was 2,5 cm; on the other hand, it was measured 1,7 cm in 36 hour waited plate.   THE SUICIDE EXPERIMENT OF E. Subtilis spors and bacteria. It kills the spors by oxidizing with its Cu ions. We prepared this solution in two ways one with the hydrogen peroxide (OFR) according to the literature and the other with distilized water (DFR). In our experiments we learned which of these two solutions is more effective on E. Coli, B. Subtilis or the the spores of B. Subtilis and degree of its effectiveness.
