Several genetically modified cells imitate the five elements in Lao’s view, constitutes “Yuanqi”, in which they promote and inhibit each other. So we can study the coexistence and cooperation of several functional cells in balance. In Lao’s conception, the five fundamental elements have a relationship of supporting and inhibit each other so as to keep the universe stable. It is our hope that through our work cells can survive in such a harmonious manner.
