TetR E. coli have evolved a resistance to the tetracycline antibiotic which is found in the tetracycline operon. This operon is regulated by TetR, the tetracycline repressor, which is a dimeric protein that binds the tetracycline repressible promoter. TetR monomer Construct Mutant Screening We selected our mutants by reading GFP fluorescence in our plate reader.
