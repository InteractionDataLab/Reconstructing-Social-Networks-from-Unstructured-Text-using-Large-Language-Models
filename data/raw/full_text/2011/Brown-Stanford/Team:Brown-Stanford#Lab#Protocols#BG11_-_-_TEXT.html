Stock 2 7.5g/liter MgSO4•7H2O Filter sterilize into a sterile bottle or autoclave. Stock 3 4.0g/liter K2HPO4•3H2O or 3.05g/liter K2HPO4 Filter sterilize into a sterile bottle or autoclave. Stock 5 (Microelements) Stock 5 (Microelements): 2.86g/liter H3BO3 1.81g/liter MnCl2•4H2O 0.222g/liter ZnSO4•7H2O 0.079g/liter CuSO4•5H2O 0.050g/liter COCl2•6H2O 0.391g/liter NaMoO4•2H2O or 0.018g/liter MoO4 (85%) Basic BG11 medium Combine the following stock solutions (for 1 liter): 10 mL Stock 1 10 mL Stock 2 10 mL Stock 3 0.02 g Na2CO3 1.0 mL Stock 5 1.5 g NaNO3 Combine stocks and adjust pH to 7.5 (use 1.0N HCl). Aliquot into flasks (50 mL/125 mL flask) with cotton stoppers on top and autoclave. After autoclaving and cooling the pH is about 7.1. For solid media add 1% noble agar. For BG-11, don't add NaNO3.
