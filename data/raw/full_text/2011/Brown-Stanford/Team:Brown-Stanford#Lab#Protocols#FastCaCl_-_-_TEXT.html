Cool cells on ice for 10 minutes. Centrifuge cells at 3000rpm, 4oC, for 5 minutes. Discard supernatant and place cells back on ice. Resuspend cells in 2mL of cold CaCl2 (50mM). Cool cells on ice for 15 minutes. Centrifuge cells at 3000rpm, 4oC, for 5 minutes. Discard supernatant and place cells back on ice. Resuspend cells in 500µL of cold CaCl2 (50mM). Transformation Take 100-200µL of the resuspended cells and place in a precooled microfugetube. Add DNA ligation mixture. Incubate on ice for 20-30 minutes. Prepare 42oC water bath. Heat shock cells in water bath for 2 minutes. Move cells back onto the ice. Add 0.5mL LB media. Incubate cells at 37oC for 1 hour. Spread cells over 1-2 LB/antibiotic plates, and invert in incubator overnight.
