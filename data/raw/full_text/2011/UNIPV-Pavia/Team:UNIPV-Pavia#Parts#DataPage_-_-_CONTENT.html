    Data For Our Favorite New Parts

    BBa_K516210

    BBa_K516210(pTet-RBS30-LuxI) - HSL synthesis device, aTc inducible. This is one of four systems with different RBSs (output modulation) tested to quantify the produced HSL. The results obtained by testing this part allowed the identification of important model parameters to predict the behavior of the whole CTRL+E circuit.

    ^top

    BBa_K516334

    BBa_K516334(pLambda-RBS30-LuxR-T-pLux-RBS34-mRFP-TT) - 3OC6-HSL biosensor, mRFP output. This is one of the four biosensors (different RBSs) built and characterized. This measurement system senses the HSL concentration in the medium and responds with an RFP output. Several RBSs have been used to modulate the output range. This is a simple measurement system, used to identify important model parameters used to simulate the behavior of the whole circuit.

    ^top

    BBa_K516230

    BBa_K516230(pTet-RBS34-mRFP-TT) - aTc inducible, mRFP output. This is one of the four pTet measurement systems (different RBSs) built and characterized. This measurement system senses the aTc concentration in the medium and responds with an RFP output. Several RBSs have been used to modulate the output range. This is a simple measurement system, used to identify important model parameters used to simulate the behavior of the whole circuit.
