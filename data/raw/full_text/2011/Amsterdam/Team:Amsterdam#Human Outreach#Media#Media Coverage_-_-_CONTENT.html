    • Media Coverage

    Team iGEM Amsterdam has been placed in the spotlight by several media sources. Below is an overview of the media coverage the team and its project has received.

    iGEM Watch Blog

    Christian Lund, a medicine student from Denmark, writes a blog on teams worth watching. Team Amsterdam was featured by him 15th of August. Read more!

    Ad Valvas

    Ad Valvas is the university paper of VU University Amsterdam. In this paper, the iGEM team Amsterdam tells their story about their 'cool' bacteria and their human outreach projects. Click here to read their story.

    C2W

    Chemical Weekly is an old but still widely used name of C2W, and is the news update magazine of the Royal Dutch Chemical Society (KNCV). C2W is a journal for chemistry, life sciences and process technology. We are proud to announce that iGEM is highlighted in C2W. Click here to read.

    Elsevier Science

    We are proud to present an article in the Elsevier Science . Elsevier Science is a publishing company which publishes scientific literature. To read our article click here .

    Bionieuws

    Bionieuws is the magazine for biologists. Every two weeks, new and exciting bionews is published. This time, Bionieuws has an article dedicated to the iGEM competition, iGEM team Amsterdam and the IcE. coli project. However, the article is published after the wiki freeze. When the wiki re-opens, we'll show you the article.
