The team is composed of 7 students of Amsterdam's two universities: the Vrije Universiteit (VU) and the Universiteit van Amsterdam (UvA). The advisors of iGEM team Amsterdam are also employed by these institutes. The members of iGEM team Amsterdam work in lab and office space offered to them by the Swammerdam Institute of Life Sciences (SILS); one of the UvA's departments.
