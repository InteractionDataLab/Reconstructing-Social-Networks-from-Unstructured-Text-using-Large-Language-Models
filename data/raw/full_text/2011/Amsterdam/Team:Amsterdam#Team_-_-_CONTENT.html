    Team iGEM Amsterdam 2011 will represent the Dutch capital in the iGEM competition this year. The team is composed of 7 students of Amsterdam's two universities: the Vrije Universiteit (VU) and the Universiteit van Amsterdam (UvA). The advisors of iGEM team Amsterdam are also employed by these institutes.
