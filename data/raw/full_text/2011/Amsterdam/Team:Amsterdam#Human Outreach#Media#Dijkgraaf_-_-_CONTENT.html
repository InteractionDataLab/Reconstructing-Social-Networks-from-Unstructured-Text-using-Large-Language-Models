    • Interview with the president of KNAW

    Two team members of iGEM team Amsterdam, Sandra and Jantine, went to the KNAW (Royal Netherlands Academy of Arts and Sciences) for an interview with the president of KNAW, prof. dr. Robbert Dijkgraaf. As well as president of the KNAW, Robbert Dijkgraaf is a professor in Mathematical Physics at the University of Amsterdam. During the interview with Robbert Dijkgraaf, we discussed dangers and impact on society of Synthetic Biology, but also the role of scientist in public debate. Watch the interview here or here for silverlight and enjoy.
