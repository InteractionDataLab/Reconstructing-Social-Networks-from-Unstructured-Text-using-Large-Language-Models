Fluorimetry

Fluorimetry protocol M9 minimal media The OD600 and fluorescence of a great number of samples can be measured at the same time with a fluorimeter. We used it for measuring pBAD/araC-cI-LVA+pcI-GFP with different concentrations of arabinose as an induction signal.

Fluorimetry protocol

Inoculate 10ml M9 medium+ antibiotics + 1ml 2% casamino acids and grow the cultures overnight at 37°C. Grow a logfase to an OD600 of 0.4. Dilute in a way that the OD600 is decreased to a value of 0.004 Add 300μl of the diluted culture into a well. The fluorimeter will measure the OD600+ fluorescence every 15 minutes. Fluorimeter used for measurements: infinite F200 TECAN. Serial no. : 811005514. Back to fluorimetry

M9 minimal media

Component Concentrations 1x M9 salts 2mM MgSO4 0.1mM CaCl2 0.4% carbon source (e.g. glycerol, glucose, etc.) In sterile H2O Protocol For 1L of media 500ml 2xM9 salts 20ml 0.1M MgSO4 200μl 0.5M CaCl2 469.8ml sterile deionized H2O Combine above solutions using sterile technique. (May notice some precipitation during preparation but precipitate should go back into solution once volume is brought up to 1l with sterile H2O.) Add antibiotics as appropriate and store at 4°C http://openwetware.org/wiki/Endy:M9_medium/minimal Back to fluorimetry
