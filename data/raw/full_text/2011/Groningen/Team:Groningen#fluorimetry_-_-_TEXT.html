We used it for measuring pBAD/araC-cI-LVA+pcI-GFP with different concentrations of arabinose as an induction signal. Fluorimetry protocol Inoculate 10ml M9 medium+ antibiotics + 1ml 2% casamino acids and grow the cultures overnight at 37°C. Grow a logfase to an OD600 of 0.4. Dilute in a way that the OD600 is decreased to a value of 0.004 Add 300μl of the diluted culture into a well. The fluorimeter will measure the OD600+ fluorescence every 15 minutes. Fluorimeter used for measurements: infinite F200 TECAN. Serial no. : 811005514. (May notice some precipitation during preparation but precipitate should go back into solution once volume is brought up to 1l with sterile H2O.)
