Plasmid Backbone

How does it work?

You can upload your raw DNA sequence using TXT or FASTA format files. For FASTA format files, if your files have incorrect or missing characters, it has to be fixed beforehand. For multiFASTA files, this aplication only reads the first sequence. For TXT files, aplication will read every character inside the file, so after it has to erase the information not useful.

You can choose the standard DNA-shipping plasmid backbone pSB1C3 or choose another different plasmid for your build construction:

  • Part Assembly
  • Reshma's low and medium copy operation plasmid backbones
  • Additional low and medium copy operation plasmid backbones
  • Inducible copy number operation plasmid backbones
  • Multi-host operation plasmid backbones
  • Eukaryotic operation plasmid backbones
  • Plasmids used in DNA synthesis of BioBrick parts
