    Instructors

    Dan JedrysiakI am in the second year of my Master's program in Cellular and Molecular Medicine. I have been involved in iGEM as an undergraduate since 2008 and this year I am participating as an instructor. I don't always clone (that's actually not true) but when I do, I prefer BrickMason!
    Alex PowerI am currently doing my Master's in Cellular and Molecular Medicine, and have been a member of the University of Ottawa iGEM team since 2009. I may not be as handsome as Dan Jedrysiak but I've still tried to be as valuable to the team as possible. We have a great project this year and I am impressed with the effort that all the team members put forth. I considered writting something about myself, like my hobbies, then I realized that I don't have any hobbies. I do, however, love synthetic biology and look forward to leveraging this love to build a billion-dollar biotech empire, or at least find interesting employment as a scientist.
