Crossover PCR was sucessful (insert image) Integrated crossover PCR product into genome of EcNR2 strain Transformed new EcnR2 strain with plasmid containing T7 polymerase and screened for appropriate colonies. Received 22 degenerate oligonucleotide sequences from Keck and performed MAGE. Cultures were grown up to midlog post electroporation and freeze-thawed approximately 14 times before plating. Suriving colonies were isolated, and we will soon put them through another cycle of MAGE. The process will be repeated.
