Add 4 µl of Dr. GenTLETM Precipitation Carrier and vortex. Add 2.5 volumes of ethanol and vortex. Centrifuge at 12,000 rpm at 4˚C for 15 min. Discard the supernatant. Rinse the pellet with 70% ethanol and centrifuge again at 12,000 rpm at 4˚C for 5 min. Discard the supernatant and dry. Dissolve the pellet in sterilized water or TE buffer.
