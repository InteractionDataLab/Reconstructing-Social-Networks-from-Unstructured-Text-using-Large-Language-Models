Who we are

　We are from Tokyo Metropolitan University in Hachioji Tokyo, Japan. Team Tokyo_Metropolitan 2011 was established in December 2010, and now have 4 instructors, 11 advisers and 18 undergraduated members. Members belong to department of Biological Sciences, Physics, or Mathematics and Information Sciences. View Larger Map

What we did

　Team Tokyo_Metropolitan 2010 had participated in iGEM and won the bronze medal. 2010's project theme is "More comfortable life style ". They planned to make E.coli producing bacterial cellulose, distinguishing rice that tastes nice, and creating patterns of animal body. Visit their wiki or Japanese Website.
