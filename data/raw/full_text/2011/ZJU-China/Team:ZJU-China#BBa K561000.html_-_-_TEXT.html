The expression of YFP under anaerobic conditions 1 2   Pvgb is a micro-aerobic promoter and it contributes to high expression of YFP under micro-aerobic conditions. It’s expression level at anaerobic condition is relatively low. The PoPS of this device under anaerobic condition is -0.375. The expression of YFP under micro-aerobic conditions (2.5% oxygen) 1 2   The expression of YFP with 2.5% oxygen concentration can reach to a relatedly high level. The expression of YFP under micro-aerobic conditions (10%) 1 2   The expression of YFP with 10% oxygen concentration can reach to a relatedly high level.
