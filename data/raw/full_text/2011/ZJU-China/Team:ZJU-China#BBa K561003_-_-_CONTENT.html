  The expressions of CFP under both aerobic and anaerobic conditions are increasing during the exponential phase. With it pTet promoter, which is constitutive, its expression intensity reaches high level. The highest expression intensity is 1.82 under anaerobic conditions and 1.69 under aerobic conditions. The PoPS of our device is 9.0269 under anaerobic conditions and 1.5055 under aerobic conditions.
