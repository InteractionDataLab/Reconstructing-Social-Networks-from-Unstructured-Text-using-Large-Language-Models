BBa_K2740014 CR1 nifD CR1 nifD encodes the subunit alpha (NifD) of the molybdenum-iron (MoFe) protein. BBa_K2740015 CR1 nifK CR1 nifK encodes the subunit beta (NifK) of the molybdenum-iron protein. BBa_K2740018 CR1 nifX CR1 nifX encodes nitrogen fixation protein NifX that favors the insertion of molybdenum-iron protein cofactors into nitrogenase. BBa_K2740019 CR1 HesA CR1 HesA encodes a NAD/FAD-binding protein, HesA, which is involved in molybdopterin and thiamine biosynthesis. It plays an important role in nitrogen fixation and is responsive to the availability of molybdenum. BBa_K2740020 CR1 nifV CR1 nifV encodes homocitrate synthase NifV that enables the synthesis of the homocitrate moiety of the molybdenum-iron protein cofactor.
