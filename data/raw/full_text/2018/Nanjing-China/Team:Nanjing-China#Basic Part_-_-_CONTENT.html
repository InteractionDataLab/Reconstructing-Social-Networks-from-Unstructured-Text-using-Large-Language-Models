    BBa_K2740012CR1 nifBCR1 nifB encodes nitrogen fixation protein NifB that is essential for biosynthesis of the active-site nitrogenase cofactor. If the CR1 nifB was deleted, the nitrogen fixation would not happen.
    BBa_K2740013CR1 nifHCR1 nifH encodes nitrogenase reductase NifH, which is an electron donor to the molybdenum-iron (MoFe) protein, contributing to the electron transport in the nitrogen fixation system.
    BBa_K2740014CR1 nifDCR1 nifD encodes the subunit alpha (NifD) of the molybdenum-iron (MoFe) protein.
    BBa_K2740015CR1 nifKCR1 nifK encodes the subunit beta (NifK) of the molybdenum-iron protein. Together with NifD, they form a α2β2 heterotetramer.
    BBa_K2740016CR1 nifE CR1 nifE encodes the cofactor NifE for the maturation of functional molybdenum-iron protein.
    BBa_K2740017CR1 nifN CR1 nifN encodes the cofactor NifN for the maturation of functional molybdenum-iron protein.
    BBa_K2740018CR1 nifX CR1 nifX encodes nitrogen fixation protein NifX that favors the insertion of molybdenum-iron protein cofactors into nitrogenase.
    BBa_K2740019CR1 HesA CR1 HesA encodes a NAD/FAD-binding protein, HesA, which is involved in molybdopterin and thiamine biosynthesis. It plays an important role in nitrogen fixation and is responsive to the availability of molybdenum.
    BBa_K2740020CR1 nifVCR1 nifV encodes homocitrate synthase NifV that enables the synthesis of the homocitrate moiety of the molybdenum-iron protein cofactor.
