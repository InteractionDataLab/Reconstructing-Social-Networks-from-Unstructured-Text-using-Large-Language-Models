    Mentor GDSYZX and GZHS-United

    Chang Yanqi, team member of SYSU-CHINA, and Li Siqi, the former team member of SYSU-CHINA, were the mentors of team GDSYZX and GZHS- United in this year's competition. Tao Kehan, team member of SYSU-CHINA, also provided help to GDSYZX and GZHS-United on the construction of biological bricks. Team GDSYZX is a high school teams of Guangdong Experimental High School, and team members of GZHS-United are from different high schools in Guangzhou. Sun Yat-sen University and these high schools have had cooperation in students’ education for many years, some of our team members even graduated from these high schools. Invited by their PI professor Jin Honglei, some of our team members became student assistants in the synthetic biology course of Guangzhou Experimental High School, instructing high school students in literature reading and experimental design. Our team members also assisted team GDSYZX and GZHS-United in synthetic biology experimental skills training on weekends. In addition, our team provided technical guidance and reagents for the construction of GZHS-United's biological brick BBa_K2840000 and GDSYZX's biological brick BBa_K2585000.

    Collaboration with SYSU-Software

    Together with SYSU Software，we hold annual Biology Festival on our campus. Our campus was opened to the public in Bio-festival, so it's a good chance for us to popularize synthetic biology knowledge. At the same time, we handed out questionnaires concerning CAR-T therapy. More about the Annual Biology Festival activities can be found in Public Engagement and Human Practice page. Special thanks to Student Union of school of life sciences for their help in the Bio-festival.

    5th CCIC in Shanghai Tech University

    During the summer vacation, our team members, Chang Yanqi and Tan Tianhao and Xu Jiaxian, went to ShanghaiTech University to participate in 5th CCIC(Conference of China iGEMer Community). Xu Jiaxian, one of our team members, took the position as a ‘judge’ in CCIC. He gave comments and advices to other teams. We gave a presentation and showed our poster during CCIC, then we received a feed back saying that more focus should be paid on the safety of U24 and our modeling is very well. Really thank you, Team ShanghaiTech!

    2018 iGEM Southern China Regional Meeting

    In May, our team members, Tang Yuluan and Ruan Shaolin and Xu Feihong, went to Shenzhen for the Southern China Regional Meeting. At that time, most of the teams haven’t made many progresses in experiment. Eight teams from southern China, not far from each other, decided to have a meeting to exchange ideas, experience and funny things. Xu Feihong, one of our team members, lost her mobile phone on the taxi. Friends from Shenzhen University connected the taxi driver and posted the mobile phone to Guangzhou. Really thank you, Team SZU-China!
