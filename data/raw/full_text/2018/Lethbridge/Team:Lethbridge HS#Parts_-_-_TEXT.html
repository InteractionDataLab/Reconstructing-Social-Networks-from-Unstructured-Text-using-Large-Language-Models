This includes usage of parts taken from: the T4 bacteriophage Cup1 from Yeast CutA from E. coli elastin-like polymers (ELPs). The regulators we are using are: an inducible promoter induced by IPTG(BBa_I712074) a medium-strong ribosomal-binding site(BBa_J61100) and a double terminator(BBa_B0014)
