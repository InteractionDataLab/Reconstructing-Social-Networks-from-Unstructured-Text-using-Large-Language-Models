Using controls of BL21 0 mM IPTG and BL21 transformed with Cas 1 and Cas 2 0 mM IPTG. We used different concetration of IPTG of 0 mM, 0.1 mM, 0.5 mM and 1 mM, and inducted for 6 hours taking samples every 30 min for 1 mM IPTG and every hour for the rest of the samples. Determining that the concentration of 0.1 mM has the highest concentration of total proteins. Conclusion In conclusion, the system is capable of inserting sequences inside of CRISPR arrays found in the genome of the bacteria. The SCRIBE system was able to produce a sequence induced by IPTG. Cas complex was capable also to introduce a sequence in the array. This system is expected to be used in other systems. Further investigation for characterization and identification are recommended.
