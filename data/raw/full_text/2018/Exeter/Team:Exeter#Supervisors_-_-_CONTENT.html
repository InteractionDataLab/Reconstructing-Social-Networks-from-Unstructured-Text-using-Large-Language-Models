We would not have gotten this far with our project without our supervisors. They guided us, taught us and, most importantly, showed us how to be independent. At the beginning of the summer no one would dare walk into the lab themselves - towards the end, it was not uncommon to see half the team in and out, like busy ants performing their individual tasks. It's safe to say all team members felt confident doing a protocol themselves, and being able to cope in a laboratory environment without any input. We all owe them our thanks and gratitude for their time, patience and knowledge. They have improved us as a team, individuals and as scientists.

Professor John Love

Role: Chair of Synthetic Biology; Director of the Exeter Microbial Biofuels Group; Director of the BioEconomy Centre
