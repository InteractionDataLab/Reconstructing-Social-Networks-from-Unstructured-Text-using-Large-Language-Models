Benjamin Alva and Ziyuan Wang presented the iGEM UMN 2018 project to current Advanced Placement Biology students of Henry Sibley High School on October 9th. This objective of this presentation was to inform up and coming high school students of the opportunity and novelty that is involved in the work of biotechnology researchers and developers. Utilizing our project to highlight the complexity and wonder of the potential of synthetic biology. We received great feedback from the many students in attendance.
