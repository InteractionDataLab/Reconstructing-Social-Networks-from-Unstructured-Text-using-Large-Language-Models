Team

Hello! We are the 2018 Stony Brook iGEM Team composed of fourteen undergraduate students from a variety of fields of study. Our team is a family, and we work well together both at the lab bench and at Taco Bell! We are excited to show you our work with cyanobacteria, and we can't wait for you to meet our team!

Matthew Mullin

Sophomore Mechanical Engineering As one of the glorious leaders of our team, Matt (aka Elon) always manages to look entirely cool and composed in front of every and any problem we encounter. This is likely due to his lifelong dream of becoming a pirate since pirates need perfect composure out on the sea. However, the ocean is not the only area Matt would love to explore. He also enjoys the more land-based activity of hiking. Worked OnConstruct, Cyanobacteria Group, Wiki Group, Human Practices/Outreach, Protocols, Construct Design Group, Collaborations, Modeling *thumbs up* "Cool"
