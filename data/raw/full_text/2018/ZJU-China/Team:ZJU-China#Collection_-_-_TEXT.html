Meanwhile, we found that SpyTag forms a spontaneous isopeptide bond to SpyCatcher, and SdyTag/SdyCatcher, SnoopTag/SnoopCatcher are the same[1][2][3]. With no cross reaction, three orthogonal tag-catcher pairs link enzymes in order via covalent bond. Design The table of best part collection is shown below.
