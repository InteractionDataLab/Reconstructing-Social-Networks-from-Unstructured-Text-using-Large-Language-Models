Our Lab Rules Always wear safety goggles, powder-free nitrile examination gloves, long pants, closed-toed shoes, and lab coats, before entering the space. Keep hair tied back at all times. Use ethanol to disinfect work table. Please have supervisors in the lab at all times. No drinking or eating in or near the lab space. Dispose of pipette tips in waste bag. Have all petri dishes, containers, erlenmeyer flasks, etc. CLEARLY LABELED in order to maintain an organized environment.
