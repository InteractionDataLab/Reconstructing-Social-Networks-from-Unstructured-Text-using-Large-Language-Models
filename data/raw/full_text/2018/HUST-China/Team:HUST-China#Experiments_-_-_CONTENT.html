    Part3: Whole design

    In this part, we mainly verify our vision by constructing MFC system and we detect the generated voltage. We did several parallel experiments to prove it:

    1. Pre-experiment of electrogenesis.

    MFC anode: Shewanella

    MFC anode: Blank medium

    MFC anode: E.coli

    2. Lactate contrast.

    MFC anode: Shewanella

    MFC anode: Shewanella+lactate

    3. Demonstrate that carbon cloth has higher electricity production efficiency than carbon rod.

    MFC anode: carbon cloth

    MFC anode: carbon rod

    4. Study the effects of oxygen on Co-culture power systems.

    MFC anode: anaerobic

    MFC anode: aerobic

    5. Contrast the symbiotic effect of wild-type strains

    MFC anode: Synechocystis PCC6803 (wild type) + Shewanella

    MFC anode: Rhodopseudomonas palustris (wild type) + Shewanella

    6. Functional verification of engineered Rhodopseudomonas palustris

    MFC anode: Rhodopseudomonas palustris (wild type) + Shewanella

    MFC anode: Rhodopseudomonas palustris(engineered type) + Shewanella

    7. Functional verification of engineered Synechococcus PCC6803

    MFC anode: Synechocystis PCC6803 (wild type) + Shewanella
