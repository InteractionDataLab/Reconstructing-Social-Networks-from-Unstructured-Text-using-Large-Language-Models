While its potential role in gene delivery will need to be continuously investigated, introducing a complex system to improve quality of life needs to be done in a careful way. The public, especially our youth, will need to be aware of what this system will entail to make informed decisions about its use when the opportunity to do so arises. ENGAGING OUR YOUTH We thus created a typing game, Biotyper, that will allow students to explore some of the images depicting cell components and processes while typing through their descriptions. Although the descriptions may be too hard to understand at first, we hope this sparks interest in the younger community to start investigating what the images mean. These images are from the Cell Image Library and students are encouraged to explore what the image entails even further by researching terms that they are unfamiliar with online.
