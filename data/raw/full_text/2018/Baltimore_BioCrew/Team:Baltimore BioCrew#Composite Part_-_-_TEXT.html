These constructs were optimized for use with E.coli, however, we also created three variations of each of these constructs, including a His tag in order to purify the proteins, we also replaced the promoter with a lac promoter for two of the samples (also including the His tag). In addition to our coagulating constructs, we also designed three constructs to reverse the coagulation process by producing tPA and a shortened version of tPA (reptilase). Two of the constructs were designed to produce tPA, with the difference between them being one having a T7 promoter, and the other having a constituent promoter. The construct expressing reptilase used a T7 promoter. However, none of these made it through the cloning process, and as such were not submitted for the registry.
