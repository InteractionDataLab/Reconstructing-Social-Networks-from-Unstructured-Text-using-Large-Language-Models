    How do cells whisper?

    There are many different cells that communicate in our daily life.

    A Secreting cell can send out the message and a Responding cell can receive a message.

    To listen what they are talking, we set a small room for each of them.We called it a "Block".

    Thousand blocks are created for those two cells.And they behave differently due to the diversity of their innate genes.

    After a period of time, some cells who have good communication get a good idea to escape the block.

    They ran out of the block and revealed the secret.Finally, a form of communication is known to us, Wnt signaling.Explore more
