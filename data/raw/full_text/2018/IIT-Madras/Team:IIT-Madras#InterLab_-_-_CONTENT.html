Interlab

    The objective of this year’s study was to reduce lab-to-lab variablity in fluorescence measurements. This was done using two methods: normalising the absorbance to a known concentration of microsphere beads, and counting the colony forming units. I Microsphere Beads Standard Curve II Fluorescein Standard Curve The parts used were: Negative control BBa_R0040 Positive control BBa_I20270 Test Device 1 BBa_J364000 Test Device 2 BBa_J364001 Test Device 3 BBa_J364002 Test Device 4 BBa_J364007 Test Device 5 BBa_J364008 Test Device 6 BBa_J364009 The study was conducted using the 6 parts listed above. The parts were in the plasmid pSB1C3 and were transformed into E. coli DH5a cells. The colonies formed were screened for fluorescence with appropriate biological and technical replicates using a plate reader and a flow cytometer.
