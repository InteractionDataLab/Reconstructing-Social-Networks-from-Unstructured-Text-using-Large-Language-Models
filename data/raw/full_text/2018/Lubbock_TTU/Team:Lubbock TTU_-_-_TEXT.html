Our mission is to expose the university and community to synthetic biology through undergraduate initiative and involvement in the International Genetically Engineered Machine (iGEM) Competition. This year, the iGEM Raiders team is composed of 8 dedicated undergraduate and graduate students with backgrounds in cell & molecular biology, biochemistry, chemistry, mathematics, physics, and chemical engineering.
