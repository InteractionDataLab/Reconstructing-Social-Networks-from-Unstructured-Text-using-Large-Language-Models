Minnesota iGEM The year, the Minnesota iGEM team plans to expand on a previous project and develop a more practical mercury detoxification method using engineered auxotrophic bacteria. As part of this collaboration, 4 members on the UCSC iGEM team, who happened to already be flying out to Minnesota in early August, will meet up with the members of the Minnesota team. As of July 23rd, our group has had skype calls with each other and we have exchanged information regarding our progress and thier current status. We, the UCSC team, noticed that the Minnesota iGEM team has not spent as much time in human practices as we have so we plan to provide them with advice on how to find contacts in local communities globally.
