Our basic part 1 includes FucO as the protein coding region and basic 2 includes GSH gene as the protein coding region. FucO aims to convert furfural to furfuryl alcohol and GSH aims to impart the overexpression of Glutathione for ultimately increasing ethanol yield and lifespan of the ethanologenic E.coli strain KO11. Our Composite part 3 includes both of the coding regions. All of our parts are RFC 10 compatible. FucO is obtained from E.coli K12 and GSH is obtained from Streptococcus Thermophilus.
