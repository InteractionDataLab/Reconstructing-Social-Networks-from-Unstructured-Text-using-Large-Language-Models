Team:Goettingen/Notebook/evolution July Overexpression of proteins in E. coli 13.07.18 Preculture over night at 37°C with agitation has to be prepared. Inoculate 4x 0.5 L LB medium with ampicillin in 2 L shake flasks to an OD600=0.05. At an OD600=0.6 add an inducer, like IPTG. Incubate the cultures for 3 h at 37°C with agitation. Harvest the cells with centrifugation for 10 min at 4°C and 5,000 rpm. Discard the supernatant. Resuspend the pellet in 25 mL buffer W (cold) and transfer the samples into 50 mL falcon tubes. Centrifuge the samples for 10 min at 4°C and 5,000 rpm. Discard the supernatant and store the pellet at -20°C. Cloning of biobricks 2 , 9 , 10 , 12 and overexpression of AroA and AroE 19.07.18 The digest and ligation reactions were performed as described before. Furthermore, a purification of the overexpressed proteins AroE from B. subtilis and AroA from E. coli was performed as decribed in the following paragraph. The AroA strain was cultured in 2 L medium, the AroE strain in 1 L as described before. Harvest the cells with centrifugation at 20,000 rpm for 20 min at 4°C. Resuspend the pellet in 15 mL buffer W (ice-cold!). Disrupt the cells with the French press. Repeat this step 2 times. Centrifuge the samples 30 min at 17,500 rpm and 4°C. Prepare the 10 mL column by BioRad with 1 mL Strep-Tactin Sepharose which equals a column volume of 500 µL. Load the supernatant from the centrifugation on the column. Save the flowthrough and load it again on the column. Wash the column with buffer W: 1. run: 5 mL. 2. run: 1.25 mL. Elute the protein with buffer E. Load ½ of column volume on the column. Save the flowthrough. Elute the protein with buffer E two times. Load the column volume on the column. Save the flowthrough. Load the samples on a SDS protein gel. Mix 15 µL sample and 5 µL Laemmli buffer (5×) and boil it for 10 min at 95°C. Perform a Bradford assay with the elution fractions.
