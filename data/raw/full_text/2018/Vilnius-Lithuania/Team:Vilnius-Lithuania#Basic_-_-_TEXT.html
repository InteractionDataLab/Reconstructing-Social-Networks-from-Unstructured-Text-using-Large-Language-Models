Team:Vilnius-Lithuania/Basic project parts notebook human practices team Basic Parts Separate Soldiers Basic parts, that we created fall into three categories: coding, regulatory and RNA. Coding parts consist of proteins, that assemble into lipid bilayer. Regulatory parts ensure that protein synthesis starts at desired temperature. RNA is used as a modification platform to produce membrane associated ribosomes which help with protein integration into membrane.
