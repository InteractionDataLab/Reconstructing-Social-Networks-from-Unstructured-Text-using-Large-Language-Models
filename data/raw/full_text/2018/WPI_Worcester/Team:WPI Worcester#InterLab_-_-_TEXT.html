We started with a standard of LUDOX absorption and used the data from the four replicates to construct standard curve for absorbance vs. particle number. We then used a serial dilution of fluorescein to construct a standard curve for fluorescence vs. fluorescein concentration. A positive control, a negative control, and six different devices that contain GFP were tested experimentally and the values from the standard curves allowed us to find the fluorescence per particle.
