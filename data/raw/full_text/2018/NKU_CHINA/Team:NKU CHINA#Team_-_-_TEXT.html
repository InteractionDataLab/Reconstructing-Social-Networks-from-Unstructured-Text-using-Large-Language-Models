Dr. Zhihui Cheng received his Ph.D. degree from the Ohio State University USA in 2008. His advisor was Dr. Yasuko Rikihisa, a member of the National Academy of Sciences, USA. He is currently an associate professor in the Department of Microbiology, Nankai University in China. His research focuses are bacterial infection and host defense. He has published more than 20 original research papers in classic journals in the field of microbiology, including MBio, Molecular Microbiology, and Cellular Microbiology.
