    • Functional exosome production system

    Overview

    In this system, we applied ODE（ordinary differential equation）- system to predict the production of the exosome and other target proteins. Also, the interactions among them are inclusive in the time-amount model.

    Assumptions

    1. Production of exosome is mainly associated with booster protein. 2. mRNA and proteins are limited by natural degradation. 3. Amount of proteins stored into exosome is proportional to the size of each exosome, so size of exosome can be ignored. 4. The packaging of protein to exosome and finishing of translation happen simultaneously because the interval is too short to take into account.
