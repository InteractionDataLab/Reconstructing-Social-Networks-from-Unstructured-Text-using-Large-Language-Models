PCR Purification (with QIAquick Kit from Qiagen)

  1. Add 5 volumes of Buffer PB to the sample and pipette up and down to mix.
  2. Transfer the mixture to a spin column and centrifuge for 1 min at 17,900 rcf. Discard flow-through.
  3. Add 750 uL of Buffer PE to the column and centrifuge for 1 min at 17,900 rcf. Discard flow-through.
  4. Centrifuge for another 1 min to remove residual ethanol.
  5. Transfer column to a clean 1.5 mL microcentrifuge tube
  6. Add 30 uL of Elution Buffer onto the center of the column membrane and let sit for 5 min.
  7. Centrifuge for 2 min at 17,900 rcf.
  8. Check the concentration of the sample with Nanodrop.
