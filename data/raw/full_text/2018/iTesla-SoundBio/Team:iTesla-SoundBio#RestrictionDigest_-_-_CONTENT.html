Double Digest with NEB Reagents

Reagent

50 uL rxn

RNase-free Water

Fill to 50 uL

10X NEBuffer*

5 uL

Template DNA

1 ug (1000 ng)

Enzyme 1

1 uL

Enzyme 2

1 uL

 

Incubate at 37 C for 1 hour and heat inactivated** for 20 min.

 

*Which buffer should be used depends on the enzymes and can be determined by the NEBcloner Digest Finder.

**Heat inactivation temperature depends on the enzymes and can be found here.
