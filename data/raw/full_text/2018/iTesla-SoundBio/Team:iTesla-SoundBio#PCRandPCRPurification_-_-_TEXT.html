Transfer the mixture to a spin column and centrifuge for 1 min at 17,900 rcf. Discard flow-through. Add 750 uL of Buffer PE to the column and centrifuge for 1 min at 17,900 rcf. Discard flow-through. Centrifuge for another 1 min to remove residual ethanol. Transfer column to a clean 1.5 mL microcentrifuge tube Add 30 uL of Elution Buffer onto the center of the column membrane and let sit for 5 min. Centrifuge for 2 min at 17,900 rcf. Check the concentration of the sample with Nanodrop.
