Hamm-Lippstadt University of Applied Sciences

Working practice-oriented and in small groups, Hamm-Lippstadt University of Applied Sciences offers high-quality study programs with a clear orientation towards current and future market requirements. The young team of professors is practically experienced. With an interdisciplinary setup, teaching focuses on both the promotion of individual strengths and the imparting of social competences, such as team work.* The abbreviation HSHL stands for Hochschule Hamm-Lippstadt, the University's German title.
