If you think of the sections in your project description as the sections in a publication, you should try to be concise, accurate, and unambiguous in your achievements. References iGEM teams are encouraged to record references you use during the course of your research. They should be posted somewhere on your wiki so that judges and other visitors can see how you thought about your project and what works inspired you.
