NOTE: Phosphate buffer is prepared by adding 4.65g monopotassium phosphate and 25g dipotassium phosphate into 200 mL PCR-grade water. ›Fresh Agar Plate Preparation Materials ~ 24 petri dishes 500 mL media bottle Corresponding amount of 100x antibiotic stock solutions (500 µL) Procedure Obtain and weigh 25g LB Broth powder/L of nanopure water. Obtain and weigh 15g agar powder/L of nanopure water. Mix into respective volume of nanopure water. Autoclave solution for 1 hour (the first setting). Retrieve solution and wait to cool. Add any necessary antibiotics, typically chloramphenicol (Cm) or ampicillin (Amp) stock solution (1000x) with 1-to-1000 dilution. Evenly pour solution into properly labeled petri dishes. Allow solution to then solidify. Keep agar plates in 4 °C refrigerator. ›Plasmid Mini-Prep Materials 1.5 mL eppendorf tubes Thermo Scientific Plasmid Miniprep Kit P1, P2, and P3 buffers Endo-wash buffer Zyppy-wash buffer PCR-grade water Procedure Centrifuge cell culture for 10 min at 4°C at 5,000rpm. Pour out the LB Broth Supernatant into bleach solution. Add 200 μL P1 buffer and resuspend pellet. Transfer into a fresh 1.5 mL eppendorf tube. Add 200 μL P2 buffer, mix by slowly inverting until transparent Get P3 buffer from fridge and add 400 μL. Invert tube 2-4 times and incubate for 1 minute. Centrifuge tube at max speed for 5 minutes. Transfer all the supernatant into the zymo-spin column immediately after. Place the column into a collection tube and centrifuge at 16,000 g for 30 seconds. Discard the flow-through and place the column back into the same collection tube. Add 200 µL of Endo-wash buffer to the column. Centrifuge at 16,000 g for 30 seconds. Add 400 µL of Zyppy-wash buffer to the column. Centrifuge at 16,000 g for 30 seconds. Centrifuge for two minutes at 21,000 g. Transfer the column into a clean 1.5 mL eppendorf tube, then add 30 µL of PCR-grade water directly to the column matrix. Let it stand for one minute at room temperature. Centrifuge at 16,000 g for 1.5 minutes to elute the DNA. Measure 50 mL of TAE Buffer into an Erlenmeyer flask and add in the agarose powder. Mix well Cover the Erlenmeyer flask and microwave for two minutes at power level ten. Let the solution cool for ~10 minutes. Add 4 µL of ethidium bromide and mix well. Pour the solution into a casting tray and let it cool for ~30 minutes. Set up the Sub-Cell apparatus and fill with enough TAE buffer to cover the gel. Carefully remove the comb. Load ~5 µL of the ladder into the leftmost well. Mix the loading dye with the DNA sample to the proper concentration and then load into well(s). Once your samples are loaded place the lid on the apparatus and run the gel at 80 volts for 1 hour. Carefully remove gel to image. ›Gel DNA Recovery Materials Zymoclean™ Gel DNA Recovery Kit ADB Buffer Wash Buffer Hot water bath Eppendorf tubes PCR-grade water Procedure Weigh the piece of cut gel, then add 3 volumes of ADB buffer to each volume of gel. (max volume in tube = 750 µL) Perform short centrifuge Incubate the 1.5 mL eppendorf tube in a 55°C water bath for 5-10 minutes. Use vortex to mix well. Add the melted agarose solution into a Zymo-spin column and place into a collection tube. Centrifuge for 30 seconds at 16,000 g. Empty the collection tube whenever necessary. Add about 100 ng of DNA then continue icing for 20-30 minutes. The added volume should be less than 10% of total volume. Place cells into 42°C water-bath for 30-60 seconds. Put cells back on ice for 2 minutes. Sterilize work area and pipet in this time. Add 1 mL of LB broth to the mixture. Leave the cells at 37°C for one hour in shaker. Meantime, prewarm agar plates. Plate cells using beads (volume varies slightly). Incubate the plate overnight at 37°C. Look for colonies. ›Restriction Enzyme Digest Materials Restriction enzymes (variable) 10x Cutsmart buffer DNA insert/plasmid Procedure Prepare 14 µL of uncut DNA insert or plasmid in PCR tube. Add 2 µL of Cutsmart buffer. Add 2 µL of each desired restriction enzyme (i.e. XbaI and SpeI). Gently mix together. Total reaction volume is 20 µL. Place in incubator for 1½ hours at 37°C. Run gel electrophoresis and perform gel DNA recovery on product. Store in 4°C chamber. ›Subcloning (pSB1C3 vector backbone) Materials T4 Ligase 10x Ligase buffer HF restriction enzymes PCR tubes PCR-grade water Procedure Perform restriction enzyme digest on vector using EcoRI and PstI. Perform restriction enzyme digest on DNA insert using EcoRI and PstI. Recover DNA after gel electrophoresis. Calculate the amount of cut vector and insert needed to have a 1:3 molar ratio. The total volume of reaction should have a DNA concentration near 10 ng/µL. Load the cut vector and insert into PCR tube. Add enough 10x Ligase Buffer so that the buffer is one-tenth the total reaction volume. Add 0.5 µL of T4 DNA Ligase into the PCR tube.
