A particle standard curve, help to convert Abs6​00 measurements to an estimated number of cells. In addition, a fluorescein standard curve that is necessary when comparing fluorescence output of test devices between teams. Cell Measurement: We measure both Abs600and fluorescence for the two colonies, with one colony at 0 hour and another at 6 hours.
