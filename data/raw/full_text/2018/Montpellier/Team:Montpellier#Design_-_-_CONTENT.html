General design

Each of our constructions contained rpsU promoter [8] which is a Lactobacillus jensenii strong promoter. This RpsU sequence also contains the putative sequence for the RBS We added spacers to all of our constructions to unable easier use of the sequence and separation of the different genes of the sequences. We used two Terminators to our sequences :BBa_B0014 & BBa_B0015 to ensure the stopping of the transcription. Our constructions were assembled in the Plem415 vector by Gibson Assembly method.
