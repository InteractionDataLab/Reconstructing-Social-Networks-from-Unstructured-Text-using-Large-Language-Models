Future Plans

The energy source of the Ark is the sun. It can maintain the system through photosynthesis and some BOD in the water, and specifically recover some elements in the water body, which not only solves the problem of water pollution, but also reuses the collected elements. If the application is applied on a large scale, the recovered resources will have a status and quantity that cannot be underestimated in the resource cycle.（see our demonstration） We have cooperated with Huazhong University of Science and Technology this year. They put forward extremely valuable suggestions in the design of the project. Our selection of phosphorus in this year's project is just an attempt to apply the Ark to our local problems. For example, metal-binding proteins, metal-releasing proteins instead of polyphosphate kinases and endo/exo-polyphosphate enzymes, etc., have developed various arks for different pollution situations for long-term treatment and prevention. of.
