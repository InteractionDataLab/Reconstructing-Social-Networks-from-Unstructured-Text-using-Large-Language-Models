Team:Utrecht/AfterJamboree

The iGEM competition 2018 was completed during the giant jamboree in Boston where a year of hard work was rewarded with great results. Team Utrecht did not only won a bronze medal, but was also nominated two times as finalist for special prizes! Utrecht was in the running for the winning project in the environment track, the largest track of the competition, and the website was also nominated for the best website among all undergraduate teams!
