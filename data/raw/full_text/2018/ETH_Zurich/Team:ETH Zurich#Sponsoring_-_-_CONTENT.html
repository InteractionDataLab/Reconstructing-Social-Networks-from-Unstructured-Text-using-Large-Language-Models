We would like to thank all our sponsors for their engagement and enthusiasum for our project. Their support with either monetary or material term, was vital for the success of our project.

Situated in the heart of Europe, yet forging connections all over the world, ETH Zurich is pioneering solutions to the global challenges of today and tomorrow. ETH Zurich is a science, technology, engineering and mathematics university in the city of Zurich, Switzerland. It has more than 19,000 students from over 120 different countries. Twenty-one Nobel Prizes have been awarded to students or professors of the Institute in the past, the most famous of whom was Albert Einstein.
The Department of Biosystems Science and Engineering (D-BSSE) unites biologists, engineers, computer scientists, and mathematicians to work towards a quantitative understanding and purposeful engineering of complex biological. The integration of experiments, theory, and development of novel systems is what sets D-BSSE apart from other departments and makes it an attractive research environment.
Distrelec is one of the biggest electronic stores in Switzerland and offers a wide variety of products. Distrelec’s products and support helped us a lot especially in terms of finding suitable components for our robot. Thank You!
Microsynth is a leading European company in the area of nucleic acid synthesis & analysis. Their main areas of activity are DNA/RNA synthesis and DNA/RNA Analysis and Sequencing. We would like to thank Microsynth for their fast and reliable sequencing and synthesis services!
Dino-Lite is a worldwide leader in high quality USB digital microscope cameras and microscope eyepiece cameras. Over the years, the Dino-Lite series digital handheld microscopes have evolved and nowadays provide a wide range of digital microscopy solutions for all sorts of applications. We would like to thank Dino-Lite for supporting our project!
Thorlabs, Inc. designs, develops, and manufactures photonics and optoelectronics products and systems. In particular Thorlabs strongly supported us by providing components for our various microscopy setups. Thanks a lot for Your support!
Eurofins is also one of the global independent market leaders in certain testing and laboratory services for genomics, discovery pharmacology, forensics, advanced material sciences and for supporting clinical studies.
Amgen is one of the world’s leading biotechnology companies and strives to serve patients by transforming the promise of science and biotechnology into therapies that have the power to restore health or save lives. Thank you for your valuable support!
Sensirion is the world's leading manufacturer of digital microsensors and -systems. Thank you for the support and for the remarkable interest in innovative projects.
We are grateful to the National Centre of Competence in Research for Molecular Systems Engineering for supporting our team of young scientists!
