Glass and plastic must be detergent free for these protocols. The easiest way to do this is to avoid washing glassware with detergent, and simply rinse it out. Autoclaving glassware filled 3/4 with DI water is an effective way to remove most detergent residue. Media and buffers should be prepared in detergent free glassware and cultures grown up in detergent free glassware. Materials CCMB80 buffer SOB Detergent-free, sterile glassware and plasticware Table-top OD600nm spectrophotometer Procedure Prechill 250mL centrifuge tubes and screw cap tubes before use.
