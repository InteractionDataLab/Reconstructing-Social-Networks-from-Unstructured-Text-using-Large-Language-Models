We have successfully assembled the plasmid for the positive control, wild-type HRP, in Escherichia coli and transformed it into Saccharomyces cerevisiae. We have also created a prototype paper-based assay for an HRP-TMB reaction and developed a mathematical model to predict the ideal concentration of protein to load on said assay.
