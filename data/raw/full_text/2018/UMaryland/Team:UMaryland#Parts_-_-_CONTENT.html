    BBa_K2825002
    PcaU Sensor Biosensor for protocatechuic acid (PCA), downstream degradation product of PET degradation. This sensor is optimized for BL21 DE3 E. coli and, when attached to a fluorescent reporter, can differentiate single micromolar PCA concentrations. PcaU itself is an inducible iclr transcription factor.
