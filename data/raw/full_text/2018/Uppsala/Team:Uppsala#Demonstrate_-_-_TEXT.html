Transcriptomics We managed to perform our entire pipeline and sequence the E.coli transcriptome five times. Our result show that transcriptomics with MinION's Nanopore Technology works if a better technique to reduce the amount of RNA in the sample is discovered. Transcriptomics Reporter System We proved that GFP is visible in horse feces and could be used as a reporter. We also integrated a previous BioBrick of the UnaG chromoprotein for possible use as reporter in our diagnostic tool.
