    Mingbo Qu 

    Research interests: biochemistry and molecular biology Research interests include insect biochemistry and molecular biology. Through genomics, transcriptome and proteomics, it is found that the insect is involved in chitin metabolism related proteins. Using the methods of biochemistry and molecular biology, these proteins were used as candidate targets of green pesticides to study their biological functions. The biological pesticides with dsRNA or protein as virulence molecules were developed with functional important proteins as targets.Professor Qu,helped us test the feasibility of the experimental program, provided us with a guided experimental program.
