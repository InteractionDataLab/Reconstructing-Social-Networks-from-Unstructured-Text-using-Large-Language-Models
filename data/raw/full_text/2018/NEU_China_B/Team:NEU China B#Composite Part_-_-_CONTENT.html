We believe that the binding of LLDR to lldPRD operon promoter under the condition of lactic acid deficiency may lead to DNA translation and transcriptional inhibition. The binding of L- lactic acid to LLDR can promote conformation change, thus destroy the DNA cycle and form a transcriptional open complex.
