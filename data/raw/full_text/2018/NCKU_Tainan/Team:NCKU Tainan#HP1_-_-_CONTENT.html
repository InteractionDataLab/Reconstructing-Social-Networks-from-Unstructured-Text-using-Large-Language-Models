Database

In order to save data from our device, we set up a database in Firebase, including carbon dioxide concentration, pH value and temperature obtained by sensors in our device.

App

To make our device more user-friendly, we designed an app to get data from our database. The manager of the factory can easily see the data immediately. Besides, we set up a notification system. As long as users connect their smart phone to the internet, they c`an immediately get the warning notification from the device if the data goes wrong.
