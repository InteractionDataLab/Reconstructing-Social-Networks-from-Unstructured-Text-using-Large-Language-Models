    Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER) Bhopal

    We would like to thank the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research, Bhopal for providing monetary support for travel and related expenses. Also, we appreciate the Department of Biological Sciences for providing UG-Lab for Lab-Space and instrumental requirements.

    Innovation and Incubation Centre for Entrepreneurship (IICE)

    IICE, the innovation cell of IISER-Bhopal, provided us with financial support for iGEM registration, various sections throughout the project such as Outreach, Public Engagement, and iGEM Day. They also financed our attendance at the All-India iGEM Meet at IISc Bangalore and finally for giving us their office space for our countless brainstorming sessions.

    Integrated DNA Technologies

    Integrated DNA Technologies specializes in DNA synthesis, gene construction, and a variety of molecular biology products. We thank IDT for being an official sponsor of the iGEM competition and providing each team with 20 kb of free gene fragments. The ordered g-Block fragments constituted of primers, genes, and even whole plasmids.

    Geneious

    Our team would like to thank Geneious for providing a 6-month student license for their software Geneious 8.0, thus helping us in our in-silico work.

    Snapgene

    The team would like to thank Snapgene for providing us with six annual subscription licenses which have helped us immensely to plan, visualize and document DNA cloning and PCR.

    New England BioLabs

    The team is grateful for NEB sponsorship of the iGEM competition, which has provided teams with the NEBuilder® HiFi DNA Assembly Cloning Kit, Q5 HF PCR Master Mix, Quick-Load® Purple 1kb Plus DNA Ladder and NEB 5-alpha Competent E. coli.
