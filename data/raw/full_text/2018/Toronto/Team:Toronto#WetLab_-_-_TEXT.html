This plasmid is a synthetic hybrid of gas vesicle proteins from wild Aphanizomenon flos-aquae and Bacillus megaterium. We were able to use empirical data from growth curves of our experimental cell lines and use it as a parameter to model our floating cells in the context of a bioreactor computer model.
