    Type

    Composite

    Length

    2430

    This BioBrick functions to increase expression of melanin in fungi. It contains melA, which encodes a tyrosinase involved in the first step of melanins biosynthesis, under control of the strong constitutive fungal promoter PtrpC derived from Aspergillus nidulans. Its terminator is derived from the Cauliflower Mosaic Virus (CaMV).
