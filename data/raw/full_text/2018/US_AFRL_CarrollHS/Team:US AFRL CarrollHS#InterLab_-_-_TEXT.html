We hope that by submitting our data to iGEM, we can help standardize measuring protocols for reproducibility within experiments. In this year’s InterLab, our team transformed E. coli DH5α with different plasmids expressing Green Fluorescent Protein (GFP). A plate reader was then used to measure fluorescence and absorbance values from the transformed strains. Competent Cells Test We performed the competent cells test and submitted the data to iGEM.
