Of these deaths, about 9% were caused by colorectal cancer, making colorectal cancer the third most lethal type of cancer. The problem Our project Using yeast to combat cancer Our project aims to diagnose and treat colorectal cancer through the use of genetically engineered probiotic yeast. Turning our project into math By modeling our project we are able to predict key parts of our project such as the production and efficiency of our anti-cancer agent or how the yeast interacts with the surrounding environment. These outcomes can then be used to improve our project design. Modeling Human practices Our project's role in society Another important part is to integrate our project into society, not only by ensuring a safe and reliable product, but also by reaching out to professionals and to the public to inform them about our project and to collect their opinions.
