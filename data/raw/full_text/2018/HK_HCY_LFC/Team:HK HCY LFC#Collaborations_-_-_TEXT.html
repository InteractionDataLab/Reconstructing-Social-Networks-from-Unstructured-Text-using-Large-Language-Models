Before we decided the title and objective of our project, HKU advisors taught us some basic knowledge of nanotechnology and introduced G-quadruplex. They also introduced a software called Tiamat from Yan Lab Resources, which can be used for designing our own DNA nanostructure. HKU advisors also assisted us a lot in the InterLab study. Through our project, HKU advisors provided constructive comment on the design of the experiments and data analysis of the experimental results.
