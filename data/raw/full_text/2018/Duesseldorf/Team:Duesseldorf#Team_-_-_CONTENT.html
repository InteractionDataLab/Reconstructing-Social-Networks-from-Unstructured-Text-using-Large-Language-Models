    About

    After the huge success of the team "optoptosis" Duesseldorf in 2016, which was continued by the team “artico" Cologne-Duesseldorf In 2017, it was obvious that the iGEM spirit couldn’t be stopped after two years and had to be continued at the Heinrich Heine University of Duesseldorf. This year’s team is a little smaller and includes nine members and three instructors, which are all life science students, who have a big support team consisting of six advisors at hand. Take a look at all the people who built this project! P.S.: To give you a clear picture about all the different personalities on our team, we included PokémonTM to describe each member. We also attached significant questions about pineapple on pizza and heat shock preferences to give you the opportunity to decide which person you like best. Just click on each picture!
