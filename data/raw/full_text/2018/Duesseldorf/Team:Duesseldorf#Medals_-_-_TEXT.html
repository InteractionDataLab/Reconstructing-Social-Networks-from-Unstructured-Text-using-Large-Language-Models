Human Practices One of the most valuable experiences of the competition was sharing our project and acquired knowledge with the public . We highly enjoyed inspired conversations about ethical and societal aspects of synthetic biology, organizing our own lecture series , and starting a worldwide postcard campaign ! Gold Integrated Human Practices In addition to engaging with the public, we expanded our Human Practices by investigating both ethical and scientific issues, opportunities and limitations of our projects. Talking to experts from international laboratories, we were happy to integrate their valuable feedback into our lab work! Improve a previous part or project We are proud to improve an existing part (BBa_C0061) to a codon optimized version (BBa_K2587000). Model your project This year modeling plays a particular role in the iGEM teams´s Düsseldorf project. The aim of our model was to find the perfect ratio of different constituents of the co-culture, such as medium composition, ratio of different organisms or incubation setups. Demonstrate your work We were able to demonstrate the functionality of our concept under real world conditions.
