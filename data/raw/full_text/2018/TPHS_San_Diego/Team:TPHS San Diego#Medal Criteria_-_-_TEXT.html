Convince the judges you have significantly worked with any other registered iGEM team in a meaningful way. Convince the judges you have thought carefully and creatively about whether your work is responsible and good for the world. Gold Expand on your silver medal activity by demonstrating how you have integrated the investigated issues into the purpose, design, and/or execution of your project. Convince the judges that your project's design and/or implementation is based on insight you have gained from modeling.
