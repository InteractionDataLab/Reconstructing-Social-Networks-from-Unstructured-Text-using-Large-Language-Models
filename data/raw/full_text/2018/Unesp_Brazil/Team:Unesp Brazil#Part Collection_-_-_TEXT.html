BBa_K2660001: T7 promoter driving the production of GFP-histag with penetratin. Biocontainment BBa_K2660006: N-Cas9: N-terminal sequence of the split Cas9 nuclease. BBa_K2660007: C-Cas9: C-terminal sequence of the split Cas9 nuclease. BBa_K2660008: nMag: Negative Magnet, a engineered protein to dimerize with pMag when exposed to blue light. BBa_K2660009: pMag: Positive Magnet, a engineered protein to dimerize with nMag when exposed to blue light. We hope that this collection will be useful to future teams interested in engineering probiotics and building new living therapeutics.
