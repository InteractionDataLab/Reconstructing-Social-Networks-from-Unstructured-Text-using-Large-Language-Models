Team

There was an idea, to bring together a group of remarkable students, to see if we could become something more. Seventeen students from information science, material science and life science academy responded to the call.As a paragon model of interdisciplinary integration, each member participates as an essential part in our team, the spark of inspiration glittered along the way.
