Therefore, with the help of modeling, we were able to select a suitable NFBL to meet our project goal. Meanwhile, we studied the transcriptional layer and used Hill function as the best description for gene transcription. Initially, our data indicated that orthogonal ribosome was efficient to prevent resource competition. Through modeling, we found that orthogonal ribosome had no negative influence on the fidelity of our expression system before and after adding the o-ribosome. After the proof-of-concept, we decided to add the o-ribosome into our expression system. Furthermore, we designed an method to optimize our NFBL circuit, and made a software. Some optimized parameters in our model still need to be tested by experiments in the future. Looking forward, our initial model could be adapted and applied to other projects that aim to fast response and fidelity control.
