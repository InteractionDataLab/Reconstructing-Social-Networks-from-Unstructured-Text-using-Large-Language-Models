Similarly, in designing synthetically engineered genetic circuits, we try to reduce the impact of complex intracellular environment on gene circuits, especially in mammalian cells. Therefore, we present our design to solve the problem of NOISE in mammalian cells and wider applications of synthetic biology on them hoping to make further contribution to HUMAN HEALTH . We, the iGEM team of NAU, inspired by MOSFETs , simulate the function of a MOSFET with designed genetic circuits in mammalian cells. On top of that, we also carry out simulations with MODELS of the gene circuits.
