With the 2019 iGEM season in full swing, we are constantly seeking funding to keep iGEM Guelph operating, and hope to establish ourselves in a sustainable way in the near future. The more funding we have, the more broad and rigorous our research can be, and the larger our contribution to global food sustainability and science in general we can make! In addition to the cost of registering a team ($4,500), we still have to pay for the science itself. Furthermore, the cost of sending each participant to the iGEM Giant Jamboree is $1,000 each, and we need to send at least a few representatives in order to share our research effectively! All told, an annual budget of approximately $15,000 is the minimum we would need to operate effectively. To that end, we kindly ask for any financial or resource assistance you can provide.
