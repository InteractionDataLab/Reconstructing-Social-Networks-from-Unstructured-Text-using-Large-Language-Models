BBa_K52684001 PelB-CotA PelB signal peptide linking with CotA to transfer CotA out of the bacteria. BBa_K52684002 PhoA-CotA PhoA signal peptide linking with CotA to transfer CotA out of the bacteria. BBa_K52684004 SpyCatcher-sfGFP SfGFP fused to the SpyCatcher domain can be covalently attached onto the biofilm displaying SpyTag.This part is used to test our immobilization strategy of CotA. BBa_K52684005 SpyCatcher-CotA CotA fused to the SpyCatcher domain can be covalently attached onto the biofilm displaying SpyTag. BBa_K52684006 CsgA-SpyTag CsgA is a major subunit of the biofilm of E. coli(1)(2). A SpyTag is fused to CsgA so that CotA laccase can be fixed on biofilm through SpyTag-SpyCatcher chemistry.
