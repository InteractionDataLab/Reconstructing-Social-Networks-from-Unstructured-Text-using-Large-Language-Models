The reactions starts from the catalytic of the enzyme and the addition of water. We first assumed that our conditions may be different as the literature, so we decided to confirm the feasibility of the reaction through Gibbs free energy calculation. (Calculation method using Spartan 16) (1) From monomeric alcohol to carrying free radicals Figure 1: (Left) Three resonance forms(Wang, Y. et al. 2013), (Right) Reaction diagram (Vanholme, R. et al. 2010) Figure 2: Monolignol as the free energy benchmark (1 hartree ≈ 2625.5 kJ/mole) In Figure 2. , we found that the free energy of this process is positive, which means it's not spontaneous. Thus, we think the way to make the reactions spontaneous is to use enzymes such as laccase and peroxidase.
