Composite Parts

Our composite part is the mTurquoise (mTQ) expression construct (BBa_K2722004). This part is composed of five subparts. These five are a constitutive promoter (BBa_J23106), a self-cleaving ribozyme (BBa_K2722006), a Ribosome Binding Site (BBa_B0034), an mTurquoise coding sequence (BBa_K2722003) and a terminator (BBa_K2722007). Full characterisiation of this part is documenten on Improved Parts.
