Influenza, as the most infectious disease in modern world, has posed great threat to the general public, especially to underprivileged regions with poor hygiene standard and inadequate primary healthcare protection. The existing quick-response test for influenza virus are still far away from being a popular diagnostic test for its unsatisfying accuracy and great expenses. In the light of this, it is to our belief that there is increasing demand and dire need for a RELIABLE and AFFORDABLE influenza quick-response test.
