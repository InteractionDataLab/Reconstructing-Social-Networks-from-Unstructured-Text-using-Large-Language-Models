Step 1: Starting Sample Preparation Measure the OD600 of the cell cultures, making sure to dilute to the linear detection range of the plate reader,e.g. to 0.05 – 0.5 OD600 range. For each Starting Sample (total for all 12 showed in italics in paraenthesis): dilute each starting sample until dilution 5 , and aseptically spread plate 100 μL on LB + Cam plates for Dilutions 3, 4, and 5. Incubate at 37°C overnight and count colonies after 18-20 hours of growth.
