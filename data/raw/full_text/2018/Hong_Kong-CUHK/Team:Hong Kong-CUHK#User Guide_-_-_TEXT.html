Using this tool kit along with our software, the general public would be able to detect if they are infected with influenza or not. Preparation: RAPID diagnostic tool kit (With Tracer and 6 probes inside); Mobile phone; Hot water(95-90 degree Celsius) Step 1: Add your nasal fluid to the probe and soak the probe in hot water about 95-90 degree Celsius for 5 minutes. Step 2: Dry off the probe and put it in room temperature for 30-45 minutes, avoid light. Step 3: Place it inside Tracer; switch Tracer on. Step 4: Take a picture at the marked position using your mobile phone. Step 5: Upload the picture along with additional information to our program. Step 6: Check your result!
