We used a Gal-promoter for the production of the receptors, therefore we used Galactose as carbon source Dilute the overnights to OD600 0.6 and let the cells grow until OD600 0.8-1. 90 ul of the cell culture in 96-well microtiterplate and 10ul of the chemical analyte (desired concentration in 10% Ethanol or 1% DMSO) shake for 20s and incubate by 30ºC during 2.5hours add 100ul of 10mM Luciferin in a pH of 3 until 5 (diluted in citrat buffer or 1xPBS) Shake the plate 20s and measure the luminescence within 20seconds.
