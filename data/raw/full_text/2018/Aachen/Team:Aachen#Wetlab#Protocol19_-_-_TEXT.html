Solution: mix 1,3196 mg LiOAC, 0,67 mg SDS and 100 µl ddH2O incubate 10 min at 75 ℃. add 300 µL 100 % EtOH, vortex. centrifuge 3 min at max. rpm, discard supernatant. resuspend pellet in 150 uL 70 % EtOH (→ for 70% EtOH mix 105 ul of 100%-EtOH with 45 ul H2O). centrifuge 3 min at max. rpm, discard supernatant. dry pellet at 37 ℃ (place tube upside down). add 50 uL ddH2O to the dry pellet and vortex shortly. centrifuge 1 min at max. rpm, use 2 uL supernatant for PCR.
