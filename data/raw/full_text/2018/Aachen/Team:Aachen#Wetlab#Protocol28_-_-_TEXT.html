Procedure: Dissolve the reagents in 900 ml deionized water. If you are making plates, add the agar after dissolving the reagents above. Autoclave at 15 psi, 121 ℃ for 20 minutes. Cool to 50 ℃ and add 100 ml of filter-sterilized 20% glucose or 200 ml of filter-sterilized 10% raffinose. Pour plates and allow to harden. Invert the plates and store at 4 ℃.
