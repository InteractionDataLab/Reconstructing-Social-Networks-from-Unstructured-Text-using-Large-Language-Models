Instead, we have uploaded all of our 12 plasmids, with the associated relevant characterization, as well as the individual devices (constitutive LuxR, regulated TraI, and so on). As a lot of the basic parts already existed on the Registry, we have chosen to work with and upload modified versions of them, taken from the literature.
