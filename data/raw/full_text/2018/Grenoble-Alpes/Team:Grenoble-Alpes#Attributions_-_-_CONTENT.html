    iGEM GRENOBLE-ALPES STORY

    1) How did we know iGEM ?

    We have never had lessons on iGEM but each member of the team has heard about the competition at Grenoble Alpes University (UGA) or Grenoble INP school, by the members of the previous team and the teachers who took them framed. Then, the team met last November via Pierre Cavailles (the main advisor and a biotechnology teacher at the UGA), and that's how the story started.
