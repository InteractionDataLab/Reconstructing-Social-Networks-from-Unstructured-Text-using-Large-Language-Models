And we constructed a promising food-safe grade chassis microorganism Lactococcus lactis NZ3900/pNZ8149-msmK-cspD2, which can provide more solutions to the acid and cold stress problems in fermentation industry. Besides, we also obtained two patents in the industrial application of Lactic acid bacteria.
