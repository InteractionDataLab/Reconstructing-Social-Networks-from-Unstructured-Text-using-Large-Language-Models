    Parts

    Part #NameTypeDescription
    BBa_K2540011Universal bacterial expression resource (UBER)basic

    Universal Bacterial Expression Resource (UBER) is a system containing T7 RNA Polymerase (T7 RNAP) which can be used for the expression of genes under T7 promoter. UBER includes T7 RNAP and TetR repressor which provides negative feedback on the polymerase levels to help reduce toxicity. The part was modified from Kushwaha & Salis.1

    BBa_K2540010 BBa_K2540002 BBa_K2540004BBa_K2540003BBa_K2540001 BBa_K2540012BBa_K2540006BBa_K2540007BBa_K2540009 Broad host range regulatory elementsbasic

    Broad host range regulatory elements consist of a constitutive promoter and a ribosome binding site which can be used for gene expression across different bacterial species. The part sequences were obtained from Johns et al.2

    BBa_K2540015Orthogonal ribosome binding sitebasic

    This orthogonal RBS was designed using RBS calculator for orthogonal 16S rRNA predicted to function across a variety of bacterial strains. Prediction was done by algorithm written by our team based on previous work by Chubiz & Rao.3
