In order to let the gene can be expressed in endophyte, we bought a shuttle vector from addgene and replace the ori and also the resistance gene, which can also be seen in the basic part. Basic Parts BBa_K2546001: This part is coding for 2-haloacid dehalogenase, which can cleavage carbon-halogen bonds of halogenated compounds BBa_K2546004: This part is a shuttle vector with red fluorescence protein (RFP). It is composed by the Ori of pMP4657 and BBa_J364007 in pSB1C3. BBa_K2546007: This part is a shuttle vector with red fluorescence protein (RFP). It is composed by the Ori of pMP4657 and BBa_J364007 in pSB1C3. BBa_K2546008: This part is a shuttle vector with green fluorescence protein (GFP). It is composed by the Ori of pBBR1 and BBa_J364007 in pSB1C3. BBa_K2546010: This part is coding for TonB-dependent receptor, which can actively transport the ferric ion carrier, siderophore, which is similar in structure with dioxin. BBa_K2546011: This part is coding for Laccase, which is related to lignin degradation. Intermediate Parts BBa_K2546002: This part is the combination of BBa_K2546001 and BBa_K2546011 which contain ribosome binding site. Composite Parts BBa_K2546005: This part is the promoter circuit of BBa_K2546002. BBa_K2546006: This part is the combination of BBa_K2546012 and BBa_K2546002 ,which is our ultimate goal to let three gene be expressed in one circuit. BBa_K2546012: This part is the promoter circuit of BBa_K2546010.
