Dioxin Bioremediation The results of the recombinant bacteria growth test, show that the three genes code for the proteins HAD, TonB, Laccase functions. In our hypothesis, HAD catalyze dehalogenation degrade TCDD, so the HAD construct grew better in TCDD medium as expect. TonB construct grew worse in high concentration TCDD, just as we suppose that TonB intakes TCDD into the cell. The growth of strain TLcc declined in high TCDD concentration.
