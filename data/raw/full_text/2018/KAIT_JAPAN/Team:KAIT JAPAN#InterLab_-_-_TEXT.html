Reducing lab-to-lab variability in fluorescence measurements by normalizing to absolute cell count or colony-forming units (CFUs) instead of OD using two orthogonal approaches. 2. Converting between absorbance of cells to absorbance of a known concentration of beads and CFUs from the sample. Protocol We followed the protocol given.
