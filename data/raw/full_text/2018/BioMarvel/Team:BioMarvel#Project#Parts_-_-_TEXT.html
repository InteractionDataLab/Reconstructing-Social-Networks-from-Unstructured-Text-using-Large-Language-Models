J23119 is the "consensus" promoter sequence and the strongest member of the family. These promoter parts can be used to tune the expression level of constitutively expressed parts. RBS (B0034, Ribosome Binding Site) RBS based on Elowitz repressilator. Designed by: Vinay S Mahajan, Voichita D. Marinescu, Brian Chow, Alexander D Wissner-Gross and Peter Carr IAP, 2003.
