With UESTC-China

University of Electronic Science and Technology of China held a lecture to our team and give us a total introduction on iGEM. They helped us to know about iGEM and explained how to set up teams and how to register the competition in detail. This year, the university established their 2018 iGEM team, UESTC-China. Since then, we have been guided by them how to fulfill the criteria and giving instruction to our team when deciding on our project.
