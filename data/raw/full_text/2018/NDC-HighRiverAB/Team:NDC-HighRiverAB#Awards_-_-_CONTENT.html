Awards

OLS Canmore Jamboree 2018

Our team presented at the Canmore Jamboree hosted by OLS_Canmore_Canada iGEM team during June 2018. We won the 'Most Engaging Presentation' Award and received valuable feedback on our project from both the judges and members of other synthetic biology teams

Top row, left to right: Emily Marasco (Judge), Micha Bermas, Emily Dudgeon, Vivian Leadbeater, Mya George, Emily Hagens, and Patrick Wu (Judge)
Bottom row, left to right: Kristina Cabungcal, Daisy Hagens, Miguel LePatourel, and Sandi Boga (Judge)

aGEM 2018

Recently, our team presented at aGEM 2018, which was hosted by the University of Calgary's iGEM team. We tied with the OLS_Canmore_Canada for the 'Best Value Proposition' Award. This event was a great opportunity for our team to practice presenting before the iGEM 2018 competition, and learn more about other Albertan iGEM team's projects.

Top row, left to right: Mrs. Côté, Mya George, Kristina Cabungcal, Todd Kuiken (Judge), Julie Legault (Judge), Daisy Hagens, Terry Johnson (Judge), Patrick Wu (Judge), Emily Hagens, Sophia Fraser, Justin Pahara (Judge), and Ms. Larocque.
Bottom row, left to right: Emily Dudgeon, Miguel LePatourel, and Micha Bermas

iGEM 2018

Our team qualified for a silver medal at the iGEM Jamboree, which was our main goal as this was our school's first time competing at iGEM.
