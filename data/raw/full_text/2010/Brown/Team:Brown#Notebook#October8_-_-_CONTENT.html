    • Friday, October 8, 2010

    Miniprep of LacI-RFC25 in pSB1C3

    Overnight cultures of LacI-RFC25-pSB1C3 in LB+Chlor were recovered and miniprepped. 5 of the 6 overnights were miniprepped in parallel

    Key: Ligation/digest 1,2 -> overnight 1.1,1.2,1.3,2.1,2.2,2.3 -> miniprep 1.1,1.3,2.1,2.2,2.3

    Nanodrop results:

    • 1.1 - 68.8ng/ul
    • 1.3 - 88.1ng/ul
    • 2.1 - 148.3ng/ul
    • 2.2 - 111.3ng/ul
    • 2.3 - 67.7ng/ul

    Notes - Made 2 glycerol stocks of LacI-RFC25-pSB1C3 XL1B and placed in -80.

    Overnight Cultures

    Went back and looked at the 10 μl transformation of Tat-L/SB1C3 from 10/4; 3 colonies of moderate size turned up.

    • Colonies still visible after 3 days at room temperature.
    • Picked overnights, using 1.5 mL of LB and 12 μg/mL chloramphenicol.
    • Also included overnight control of random bacteria to test chloramphenicol efficacy; started incubation at 1:00 PM.
    • Took 20μl Tat-L/SB1C3 transformation (10/5) and placed in 37°C gain to see if colonies similarly emerge.

    Checked plates in incubator. No growth on Kan+ Bistable. No growth on PLacMnt/J06702. Maybe PLac/Mnt is not in the vector at all?

    • Re-eluted Bistable part from filter paper (all)
    • Transformed CI/TerTer ligation into XL1 Amp+ (at 6:20 PM)
    • Transformed Bistable (in DH5α) at 7:20 PM
    • Overnight culture of 3 pTetR+RBS+LacI+terter colonies (7:20 PM)

    Miniprep

    Miniprepped Chlor+ overnights

    • Jen: 8 total. Little AraC 1-4, AraC B 1-4.
    • Lily: J13002 LacI in pSB1C.

    All eluted in 40μl Buffer EB.
