    • Friday, August 13, 2010

    Gel extractions from yesterday's gel

    Gel extracted the following using Qiagen kit:

    • CI - 17.3ng/ul
    • Mnt - 15ng/ul
    • AraC - 14.2ng/ul
    • RFC25_AraC (little tube) - 9.5ng/ul

    Gel of AraC-RFC25 re-PCR

    17ul DNA, 3ul loading buffer

    Gel product was extracted in 30ul EB. Final concentration: 14.4ng/ul

    Ligations of CI PCR, Mnt PCR, AraC PCR into pGEM

    1:3 vector to insert ratio. 15ul total volume:

    • 7.5ul ligase buffer (2x)
    • 1ul T4 DNA ligase
    • 0.5ul vector (pGEM-T-easy)
    • 5.5ul DNA
    • 0.5ul dH2O?? (was not clear from paper-based notebook)

    Control ligation

    15ul total volume:

    • 7.5ul ligase buffer (2x)
    • 1ul T4 DNA ligase
    • 0.5ul vector (pGEM-T-easy)
    • 1.5ul DNA
    • 4.5ul dH2O

    Digest of Lovtwo miniprep w/ EcoRI and BglII

    50ul total volume:

    • 5ul NEB buffer 2
    • 2ul DNA
    • 0.5ul 100x BSA
    • 1ul EcoRI OR BglII
    • 41.5ul dH2O

    Gel of products

    • Ladder - 14ul
    • Lovtwo-EcoRI - 13ul
    • Lovtwo-BglII - 12ul
    • Lovtwo-miniprep - 9ul

    PCR of Lovtwo wt into Lovtap2 (Lovtap2 has Bgl sites?)

    50ul total volume:

    • 2ul fwd primer
    • 2ul rev primer
    • 1ul template (lovTwo wt miniprep at 192.5ng/ul)
    • 20ul dH2O
    • 25ul master mix
