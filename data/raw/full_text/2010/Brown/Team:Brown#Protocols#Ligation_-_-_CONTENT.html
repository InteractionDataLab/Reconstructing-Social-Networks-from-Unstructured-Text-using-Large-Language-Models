    • Ligation

    • 7.5 µl 2X DNA ligase buffer (rapid)
    • 1 µl T4 DNA ligase
    • 0.5 µl vector
    • 1.5 µl insert
    • 4.5 µl dH2O

    15 µl total volume

    24-36 hours at 4°C. Alternatively 1 to 2 hours at room temperature may also work, although we found a generally lower ligation efficiency using this method.

    Note: Generally aim for a 1:3 vector to insert ratio.
