    • Thursday, June 24 2010

    Plated both immotile strains (DFB22, DFB 201) and three inducibly motile strains (pDB28, pDB101, pDB102) all in null media, amicillin + IPTG, and chloramphenicol + arabinose for a total of 6 x 3 = 18 plates and stabs (including stabs).

    The expected results are:

    Strain null plate amp+IPTG Chlor+arab
    DFB 22immotile growthno growthno growth
    DFB 201immotile growthno growthno growth
    DFB 22 w/ pDB28immotile growthmotile growthno growth
    DFB 201 w/ pDB101immotile growthno growthmotile growth
    DFB 201 w/ pDB102immotile growthmotile growthno growth

    MRS Plates

    • 250 mL Null plates
    • 250 mL Chloramphenicol plates at 15 µg/mL
