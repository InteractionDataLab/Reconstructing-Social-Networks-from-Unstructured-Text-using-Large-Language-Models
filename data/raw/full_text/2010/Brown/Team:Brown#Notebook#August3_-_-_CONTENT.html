    • Tuesday, August 3, 2010

    Results of overnight plates

    J04421 transformation grew extremely high concentration of colonies, we will redo with much less DNA.

    • Calculations showed that we used 3ul @ 162ng/ul: way too much DNA.

    AraC reporter showed no growth, We predict it was because of a failure in the ligation from 7/29.

    Retransformation of LacI reporter (J04421)

    • Dilute the J04421 miniprep 1:100 (so that DNA is 1.6ng/ul).
    • Add 5mL dilution: ~8ng DNA

    Gel of yesterday's PCR

    Ran a gel of the PCR product from yesterday. Isolated a band that (hopefully) corresponds to the amplification of the LovTAP w/ BglBrick primers. We will gel-extract the DNA tomorrow.
