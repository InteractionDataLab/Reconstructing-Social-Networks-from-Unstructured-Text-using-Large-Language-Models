    Digest and ligation of LacI into pSB1C3

    LacI (linear, blunt ends) will be double digested with PstI and EcoRI and inserted into pSB1C3.

    Digest

    50ul total volume

    • 25ul dH2O
    • 5ul 10x buffer H
    • 2.5ul EcoRI
    • 2.5ul PstI
    • 15ul DNA

    Ran 2 tubes in parallel. the dH2O used for the first tube had a plastic-y smell, so the tube was discarded and this was noted in case of later error

    Cleanup

    Followed Qiagen PCR cleanup protocol

    Ligation

    30ul total volume

    • 15ul 2x rapid buffer
    • 2ul ligase (T4)
    • 1ul vector
    • 10ul insert (LacI)
    • 2ul H2O

    Digest AraC (2 Tubes)

    50 μl total 25 μl H2O 5 μl 10x buffer H 2.5 μl EcoRI 25 μl Pst1 15 μl DNA (AraC-RFC25)

    37°C at 21:36 out at 22:36 PCR purification, eluted in 30μl

    Ligation 30 μl total 15 μl buffer 2x 2 μl ligase 2 μl vector (psB1C3) 11 μl insert (AraC)

    in 4°C at 23:13

    Nanodrop Results LacI - 147 ng/μl

    Transformed Tat-L+pSB1C3 into XL1B (From 7/16)

    • Used 10μl on a Chlor+ plate from 6/21
    • No colonies seen after overnight at 37°C
