    • Tuesday, August 10, 2010

    Miniprep of LovtapTwo WT overnight cultures

    7 tubes run in parallel. Nanodrop concentrations:

    1. 249.2ng/ul
    2. 208.1ng/ul
    3. 192.5ng/ul
    4. 280.1ng/ul
    5. 261.3ng/ul
    6. 250.2ng/ul
    7. 211.6ng/ul

    Digest of Lovtwo-Bgl with EcoRI

    Ran a 1% agarose gel. Notice the colllapsed wells (did not significantly affect results).

    Interpretation - ligation failed. Will re-ligate and re-transform using Blue-white selection.

    Re-ligation of LovTwo into pGEM

    1:3 vector:insert ratio. 15ul total volume:

    • 7.5ul ligase buffer (2x)
    • 1ul T4 ligase
    • 0.5ul vector DNA
    • 1.5ul insert DNA
    • 4.5ul dH2O

    Transformation of BL21 with LacI reporter construct (J04421)

    We used BL21 because CFP expression will be more visible in a high protein expression strain. We used 800pg of DNA (0.5ul of 1.6ng/ul miniprep dilution).
