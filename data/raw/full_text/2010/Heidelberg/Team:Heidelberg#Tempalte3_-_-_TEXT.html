We engineered a toolkit for standardized measurements of interactions between artificial miRNAs and their binding sites. Thus, the expression level of any gene of choice could be arbitrarily adjusted by employing the corresponding binding site design. To produce tissue specific miRNA gene shuttles, we developed an evolution-based method for synthesis of new adeno associated viruses. In the future, miBricks could open the doors to new Synthetic Biology based medical approaches.     Retrieved from " http://2010.igem.org/Team:Heidelberg/Tempalte3 " Recent changes What links here Related changes Special pages My preferences Printable version Permanent link Privacy policy Disclaimers
