PCR amplification of K343005 with dobbelt terminator

Date: 8/10 Done by: Marie Methods: PCR Protocols: CP1.1[1] Notes: 8 colonies were selected of colony 63 form colony PCR. VF2 and VR was used as primers in the PFU PCR PCR Program:

Start94  C2 min
Denaturing94 C1 min
Annealing55 C1 min
Elongation72 C2:30 min
Goto2rep29x
End72 C5 min
Hold4 C
Afterwards a gel was run.
