Primers

On this page we list all the primers we designed and used during the summer. All primers were kindly provided by DNA Technology. In the following, coding sequences are colored blue, restriction sites are colored green and nucleotides that are both are shown in orange. The mutation sites in the FlhDC mutation primers are shown in purple.

FlhDC mutation rv*5'-CATAAGCTGCGGGCAAAGCTGCCAA-3'61.0
