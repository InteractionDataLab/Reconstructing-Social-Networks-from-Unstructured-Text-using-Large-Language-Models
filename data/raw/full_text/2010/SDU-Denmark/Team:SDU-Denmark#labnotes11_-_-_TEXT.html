N and J from the coloni PCR was selected and miniprep'ed according to protocol. Sample 1 of each colni was eluted in 20uL elution buffer, and sample 2 of each coloni was eluted according to protocol. To ensure thet the plasimids contained the correct insert, the miniprep'ed plasmids were digested with EcoRI and PstI. Restriction mixture: 12uL H2O 2uL FD green buffer 1uL EcoRI 1uL PstI 5uL Plasmid from either col. N or J The samples are digested for 10 min. at 37C, and samples are loaded onto a 1% agarose gel. Undigested plasmid is used as controle and gene ruler DNA ladder mix is used as marker. Loading scheme: Lanes Sample 1 marker 2 col. J  3 col J (uncut) 4 col N 5 col N (uncut) Results: Analysis: Two bands are observed en the lanes of the digestes samples representing undigested plasmid and insert.
