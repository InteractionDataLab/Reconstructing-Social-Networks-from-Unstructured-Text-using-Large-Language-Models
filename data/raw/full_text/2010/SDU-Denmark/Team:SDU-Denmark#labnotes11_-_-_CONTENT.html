    Miniprep of PS (composit part) in pSB3T5

    Date: 9/22 2010Done By: Lc and MariaProtocol:MP1.2Notes: col. N and J from the coloni PCR was selected and miniprep'ed according to protocol. Sample 1 of each colni was eluted in 20uL elution buffer, and sample 2 of each coloni was eluted according to protocol. To ensure thet the plasimids contained the correct insert, the miniprep'ed plasmids were digested with EcoRI and PstI. Restriction mixture: 12uL H2O 2uL FD green buffer 1uL EcoRI 1uL PstI 5uL Plasmid from either col. N or J

    The samples are digested for 10 min. at 37C, and samples are loaded onto a 1% agarose gel. Undigested plasmid is used as controle and gene ruler DNA ladder mix is used as marker. Loading scheme:

    LanesSample
    1marker
    2col. J 
    3col J (uncut)
    4col N
    5col N (uncut)

    Results:Analysis: Two bands are observed en the lanes of the digestes samples representing undigested plasmid and insert. Miniprep samples are sent for sequencing and is stored at -20C. --Tipi 11:58, 30 September 2010 (UTC)
