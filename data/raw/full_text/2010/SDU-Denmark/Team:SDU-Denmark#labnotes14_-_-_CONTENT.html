    Restriction digest on K343002 and PSB1C3

    Date: 12/10 Done by: Tommy Methods: Restriction digest Protocols: RD1.3[2] Notes: EcoRI and SpeI were used, the producted was purifided on a gel nanodrop: PSB1C3: 41,6 K343002: 64,5

    Ligation of K343002 into PSB1C3

    Date: 12/10 Done by: Marie & Christian Methods: Ligation Protocols: LG1.1[3] Notes: For each of the ligations three ligation mixtures were prepared. vector concentrations of 41,6ng/uL (PSB1C3) was used for mixture. Appropiate amount of insert was added to reach vector:insert ratios of 1:1, 1:2 and 1:3 respectively. Ligation mixtures (B0015 in pSB3C5):

    L1L2L3
    T4 ligase buffer2uL2uL2uL
    T4 ligase1uL1uL1uL
    pSB1C31uL1uL1uL
    K343002 0.65uL1.30uL1.96uL
    H2034.35uL33,70uL33.04uL

    The samples was incubated at 17C ON at used for transformation

    Transformations of K343002 in PSB1C3 into top10 E. Coli

    Date: 12/10 Done by: Tommy Methods: Ligation Protocols: LG1.1[4] Notes: 16 colonies and RFP control was selected from the colonie PCR. All of the colonies contain K343002 in PSB1C3 VF2 and VR primers was used PCR Program:

    Start94  C2 min
    Denaturing94 C1 min
    Annealing55 C1 min
    Elongation72 C2:30 min
    Goto2rep29x
    End72 C3 min
    Hold4 C

    Coloni PCR on transformations of K343002 in PSB1C3

    Date: 30/09 Done by: Tommy Methods: PCR Protocols: CP1.3[5] Notes: Because the first coloni PCR of the transformants didn't give us the the right pieces 32 new colonies where chosen: 1-16: ninaB in PSB1C3

    VF2 and VR used as primers in the TAQ PCR:

    PCR Program:

    Start94  C2 min
    Denaturing94 C1 min
    Annealing55 C1 min
    Elongation72 C2:30 min
    Goto2rep29x
    End72 C2:30 min
    Hold4 C
    Colonies 1, 2, 3 and 4 was selected for minipreps and freezing cultures.
