Mathilde Guzzo Student in the fifth year at the INSA Lyon, I am interested in Research and Development in industry. Chassis building, now called Synthetic Biology, is an important tool for the future which has to be controlled. Therefore for me, this project is both a personal and scientific improvement that gathers team work and scientific approach.
