Measure of temperature and shaking speed influence
  • Introduce 5 mL of half LB in a sterile tube
  • Resuspend bacteria in 0,5 mL of sterile water. Vortex
  • Add 10 µL of the mixture in the LB tube. Vortex
  • Incubate 24 h in a waterbath at the temperature and the shaking speed wished
  • Measure of the fluorescence : input wavelenght 490 nm, output one 510 nm.
  • Dilute 250 µL of culture in 750 µL of water. Measure the biomass (absorbance 600 nm)
