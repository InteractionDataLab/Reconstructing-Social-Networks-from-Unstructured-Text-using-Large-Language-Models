Results

The data we compiled showed a very high-level of similarity between the FAS I. They all contain conserved domains :

  1. β Ketoacyl-ACP Synthase (2.3.1.41),
  2. Malonyl/Acetyl transferase (2.3.1.38/39),
  3. 3 Hydroxyacyl-ACP Dehydrase (4.2.1.61),
  4. Enoyl Reductase (1.3.1.10),
  5. β Ketoacyl-ACP Reductase ( 1.1.1.100),
  6. Thioesterase (3.1.2).
