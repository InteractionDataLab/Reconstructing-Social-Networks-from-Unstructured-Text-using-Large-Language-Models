Grow the cells on a shaker at 37°C until they reach an OD at 600 nm of 0.4 (about 108 bacteria/mL) It is possible to inoculate directly in 100 mL until they reach an OD at 0,4. Transfer the culture in 40 mL falcon tube. Chill the cells on ice for 10 min. From now on always work on ice Spin the cells down at 5000 rpm for 10 min at 4°C Remove the supernatant using a sterile pipet Resuspend each tube with 1/2 volume of sterile and cold CaCl2 100 mM (25 mL).
