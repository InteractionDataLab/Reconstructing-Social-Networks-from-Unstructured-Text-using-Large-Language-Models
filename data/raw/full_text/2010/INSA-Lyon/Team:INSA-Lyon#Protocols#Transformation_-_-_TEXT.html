Add 1 µL of DNA (can be adjust with the DNA concentration, for example transform the entire ligation product) to 200 µl of competent cells. Mix carefully by moving the tip around. Incubate cells on ice for 30 min. Heat-shock cells for 2 min in a 42°C water bath. Incubate cells on ice for 5 min. Add 0.8 ml of room temperature LB. Incubate 1h at 37°C.
