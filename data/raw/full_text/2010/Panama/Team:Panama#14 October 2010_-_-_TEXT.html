Then, we ran a gel of 1% agarose to see if we could see a difference in the size of the linear band of the plasmid to be able to determine if it contains the insert (Rh1AB) or not. alt text alt text Miniprep Protocol: For this procedure we used what is called a "dirty miniprep". And by dirty we don't mean that we did it with unclean utensils nor did we do it after coming from a soccer game; it means that the reagents were prepared in the lab and they weren't obtained from a commercial kit.
