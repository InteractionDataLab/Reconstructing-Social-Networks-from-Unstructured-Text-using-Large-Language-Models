We verified the presence of fecI and PfecA parts in colonies by colony PCR reactions and 1.5% agarose gel electrophoresis analysis. In other way, to obtain fecA* part we digested by restriction enzymes the amplified by PCR part again, ligated it and transformed competent bacteria which remained overnight at 37ºC in LB+Km plate.
