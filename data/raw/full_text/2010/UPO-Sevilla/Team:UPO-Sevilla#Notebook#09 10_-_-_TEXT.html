Templates: we diluted the high concentrated fragment (1/10) and used 2ul instead of 1ul of the low concentrated one. 0’8% gel electrophoresis analysis showed a line of 1Kb, which is the length of fecR. There was also a 0’2Kb line. It looked like that the new conditions carried us to the goal. We had to isolate from gel the new part: WE GOT FECR! Assembly Team We realized a new ligation of the failed biobricks and digested not available biobricks (2+6) and vectors (pSB1C3 and pSB1T3). Assay Team We developed different assays by changing different features; the plates were incubated overnight.
