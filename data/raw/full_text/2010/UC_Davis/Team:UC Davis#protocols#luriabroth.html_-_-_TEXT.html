Mix until dissolved. Check to see if the pH is 7.0 using a pH meter. If needed, add 5N NaOH to adjust the pH to 7.0. Fill solution to 1L with deionized water. Aliquot into smaller glass bottles. (Optional) Autoclave. Purpose For culturing desired microbes.
