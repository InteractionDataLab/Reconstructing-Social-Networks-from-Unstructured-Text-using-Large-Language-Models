Mix until dissolved using a stir bar. Check to see if the pH is 7.0 using a pH meter. If needed, add 5N NaOH to adjust the pH to 7.0. Fill solution to 1L with deionized water. Autoclave. Let it cool, but not cool enough to let it solidify. It is recommended to keep it in 45 degree waterbath. Add the appropriate amount of antibiotic of your choice. Recommended amounts are posted on openwetware.org . Mix antibiotic. Pour into sterilized plates. Purpose Nutrient agar plates are used to grow and select for desired microbes.
