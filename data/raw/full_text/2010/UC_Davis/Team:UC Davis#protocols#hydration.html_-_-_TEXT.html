Procedure Find the plate and well with the desired DNA. Use a pipette tip to open well. Add 10μL of milliQ water into the well. Wait 10 minutes. Place 10μL into microcentrifuge tube. This will be the stock DNA In a fresh new microcentrifuge tube, create a 50% dilution by aliquotting 3μL of stock DNA and 3μL milliQ water. Keep stock (undiluted) DNA at -80°C. Keep diluted DNA at -20°C. Purpose To extract DNA from registry distribution plates and keeping stocks of them.
