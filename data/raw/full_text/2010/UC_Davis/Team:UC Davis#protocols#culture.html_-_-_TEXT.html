Pour 4 mL of luria broth into each polypropylene tube. Pipette the required amount of antibiotic resistance into each tube. Recommended amounts can be looked up on openwetware.org . Use a small pipette tip to lightly touch one isolated colony, then place into the respected tube. Close tubes and then incubate at 37°C for approximately 12 to 16 hours. Purpose To grow the desired microbe in slightly larger quantities.
