23.07.2010 Friday Note: We are not totally satisfied with the results, because of the performance of the cells during the flow cytometry examination, they where adhering to each other.
Previous 2 days: Set up and culturing Hela cell culture [Kamil]
Conducted according to the protocol included in "Quantitative measurement of mamallian cell invasion by bacteria using flow cytometry" standard (RFC 59 standard point 5)
Previous day: Set up liquid cultures of E.coli with different constructs [Ania P.]
Conducted according to the protocol included in "Quantitative measurement of mamallian cell invasion by bacteria using flow cytometry" standard (RFC 59 standard point 5)
Testing the invasivnes of E.coli in Hela cells [Kamil,Cherry]
1. Incubation of Hela cells with E.coli with different constructs. 2. Preparation of samples with incubated cells and examining them in flow cytometer. Conducted according to the protocol included in "Quantitative measurement of mamallian cell invasion by bacteria using flow cytometry" standard, but without using lysozyme (RFC 59 standard points 6 and 7)
