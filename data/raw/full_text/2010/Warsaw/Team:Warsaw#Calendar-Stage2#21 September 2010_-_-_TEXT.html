Cultures were grown for 6 h in 37 oC. Plasmids were purified with Plasmid Mini kit (A&A) biotechnology. The obtained sample, as well as the initial (unmutated) plasmid, were then digested with EcoRI and PstI for 1 h and run on agarose gel. M - size marker; 32a,b,c - plasmids harboring MinC under weak RBS; 34a,b,c - plasmids harboring MinC under strong RBS Samples 32a,b&c ans well as 34b&c appear to contain a correct construct. Smaller amounts of DNA with B0034-containing constructs may indicate a slight leaking of the construct (probably due to a read-through from the resistance gene).
