Protocol Prepping DNA from the kit plates With a pipette tip, punch a hole through the foil cover into the corresponding well to the Biobrick^(TM)-standard part that you want. Make sure you have properly oriented the plate. We recommend that you do not remove the foil cover, as it could lead to cross contamination between the wells. Add 15uL of TE (MilliQ),and pipetting Pipette 1uL of the resuspended DNA Transformation into your desired competent cells. Hold on ice for 30 mins. Heat shock at 42°C for 45 seconds. Add 300uL of LBborth in each epp. Wait 10 mins. Hold at 37℃ for 30 mins. Plate out.
