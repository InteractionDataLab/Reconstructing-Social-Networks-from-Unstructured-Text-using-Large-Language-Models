Agarose Gel

Preperation

  • ・Agarose
  • ・TAE buffer
  • ・MilliQ

Procedure

To make 1% agarose gel, make 200 ml （3 big gels and 6 small gels）

  • 2g　Agarose
  • 2ml　50 x TAE buffer
  • 200ml　MilliQ
  • 1. Melt by microwave oven
  • 2. When the solution is cooled enough to touch for 4 seconds, pour the gel to the mold
