Materials 1000x stock antibiotic solution LB-agar plates, clean Glass beads, sterile Procedure Warm plate to 37°C. Add 50μL of 1000x antibiotic to the surface of the agar. Distribute antibiotic evenly using glass beads. Dispose of beads and allow plates to dry in biohood or near flame.
