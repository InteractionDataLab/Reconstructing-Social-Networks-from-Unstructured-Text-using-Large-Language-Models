Do not let EC cells warm to above ice-cold. Add 1μL of ligation product to the 50μL aliquot. Don't forget an aliquot for positive control. Transfer DNA/cell mixture to a cuvette and pulse at 2.5V. Rescue the cells immediately by adding 0.75mL warm SOC to the cuvette. Ensure that the time constant is above 3.0 for each. Incubate for 1 hour at 37°C. Plate the entire mixture on an LB-agar plate with the appropriate antibiotic resistance, grow overnight.
