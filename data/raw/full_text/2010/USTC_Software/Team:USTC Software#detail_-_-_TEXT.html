Only LacI tetramer binds with pLacI gene and thus repressing expression of its downstream genes. TetR protein only forms dimer. Only tetR dimer binds with pTet gene and thus repressing expression of its downstream genes. One IPTG molecule binds with one LacI tetramer to form complex IPTG:LacI4. cIlam protein only forms dimer. Only cIlam dimer binds with pcIlam gene and thus repressing expression of its downstream genes.
