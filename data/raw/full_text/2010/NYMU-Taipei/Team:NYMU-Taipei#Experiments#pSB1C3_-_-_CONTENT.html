Preparing pSB1C3

2010-09-04

  • Autoclaved 500ml of LB-Agar (1.2%) and left it in the 56oC incubator overnight

2010-09-05

  • Added chloramphenicol resistance @50mg/ml to the LB-agar and made two stacks of Chr-resistance plates (1:00-1:30pm).
  • Transformed BBa_J04450 on a pSB1C3 plasmid (2009 kit 1 well 5A) onto a Chr-resistant plate (4:15-5:00pm -- waited extra long at the ice step).
  • 15uL ddddH20 to get the plasmid out from the biobrick plate.
  • used 2uL of plasmid in 20uL DH5α competent cell for transformation.
  • also transformed 2uL of E0240 (on pSB1A2 (amp resistance)) on a chr-resistant plate as a negative control.

2010-09-06

  • Plate to 4oC
  • 3-in-1 of the BBa_J04450 plate.

2010-09-07

  • Plasmid extraction of BBa_J04450 then digested with (XP)
  • Gel extraction of the pSB1C3 plasmid.
