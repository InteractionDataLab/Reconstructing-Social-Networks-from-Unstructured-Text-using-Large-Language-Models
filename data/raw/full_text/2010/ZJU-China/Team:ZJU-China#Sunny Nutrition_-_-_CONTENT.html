Brief Introduction

    The new century witness the vigorous development of Chinese enterprises, and also the prosperity of Sunny Nutrition Technology Co.,Ltd. Sunny Nutrition Technology Co.,Ltd. is a state-level high-tech enterprise specializing in the field of feed additives and animal health products. Established in 1993,Sunny has grown into a leading high-tech company integrating research, manufacturing, sales and services.

    Located in the picturesque city Hangzhou, Sunny has two production bases separately know as Sel-Biochem Co.,Ltd and Zhejiang Huzhou Sunny Feed Additive Manufacturing Co.,ltd,and Zhejiang Qianjiang Bio-Technology CO.,Ltd, which is sales and marketing company, Covering an total area of 80,000 square meters, the company has four feed additive production lines and fermentation capacity of 2000 cubic meters, with a team of 185 technicians and professionals. Key products we produced include Tiamulin, Bambermycin, Pellet Binder, Phytase, Cysteamine, etc, which enjoy great market share both at home and abroad.

    This is the journey we continue today, encourage innovation, produce quality products, promote effective management model and development strategy, and bring the advanced new technology to Chinese feed industry with the principle of Quality First, Good Faith and Humanistic Management. With the great aim cherished in our hearts, all Sunny members are stepping forward steadily and sturdily. Looking back on the 17(1993-2010) years Sunny has experienced, we are proud of what we have achieved and what we have contributed. With Sunny's brilliant presence in the stage of feed industry, we know we have more responsibility to shoulder, for the prosperity of national industry and for the benefits of mankind. We know we have a long way to go. All this we will do. And all this we can do.
