Fluorescence-based enzyme assay measures the rate at which fluorescence in the testing media is released and the amount fluorescence depends on the rate at which fluorophore-quencher linkage is disrupted. Our substrate PDGA contains a linked fluorophore-quencher component. The faster fluorophore-quencher component is cleaved, the higher the amount of fluorescence is released and thus the greater the enzymatic activity observed. Run one transpeptidase reaction and one hydrolysis reaction per enzyme (always run control for each set) Prepare 10x Master Mix with these concentrations. Pipette 10uL of 50mM L-Glu into each transpeptidation reaction well.
