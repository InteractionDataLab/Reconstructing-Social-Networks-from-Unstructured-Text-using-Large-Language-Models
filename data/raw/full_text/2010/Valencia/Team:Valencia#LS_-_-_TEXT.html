This will make the signal useful to real time adquisition and ionterpretation. A photodiode can behave in tho very diferent ways, depending on the conection configuration: photoconductive or photovoltaic mode. We are interested in the first one, because it make posible a precise and stable relationship between the energy of the incident radiation and the output current.
