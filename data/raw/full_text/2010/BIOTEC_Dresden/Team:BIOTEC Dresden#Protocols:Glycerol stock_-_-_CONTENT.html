    • Materials

    • 40% sterile Glycerol stock
    • 1mL overnight culture

    Procedure

    • Chill electroporation cuvettes, DNA samples and tubes on ice.
    • To an empty 2.0mL microfuge tube, add 1mL 40% Glycerol
    • add 1mL of your overnight sample
    • gently vortex to mix
    • store in a freezer box at -80°C degrees
