Prepare a Master Mix: into a 1.5mL microfuge tube, add the following per 50 μL reaction: 38μL ultra pure water 10μL 5X HF Buffer 1μL 10mM dNTP 0.25μL forward primer 0.25μL reverse primer 0.5 μL Phusion Pipet 1μL (usually) of your DNA template into a sterile 0.2mL PCR strip or plate Note: also prepare one or two extra reactions to allow for pipetting errors. Vortex Mastermix and pulse spin down Pipet 50μL of your Mastermix into PCR tube Pulse spin PCR reactions using the PCR strip centrifuge (in Anni’s lab; front bench) Set up PCR machine according to need (denaturation temperature, elongation time).
