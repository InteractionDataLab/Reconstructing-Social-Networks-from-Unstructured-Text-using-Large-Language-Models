Prepare a Master Mix: into a 1.5mL microfuge tube, add the following per 50 μL reaction: 37.5μL ultra pure water 5,5μL 10X Taq PCR Buffer 1,1μL 10mM dNTP 0.25μL forward primer 0.25μL reverse primer 0.5 μL Taq Polymerase Note: also prepare one or two extra reactions to allow for pipetting errors. Pipet 1μL (usually) of your DNA template into a sterile 0.2mL PCR strip or plate Vortex Mastermix and pulse spin down Pipet 50μL of your Mastermix into PCR tube Pulse spin PCR reactions using the PCR strip centrifuge (in Anni’s lab; front bench) Set up PCR machine according to need (denaturation temperature, elongation time).
