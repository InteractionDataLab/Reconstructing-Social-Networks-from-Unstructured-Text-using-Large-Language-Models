This allowed us to introduce synthetic biology in a pleasant, but constructive way to a broad public. To measure the educational impact of our movie, we created a survey and showed participants either our movie or a "neutral" one as a negative control. Our overall aim therefore is to make the public more aware of the benefits that synthetic biology can bring to society by introducing its concepts in amusing, but nevertheless instructive fashion.
