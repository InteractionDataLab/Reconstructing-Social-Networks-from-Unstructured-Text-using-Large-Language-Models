Lab Diaries We have ensured that everything we do in the lab is available for the rest of the team, as well as anyone else, to see. Transparency is a really important aspect of synthetic biology, and we'd love it if other lab teams would do the same. Keeping up-to-date lab diaries was also a really easy way of keeping track of where each team is, especially for our advisors!
