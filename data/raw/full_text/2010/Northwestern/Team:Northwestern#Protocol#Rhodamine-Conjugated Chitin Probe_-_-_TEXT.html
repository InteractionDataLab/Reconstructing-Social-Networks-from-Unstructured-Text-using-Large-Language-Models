Soak a paper towel with PBS and place it at the bottom of the petri dish. Use 2 toothpicks to support the slide above the paper towel. This prevents any wicking action from occurring and pulling the stain off the slide. Wash 3 times with 1X TBS (submerge in 1X TBS for 5 minutes per wash) Place a cover slide over sample.
