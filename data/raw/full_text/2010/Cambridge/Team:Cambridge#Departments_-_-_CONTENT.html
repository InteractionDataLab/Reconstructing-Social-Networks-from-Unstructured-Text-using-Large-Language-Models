    We would like to thank the following departments of the University of Cambridge for their generous support with money and material, facilites and advice.

    Department of Plant Sciences

    For letting us use the spacious Teaching Lab over the summer and enduring our abuse of expensive lab equipment. Thank you for providing lots of advice on techniques and helping us to balance imagination and feasibility.

    Special thanks to: Jim Haseloff, James Brown, Fernan Federici, PJ Steiner and Barbara Landamore.

    Department of Genetics

    For advice on scientific background and smooth logistics.

    Special thanks to: Duncan Rowe, Gos Micklem and David Summers

    Department of Pathology

    For providing us with a new home after the start of term and practical help.

    Special thanks to: Jim Ajioka and Shuna Gould

    Royal College of Art

    For tips and ideas on Human Practises and design.

    Special thanks to: Daisy Ginsberg

    Further Thanks to:

    Department of Physiology, Development and Neuroscience

    Department of Biochemistry

    Department of Engineering

    School of Technology

    School of Biological Sciences

    James Slock
