Our Firefly genes were kindly optimised by DNA 2.0 using their advanced in-house algorithms, this should result in improved expression in E. coli since there is a significant gap between codon usage in E. coli and fireflies. Every single BioBrick we submitted based on genes from Photinus pyralis and Luciola cruciata is codon optimised.
