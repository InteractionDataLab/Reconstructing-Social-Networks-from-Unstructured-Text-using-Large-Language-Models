    Colony PCR

    Materials

    • 2x Phusion Mastermic
    • Primers
    • Nuclease free H2O
    • Cell culture!

    Method

    • Pick a single colony and place in 20µL of H20.
    • Cell lysis:
    • Incubate 10 min @ 98°C
    • Freeze 10 min @ -80°C
    • Vortex for 2-5 min
  • In a PCR tube mix:
  • 10µL of 2X Phusion Mastermix
  • 1µL of DNA template (lysate
  • XµL of Primer 1
  • XµL of Primer 2
  • 7µL of Nuclease free H2O
  • Run PCR:
  • Initial Denaturation: 30s @ 98°C
  • Denaturation: 10s @ 98°C
  • Annealing: 30s @ Y°C
  • Elongation: 30s per kb @ 72°C
  • Reapeat steps 2-4, 25 to 35 times
  • Final Elongation: 10 min @ 72°C
  • Final Hold: ∞ @ 4°C

    • Notes

    • For Biobrick primers (VF2 and VR) from James Brown we used the following values:
    • X = 1µL
    • Y = 65°C
