    Experiments

    1. Test luminescence with the Luciferase from the registry

    • Transform WT E.coli with the luciferase from the registry.
    • Feed with D-luciferin, develop a method for measuring light output
    • Variables - concentration of inducer (if luciferase promoter inducible), concentration of D-luciferin
    • Controls - no luciferin, no inducer

    2. Test use of CHBT as a substrate

    With sucessfully transformed cells from 1 compare emission when adding:

    • D-luciferin
    • CHBT
    • CHBT + D-Cysteine
    • CHBT + L-Cysteine
    • (CHBT + L-Luciferin? if necessary)
