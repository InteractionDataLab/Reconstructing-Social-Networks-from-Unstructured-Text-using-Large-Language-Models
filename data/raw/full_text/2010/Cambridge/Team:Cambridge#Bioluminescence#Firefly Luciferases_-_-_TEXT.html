We were aware that a luciferase from this organism was already present in the registry ( BBa_I712019 ). We wanted to improve on this by three techniques: Codon optimisation for expression in E. coli to increase the rate of translation Using a mutant with increased substrate affinity Parallel use of the Photinus pyralis luciferin regenerating enzyme to both relieve inhibition by oxyluciferin and increase availability of luciferin.
