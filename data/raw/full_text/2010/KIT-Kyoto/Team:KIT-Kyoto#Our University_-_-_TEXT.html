KIT has ten departments covering three academic fields such as Materials and life science, Engineering design and Design science. We got interested in the poster presented in iGEM by Tokyo Institute of Technology at summer school of biological science held in Japan. And decided to participate in iGEM 2010. To make our iGEM team KIT-Kyoto, We got contact with some iGEM team in Japan and collected many information of iGEM 2010. Philosophy in making our team is a combination of biology and design. Therefore we have 2 students from design engineering department and ○ students from applied biology department in our iGEM team.
