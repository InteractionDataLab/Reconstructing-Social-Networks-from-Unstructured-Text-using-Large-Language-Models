Exression experiment Peter & David For this experiment, the following B. subtilis 168 strains were used: All cultures were grown overnight at 37 degrees Celsius in a shaker room. Overnight cultures were used to dilute to a B. subtilis culture of 0,1 OD, these strains were divided into ‘’induced’’ and ‘’non-induced’’. Induction with 0,5% subtilin was done at a OD of 0,5 (approximately 2,5 hours after growth of the 0,1 culture started). After that the OD of the cultures was measured every .. hours. Sample preperation After .. hours, .. after induction, the samples were collected and processed.
