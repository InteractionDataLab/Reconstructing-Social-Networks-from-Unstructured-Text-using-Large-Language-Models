David & Peter Extraction of Streptomyces cellwalls & purification of chaplin proteins Harvesting of chaplin coated mycelium of Streptomyces with a razorblade. The mycelium was disrupted to extract the cell walls ( ExtractionCellwallsGR ) of Streptomyces. After extraction the freeze dried cellwalls consist for approx 5% of their dry weight of assembled chaplin proteins. Further purification and monomerisation of the chaplin proteins is achieved by TFA treatment ( TFA treatmentGR ) of extracted cellwalls. TFA treated chaplins are monomeric and can be rediluted in demiwater or trisbuffer. Note that after redulution the monomeric chaplins will immediately start to reassemble in amyloid fibres.
