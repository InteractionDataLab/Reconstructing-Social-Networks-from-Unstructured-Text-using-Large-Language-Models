Biobricks

Here is our registry of our biobricks: <groupparts>iGEM010 Groningen</groupparts>

Summary:

Below is given a short description of all the biobricks in our registry.

Chaplins

<partinfo>BBa_K305001</partinfo> Hydrophobic protein chaplin C (chpC)

Strongly hydrophobic protein originating from Streptomyces coelicolor and codon optimized for Bacillus subtilis.

<partinfo>BBa_K305002</partinfo> chaplin C (chpDC) with modified sortase binding motive

Strongly hydrophobic protein originating from Streptomyces coelicolor and codon optimized for Bacillus subtilis.

Modified C-terminal Sortase(SrtA from S. aureus.) recognition site.

<partinfo>BBa_K305003</partinfo> Hydrophobic protein chaplin E (chpE)

Strongly hydrophobic protein originating from Streptomyces coelicolor and codon optimized for Bacillus subtilis.

<partinfo>BBa_K305004</partinfo> Hydrophobic protein chaplin H (chpH)

Strongly hydrophobic protein originating from Streptomyces coelicolor and codon optimized for Bacillus subtilis.

<partinfo>BBa_K305005</partinfo> Sortase srtA

Staphylococcus aureus sortase, an enzyme that anchors surface proteins to the cell wall

Promotors

<partinfo>BBa_K305006</partinfo> yqxMpr

yqxM promotor effective in biofilm formation, regulates tasA production

<partinfo>BBa_K305007</partinfo> srfApr 1

Promotor for the surfactin regulatory gene.

<partinfo>BBa_K305008</partinfo> srfApr 2

Promotor for the surfactin regulatory gene.

<partinfo>BBa_K305009</partinfo> srfApr1>GFP

Promotor for the surfactin regulatory gene with GFP.

<partinfo>BBa_K305010</partinfo> srfApr2>GFP

Promotor for the surfactin regulatory gene with GFP.

Plasmid Backbone

<partinfo>BBa_K305011</partinfo> Subtilin inducible expression plasmid for Bacillus subtilis

Adapted from the system for subtilin-regulated gene expression (SURE) in Bacillus subtilis [1], this plasmid was constructed to express proteins present in RCF 10 BioBricks upon subtilin induction.
