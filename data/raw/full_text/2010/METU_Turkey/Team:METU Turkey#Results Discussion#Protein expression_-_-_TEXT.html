Alternative Check List Procedure - Inoculate single colony for 5 hrs at 37 C in 10 mL LB medium containing antibiotic - Inoculate 4 ml pre-cultured cells into 400 ml of TB medium containing antibiotic in a 2-liter cultivation flask - Culture it for 5 hrs at 37 C (OD600 to 0.5 - 0.6) with a rotary shaker at 180 rpm - Add 1 mM IPTG in a final concentration ( 0.5- 2.0 mM recomended for pT7) - Continue the cultivation for 3-4 h at 37 C (15 hrs at 22 C.) - Harvest the cells with centrifuge.
