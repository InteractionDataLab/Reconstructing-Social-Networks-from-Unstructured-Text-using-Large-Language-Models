Team:METU Turkey/Results Discussion/Alkaline Phosphatasen

From 2010.igem.org

MATERIALS ________________________________________

- Heat block - Spin

- Linear DNA - 10X reaction buffer for AP used in reaction - FastAP™ Thermosensitive Alkaline Phosphatase - Nuclease-free water

- P10 micropipette

CHECK-LIST PROCEDURE ________________________________________

- Prepare the following reaction mixture: Linear DNA (~3 kb plasmid) 1 μg (~1 pmol termini) 10X reaction buffer for AP used in reaction 2 μl FastAP™ Thermosensitive Alkaline Phosphatase 1 μl (1 u) Water, nuclease-free (#R0581) to 20 μl Total volume 20 μl - Mix thoroughly, spin briefly and incubate 10 min at 37°C. - Stop reaction by heating for 5 min at 75°C.

NOTES ________________________________________

- For efficient dephosphorylation plasmid DNA should be free of RNA and genomic DNA. - This protocol is suitable for removal of 3’ and 5’ -phosphate groups from DNA and RNA.
