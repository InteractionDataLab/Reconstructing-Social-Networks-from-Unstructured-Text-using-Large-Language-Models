Poor plates. Discussion: This experiment failed at some point as noted by the lack of bands after running a gel to isolate the 9A plasmid backbone. The successful banding patterns by Kristin’s PUC plasmid indicate that the error occurred in either the restriction digest or during the purification process. It is most likely that the problem was in the restriction digest. The enzymes used were fast-digest enzymes and were not inactivated after the 5 min incubation. They were placed at room temperature for approximately an hour before being purified. It is believed that the enzymes continued to digest the DNA to completion. Future attempts should require a quicker transition time from the digest, an inactivation step, and potential postponing of the purification step until after the ligation has been performed.
