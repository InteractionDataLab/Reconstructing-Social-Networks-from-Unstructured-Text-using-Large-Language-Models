Module I, Group 2c To decrease the entropy of the reaction system, we decide to try a new ligation condition-1/10 system, which means all reacgents in the system is 10 percentage of normal quanlity and the total volume of reaction system is 10ul. After purification, ligation reaction took place in a mixed system as following. eGFP 0.5ul mCherry 0.8ul Kan 0.3ul T4 ligase(NEB) 0.5ul T4 ligation Buffer 1ul H2O 6.4ul Negative control T4 ligase(NEB) 0.5ul T4 ligation Buffer 1ul H2O 8ul Incubate at 16°C. After 8h, add 0.5ul digested and dephophorylated PSB1Ca3 into reaction system. Continue to incubate at 16°C overnight.
