    The Biobricks Foundation: RFC

    Investigators: Chris W., Bea, Kira, Hanna, Julian, Anna, Jessica (Tobias,Sven, Kristian)

    The BioBricks Foundation is dedicated to promoting and protecting open development, sharing, and reuse of BioBrick™ standard biological parts. Homepage: [[1]]

    Two important standards:

    BBF RFC-10: This standard defines the required sequence properties for a Biobrick(tm) standard biological part. [[2]]

    BBF RFC-25: Fusion Protein (Freiburg) Biobrick assembly standard This Request for Comments (RFC) describes an extension to the original BioBrick assembly standard (BBF RFC 10). Media:Freiburg10 BBF RFC 25.pdf
