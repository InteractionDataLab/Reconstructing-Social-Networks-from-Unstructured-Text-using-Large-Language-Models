Add one-tenth volume of 3 M NaOAc, ph 5.2, to the DNA solution to precipitated. 2. Add 2.5 volumes of cold 100% ethanol. 3. Place in -80°C freezer for 30 minutes of in an ethanol/dry ice bath for 5 minutes. 4. Spin at 15,000 rpm in microcentrifuge for 30 minutes at 4°C. 5. Rinse with cold 70% ethanol. 6. Spin at 15,000 rpm in microcentrifuge for 10 minutes at 4°C. 7. Remove supernatant, taking care of pellet, and dry fro 1 hour at 37°C. 8. Resuspend in appropriate volume of buffer.
