Used each to inoculate a liquid culture, streak an index plate, and run a colony PCR reaction (after appropriate lysing in water). Each PCR reaction mixture contained 2 uL of VF2 primer, 2 of VR primer, 9.8 uL of water, 4 uL of Phusion buffer, 0.6 uL of DMSO, 0.4 uL of dNTPs, and 0.2 uL of polymerase. They were run on the 'VR' thermocycler protocol The PCR products were then run on a 1.0% agarose gel at 90 V vs. a 1 kb ladder. Loaded ladder at the far left and samples 1 through 14 from left to right in order. Only sample 6 is even close the size range of the desired ligation product (8.3 kb) and even it is still at least a kb too small to be the right construct.
