IGEA- The International Genetically Engineered Art Competition iGEM began in January of 2003 with a month-long course during MIT's Independent Activities Period (IAP).We hope our International Genetically Engineered Art Competition will spur artists and designers to engage with the same intensity as engineers. The first IGEA was held at Srishti and was organized by our advisors James, Daisy and Yashas.
