The potential bio-security risk, however, is just around corner.     Gene horizontal transfer, for example, is worth special attention. This summer, we did a thorough research on this topic, aiming at proactively finding out the current situation in China, a beginner in synthetic biology, and raised the awareness of researchers on such issues.                                                                  learn more Gallery     It's an unforgettable summer, during which we spared no effort to fight for our dreams and the honour of Peking iGEM team.
