    Part:BBa_K379000

    AHL induced concentration Band detect, Part Type Composite, Length 6457bp

    This component detects the concentration of AHL and activates the reporter protein GFP when the concentration is in the correct band. If the concentration of AHL is either higher or lower than the band specific to this component, the reporter is not activated.
