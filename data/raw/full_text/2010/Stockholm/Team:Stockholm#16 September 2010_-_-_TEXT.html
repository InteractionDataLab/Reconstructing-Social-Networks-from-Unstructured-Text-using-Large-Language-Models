N-TAT and N-Tra10 not verified, since gel was run too far, but plasmid linearization should indicate successful digestion. Most samples show somewhat incomplete digestion. For digestions with FastDigest enzymes, this may be an indication of old/inactive enzymes. Especially PstI should be analyzed for activity.
