    over expression

    • Start culture
    • 10ml LBAMP + 100µl old culture (8:30)
    • Measure OD=0.6 (11:30)
    • At OD=0.6 add IPTG 1mM
    • Take samples after:
    • 0h
    • 2h
    • take 500µl, spinn down and remove LB
    • resuspend in 50µl SDS buffer
    • Heat in 95°C, 5min
    • Freeze
    • Re-heat in 95°C, 5min
    • Run gel
