    Gel verification

    Colony PCR gel verification of pSB1C3.MITF, pSB1C3.SOD*His/.His*SOD and pEX.SOD*His/.His*SOD.4 μl λ; 5 μl sample.λ = O'GeneRuler 1 kb DNA ladder.

    1 % agarose, 90 V, 50 min

    Expected bands

    • MITF: 1586 bp
    • pC.SH/pC.HS: 815 bp
    • pEX.SH/pEX.HS: 702 bp

    Results All MITF samples resulted in ≈300-350 bp bands, which corresponds to re-ligated pSB1C3 vector (316 bp). This is strange, since re-ligation should not be possible. All other samples correspond very well to expected sizes. pC.SH3, pC.HS1, pEX.SH1 & pEX.HS1 were selected for plasmid prep and glycerol stocks.
