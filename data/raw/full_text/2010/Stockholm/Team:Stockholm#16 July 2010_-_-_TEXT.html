Cells grown on LB agar + 25 μg/ml Cm. Colony PCR verification of pSB1x3 constructs from 13/7 colonies pSBX verification primers VF2 and VR arrived. Decided to verify clones from 13/7 by colony PCR. Colony PCR From 13/7 colonies, pSB1x3 Picked new colonies from LB agar plates. Since the plates had now been left a few days in 4°C, the white (positive) clones were easier to discern from the red, as the red colonies now had a deeper red color.
