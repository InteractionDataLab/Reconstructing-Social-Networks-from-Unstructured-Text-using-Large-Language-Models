White and red colonies on all pSB1x3 plates, with red colonies being negative clones re-ligated with BBa_J04450 RFP cassettes, and white colonies being our positive clones carrying insert of interest. Colony verification Two positive clones were picked from each construct and dissolved in 10 μl LB. Colony PCR Colony PCR was performed on all pEX-carried inserts. No colony PCR was performed on pSB1x3 clones as the verification primers were not available at the time. PCR tubes - illustra PuReTaq Ready-To-Go™ PCR Beads: 1 μl 10 μM forward primer (pEXf) 1 μl 10 μM reverse primer (pEXr) 0.5 μl LB cell suspension 22.5 μl dH2O PCR settings 95°C - 5:00 95°C - 1:00 60°C - 0:30 72°C - 1:30 (Cycle to step 2. 29 times) 72°C - 10:00 15°C - ∞ PCR samples stored in -20°C for later gel separation. Restreakings Picked pSB1x3 clones were restreaked onto 1/4 or 1/2 LB agar plates with appropriate antibiotics to verify that the picked clones were indeed positive (RFP fluorescence is sometimes difficult to identify). Plates incubated ON in 37°C.
