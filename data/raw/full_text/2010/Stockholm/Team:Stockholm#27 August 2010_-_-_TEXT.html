Andreas Troubleshooting of SOD⋅His fusion fail Restreak results From 26/8 All restreaked clones (pSB1K3.SOD⋅His 1 & 2 and pSB1K3.His⋅SOD 1 & 3) were able to grow on both Cm 25 and Km 50. This raises the question whether our plasmid actually carries resistance to both Km and Cm. To test this further, new restreaks were made, where colonies from the Cm 25 plate were streaked on a new Km 50, and vice versa. Also, pSB1K3.RFP, pSB1C3.RFP and untransformed Top10 cells were streaked.
