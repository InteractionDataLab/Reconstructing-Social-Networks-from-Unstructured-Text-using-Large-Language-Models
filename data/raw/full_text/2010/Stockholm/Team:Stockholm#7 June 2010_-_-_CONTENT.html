    Preparation of competent DH5α Escherichia coli cells

    Autoclaving bottles & solutions for next day:

    • Two 1000 ml empty bottles
    • 500 ml 50 mM CaCl2 for 1000 ml cell culture
    • 16 ml 50 mM CaCl2 + 15 % Glycerol

    Set a 5 ml LB ON culture of DH5α in 37°C, 250 rpm.

    Johan

    Autoclaved stuff for making 1000 ml competent cells

    • Two 2L bottles, each with made 500 ml 50 mM CaCl2
    • 500 ml 50 mM CaCl2
    • Small bottle with 16 ml CaCl2 + 15 % Glycerol

    Made from 87 % Glycerol.

    c1 * v1 = c2 * v2

    87 % * ? = 15 % * 16 ml

    v1 = (15 % * 16 ml) / 87 % = 2,76 ml

    2,76 ml 87 % glycerol was diluted with 16 - 2,76 = 13,24 ml 50 mM CaCl2

    Step 1 in competent cell protocol

    5 ml of LB in each of two 50 ml Falcon tubes.

    Pipette tip dipped in commercial NEB5α (=DH5α) cells, then dipped in the Falcon tubes and put in 37 °C shaker.
