Based on an article by Ahl, Lindberg and Tibell (2004) , we decided that the two proteins should be expressed in equal amounts (1:1) from the same vector. Since we only have one expression vector (pEX) available, this requires some modifications. Our idea is to construct a SOD/yCCS operon from which the two genes can be co-transcribed. This will require a new Shine-Dalgarno (RBS) sequence for translation of the second gene in the operon.
