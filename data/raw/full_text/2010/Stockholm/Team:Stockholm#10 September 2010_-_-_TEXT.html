RBS 30 4 5 ml LB + 100 Amp 37 °C, 220 rpm. 3 ml LB + 100 Amp 30 °C Extraction of RBS BioBrick (BBa_B0034) After studying the original RBS of our expression vector pEX, we decided that BBa_B0034 was a better candidate for our SOD/yCCS operon than BBa_B0030, as it better resembles the RBS of pEX, as well as minimizes the distance from the first gene in the operon. Extracted BBa_B0034 (RBS 34), carried on pSB1A2, from iGEM plate 1, well 2M. Transformed into Top10. Quick transformation 1 μl DNA Amp 100 Preparation of chemically competent Top10 Since I've previously experienced slight AmpR contamination in our latest batch of competent Top10, I streaked an LB agar plate with competent Top10 cells to isolate single clones. Plate grown ON in 37 °C.
