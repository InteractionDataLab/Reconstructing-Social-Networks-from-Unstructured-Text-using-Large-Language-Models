Transform the ligate samples I transformed both of the ligated samples in each 100 ul Top 10 cells. The transformation method is according to the procedure decribed in protocols. However in the step 1 I thawed for 15 min instead of 10 min. In step 2 I added 3 ul of DNA, in step 6 I used LB instead of SOC and finally in step 10 I spread 100 ul of sample on a chloramphenicol plate.
