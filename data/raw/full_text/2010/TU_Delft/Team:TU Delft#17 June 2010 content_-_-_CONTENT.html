Procedure:

  • Add 1 ul of DNA to 33 ul of competent cells
  • Put the tubes on ice for 10 min
  • Warm the tubes in a water bath at 37º C for 5 min.
  • Add 250 ul of LB medium
  • Incubate the cells 1 hour at 37ºC, shake at 160 rpm
  • Plate 200 ul over selective medium
