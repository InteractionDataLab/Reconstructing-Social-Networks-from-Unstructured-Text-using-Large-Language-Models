The cultures were grown at 30 C and 37 C because Esengul suggested that IPTG induction works better at a lower temperature. IPTG was added when the cultures reached an OD600 of about 0.1. The protein isolation was started when the 37 C cultures reached and OD600 of approximately 0.6. After centrifugating 10' at 10.000 x g the cell free supernatant and the pellet were stored in the -20 C. The isolation protocol will be continued on monday.
