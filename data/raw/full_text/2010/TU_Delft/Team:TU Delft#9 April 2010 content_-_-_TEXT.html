Risk is represented with a value from 0 (no risk) to 10 (very risky) Lac operon (or other piece of cake) + our biobrick Less than 5 genes or biobricks (copy-paste) + our biobrick Less than 10 genes or biobricks (copy-paste) + our biobrick Biobrick engineering <5, improvement of things already done Multi biobrick engineering >5, improvement of things already done Site directed mutagenesis of several genes, results unknown a priori Evolutionary engineering involved = sequencing Protein engineering involved, results unknown a priori A lot of genes and/or biobricks (>20), known genes in other species and characterized.
