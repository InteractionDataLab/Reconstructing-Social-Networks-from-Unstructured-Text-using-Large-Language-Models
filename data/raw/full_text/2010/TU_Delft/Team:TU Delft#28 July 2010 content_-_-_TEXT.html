Alkane degradation The plates containing yesterday's ligations contained colonies, to check whether they really contain the desired BioBrick a colony PCR was done, and the used colonies were grown in liquid LB medium over night. The results from the PCR were analysed on a 1% agarose gel. 1% agarose of colony PCR part 1. Gel runned at 100V for 1 hour.
