It is interesting to see how much product could be made from hydrocarbons. Isoprene is a volatile product found in plants. E. coli will not be able to produce is in the near future, but it is an interesting product It is a very reduced product, with a similar amount of electron per carbon atom. Hydrocarbons have 6 - 6.3 electrons per carbon atom depending on the length and isoprene has 5.6 electron per carbon electron. If these values are close to each other, it has a positive influence on the maximal theoretical yield.
