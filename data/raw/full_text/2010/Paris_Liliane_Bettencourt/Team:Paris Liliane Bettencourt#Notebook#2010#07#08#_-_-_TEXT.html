Elute DNA into bugs, keep micropipette plunger constantly depressed, swirl using pipette tip, remove micropipette. In general, use 2 µl of a 20 µl ligation rxn, 2 µl of a 6 µl TOPO rxn, or 1 µL of plasmid DNA. 3- 15-30' on ice. 15' sufficient for Amp. 30' for Kan Can be left on ice for a few hours, some people leave it overnight but it is not recommended.
