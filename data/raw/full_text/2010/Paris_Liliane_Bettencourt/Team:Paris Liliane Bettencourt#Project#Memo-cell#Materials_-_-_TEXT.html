We then integrate between this arms the cassette for Kanamycine resistance and LacZ. Once this construction obtain we put it on the chromosome thanks to a plasmid with pir dependent origin of replication and transform the plasmid carrying Pbad Int Xis Tn916. The last part was to induce the transposase.
