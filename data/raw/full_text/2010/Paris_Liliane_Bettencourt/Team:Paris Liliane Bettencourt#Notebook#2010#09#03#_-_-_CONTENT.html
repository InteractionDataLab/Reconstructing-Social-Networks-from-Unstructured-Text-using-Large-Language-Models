Aleksandra and RaphaëlTesting of the counter on the plasmid (with mRFP) on the example of Ps/sRBS/LuxR/attC/term/attC/mRFPProtocol : we took an overnight culture and diluted it 1/100 in 5 ml LB. When the OD was 0.4, we induced with Arabinose (0.1%, 0.2% and 0.4%). Then we plated every hour the 1/100 and 1/1000 dilutions on the plates without Ara. The Ara serves to induce the expression of the integrase downstream of the pBAD/araC promoter on the pSWK plasmid. The integrase then recognises the attC sites on the pSB1A2 plasmid and excises the sequence between 2 sites : P/LuxR/attC/term/attC/mRFP → P/LuxR/mRFP. Thus, the terminator being excised the mRFP can be expressed downstream of the constitutive promoter. Red fluorescence appears only when Ara induced the integrase and it excised the terminator. Here we have tested different ara pulses necessary for the integrase activation and action. We decided to use 0.1% ara induction for at least 4 hours, and we will be plating the 1/1000 dilutions.
