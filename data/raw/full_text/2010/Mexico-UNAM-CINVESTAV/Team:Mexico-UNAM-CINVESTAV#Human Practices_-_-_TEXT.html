We invite you to see in detail this in the section 1st Mexican Workshop in Synthetic Biology . During the development of our project our team had serious problems with intellectual property and patents. For this reason we elaborate a survey with the objective to know the opinion of the community about these topics and promote the Biobrick Public Agreement. We invite you to see the survey results section . We answered the safety questions in the safety section . Finally our team is part of the Mexican Network of Synthetic Biology "Biosintetica". Biosintetica is a non-profit organization. Currently is integrated by the UNAM (National Autonomous Univeristy of Mexico), the CINVESTAV (Advanced Research Center from the National Polytechnic Institute), and a selected group of Mexican companies.
