This protein prevents ice crystal formation in the cell, which in turn allows survival at very low temperatures. We develop a switch by adapting the cold-shock E. coli operon with AFP from a generus of fishes (Zoarces sp.) using a positive feedback circuit. A very important potential application we are interested in is the use of AFP to designing systems helping crops to avoid potential damage from frosts. There are other possible important applications in tissue and organ preservation.
