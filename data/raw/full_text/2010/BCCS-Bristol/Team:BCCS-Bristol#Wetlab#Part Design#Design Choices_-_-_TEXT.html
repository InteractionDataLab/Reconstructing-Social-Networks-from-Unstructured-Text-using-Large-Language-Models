Public Perception Materials Campaign Public Engagement Team Contents 1 Design Choices 1.1 Promoter: PyeaR 1.2 Signal: GFP 1.3 Chassis: MG1655 Design Choices Promoter: PyeaR Proteins involved native in most 'E. coli' strains. Doesn't depend on metabolic state of bacteria (i.e. doesn't require anaerobic environment). Already available in parts registry. Signal: GFP Easily detectable with relatively low tech equipment. Quantifiable signal. Doesn't require any substrate (as opposed to luciferase). Wide range of fluorescent proteins available in parts registry; allows us to easily extend project by connecting these to other promoters, without needing additional detection equipment. Chassis: MG1655 Minimally engineered; meaning chassis robust enough to survive in the environment, without posing a public health hazard.
