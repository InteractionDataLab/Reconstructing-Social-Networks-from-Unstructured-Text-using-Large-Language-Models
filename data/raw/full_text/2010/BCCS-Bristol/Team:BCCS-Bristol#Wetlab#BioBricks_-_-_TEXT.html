Public Perception Materials Campaign Public Engagement Team Biobricks Biobricks Used in Preliminary Experiments BBa_I13522 - pTet GFP GFP under the control of a constitutive promoter. This was used to visualise E.coli on soil under a fluorescence microscope during the preliminary testing stage. Vector used: pSB1A2. Ampicillin resistance used as selective marker. BBa_K216009 - PyeaR with lacZ' Nitrate- and nitrite-sensitive promoter upstream of lacZ' reporter. Used to characterize the promoter through a Miller assay. Vector used: pSB1A2. Ampicillin resistance used as selective marker. Biobricks to be used in the Project BBa_K216005 - PyeaR promoter Nitrate- and nitrite-sensitive promoter. This will be combined with BBa_E0840 or BBa_E0240 to create a nitrate-sensitive system which signals through expression of GFP. Vector used: pSB1A2. Ampicillin resistance used as selective marker. BBa_E0840 - GFP generator GFP plus strong RBS and termination sequences. Vector used: pSB1A2. Ampicillin resistance used as selective marker. BBa_E0240 - GFP generator GFP plus weaker RBS and termination sequences. Vector used: pSB1A2. Ampicillin resistance used as selective marker. BBa_J04450 - RFP coding device RFP under the control of a constitutive promoter. This will be included in the E.coli chassis as a background fluorescence to aid in sensing and identifying the GFP signal, plus to act as a reference point for analysis of expression level of the GFP signal. Vector used: pSB1K3. Kanamycin resistance used as selective marker. BBa_I732017 - lacZ full-length (RBS+ TERM-) Full length copy of lacZ which will be combined with PyeaR to further categorise the promoter used in our Nitrate sensor system.
