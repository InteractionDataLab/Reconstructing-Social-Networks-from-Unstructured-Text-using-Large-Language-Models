This Wednesday (10/27/10) our team will be filmed for the Surrounded by Science Parkland College Television (PCTV) series. The series, sponsored by Parkland’s Institute for Science Education, is designed to promote science and science literacy, to encourage young people to study science, to showcase the science taking place in this community, and to make people aware of the many jobs in science.
