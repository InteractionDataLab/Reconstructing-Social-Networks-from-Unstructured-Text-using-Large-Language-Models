PCR

10 ul 5X Buffer 1 ul dNTP's 2 ul Primers (10 uM) 1 ul Template 0.5 ul high fidelity polymerase 35.5 ul water initial denaturation 98°C 30s denaturation 98°C 30s touchdown annealing 58°C 30s, -0.2 °C/cycle until 55 °C reached polymerisation 72°C 60s/1kb -> 40 cycles final polymerisation 72°C 5min hold 4°C

PCR clean-up

according to the protocol of GenElute PCR Clean-Up Kit (Sigma-Aldrich)
