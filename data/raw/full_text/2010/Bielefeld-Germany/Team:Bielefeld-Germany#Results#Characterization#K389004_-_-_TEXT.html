Fig. 1A shows the specific production rate qP which was determined with equation (1) (1) with the amount of product P, the cell count X and the relative luminescence units RLU plotted against the specific growth rate µ which was determined with equation (2) . Kinetic of luciferin conversion Look up the general luminescence measurement protocol here . To determine the ideal time of RLU measurement after adding luciferin to the cell lysate a kinetic of this reaction was recorded with the luminometer (fig. 2). A maximum is seen between 20 - 40 s. So a measurement with a delay of at least 20 s from the time luciferin is added to the cell lysate is recommended. In addition, the time between adding the luciferin to the cell lysate and the measurement should not be more than 40 s. Fig. 2: Kinetic of the luciferase reaction after adding luciferin to a cell lysate. For this calculation the noise (standard derivation sR) and the arithmetic mean (xR) of the reference measurement, so cells without luciferase gene, are collected.
