Other culture (I7-1, I7-2, I7-4, I8-1, I8-2, I8-3) did not produce GFP, so they were thrown away. A furhter screening were performed on I7-3, I7-5, I8-4, I8-5 and this time all the clones are OK . Inoculum of BBa_J72007 and BBa_J72013 from LB agar plates.A glycerol stock was prepared for each culture. Inoculum of BBa_J72008 from LB agar plate grown at 30°C 220 rpm overnight.
