MAY

Find a winning idea and to agree people with different interests and personalities was really hard, so, also in May, Brainstorming was the main activity.

DATEACTIVITY
May, 6th6° Meeting - Observations, doubts, problems and solutions about the 4 ideas destined to become The Project.At the end of the meeting, Prof. Paolo Magni divided the team into 4 groups. Task of each group was to produce a comprehensive report on the assigned project.
May, 17thiGEM 2010 DNA Distribution.
May, 20th7° Meeting - Reading and discussion of the developed reports. New task: take time to reflect!
May, 24thWiki training.
May, 26th8° Meeting - At last The Project was decided. Toast to celebrate the real beginning of iGEM 2010!
May, 28thOfficial start of Wiki.
