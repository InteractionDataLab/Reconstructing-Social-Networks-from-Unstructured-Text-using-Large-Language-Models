Heat the innoculation loop till red hot to sterilize the loop. Allow the innoculation loop to cool down for approximately 30 seconds. The innoculation loop is then used to pick a single colony of bacteria on the agar plate. Innoculate the LB or appropriate media with the bacteria. Resterilize the loop. The culture is then left overnight at 37°C in a shaking incubator at 200 rpm.
