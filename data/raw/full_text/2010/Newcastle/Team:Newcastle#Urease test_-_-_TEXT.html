Sterilize at 115°C for at least 20 min. Add the following to the molten base: D(+)-Glucose 1.0 g Phenol red, 0.2% 6 ml Note: Ensure that the work was done using aseptic technique. Transfer 10 ml of the molten base to universal tube and allow the agar to set in a slanted position. Store the harden agar in the fridge. Procedures Perform the experiment using aseptic technique. Pick up single colony of B. subtilis 168 and streaking it onto the slanted agar tube. Loosen up the cap to allow air exchange between the interior of the tube and the external environment. Incubate the tubes overnight at 37°C.
