Grow the cell at 37°C, 200 rpm in an orbital incubator. Chill cells by placing the flask on ice and harvest by centrifugation at 4°C for 10 minutes. Resuspend the pellet in 100 ml of ice cold TFB1 and gently shake the tubes whilst placed on ice. Repeat the above mentioned step and carefully resuspend pellet in 20 ml ice cold TFB2. Aliquot 200 µl volumes of the cell suspension into cold sterile microfuge (1.5 ml) tubes and flash freeze in dry-ice . Store at -80°C.
