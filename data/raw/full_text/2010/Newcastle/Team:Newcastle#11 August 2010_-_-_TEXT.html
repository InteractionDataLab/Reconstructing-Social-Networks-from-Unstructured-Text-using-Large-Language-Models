Materials and protocol Please refer to the gel electrophoresis protocol. Results Figure 1: Gel electrophoresis of the PCR products of the parts required for the subtilin immunity BioBrick. Lane 1: 1 kb DNA ladder Lane 2: Plasmid Vector (pSB1C3) Lane 3: Promoter and RBS (pVeg-SpoVG) Lane 4: spaIFEG Gene Cluster Lane 5: Double terminator Lane 6: 100 bp DNA ladder Discussion The correct band size were observed for for the Plasmid Vector (lane 2), Promoter & RBS (lane 3) and Double terminator (lane 5). However no band was observed for the spaIFEG(lane 4). Conclusion No band was observed for the spaIFEG. This could be due to the wrong melting temperature.
