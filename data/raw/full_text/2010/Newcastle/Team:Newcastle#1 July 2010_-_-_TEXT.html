Procedures Please refer to urease test Inference Christensen's Urea Agar was formulated to detect and differentiate urolytic and urea degrading microorganisms. The addition of gelatine peptone, dextrose and reduced content of buffer supports a luxuriant growth at an early stage. Urea is the substrate and can be degraded by certain organisms, which results in ammonia building. The ammonia makes the media alkaline and therefore the indicator phenol red will change from orange color to pink-red color. Negative control - No color change (Orange color) Test 1 (Duplicate) - Pink-red color Test 2 (Duplicate) - Pink-red color For actual results, please see 02.07.10 lab notebook.
