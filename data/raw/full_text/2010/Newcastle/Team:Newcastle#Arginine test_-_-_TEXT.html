Flame (streaking) loop LB media consisting of arginine Auto pipette Bursen Burner Universal Tube pH indicator paper Procedures Perform the experiment using aseptic technique. Transfer B. subtilis 168 colonies into universal tubes containing 5 ml of LB media and allowed to grow overnight at 37° C. Transfer 1 ml of the overnight culture to another universal tube containing 4 ml of the following media: Control (1) - LB media Control (2) - LB media with 10 mM of arginine Control (3) - LB media plus B. subtilis 168 Test (1) - LB media with 10 mM of arginine plus B. subtilis 168 Test (2) - LB media with 10 mM of arginine plus B. subtilis 168 Incubate the culture at 37° C with shaking. Record the pH at every 30 min interval.Use 20 ul of the culture and measure the pH using the pH indicator paper.
