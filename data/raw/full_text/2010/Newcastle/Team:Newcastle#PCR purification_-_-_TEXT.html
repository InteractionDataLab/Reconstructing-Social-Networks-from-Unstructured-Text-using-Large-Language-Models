Put a QIAquick spin column into a 2ml collection tube. Apply the mixture to the QIAquick column to bind the DNA and centrifuge for 30 to 60 seconds at 13,000 rpm. Discard flow-through. Add 0.75 ml of Buffer PE to the spin column to wash and centrifuge for 30 to 60 seconds at 13,000 rpm. Discard flow-through and centrifuge for another 1 minute at 13,000 rpm. Place QIAquick spin column into a clean 1.5 ml microcentrifuge tube. Add 50 µl Buffer EB to the center of the membrane of spin column and centrifuge for 1 minute at 13,000 rpm. If analysing purified DNA using gel, add 1 volume of Loading Dye to 5 volumes of DNA. Store the PCR product either at 4°C or at -20°C.
