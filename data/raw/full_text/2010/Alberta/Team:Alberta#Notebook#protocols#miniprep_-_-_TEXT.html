Notes: Low speed pellet is easier to resuspend. To resuspend, take an empty micro centrifuge rack and rub the tube quickly over the apparatus and vortex. Step 2 in Qiagen, the alkaline lysis with buffer P2, should be brief to avoid damaging DNA.
