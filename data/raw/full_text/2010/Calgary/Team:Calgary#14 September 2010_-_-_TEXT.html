Chris Today, I ran verification digests in the attempt to confirm that the CpxP promoter and ibpAB-fsxA promoter had been inserted into Biobrick plasmids. They were cut with EcoRI and PstI which are present on the Biobrick vector. The gels are visible to the right. The first gel includes the ibpAB-fsxA fusion promoter in lanes 1 to 3 and MalE31 in lanes 4 to 11. The second gel underneath includes all the CpxP attempts. The result of the restriction digest run on a gel is that lanes 3, 6, and 10 seem to have the correct sizes for the promoter and will be followed up on with a colony PCR.
