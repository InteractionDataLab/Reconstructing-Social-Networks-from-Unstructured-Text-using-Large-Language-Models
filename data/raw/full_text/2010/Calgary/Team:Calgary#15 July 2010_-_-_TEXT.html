We will antarctic phosphotase, ligate and then transform this part tomorrow. Patrick I ran a gel of the gradient PCRs. After analyzing a few samples of the gradient, it's determined that most of them produced about 310-320 ng/μL of product. Along with Chris and Emily, we purified the PCR product and digested them to be ligated into plasmid tomorrow.
