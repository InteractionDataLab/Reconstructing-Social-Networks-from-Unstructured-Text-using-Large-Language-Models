Lane contents can be seen below. Emily Today I PCR Purified malE and malE31. I then digested these as well as the psB1AK3 vector with a combination of restriction enzymes to try to get it into an AK BBK construction vector. I ligated these and transformed them into TOP10 competant cells and plated on K plates, leaving for overnight growth. I also made more K plates as well as more AK broth.
