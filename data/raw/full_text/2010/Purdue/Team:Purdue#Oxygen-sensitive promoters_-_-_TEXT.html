Because these bacteria must produce enzymes to modify environmental nitrogen, they are also able to detect levels of oxygen using the FixL-FixJ two-component system. [6] -- Skearney The DosT/DevS system found in Mycobacterium tuberculosis has an analogous oxygen-sensing function.
