    • Restriction Enzyme Digestion

    Materials

    Plasmid DNA: 10.5 µL/5 µL

    Restriction Enzymes (depend on the requirements of the parts) 

      EcoRI: 1 µL

      PstI: 1 µL

      SpeI: 1 µL

      XbaI: 1 µL

      10× NE buffer: 1.5 µL/1 µL (lid)

      BSA: 1 µL (lid)

    Method

    1. Add all the contents listed above to labelled eppendorfs.

    2. Spin for 30 sec.

    3. Incubation at 37°C for 1hr.

    4. Leave on ice before the gel electrophoresis
