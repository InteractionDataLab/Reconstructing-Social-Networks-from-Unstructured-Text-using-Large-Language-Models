    On behalf of University College London iGEM team 2010, we would like to thank the following organisations and peoples for their contribution and support throughout this year's competition.

    Main Sponsors

    Dry Consumables

    Russell Golson at Biosilta for providing us with 10 shake flasks as well as Enbase Media Tablets.
    Colleen Holgate at Thermo Fisher for sending us 30 of their finest boxes of gloves.
    Carsten H. Müller at m2p-labs GmbH for supplying us with e.coli synthetic media and synthetic media supplements.

    Design

    Guilia Ricci for implementing her artistic designs into various aspects of our project.http://giuliaricci.blogspot.com/2010/02/entropia-blue-entropia-black-entropia.html.
    David Bennett for adding a creative touch to our project in terms of designs and ideas.

    WikiPage

    Nour El Saidi for the development and redevelopment of the wikipage and implementation of ALL its unique features. We dedicate the whole wikipage to him! Awesome work!
    Loizos Pallaris for the video editing and the soundtrack music arrangment.
    Peter Badida for his brief role in suggesting slight changes to the wikipage's user friendly aspect.
    Andrew MacMurray for his assistance in animations for the wiki.

    Acknowledgments

    Dr Jane Gregory, Reader in Science & Technology Studies and Undergraduate Admissions Tutor and Affiliate Tutor for the STS Department at UCL for her role in organizing us as a team and identifying both our strengths and weaknesses.
    Lawford Davies Denoon for there services and information on legal issues. There 25 years of experience in the legal side of life sciences has helped us understand the complexity of IP rights in this sector.
    Alessandro SineNomine for his photographic services during a photography session in the Quad.
    Department of Biochemical Engineering Many many thanks to Zarah Ali for providing the bioprocess flowsheets and David Brindley for all his involvement and contribution in the economical assessment aspect of the project.
    Mechanical Engineering Department and in particular Taaha Hussain at UCL for aiding us in the development of a fermenter design using SolidWorks.
    Chemical Engineering Department and special thanks to Achilleas Constantinou for all his help with COMSOL Multiphysics 4.0a.
    PAP-Medical Cyprus and special thanks to Tatiana Theodosiadou for all her involvement in supporting this project financially.
