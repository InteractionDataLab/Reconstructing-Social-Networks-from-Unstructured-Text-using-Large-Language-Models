Project Description

Content

Our project is to create a series of tools for inducing, measuring and counting intracellular calcium spikes in both bacteria and yeast. Calcium is known to be extremely important in its function as a signaling ion in eukaryotic organisms; thus, it is valuable to develop synthetic biology tools for studying calcium dynamics within cells. The tools we are creating with this project will enable future study of calcium in bacteria and yeast, as well as create a groundwork for future genetic circuits to be created to study calcium in these organisms, and, eventually, in multicellular organisms.
