Transformation with Electroporation

  1. Centrifuge your culture and discard the supernatant.
  2. Wash with glycerol at 10%. (x3)
  3. Put in 100µl of glycerol.
  4. Add 1 µl pBT340.
  5. Turn on the Electroporator. Put the bacteria. Prepare 1ml LB medium cold.
  6. After the electroporation, add quickly the LB medium cold.
  7. Culture at 37°C during 1h
  8. Spread on petri dishes: (Discard 600µl and spread 50µl, 100µl and the rest)
