Purification of the chromoprotein gene from the agarose gel by Sean We used the kit PCR-Clean-UP to extract the DNA from the agarose gel. Elution volume : 15µl Gel electrophoresis of the purification product by Sean The goal is to check if the purification of the chromoprotein gene has worked. PCR clean-up was performed ealier today . 1µl of each result was used for the electrophoresis. Nothing appeared under UV. Ligation of chromoprotein by Sean components volumes H2O 6μl T4 DNA ligase buffer 2µl purified chromoprotein 10µl ligase 1µl pSB1C3 1µl Leave overnight at 16°C. Collection of pellets containing pGEMTeasy+chromoprotein by Sean Three liquid cultures (pGEM+chromoprotein) were prepared. 2ml from each were collected in 2ml microcentrifuge tubes, then centrifuged. The pellets were put in the freezer.
