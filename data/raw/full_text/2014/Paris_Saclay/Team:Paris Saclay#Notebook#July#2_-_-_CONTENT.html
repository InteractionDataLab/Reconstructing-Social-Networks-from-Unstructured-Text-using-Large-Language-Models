    Discussion about questions raised from our project. At the end of the meeting, we agreed to conduct surveys targeted to the general public and to people with a more scientific background.

    Modeling

    by Arnaud, Juliette and Pierre

    After long discussions, we have found an agreement : Arnaud will focus on the 3D-structure of proteins and then try to model the turning of the lemon from green to yellow, Eugène will work on the odor diffusion and Pierre & Juliette will put their attention on the bacterial population growth in space and time.

    Then, we just raided the university library.
