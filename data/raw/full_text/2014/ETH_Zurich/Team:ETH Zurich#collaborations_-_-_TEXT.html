As they focus on metabolic wiring and we search for orthogonal ways for cells to communicate, it seemed natural to combine our two projects, at least on the modeling level. You can find the fusion of our models here . Aalto Helsinki: Low Budget PCR of Colombia iGEM team The Colombia iGEM team organized a challenge to develop low budget protocols. The importance of this challenge is to make molecular biology processes more cost-efficient and make them available worldwide. The following three processes were suggested for total or partial modification: DNA Extraction Transformation PCR We participated in this challenge and attempted together with the Aalto-Helsinki iGEM team to develop a PCR method not requiring a thermocycler. Watch the video for a more detailed description of the process and check the pdf document below for our results considering the costs and in terms of experimental outcomes.
