    Bronze medal criteria

    Team registration Complete Judging form Team Wiki
    Present a poster and a talk at the iGEM Jamboree Clearly attribute work Document at least one new standard BioBrick Part or Device
    Jamboree, here we come!

    Silver medal criteria

    Experimental validation of new biobrick Documentation of part characterization in the Registry Submission of this new part to the Registry Articulate at least one "beyond the bench" question encountered by your team

    Gold medal criteria

    Improve the function OR characterization of an existing BioBrick Part or Device Help any registered iGEM team from another school or institution Describe an approach that your team used to address at least one of the questions beyond the bench.
