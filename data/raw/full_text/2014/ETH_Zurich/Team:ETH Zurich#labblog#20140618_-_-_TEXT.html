Second draft of plasmids. The repressors production genes have been moved to the sensor plasmid in order to distribute more evenly the number of kb. Sensor plasmids will be approx. 9.3kb long, and logic plasmids will be approx. 5,2kb long. From the modeling side, the whole model has been modularized and we have calculated and simulated steady state for the quorum sensing module.
