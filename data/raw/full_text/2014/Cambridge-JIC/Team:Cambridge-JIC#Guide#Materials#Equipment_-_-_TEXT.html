Contents 1Physical Equipment 1.1Marchantia 1.1.1Growth 1.1.2Transformation 1.1.3Other 1.2Agrobacteria 1.2.1Growth 1.2.2Transformation Physical Equipment Marchantia Growth You will need a room/compartment at room temperature under full illumination. Ventilation is also recommended, but not essential Transformation For transformation using cocultivation , you will need a shaker under full illumination capable of shaking 200ml conical flasks. Otherwise, a ventilated illumination space at room temperature is sufficient. Other Porous tape to seal plates from fungal contamination, but enabling gas exchange, is essential. Agrobacteria Growth You will need a 30degree incubator, as well as a room temperature shaker for growing liquid cultures. Transformation You will need electroporation equipment capable of delivering at least 1.25kV (if using 1mm cuvettes). Do not build your own unless you have the prerequisite safety qualifications!
