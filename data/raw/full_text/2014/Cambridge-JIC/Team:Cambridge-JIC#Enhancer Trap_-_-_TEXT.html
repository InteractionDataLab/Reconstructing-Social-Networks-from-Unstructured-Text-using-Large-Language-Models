The viral promoter '35S' and the '?' are the only highly tested promoters for each. Consequently, we are randomly integrating a plasmid with a minimal promoter driving a GFP gene into the Marchantia polymorpha genome, and observing expression levels. After screening several thousand transformants for sex-specific/developmental-specific/highly strong promoters, we will reverse PCR several kb upstream from the inserted genes. These shall then be released as parts, if successful. Lab Notebook Objective: randomly integrate a plasmid with a minimal promoter into the Marchantia genome, using a Venus flo-protein reporter. Then screen, and establish enhancer lines which are tissue or developmental stage specific, or of great strength.
