    Checklist

    This checklist contains the minimum you will need for setting up transformation.

    DNA

    - A helper plasmid for Transformation

    - Your T-DNA plasmid for transformation

    Bacteria

    - Competent E-Coli (chemically/electro)

    - Electrocompetent agrobacteria

    Marchantia

    - Sporangia

    OR

    - Male and female Marchantia polymorpha [from the garden], for growing spores

    Miscellaneous

    - Gamborg's B5 salt mix

    - LB/SOC for bacterial transformation and growth

    - 100mM Acetosyringone

    Equipment

    - Ventilated cupboard/room/box under full illumination [larger space = more transformations]
