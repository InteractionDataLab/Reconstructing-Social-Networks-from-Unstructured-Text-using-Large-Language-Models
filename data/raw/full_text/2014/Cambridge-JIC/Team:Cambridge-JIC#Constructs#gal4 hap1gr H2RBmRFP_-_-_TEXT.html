A positive control for lab technique will be 35s->Venus N7 We'll also use FF's original plasmid to try 35s-Hap1GR-mRFP, as a control for the Hap1GR. I expect low transformation rate as in the original plasmid, we're using 35s as promoter for HygR selection marker.
