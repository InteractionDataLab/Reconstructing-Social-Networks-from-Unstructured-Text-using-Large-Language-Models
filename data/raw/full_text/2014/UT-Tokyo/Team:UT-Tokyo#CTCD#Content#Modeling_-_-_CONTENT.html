Characterization of BBa_K747096

During DNA assembly, we found that this promoter was activated in Escherichia coli. Then we decided to characterize this promoter activity by comparing with constitutive promoter (BBa_J23101) using GFP (BBa_E0040) (Figure 1).As seen in Figure 1, this promoter shows weaker activity than BBa_J23101.

<img src = "" class = "figure" style="width:400px; float:left;" /><img src = "" class = "figure" style="width:400px; float:left;padding-left:0px;" /><legend style="clear:both">Fig. 1The activity of BBa_K747096GFP was activated by 501nm excitation laser.</legend><legend style="width:300px;float:left;margin-left:120px;margin-right:100px;">a)Measured when OD600 is 0.9~1.1.</legend><legend style="width:300px;float:left;margin-left:0px;margin-right:0px;">b)Measured after cultured O/N.</legend>

In order to calculate RPU (relative promoter unit [1]), we performed real-time measurement of GFP fluorescence (Figure 2). We measured activity of GFP and OD600, which are necessary for calculation of RPU.

<img src = "" class = "figure" style="width:400px; float:left;" /><img src = "" class = "figure" style="width:400px; float:left;padding-left:0px;" /><legend style="clear:both;margin-left:250px;">Fig. 2Real-time measurement of BBa_K747096</legend><legend style="width:400px;float:left;margin-left:60px;margin-right:150px;">a)Activity of GFP (activated by 488nm excitation laser).</legend><legend style="width:200px;float:left;margin-left:0px;margin-right:0px;">b)OD600.</legend>
