    José Luiz - He graduated in Medicine from the Federal University of Pernambuco (1983) and a Ph.D. in Biochemistry and PhD in Microbiology - University of St Andrews (1987). Holds a post doctorate in Germany (GBF), Japan (Tokyo Institute of Technology) and United States (NIST) in the area of ​​diagnosis and biosensor development. He is currently Professor Federal University of Pernambuco, scientific director of LIKA.

    Eduardo Campello Peixoto - M.Sc. in Electronic Engineer, with distinction, from Technical University of Eindhoven-Holland and an MBA from Kellogg School of Management, Evanston-USA. He has over 20 years of experience in IT and telecommunication projects, having performed technical and management activities for different organizations in Brazil, Holland and Switzerland. Peixoto is one of the founders of the PMI Chapter in Recife, speaker at Recife Summer School and innovation consultant for various organizations. He is currently the Chief Business Officer at C.E.S.A.R.

    Tiago Guedes Ferreira Barros - M.Sc. in Computer Science (UFPE -2007), B. Sc in Computer Science (UFPE 2003) and Technician in Eletronics (CEFET/PE 1998). Tiago has been developing sofwtare for more the 20 years in a wide variety of languages and processors. Since 2002 he works at C.E.S.A.R, where he is specialist systems engineer, researcher and consultant, responsible for leading to research teams and has particpated in over 15 innovation projects, from embedded systems to cloud computing. Tiago also teaches pos-graduated courses of physical computing and software architecture for mobile devices and as invited professor at CIn/UFPE-Motorola and Positivo University

    Fabio Silva - PhD in Computer Science from the Laboratory for Foundations of Computer Science, University of Edinburgh, Scotland, since 1992. He works with research, teaching and consulting include human factors in software teams and projects of software engineering, innovation management, local and national systems of innovation, and entrepreneurship.
