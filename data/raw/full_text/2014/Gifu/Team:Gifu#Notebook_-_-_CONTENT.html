Glossary

His
Histidine tag
GFP
Green Fluorescence Protein
RFP
Red Fluorescent ProteinThis is BBa_E1010.
5'-intron
The 5’ side of the intron from td gene of T4 phage
5'-intron M
The 5’ side of the intron from td gene of T4 phage without stop codon
3'-intron
The 3’ side of the intron from td gene of T4 phage
Lac
LacI regulated promoter.This is BBa_R0010.
Amp
Ampicillin resistance gene
Cm
Chloramphenicol resistance gene.This is pSB1C3.
RBS
Ribosome Binding Sites.This is BBa_B0034.
DT
Double Terminator consisting of BBa_B0010 and BBa_B0012. This is BBa_B0015.
EcoRI
Restriction site that is cut by EcoRI or restriction enzyme
XbaI
Restriction site that is cut by XbaI or restriction enzyme
SpeI
Restriction site that is cut by SpeI or restriction enzyme
PstI
Restriction site that is cut by PstI or restriction enzyme
TTHA0715
Cold shock protein (attached amino acid sequence cut by thrombin)
SmtA
Metallothionein that can catch heavy metal ions such as Zn2+. This is BBa_K519010.
