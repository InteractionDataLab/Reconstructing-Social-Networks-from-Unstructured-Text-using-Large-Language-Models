Aachen iGEM MeetUp 2014 On September 13th and 14th all German teams were invited to attend the Aachen iGEM Meetup 2014 to share knowledge, fun and some drinks. iGEM Meets CASLMU - Munich meetup Early into the competition we were invited to the meetup of German iGEM teams in Munich. Arne, Markus and Michael attended the event and had a great time meeting the other iGEMers. Meeting the other teams and talking about each others problems, was also a factor in starting some of our own collaboration projects.
