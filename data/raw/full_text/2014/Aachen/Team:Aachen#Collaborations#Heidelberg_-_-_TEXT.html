This way RBS-CDS parts can be cloned into the vectors by standard BioBrick assembly and then expressed in BL21(DE3) by induction with IPTG. Heidelberg X-press vector design The iGEM Team Heidelberg created new expression vectors with an integrated T7 Promoter In collaboration with the iGEM Team Heidelberg the iGEM Team Aachen tested 8 of these vectors for their expression capabilities. We successfully cloned and expressed one of our fusion proteins K139020 in a pSBX1A3 vector. You can read more about the background of this expression on the Galectin-3 page .
