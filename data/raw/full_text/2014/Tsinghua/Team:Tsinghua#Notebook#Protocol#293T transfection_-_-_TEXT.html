Vortex gently. Add 3ul of MegaTran 1.0 to the diluted DNA (not the reverse order!) and vortex the solution immediately for 10 seconds. Incubate for 10 minutes at room temperature. 3.Transfection Add the Megatran 1.0/DNA mixture from step 2 to each well (already containing about 2ml culture medium) gently. Gently rock the plate to achieve even distribution of the complexes.
