This noisiness may be due to differences in extracelluar environment or intracellular molecular makeup. Yet these cells often need to coordinate and respond in a concerted way. Our project seeks to understand the complex intercellular interactions that must happen to produce such a specific community phenotype. Our goal is to create a synthetic framework that uses cellular communities to model collective behaviors emergent from individual autonomous rules. Explore Our Project Judging Judges!
