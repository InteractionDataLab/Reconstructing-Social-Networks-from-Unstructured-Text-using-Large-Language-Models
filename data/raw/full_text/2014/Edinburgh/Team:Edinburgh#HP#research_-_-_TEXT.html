Communication: how do individuals transfer knowledge and information between one another? Robustness: how does the system respond to emergencies and unexpected events? Hierarchy: how is the internal structure organised? Supervision: is there someone/something external monitoring the progress? These emerged as the five most important factors that characterise the work of a modular system.
