Transformation of Pseudovribrio with pBBR1MCS After made a purification of pBBR1MCS with NucleoSpin Plamsid Kit (Macherey Nagel), electrocompetent Pseudovibrio cells were tranformed with the plasmid by electroporation (1µL of plasmid for 40µL of cells, 1800V), then they were incubated for three hours and showed on MB 1X+Cam (1:1000) plates. Plates are let in incubator overnight.
