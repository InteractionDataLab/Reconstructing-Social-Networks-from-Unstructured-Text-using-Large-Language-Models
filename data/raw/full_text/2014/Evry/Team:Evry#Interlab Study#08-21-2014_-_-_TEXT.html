The transformation was performed on DH5 alpha E. coli, as followed: Remove E. coli competent tubes from -80°C and keep it on ice Add 3 µl of template (here solubilized plasmids from the registry distribution kit) and mix gently Incubate 10 minutes on ice Perform an heat shock 30 seconds at 42°C Incubate 2 minutes on ice Add 2 ml of LB medium and incubate 60 minutes at 37°C with an agitation at 200 rpm Plate 200 µl of BBa_K823012 or BBa_J23101 on a chloramphenicol LB agar plate.
