Gel extraction Extraction of GFP B0031/32 digestion product [XbaI-PstI] After confirmation of relevant electrophoresis bands, cut the gel all around the band and as close as possible to it. Place the resulting piece into a 2ml microcentrifuge tube. Add 1µl of Binding buffer to 1 µg of gel. Place the tube at 55°C to 60°C during 10min or until gel turn completely liquid. Follow classical DNA purification protocol.
