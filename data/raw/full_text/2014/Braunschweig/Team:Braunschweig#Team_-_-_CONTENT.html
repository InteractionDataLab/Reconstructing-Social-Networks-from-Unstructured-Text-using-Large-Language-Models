    • Our Team

    After last year's successful participation of the first iGEM Team, we, the iGEM Team Braunschweig 2014, want to represent the collegiate research from Braunschweig on international floor. This year we participate with a mixed team of motivated bachelor and master students from the fields of biology and biotechnology advised by Dr. Torsten Meyer.

    You can find out more about our team members in our profiles.
