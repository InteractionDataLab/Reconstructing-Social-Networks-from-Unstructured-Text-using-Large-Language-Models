    • Sponsors

    We would like to express our sincere gratitude to all of our sponsors
      
    Foundation 
      
      
    Tokyo Institute of Technology Foundation
      

    This foundation aims to do the assistance of student scholastic and develop the environment for education and research.

      
    Aizawa Foundation
      

    Aizawa Foundation was established by Masuo Aizawa, who is the ex-president of Tokyo Institute of Technology. The foundation provides financial support for students, especially those who belong to the Department of Bioscience and Biotechnology.

      
      
      
    Association & Organization
      
      
    Tokyo Tech Alumni Association headquarters
      

    This association aims to promote the progress of technology and industries, and provides students with financial support.

      
    Tokyo Tech Alumni Association Kanagawa branch
      

    This association aims at promoting the progress of technology and industries, and financially support students, especially those who belong to the Department of Bioscience and Biotechnology. They also collected donations for our team at the association’s summer night festival.

      
    Japan Student Services Organization (JASSO)
      

    This organization wants to develop creative human resources that can work around the world for the next generation’s society by doing the assistance of student scholastic and preparing the appropriate environment for students studying in Japan.

      
      
      
    Company
      
      
    Leave a Nest Co
      

    Leave a Nest Co., Ltd. is a company that introduced our team to Promega KK., and provided us with material used in experiment.

      
    Promega 
      

    Promega KK. is a company that supports people engaged in the field of life science. This company supported us by providing the parts of restriction enzyme used in experiment.

      
      
    COSMO BIO
      

    Cosmo Bio Co., Ltd. aims at supporting young scientists who bear the next generation, and this is reflected in the company’s corporate philosophy “Contribute to significant achievement in life science”.

      
      
    The MathWorks
      

    The MathWorks, Inc. is a company that kindly allowed us to use their excellent software MATLAB. We could not make our modeling programs without it.

      
      
    Integrated DNA Technologies
      

    Integrated DNA Technologies Inc. (IDT) is a company that supports DNA composition, especially the transformation of ideas into real products.

      
    Medical & Biological Laboratories (MBL)
      

    Medical & Biological Laboratories (MBL) Co., Ltd. is a company that supports DNA composition, especially the transformation of ideas into real products.

      
      
      
    Individual
      
      
     Mr. Isao Ono
      

    He graduated from Tokyo Institute of Technology in 1963. He is now working as a consultant for Hitachi Solutions, Ltd. and a vice president of our school Tokyo Institute of Technology. He supported our team personally through Tokyo Institute of Technology Foundation.
