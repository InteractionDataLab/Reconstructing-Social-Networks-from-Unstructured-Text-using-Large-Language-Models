    Economic Wave

     

    Contents

    1. Abstract

    2. Integration to our circuit

    3. Results

    3.1. Bank helps Company and Customer even in the presence of economic wave

    3.2. Bank cannot help Company and Customer enough in the presence of harsh economic wave

     

     

    1. Abstract

    From the advice we received from entrepreneurs, we introduced economic wave into our system. Even though the introduction of economic wave destructs the system of Company and Customer, Bank helps Company and Customer to grow well.

     
     

    2.  Integration to our circuit

    Our integrated economic wave is expressed as in Fig. 4-4-2-1.

     
    Fig. 4-4-2-1. Economic wave term
     

    This term is added to the C4HSL equations as in Fig. 4-4-2-2.

     
    Fig. 4-4-2-2. Integration of economic wave
     
     

    3. Result

    3.1. Bank helps Company and Customer even in the presence of economic wave

    Even though the Company and Customer can grow well only by themselves, introduction of economic wave destructs the system of Company and Customer. This is shown in Fig. 4-4-3-1.

     
    Fig. 4-4-3-1. Introduction of economic wave destructs the system of Company and Customer
     
    Fig.4-4-3-2 shows the C4HSL concentration during the simulation.
     
    Fig. 4-4-3-2. C4HSL concentration during the simulation shown in Fig. 4-4-3-1
     

    As shown in Fig. 4-4-3-2, the system of Company and Customer has enormous amount of money when there are no economic wave. But once the economic wave has been introduced, then because of the recession during the wave, they all die.

    When the Bank is added, Bank helps Company and Customer to grow well. This is shown in Fig. 4-4-3-3(left). The C4HSL concentration during the simulation is shown in Fig.4-4-3-3(right).

     
    Fig. 4-4-3-3. Bank helps Company and Customer to grow well even in the presence of economic wave
     

    During the recession in the economic wave, the Bank supplies money to the economy. Because of this, they all can grow well.

     

    3.2. Bank cannot help Company and Customer enough in the presence of harsh economic wave

    Additionally, we simulated how the system will behave in the presence of harsh economic wave. Harsh economic wave we introduced is described as in Fig. 4-4-3-4.

     
    Fig. 4-4-3-4. description of harsh economic wave
     

    With the presence of the harsh economic wave, the Company and Customer cannot survive. When the Bank is included, even though the Bank helps Company and Customer they cannot grow stably. This result is shown in Fig. 4-4-3-5.

     
    Fig. 4-4-3-5. With the presence of harsh economic wave, Company and Customer cannot grow well (left). When the Bank is included, all of them can survive but the Bank cannot help sufficiently (right).
     

    Although the Bank tries to stable the money supply by switching its state many times(Fig. 4-4-3-6), since the fluctuation of money supply is very harsh (Fig. 4-4-3-6), Company and Customer cannot endure the fluctuation enough.

     
    Fig. 4-4-3-6. C4HSL concentration during the simulation with harsh economic wave with addition of Bank
