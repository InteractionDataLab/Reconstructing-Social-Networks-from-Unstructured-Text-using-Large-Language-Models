The conference incorporated two main events. The first was a discussion between experts, held under Chatham House rules, to discuss and advance a pre-written framework for bacteriophage applications and associated safety and regulatory considerations. This was followed by a public forum to present and discuss the results and implications with a more general audience before shifting to guided and informal discussion.
