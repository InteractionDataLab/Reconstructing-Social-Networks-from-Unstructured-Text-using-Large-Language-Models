Our Project The AcCELLerator facilitates one central process in mammalian cell culture: Gene delivery. It provides you with the possibility to stably insert and express multiple genes with high spatio-temporal resolution. The two component system combines the advantages of optogentics and viral vector based gene delivery. Read More Results To validate our system we used different experimental methods including FACS-analysis, immunoblotting and confocal microscopy.
