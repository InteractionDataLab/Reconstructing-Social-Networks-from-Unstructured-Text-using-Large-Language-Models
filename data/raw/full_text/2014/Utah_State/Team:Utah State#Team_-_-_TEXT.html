Instructor Dr. Charles Miller Dr. Charles Miller is the faculty advisor for this year's Utah State iGEM team. About Utah State University Logan, Utah is home of the Utah State University Aggies. Originally established as a small land grant agricultural college in 1888, Utah State University has grown to become a nationally recognized institution both for its high level of academics and athletics. Its undergraduate research program is the second oldest in the nation with more than a fourth of the undergraduate researchers involved in their own research. Utah State University continues to excel in many academic areas, especially engineering, math, and science.
