Maintained as one of the best and one of the center for diagnostic and study of Human Immunodeficiency Virus that has mutated and resistant from drugs (multiple drug or even extreme drug resistance) in Indonesia, has teached us a lot of things about biosafety and biosecurity. The lab which located on the eight floor of IASTH building, Salemba 4, Central Jakarta, also focuses on education by accepting undergraduates, master, and even postgraduates students to study inside. The lab itself held together by a strong bond of friendship, under the guidance of dr. R. Fera Ibrahim, M.Sc., Ph.D., Sp.MK.
