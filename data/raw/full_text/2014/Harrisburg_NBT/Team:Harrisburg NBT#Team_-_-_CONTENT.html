Team Information

Upload pictures of your team!

Our iGEM team consists of Zach Fabey, Devon Miller, and Jacob Grove, three seniors in the Nanobiotechnology and Nanofabrication program of Harrisburg University of Science and Technology. With the guidance of project advisors Dr. Leena Pattarkine and Dr. Guray Guven, our three man team has sought to test the knowledge and understanding that instilled in us through our 3 ½ years of undergraduate education. Additionally, our team seeks to prove the bring esteem to Harrisburg University’s Nanobiotechnology and Nanofabrication program, which has been developed in over the past 6 years, as well as to Harrisburg University, an 8 year institution.
