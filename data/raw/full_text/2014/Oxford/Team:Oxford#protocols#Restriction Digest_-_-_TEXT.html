For a double digest, make sure that both enzymes are compatible and that a buffer exists in which both of them can work. Add 6 uL of water into an empty Eppendorf tube. Add 1uL of the buffer and 2 uL of the DNA or plasmid. Add 0.5uL of each enzyme solution. Make sure the restriction enzymes are kept on ice before use. Incubate for 1-2h at 37°C.
