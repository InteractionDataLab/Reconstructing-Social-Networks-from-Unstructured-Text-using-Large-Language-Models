Switch on the lamina flow hood and carry out steps 2 and 3 in the hood. Pipette 5ml LB broth into a 15ml Falcon tube. Inoculate the Falcon tube with strain being grown using an inoculation loop. Grow at appropriate temperature (37°C for E. coli and 30°C for Pseudomonas) with shaking overnight.
