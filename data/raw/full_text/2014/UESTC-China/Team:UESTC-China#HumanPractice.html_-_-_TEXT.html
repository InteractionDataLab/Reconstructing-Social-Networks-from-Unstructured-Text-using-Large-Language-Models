As for that, we tried to use the principle of synthetic biology to make a plant to absorb formaldehyde largely. And on June 6, we conducted a knowledge quiz activity to popularize IGEM, letting more Chinese know the competition, and drew their attraction on synthetic biology. To crystallize the goal, we bought many gifts such as delicate plants, cartoon fans for the people who joined in our activity. Knowledge quiz activity  The two in our first sight were our team leader Yasong Cui (the right) and our wiki manager Zhiqiang Yan (the left), you could see the smile on their face. Both in their hands held our cartoon fans and delicate plants which were used as little gifts for this activity. Behind of them was our activity's banner. Incentives  Our team members were packing our little gifts for the activity in this picture. We bought 200 cartoon fans and 30 green plants as an incentive to attract people who were passing by. Once the person answered our questions can he own a cartoon fan, if he answered two questions correctly then he could win a plant. Participation of the olds  The olds were also interested in our activity. In this picture you could see the old asking our team member question. The slips in their hands are our questions. By answering the paper questions, passers-by could be more eligible about iGEM and our team's project. And meantime, the activity here attracts more and more people to join in. Winners  When the activity was ending, people who answered the questions accurately gained one pot of our plant. The lovely girl in the picture is one of them. The most meaningful thing is that they thought they had learned a lot about synthetic biology. They all felt satisfied with our little gifts.
