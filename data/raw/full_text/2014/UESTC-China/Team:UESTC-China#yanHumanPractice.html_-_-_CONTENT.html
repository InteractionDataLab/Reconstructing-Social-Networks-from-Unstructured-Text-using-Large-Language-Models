Overview

This year , UESTC-China 2014 IGEM has been working on the problem of formaldehyde,we try to use the principle of synthetic biology to make a plant to absorb formaldehyde largely.

First,we conducted a knowledge quiz activity to popularize IGEM,and let more Chinese know the competition,and drawed their attract on synthetic biology.To crystallize the goal,we bought many liitle gifts such as delicate plants,cartoon fans for the people who joined in our activity and did a good job.

Then,we drawed up a questionnaire survey to gather the information of public awareness of synthetic biology and the population of formaldehyde,and last but no least,their perspective on the trangenetic plant which can absorb formaldehyde,if they mind this man-made thing.

Also,we do a on-the-spot research in many residential district,where the houses are furbishing,so the formaldehyde pollution is relatively critical.

Last,we analized ur research data,and come to a conclusion of our activity,and made many charts or diagrams to make people easy understand.In addition to these above,we also communicate with other IGEM teams,such as USTC-China ,HZAU-China ,etc.
