    BCP/BRP

    Molecular biology techniques: SOE(Splicing by overlap extension) PCR

    A. Primary PCR reaction

    Segment1-smtBOP

    primersF5' CGCGGATCCCTAGCGACACTCTTGTAAGTGA 3'
    R5' TTTAGCGATCACACTCATGACAGCAACTCCTTTGA 3'
    PCR system (50ul)parameters
    proceduretemperaturetime
    pfu 0.5ul PreDenature94 ℃ 2 min
    primer F 2ul Denature94 ℃ 30 sec
    primer R2ulAnnealing53 ℃ 30 sec
    smt locus(PCR product) diluted 100×3ulExtension72 ℃ 30 sec
    dNTPs8ulFinal Elongation72 ℃ 5 min
    buffer10ulFinal Hold16 ℃ ∞
    H2O24.5ulCycle30 cycles

    Segment2-amilCP(BBa_K592009) or RFP(BBa_E1010)

    primersamilCPF5' GGAGTTGCTGTCATGAGTGTGATCGCTAAACAAATG 3'
    R5' CCGGAATTCTTATTAGGCGACCACAGGTT 3'
    RFPF5' GGAGTTGCTGTCATGGCTTCCTCCGAAGACG 3'
    R5' CCGGAATTCGCGATCTACACTAGCACTATCAG 3'
    PCR system (50ul)parameters
    proceduretemperaturetime
    pfu 0.5ul PreDenature94 ℃ 2 min
    primer F 2ul Denature94 ℃ 30 sec
    primer R2ulAnnealing53 ℃ 30 sec
    registered parts diluted 100× 3ulExtension72 ℃ 1 min
    dNTPs8ulFinal Elongation72 ℃ 5 min
    buffer10ulFinal Hold16 ℃ ∞
    H2O24.5ulCycle30 cycles
