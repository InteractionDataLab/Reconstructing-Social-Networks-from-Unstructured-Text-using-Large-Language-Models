About Safety

In our university, in order to prevent them from touching toxic and corrosive chemicals, every students who is going to enter a lab are required to wear gloves. Those chemicals are not allowed to be taken outside of our lab for most of those substances will be harmful to human and damage our environment. Moreover, in certain labs which might have physical radiation, like ultraviolet and, students must wear an anti-radiation protection to get the permission to get in. By following these rules of our labs above, experts and students can be well-protected, and our lab also have many other rules that students has to obey for the safety guarantee.
