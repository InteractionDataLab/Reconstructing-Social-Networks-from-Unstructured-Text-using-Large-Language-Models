Simulations

The simulation section of our project consisted of two main parts:

  1. Explore the virtue of our cathelicidin BioBrick system as a new treatment for inflammation in acne from a systems level approach.
  2. Introduce a new style of computer simulations without reaction constants to iGEM in the form of Boolean network models.
