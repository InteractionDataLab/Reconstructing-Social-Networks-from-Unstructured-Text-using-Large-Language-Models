The module aimed to teach the general public about the action and importance of enzymes and bring awareness to our research. On October 16th, 2014, 3 of our team members presented the module to attendees of a Pitt football game. The audience included a wide range of people of all ages and backgrounds. We were able to demonstrate the activity of enzymes using a basic experiment with saltine crackers and iodine. Our module was well-received and we had a great time experimenting in a new and exciting setting!
