The Göttingen iGEM team from the previous year inspired us so much in their propaganda meeting. They not only presented us the essentials of the competition, gave us pointers as to what we can do differently, but most importantly, they also encouraged us to carry their legacy forward. Our team is supported by Prof. Dr. Gerhard Braus, Dr. rer. nat. Christoph Sasse (both from the Dept. of Molecular Microbiology and Genetics) and PD Dr. Joachim Uhrig (Department of Plant molecular Biology and Physiology). They not only give advises on the research of our topic, but also support us in how to organise the human project and raise funds.
