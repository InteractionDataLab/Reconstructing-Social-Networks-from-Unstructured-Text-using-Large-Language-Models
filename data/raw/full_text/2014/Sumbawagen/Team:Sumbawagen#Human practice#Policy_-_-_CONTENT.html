    Moyo Festival

    Moyo festival is an annual Sumbawa citizen festival held every year. This year the festival held from September 27th - October 6th, 2014 at Pahlawan Square of Sumbawa Besar and Moyo Island. This festival is eagerly awaited by the people of Sumbawa and even a attract domestic and international tourist. People gather to get some fun and to learn about Sumbawa culture. Taking this oppurtunity we introduce our ECONEY project as the contribution of synthetic biology in upholding local treasure, Sumbawa honey. More Detail

    Virtual Outreach

    In addition to reach out to people who are in Sumbawa, we’d like to introduce Sumbawagen to the public in Indonesia in particular and the world in general through social media. Sosial media also we used to introduce the syntetic biology and iGEM by save a link to the Sumbawagen wiki. Virtual outreach we used for facilitate anyone to access the information.
