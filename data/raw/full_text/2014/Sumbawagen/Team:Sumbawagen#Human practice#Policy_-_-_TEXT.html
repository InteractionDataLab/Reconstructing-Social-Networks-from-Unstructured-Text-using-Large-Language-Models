This year the festival held from September 27th - October 6th, 2014 at Pahlawan Square of Sumbawa Besar and Moyo Island. This festival is eagerly awaited by the people of Sumbawa and even a attract domestic and international tourist. People gather to get some fun and to learn about Sumbawa culture. Taking this oppurtunity we introduce our ECONEY project as the contribution of synthetic biology in upholding local treasure, Sumbawa honey.
