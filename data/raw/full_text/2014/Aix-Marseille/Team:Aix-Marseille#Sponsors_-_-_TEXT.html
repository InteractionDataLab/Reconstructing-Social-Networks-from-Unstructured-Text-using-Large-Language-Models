As the largest fundamental research organization in Europe, CNRS carried out research in all fields of knowledge, through several institutes. The CNRS has the missions to carry out all research capable of advancing knowledge and bringing social, cultural, and economic benefits for society. It contributes to the development of scientific information and supports research training. The FSDIE is a fund for Aix-Marseille University destined to support of student projects and provide social services for students in difficulty. It raises its funding from a levy on the student tuition fees. Student projects funded by the FSDIE must be described with clear objectives, actions, the evaluation method and presenting a balanced budget. Projects are evaluated competitively and the best are funded.
