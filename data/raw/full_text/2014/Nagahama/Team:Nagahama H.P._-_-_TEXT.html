SAFETY Nagahama - Japan September 17 - September 20 Follow them on Facebook We’re iGEM Nagahama in Japan. We've been contacting all iGEM teams of Japan. On Septembe 17th-20th, a meet-up for iGEM teams of Japan will be held in Nagahama. we've invited UT-Tokyo, Kyoto, Osaka , HokkaidoU, Gifu, Tokyo_Tech, and TMU-Tokyo to this event. This Meet up is The 86th Annual Meeting of The Genetics Society of Japan. This is an event open to public and everyone is welcome to join us! Time: Septembe 17th-20th, 2014 8:30-18:30 Venue: Nagahama Institute of Bio-Sience and Technology （Building: Meikoukan(Lecture Facility),Room No.: 4） Schedule: a 15-minutes-long presentation about synthetic biology for instructors 5-minutes-long presentation and poster session for each team talk about the story of each team free talk, exchange the latest information about iGEM(especially Jamboree), as well as discussing the establishment of each team . Please send us the contact information ( including e-mail address ) of those who decide to come to this event. Our contact e-mail is igem.nagahama@gmail.com.
