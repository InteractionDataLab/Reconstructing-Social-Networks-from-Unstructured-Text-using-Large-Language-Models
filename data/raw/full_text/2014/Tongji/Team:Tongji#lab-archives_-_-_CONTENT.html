    Parts

    We prepared two versions of our parts for you. One is iGEM standard parts, with the official vector. They are universal but may not be so efficient. The other one is our modified parts, with the pET vector and can perfectly work with BL21(DE3) E.coli in our wetlab experiment. You can order from iGEM Registry for our iGEM standard parts or directly order from us for modified parts.
