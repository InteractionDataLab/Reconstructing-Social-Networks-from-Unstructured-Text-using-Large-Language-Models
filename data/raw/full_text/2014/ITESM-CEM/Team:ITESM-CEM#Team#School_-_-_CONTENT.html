iGEM ITESM-CEM Team

Our university is such a great place not only to study, but to develop different abilities, such as entrepreneurship, leadership and team work. It is the second biggest campus of the ITESM system. This Campus counts with several auditoriums and also infrastructure to develop social and cultural activities for the alumni. Specifically to establish and to make this project possible, ITESM CEM has specialized laboratories such as "Chemistry and bioprocess Lab", "Tissue Engineering Lab", "Microbiology and Genetic Engineering Lab", "Scanning Electron Microscope".
