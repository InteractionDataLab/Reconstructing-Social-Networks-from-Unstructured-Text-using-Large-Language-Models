Our Training All of our team members took the basic laboratory courses offered by UBC Risk Management, in accordance with our institutional training requirements . This course covers topics such as WHMIS, biohazards, hazardous chemicals and radioactive material. All work was done within the institutional biosafety guidelines , which were developed to meet the Canadian Biosafety Standards Our Organisms and Parts We only used common BSL1 laboratory strains as our chassis organisms - E. coli and Caulobacter crescentus - and parts derived either from from BSL1 organisms - T7 phage - or are synthetic small peptides. © 2014 UBC iGEM Retrieved from " http://2014.igem.org/Team:British_Columbia/safety " Recent changes What links here Related changes Special pages My preferences Printable version Permanent link Privacy policy Disclaimers
