Put the reagents on ice. Calculate insert vector amount. Add the calculated amounts into eppendorf tube. *Formula 1: insert mass(ng) = (1/6) x (insert length/vector length) x vector mass (ng) *Formula 2: volume of insert(µL) = (total plasmid length/ insert length) x (insert mass/plasmid concentration) Make a reaction mix with everything but the insert. Aliquot the master mix into eppendorf tubes. Add insert DNA. Let incubate at room temperalerature for at least 2h or overnight.
