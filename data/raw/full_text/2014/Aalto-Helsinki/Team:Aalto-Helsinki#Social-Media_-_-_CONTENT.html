Social Media

As a part of improving the knowledge in synthetic biology and promoting synthetic biology as science, we are also concentrating on the visibility of our project.

Website and blog

You should visit our website at www.aaltohelsinki.com.Our websites provide information on our team, project and our partners. We also publish blog on our website. From our blog you can read more specific information on how our project proceeds. At the moment blog is mainly in Finnish, but we might add some brief summaries in English also.

Follow us

You can also follow us on Twitter (@AaltoHelsinki), Facebook, Flickrand Youtube.
