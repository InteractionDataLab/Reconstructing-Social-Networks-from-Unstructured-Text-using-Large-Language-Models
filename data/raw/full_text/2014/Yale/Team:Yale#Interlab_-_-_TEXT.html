Section II: Protocol What protocol did you use to prepare samples for measurement? Devices 2 and 3 were made using PCR with the specific promoter region in the forward and reverse overhangs, and BBa_E0240 as the template. The DNA was then DpnI digested and self-annealed by Gibson Assembly. This device was transformed into Mach1 competent cells, and colonies were screened using PCR before a plasmid miniprep was transformed into E.coli strain DH5α (F- endA1 glnV44 thi-1 recA1 relA1 gyrA96 deoR nupG Φ80dlacZΔM15 Δ(lacZYA-argF)U169, hsdR17(rK- mK+), λ–). What sort of instrument did you use to acquire measurements? Measurements were conducted on the Synergy H1 Hybrid Multi-Mode Microplate Reader manufactured by Biotek. It was configured for monochromator fluorescence, monochromator absorbance, full-light luminescence, and time resolved fluorescence. The temperature control was set to 45°C. Measurements were taken with Gen5 data analysis software. Fluorescence Activity Assay Protocol Grow 1 mL culture with selection marker and inducer overnight. Transfer 150 µL culture into V-bottom plate. Spin down cells in plate with plate spinner set to a temperature 24-25ºC for 4 minutes. The plate shaker will pellet the cells in the v-bottom well over 4 minutes. Remove the supernatant and resuspend in 110 µL 1X PBS. Transfer 110 µL of 1X PBS into clear bottom plate. Transfer 10 µL of suspended cells into 100 µL fresh PBS in an adjacent well. Transfer 10 µL of second dilution into 90 µL of fresh PBS in an adjacent well. Using Synergy H1 plate reader (BioTek, Winooski, VT), measure optical density and fluorescence. Calculate expression of GFP What protocol did you use to take measurements? Shake plate for 10 seconds. easure optical density (OD) at 600 nm. Measure GFP fluorescence by exciting cells at 485 nm and detecting at 528 nm, with a bandpass of 4 nm on each side. What method is used to determine whether to include or exclude each sample from the data set? Fluorescence readings that diverged from the other samples of the same type by more than 50% were excluded from the data set; however, no such issues were encountered. What exactly were the controls that you used? During the growth phase of the cell cultures, the ancestral DH5α strain was also grown in LBmin and subject to the same antibiotic selection marker: kanamycin or chloramphenicol. Clear cultures after a day of incubation signified selection for the retention of the target plasmids. All cultures were seeded at the same time and allowed to grow for the same duration. What quantities were measured? (e.g., red fluorescence, green fluorescence, optical density) Green fluorescence and optical density were measured. How much time did it take to acquire each set of measurements? A time lapse of about five minutes occurred between each data set. The plates were seeded and incubated for 12 hours prior to measurement. How much does it cost to acquire a set of measurements? We are only considering the cost of the non-renewable materials, which were consumed during our measurements.
