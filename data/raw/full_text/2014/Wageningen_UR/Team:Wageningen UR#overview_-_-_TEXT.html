Background Panama disease is caused by Fusarium and is threatening the banana production worldwide. Solution BananaGuard is able to detect Fusarium in the soil, inhibit fungal growth and self-destruct afterwards. Results We have built a fusaric acid dependent promoter, kill-switch parts, antifungals, modeled BananaGuard and much more ...
