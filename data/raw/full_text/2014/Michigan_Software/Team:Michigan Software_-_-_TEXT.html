Choosing reliable protocols is a recurrent problem in scientific research. Experimental practices may vary immensely across different laboratories, diminishing both the reproducibility of procedures and overall scientific method. Learn more about problem In response, we developed ProtoCat, a free online catalog of laboratory protocols. Our software condenses the vast array of protocols into a single location, giving users access to top-rated procedures from across the globe.
