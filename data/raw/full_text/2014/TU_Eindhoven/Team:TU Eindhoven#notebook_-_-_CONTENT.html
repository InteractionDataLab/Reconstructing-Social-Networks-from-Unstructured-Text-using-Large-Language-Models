TU Eindhoven iGEM 2014 Project

This year we will be trying to create bacteria that are able to synthesize their own encapsulation that will protect them from the human body’s immune system. In order to do so, the bacteria will have to be able to create a biopolymer with high anti-fouling properties and attach that to their membranes. We are currently researching what the best way is to achieve this encapsulation, so stay tuned to our website and Facebook page to see the latest updates on our project!
