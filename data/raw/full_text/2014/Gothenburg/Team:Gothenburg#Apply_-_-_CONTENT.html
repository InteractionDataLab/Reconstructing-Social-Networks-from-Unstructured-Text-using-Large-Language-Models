    What is Synthetic Biology?

    There is a 7.5 credits course at the Biotechnology Master programme in Chalmers University of Technology associated with iGEM (KBT225 - Synthetic Biology - development of an iGEM project).

    To get more information about iGEM at Chalmers, please contact Verena Siewers (siewers@chalmers.se) or David Jullesson (davidju@chalmers.se).
