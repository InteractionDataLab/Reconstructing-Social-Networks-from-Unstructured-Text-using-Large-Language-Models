"BTB", Beyond The Bench... In addition to the laboratory work, an important part of the iGEM competition is to inform the world about synthetic biology, the chances and risks that emerge from this research field. Our contribution to spread information is to be found in our Human Practice Section . Thoughts about the ethical aspects of creating genetically synthetic organisms can be found under Ethics .
