CWLC Vision: The Christian Women's Leadership Circle will be an active, intentional partner with Wisconsin Lutheran College in implementing servant leadership as a core value and in developing programs for women who embrace this principle. The WLC iGEM Team thanks the CWLC for funds and encouragement that they provided to our team! GenScript GenScript is the leading gene, peptide, protein and antibody research partner for fundamental life science research, translational biomedical research, and early stage pharmaceutical development. Since its establishment in 2002, GenScript has exponentially grown to become a global leading Contract Research Organization that provides services and products to scientists in 86 countries worldwide. Learn more at www.genscript.com. The WLC iGEM team thanks GenScript for its sponsorship! WLC Biology Department The WLC Biology Department is comprised of an incredible group of educators, most of which hold their PhDs in their respective fields. The faculty who comprise this department are both active in educating young minds in the field of biology and are actively undergoing research to progress the field. Members of the department have served as constant mentors throughout the iGEM team's project this year and were always willing to lend their advice. The WLC iGEM team thanks the WLC Biology Department for their support in the form of funds and in guidance! The WLC iGEM Team also thanks Mr. and Mrs. Henkel for their contribution and support.
