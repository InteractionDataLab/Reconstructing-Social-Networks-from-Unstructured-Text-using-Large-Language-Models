However, just because we are a smaller school does not mean we are not doing incredible things. The school is committed to creating Christian leaders while giving students a rigorous, yet rewarding, education. Many different scientific disciplines are studied at WLC and include pre-professional programs, biochemistry, chemistry, and varying disciplines within the field of biology. Our smaller size also allows students to work one-on-one with professors in research within a variety of academic disciplines.
