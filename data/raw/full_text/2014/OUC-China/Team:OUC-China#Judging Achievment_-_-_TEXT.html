In our policy & practice project we achieved…… 1.Last November, we went to Peking University to have an academic communication with 2013 Peking iGEM team. 2.Consulted professor on several problems in Qingdao Institute of Biomass Energy and Bioprocess Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences. Besides, Dr. Huang made a professional lecture about digital simulation and bioapplication, and provided us with advice on modeling. 3.Invited Beijing Normal University iGEM team to our university. We had a discussion on our projects, and we exchanged gifts to each other. 4.Held the Fourth Science and Technology camp for new sophomores and passed some knowledge of synthetic biology to them. We also held a mini jamboree for them. 5.Attended the Central China iGEMers' Consortium (C C i C) held in Wuhan, China. We presented our project and communicated with others teams in China. 6.We activated and sent two standard biological parts of gas vesicle genes, GvpA and GvpC to Peking iGEM team. And we have helped Peking to test the function of the two parts to make bacteria float. 7.To evaluate our project's application in future, we visited the fish farms and consulted the engineers about the ways of curing and precaution in terms of fish diseases. Besides, we also visited Hu Haibin who is the most authoritative expert in aquaculture feeds in China. And we got information about coated pellet technology. If we combined coated pellet technology with our project, we could apply the feed with outer coating on the ornamental fish industry. 8.In terms of the transportation of biological products, we finished the investigation, and we gave our solutions to the problems. In terms of the double plasmids system 1.Test of the conjugation ability of BBa_J01003. 2.Construction of the parts: BBa_K1439000 , BBa_K1439001 , BBa_K1439002 , BBa_K1439003. 3.Construction of the mini plasmid (OriTRP4-BBa_J04450-pSB1C3). 4.Proved that the mini plasmid can't transfer if there is no plasmid RP4. 5.Constructed the double plasmid system with mini plasmid (OriTRP4-BBa_J04450-pSB1C3) and plasmid RP4. And proved that the mini plasmid can transfer between cells with the help of RP4. 6.Proof of conjugation between E.Coli HB101 and Vibrio harveyi.
