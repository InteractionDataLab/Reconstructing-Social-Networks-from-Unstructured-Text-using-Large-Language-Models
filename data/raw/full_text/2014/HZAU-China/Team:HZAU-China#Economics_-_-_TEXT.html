So, in preparation of future market possibilities, we analyzed the market failure instances of GMO using game theory from economics as a reference for synthetic biology. However, what started out as a possible preparation for future policy making ended up throwing itself over when we carefully examined the underlying assumptions and found the unwarranted ground these policies might be built on.
