The equipment has to be cooled on ice before usage.  2.0 ml of a bacterial pre-culture are transferred to 100 ml of 37 °C warm LB medium  Incubate at 37 °C until the culture reaches a OD600/ml of ca. 0.5  Transfer the culture with the required OD in 50 ml culture tubes  Centrifuge for 5 min with 5000 rpm at 4 °C  Resuspend the pellet in 15 ml cold and sterile 100 mM CaCl2 and incubate for 30 min on ice  Centrifuge for 5 min with 5000 rpm at 4 °C  Resuspend the pellet in 4 ml cold and sterile 1 M CaCl2 and incubate for 15 min on ice  Add 840 μl 87 % (w/v) glycerine (end concentration of 15 %)  Aliquot 200 μl of the cell suspension in Eppendorf-tubes and freeze in liquid nitrogen. Store at -80 °C.
