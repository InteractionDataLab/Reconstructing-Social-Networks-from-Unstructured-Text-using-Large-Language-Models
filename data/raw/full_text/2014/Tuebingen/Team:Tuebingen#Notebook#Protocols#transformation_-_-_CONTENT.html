    Transformation of E. coli

    Procedure

    1. Add desired volume of DNA (not more than 1/10 volume, for transformation of ligations: add complete 10 μL ligation reaction) to the 100 μl-culture in microreaction-tube.
    2. Incubate 30 min on ice.
    3. Heat shock for 45 s at 42°C.
    4. Incubate 10 min on ice.
    5. Add 0.9 ml of culture medium (no AB added) and let the bacteria grow at 37°C for 2h.
    6. Plate on LB plates + antibiotic and incubate at 37°C o/n.
