    Mutagenesis PCR

    Reagents

    200 ngTemplate
    1 µLPrimer forward (2 µM)
    1 µLPrimer reverse(2 µM)
    1 µLdNTP mix (10 mM)
    5 µL10x PCR buffer
    1 ngPfu Polymerase (1 U/µL)
    to 50 µLH2O

     

    Procedure

    1. Run cycler: PCR-program
    2. 5 min / 95 °C
    3. 1 min / 94 °C
    4. 1 min / 56 °C
    5. 2 min per kb / 68 °C
    6. 10 min / 68 °C
    7. infinite / 20 °C
    8. Add 1 μl DpnI, incubation for 2 h at 37 °C
    9. Transformation in E. coli (5 μl)
