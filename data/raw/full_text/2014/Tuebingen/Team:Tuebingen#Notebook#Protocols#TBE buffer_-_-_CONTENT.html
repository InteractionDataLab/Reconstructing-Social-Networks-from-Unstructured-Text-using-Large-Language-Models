    TBE buffer

    Reagents

    1 MTris
    0.85 MB(OH)3
    1 MEDTA 0.5 M pH 8.0

     

    Procedure

    Add the reagents to 1000 ml H20. Adjust to pH 8.3 with B(OH)3.
