Incubate 30 min on ice. Heat shock for 45 s at 42°C. Incubate 10 min on ice. Add 0.9 ml of culture medium (no AB added) and let the bacteria grow at 37°C for 2h. Plate on LB plates + antibiotic and incubate at 37°C o/n.
