After removing the tube from the water bath, repeat the first step, instead adding SpeI as the enzyme. Incubate the tube for 1-2 hours at 37°C. Remove tube from the incubator and place it in a -20°C freezer until the product is to be analyzed by agarose gel electrophoresis.
