Biobrick Blast Online

Biobrick Blast Online, short for BBO, is an online Biobrick alignment and search toolkit made by SYSU-Software and SYSU-China. If you still have trouble or feel monotonous when you want to find some Biobricks containing or related to the known sequence which is needed, you should try our software. BBO is not only containing the latest complete iGEM part registry data, but also helping you search Biobricks in 2013 or 2014 iGEM DNA Distribution.

It is easy and convenient for everyone to use it. Enter your sequence or import the sequence from your file, and then choose the E-Value, database and output format as you like, you will soon get all Biobricks that contain or are related to the sequence, as well as the overall information on those Biobricks. What’s more, our software is nonprofit and practical. Now, move on and have a try!

The online version of software is released: http://biobrickblast.sysusoftware.info/
