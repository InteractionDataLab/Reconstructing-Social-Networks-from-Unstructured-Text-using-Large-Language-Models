The backbone vectors were digested with BamHI and PstI. The inserts were amplified with primer containing the BglII, EcoRI, and XbaI restriction site and the BamHI, SpeI, and PstI hestrictions site. The backbones were ligated with the inserts and Top10 Zells were transformed with the products. The colonies were screent via colonie PCR and positive colones were grown in LB-Cmp-Medium. The plasmids were recoverd via Mini-Plasmid-Preparation.     Figure 1: Backbone production for scaffold double domain vectors.
