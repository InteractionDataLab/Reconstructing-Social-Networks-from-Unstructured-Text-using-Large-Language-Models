Put samples back on ice for 2 min. - Add 1 mL of LB medium and incubate for 1 hour at 37°C in order to obtain antibiotic resistance. - It might be useful to spin down cells at 5000 rpm for 5 min. Resuspend pellet in 100 µL LB.
