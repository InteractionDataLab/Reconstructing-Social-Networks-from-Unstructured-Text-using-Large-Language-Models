The length of the PCR product was controlled via gel-electrophoresis using a 0.8% agarose gel. The PCR product was cut with EcoRI and PstI and then ligated into pSB1C3, cut with EcoRI and PstI. After transformation into E. coli TOP10 and after 24 hours of incubation the plasmid was extracted via Wizard PCR Clean Up Kit (Promega) gaining the BioBrick BBa_K1497008 and the DNA sequence was verified by Eurofins.
