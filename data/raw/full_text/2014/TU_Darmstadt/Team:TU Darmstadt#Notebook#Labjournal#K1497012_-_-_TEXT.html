To get a functional enzyme, we integrated a strong ribosomal binding site (RBS; B0034). For the RBS integration we used the K1497009 as template for a PCR with the following primers : F3H_RBS_Prefix and F3H_Suffix. With gelelectrophoresis and sequencing we verified that we produced the BioBrick BBa_K1497012 .
