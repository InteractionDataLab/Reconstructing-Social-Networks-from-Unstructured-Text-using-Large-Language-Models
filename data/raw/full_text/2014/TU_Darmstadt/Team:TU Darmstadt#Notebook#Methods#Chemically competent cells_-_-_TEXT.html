Mixtures: CaCl2-Solution - 5.55 g CaCl2 - Add ddH2O to 1 L - Sterilize by autoclaving Cryo solution - 0.278 g CaCl2 - 10 ml glycerine - Add ddH2O to 50 ml - Sterilize by autoclaving References: Mandel, M. and Higa, A.: Calcium-dependent bacteriophage DNA infection.
