Start the programme for the NanoDrop and click on "Nucleic Acids" 2. Pipette 1 µL of a water sample onto the measuring surface and click “OK” for initialising 3. Load your blank and click "Blank" 4. Load your DNA samples and click "Measure" The system calculate automatically the 260/280 value and the 260/230 value.
