    • Our Team

    Our University of Calgary iGEM Team consists of 14 undergraduate researchers, as well as 4 graduate advisors and 2 professors. Our team members stem from a diverse range of academic backgrounds including Biology, Engineering, Medicine, Computer science, Neuroscience and Physics. We all share a common goal of creating an innovative project through interdisciplinary collaboration.
