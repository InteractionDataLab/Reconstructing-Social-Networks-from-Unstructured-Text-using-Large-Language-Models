The successful replacement of the constitutive alanine racemase was verified via kanamycin selection and Colony PCR ( alr_Ec_control1 , alr_Ec_control2 ) Annealing temperature: 55 °C Bands as expected (3004 bp) Resulting in the genotype KRX kan:alr, DH5aplha kan:alr repsectivly. Week 5     06/30 - 07/06 Week 5 06/30 - 07/06 The successfull deletion of the chromosomal kanamycin resistance was established by the plasmid Flp705 as described in the Genebridge RedET-System protocol . The plasmid is removed by a temperature shift and the removal of the kanamycin resistance is verified by streaking colonies in parallel on LB-kanamycin and LB agar. Resulting in the genotype KRX ∆ alr, DH5aplha ∆ alr repsectivly.
