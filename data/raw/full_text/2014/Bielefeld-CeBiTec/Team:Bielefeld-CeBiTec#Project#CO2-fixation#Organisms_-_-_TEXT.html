It is obligate aerob. It is know to tolerate and metabolize high amounts of sulfide concentrations. Halothiobacillsu neapolitanus is a chemolitoautotroph modell organism for the carboxysome with a diameter of 120nm. This type of carboxysome dominates in oligotrophic oceans (cso-carboxysome or alpha type). It has to be distinguished from the ccm-carboxysome found in several other marine- and freshwater cyanobacteria (beta type). The first occurence was 1957 by Parker et al. Synechoccus elongatus Synechococcus elongatus is a cyanobacteria which is found in surface waters and freshwater. It carries a carboxysome like Halothiobacillus neapolitanus but it has the beta type.
