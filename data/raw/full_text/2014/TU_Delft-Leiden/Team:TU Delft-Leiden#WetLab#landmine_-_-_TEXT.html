Module Landmine Detection Context Integration of Departments Cloning Characterization Keen to see our conclusions for this module? See the list below! The two best BioBricks for Landmine detection are LD5 (p[J] incl. N-enzymes) and LD4 (p[F] incl. N-enzymes). The N-genes contribute to sensitivity of the promoters. The LD5 construct seems to be the most suitable construct for Landmine Detection. Evidence suggests that the ybiJ and yqjF promoters are activated by 2,4-DNT, but it cannot be totally discarded the possibility that acetonitrile is the trigger of the response of these promoters.
