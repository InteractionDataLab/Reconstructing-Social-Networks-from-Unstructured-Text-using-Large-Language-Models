Qing Zhang

In iGEM hust, I’ve experienced how to consummate a idea, how to complete a task using my own mind. For constructing a plasmid with Ompc and CBP/His, approximately 3 months was spent because of a tiny error; for the growth curves of E. coli with vectors containing different genes, we work all day. One year, we worked together for one aim. To my team members, my dear friends, I’d like to say lucky to be with you.
