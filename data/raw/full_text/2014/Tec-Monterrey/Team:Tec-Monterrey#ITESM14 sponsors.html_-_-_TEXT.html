Cátedra i4 & Tecnológico de Monterrey For this project we were positioned under the scheme of this chair to administrate our funding and role in the school. The students chair (i4) is a opportunity offered by the Tecnológico de Monterrey in Monterrey, through its School of Biotechnology and Health Sciences, to students interested in biotechnology and food industries develop projects in the area. The concept is based on four core values: Imagine. Investigate. Innovate. Incubate. Student teams, like ours, that want to apply your project may reach hatching their own business, running an article in scientific research, process your patent application and compete internationally. We are specally grateful with them for their generous sponsorship, that allowed us to get the genes and some material for the project. GenScript The largest gene synthesis supplier in the US, offered special discount to iGEM teams to get the parts we needed. Thus, we thank for their help in making the project more feasible. IDT-UNIPARTS Based in Monterrey NL, UNIPARTS is partnered with IDT for integral services of nucleotide synthesis. We would like to thank them for the discount they granted on the primer synthesis. Vitara A local water bottling company company based in Monterrey, their aid was welcomed when they provided water bottles for the race.
