    • The Project

    We are developing a low concentration bio-detector.

    The system is based on an intricate gate construct, utilizes quorum-sensing and an altered two-component signalling system. In addition, groundbreaking organic synthesis of a photo-switching molecule – Azobenzene – is used in a pioneering attempt to mimic biofilm formation. We have planned and built a low concentration detector using these elements.
