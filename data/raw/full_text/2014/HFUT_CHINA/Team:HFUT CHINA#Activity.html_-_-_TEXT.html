The attending teams include SYSU, SJTU, XMU, USTC and other professional teams. Meeting included America race simulation, project discussions, software construction discussions, and casual exchanges. The opening ceremony was at 9:00 on August 15, and we did a speech on behalf of the team to show a young and energetic team spirit. In the next Chinese show, we clearly express our project ideas. The participant teacher appreciates our ideas. They thought it newfangled and it had good prospect. In English display links, we introduce the project in fluent English, and what we did could be comparable with other top teams. Through this meeting, not only did we learn a lot from others, but also understood the direction of other top software teams, the gap from the other teams, the future breaking points, enhanced our sense of urgency.
