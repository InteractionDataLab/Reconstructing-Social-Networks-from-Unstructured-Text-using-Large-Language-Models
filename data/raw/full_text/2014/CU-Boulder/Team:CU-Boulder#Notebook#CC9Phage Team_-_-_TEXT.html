Also, for the pCas9(gRNA3) sample, there was considerably more growth on the ampicillin plate than on the chlor plate. This could be because pSB1C3-M13ori-pCas9(gRNA3) successfully killed the BW23115F’ cells as desired or the phagemid was not effectively packaged Transformed pSB1C3-M13ori-pCas9(unmod) into chemically competent ER2738 cells that we know work from July.
