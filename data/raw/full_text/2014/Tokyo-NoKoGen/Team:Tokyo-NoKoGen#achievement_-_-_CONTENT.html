Project Team Result Parts Protocols Notebook Safety Human Practice Achievement

Judging criteria

For Bronze Medal

  1. We completed the Team registration.
  2. We submitted the Judging form
  3. We described our project using a team wiki and parts registry.
  4. We have prepared to present a poster and a talk for the iGEM Jamboree.
  5. We have attributed our all work to the proper person.
  6. We constructed new parts and have submitted the parts.

For Silver Medal

  1. We experimentally validated our parts, BBa_K1339016,BBa_K1339021,BBa_K1339022.
  2. We documented characterization of our parts on the registry.
  3. We submitted new parts that we experimentally validated.
  4. We considered about our encountered problem.>>What is the risk of our project?

For Gold Medal

  1. We characterized the function of existing parts,BBa_K200017.
  2. We helped other iGEM team,UI Indonesia.
