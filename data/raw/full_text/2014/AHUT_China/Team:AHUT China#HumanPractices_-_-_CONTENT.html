MILITARY TRAINING
“

In order to spread iGEM more widely, we are going to advertise igem and synthetic biology during the freshman military training.We made some brochure to them, and explain some knowledge to them in the field of igem and synthetic biology.After that ,they has a great interest on this game and hope to further understand the igem and synthetic biology.

 ”
SOCIETY

In June, we established a iGEM community in Anhui university of technology, community mission is to promote iGEM and synthetic biology ,we also carried out several times to recruit new students in the new semester , many students come to understand and want to join in the community.In order to make students further understanding, we organized a briefing session, to simple introduce the synthetic biology and iGEM to everybody.

APP AND SINA WEIBO

We do a new way spread the knowledge of biology to the public, and we update the dynamic of our team work.
