Steps: 1. Make a master mix with everything except the DNA. Make sure that: • The final concentration of BSA and buffer is 1x. • 5 to 10 enzyme’s unit are necessary for 1 µg plasmidial DNA digest. • Add to digest system the quantity of DNA enough for 0.5 – 1 µg. If the system will be purified from the agarose gel lately, add quantity of DNA for the digest system of 1 – 2 µg. 2. Pipet aliquots to each sample tube and add DNA. 3. Incubate at 37°C for 2 – 3 hours. Check the eletrophorectic profile.
