Hello! Our lab journey is getting to its end. We will use the bioremediation Mercury Bacter (BBa_K1355004) cell growth test in medium with Hg to measure quantity of bioremediated Hg after 3 days of cell growth at concentrations of 20ug/ml, 10ug/ml, 5ug/ml, 2.5ug/ml, 1 ug/ml and 0ug/ml.
