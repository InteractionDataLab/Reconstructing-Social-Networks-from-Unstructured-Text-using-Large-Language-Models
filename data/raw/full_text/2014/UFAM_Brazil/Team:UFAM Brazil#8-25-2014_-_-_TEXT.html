We selected: 6 colonies of DH5a with BB_Essential in PSB1C3. 4 colonies of DH5a with BB_Bioremediation in PSB1C3. 6 colonies of DH5a with BB_Bioremediation + BB_Essential in PSB1C3! We used the following system: Used primers: VR, VF2, for PSB1C3. Electrophoretic PCR’s profile: 1– 4: BB_Bioremediation in PS1BC3 5 – 10: BB_Essential in PSB1C3 11 – 16: BB_Essential + BB_Bioremediation in PS1BC3. Yaaaayyy! BB_Bioremediation and BB_Essential were amplified, with the size we wanted. Now we have our bio bricks cloned separately in PSB1C3! Unfortunately BB_Essential + BB_Bioremediation wasn’t amplified. We made inoculum of the amplified colonies 2 and 3 from BB_Bioremediation in PSB1C3; 2 and 4 from BB_Essential. Also – just out of curiosity – we made an inoculum of samples 1 and 4 from BB_Essential + BB_Bioremediation.
