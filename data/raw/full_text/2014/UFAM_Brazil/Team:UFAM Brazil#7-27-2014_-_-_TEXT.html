Spartaco Astolfi’s advice, instead of using NEB 3 buffer, recommended for double digestion with XbaI and PstI, we mixed buffers 2 and 3, to enhance digestion’s performance. We also added BSA! 1. E0840 not digested 2. E0840 digested with XbaI and PstI 3. K346004 not digested 4. K346004 digested with XbaI and PstI It worked!!! We know that digestion worked, because of the fragment's size after cutting with XbaI and PstI. It was compatible with the biobrick. The size of the biobrick E0840 is 902 base pairs and K346004 is 502 base pairs.
