First test for quantification of GFP induced by mercury in E. coli DH5α. We’re nervous! Will it glow or not?!?!?! We’re going to use for our analysis: - DH5α with construction BB_Essential + E0840 not induced. - DH5α with construction BB_Essential + E0840 induced for 4 hours on concentrations 1ug/ml, 0.2ug/ml, 0.1ug/ml, 0.02ug/ml and 0.01ug/ml of mercury chloride. The procedures were made according to the “Quantification of Green Fluorescent Protein (GFP) induced by different mercury concentrations” protocol (link). The average results with or without induction for 4 hours: It glooooooows!!!! *-* As we can see on the graph, the fluorescence intensity after 4 hours of mercury induction rises to 3624!! We’re super happy about it!!! This was the first test. Tomorrow we will have a meeting to make the experiment design!
