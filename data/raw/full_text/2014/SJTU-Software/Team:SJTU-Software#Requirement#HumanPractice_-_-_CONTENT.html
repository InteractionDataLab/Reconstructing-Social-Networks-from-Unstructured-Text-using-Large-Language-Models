    • Attend 2nd IGEM Conference in NCTU

    Time: 8.3~8.6

    We are very happy that we can attend this conference, we learn from each other, got exercise and visit many scenic spots at Taiwan. The first thing I want to mention is that it gives us a state to change our ideas, discuss with other people, so we can realize the disadvantages and inconvenience of our software. We think people's opinions are most important for our software team, and we will try our best to make our software suitful for most users. Besides that, we also make many friends, we even play cards until 5 o'clock in the morning, pretty enjoy!

    Attend Software Track in USTC

    Time: 8.14

    On August 14th, three members on our team attended the meetup for software track in USTC. Seven software teams attended this meetup including SJTU-Software, SYSU-Software, USTC_Software, SJTU-BioX-Shanghai, HFUT_CHINA, NEAU_Harbin and USTC-China. At the event, we shared our ideas with other teams, did presentations in both Chinese and English, and had professors from USTC comment on our ideas. Haiyan Liu of the committee attended this meetup.

    Meet with student from Peking University

    Time: 8.20

    Team Peking have come to visit us on August 20th. They have put forward some reliable advice on our software and been great help for us.
