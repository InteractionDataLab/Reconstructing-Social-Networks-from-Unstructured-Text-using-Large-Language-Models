Rules and Regulations Basic Safety Equipment Required Gloves - Protect your hands from bacteria and the epoxy Safety Goggles - Protect your eyes from possible spillages / toxic materials Lab Coat - Protects clothes and body from hazardous reagents and fire Basic Sterilization Procedures: Ethanol - Kills 99.8% of all bacteria and is used to disinfect lab equipment and tools Alcohol Lamp - takes care of the remaining .2% and provides a small rising air current which makes it harder for bacteria to contaminate our cultures.
