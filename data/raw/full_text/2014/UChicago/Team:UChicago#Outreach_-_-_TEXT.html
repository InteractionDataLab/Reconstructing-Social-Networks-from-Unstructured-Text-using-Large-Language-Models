We taught four total lessons over two weeks to seventh and eight graders, and had a lot of fun doing so! We came up with several hands-on lesson plans to introduce some basic synthetic biology concepts to the students, ranging from DNA extraction from strawberries to testing genetically inherited ability to taste bitter substances, as well as leading a discussion on real-world applications of synthetic biology.
