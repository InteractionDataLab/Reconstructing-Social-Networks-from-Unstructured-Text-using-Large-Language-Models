Speech
 
Held iGEM speech at Senior High Schools.
Promote iGEM and synthetic biology to local senior high school students.
 
 Theme:

Extending Synthetic Biology-National Hualien Girl’s Senior High School

Date:

July.31st, 2014

Location:

National Hualien Girl’s Senior High School

Aim:In order to let more people understand the idea of synthetic biology, a 2014 iGEM team, TCU_Taiwan, from TzuChi University decided to take their first step in local senior high school. They began with basic biology knowledge of these students learned, gradually into the concept of synthetic biology.
 
    
 
Conference
 
Attend iGEM conference in NCTU.

 Theme:

2014 iGEM conference

Date:

Aug 3rd ,2014 to Aug 7th, 2014

Location:

National Chiao Tung University

Project Presentations,Poster Discussion,Final Discussions and Future Expectations,The Insightful Journey of Synthetic Biology.
