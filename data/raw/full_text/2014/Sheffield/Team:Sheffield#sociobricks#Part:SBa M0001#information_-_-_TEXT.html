As the interviews are carried out with individuals, there are personal biases. However, these may be important when used for data analysis when thinking about why a person may feel this way. Limitations The interviewees may not want to honestly discuss the past events or remember details entirely so recollections may not be entirely truthful or accurate. As time passes since the experience, information becomes more condensed and so critical actions and judgements may be omitted during the interview. As well as this, they can take a very long time to conduct, transcribe and analyse. Further information http://www.ohs.org.uk/ Charlton, Thomas L.; Myers, Lois E.; Sharpless, Rebecca, eds. (2007).
