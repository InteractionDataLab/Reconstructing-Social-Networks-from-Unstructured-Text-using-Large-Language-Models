They can be used for statistical or qualitative analysis of responses. Essentially, it is a type of survey with standardised answers. The answers for closed-ended questions should be exhaustive and mutually exclusive. There are four types of answers that could be used: Dichotomous - two options; one or the other Nominal-polytomous - more than two options, unordered Ordinal-polytomous - more than two options, ordered Continous - continous scale The questions should also flow logically from one to the other i.e. from the least sensitive and general to the most sensitive and specific.
