    SBa_M1001: Lecture

    Description

    A lecture is an educational presentation given orally to teach about a particular subject, for example, by a university. They can be used to present many forms of information including:

    • History
    • Background
    • Theory
    • Equations

    The typical format of a lecture is for the lecturer to stand at the front of the room in front of the class and talk about the information relating to the lectures content. Many lecturers use visual aids to help convey information such as a presentation or video.
