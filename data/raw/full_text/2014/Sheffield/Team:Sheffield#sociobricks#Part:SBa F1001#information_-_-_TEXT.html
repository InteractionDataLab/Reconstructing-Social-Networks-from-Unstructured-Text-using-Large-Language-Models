Limitations Giving people more information and facts regarding the new advances may not always lead to acceptance of the technologies as everyone still has the right to their own opinion; a general assumption that people will be happy after hearing the facts is incorrect. Individual experiences and attitudes are different meaning a desired outcome may not be achieved as people approach the problem or task.
