    Ethics

    The ethics parts presented here are a group of parts that may or may not be needed depending on your universities guidelines on ethics for research as well as your countries requirements for working with young people. The parts below are some of the requirements of The University of Sheffield's iGEM team but may not apply to all teams however they may be useful to explore further for teams to ensure requirements are met:
