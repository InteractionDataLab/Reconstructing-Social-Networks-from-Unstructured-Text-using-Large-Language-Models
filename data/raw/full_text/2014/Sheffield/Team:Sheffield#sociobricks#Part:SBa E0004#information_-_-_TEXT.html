It can also help the participant determine whether they would be able to make a significant contribution to your research. Essentially, it helps them feel at ease as you are able to clearly state what, why and how things will happen during the research. Limitations There aren’t many limitations to providing an information sheet unless not all aspects as state above are outlined clearly.
