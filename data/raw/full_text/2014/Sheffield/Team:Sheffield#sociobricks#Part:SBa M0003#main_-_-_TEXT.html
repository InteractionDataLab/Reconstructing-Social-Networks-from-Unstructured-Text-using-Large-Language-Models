This approach ensures that each interview has the same questions, in the same order to minimise context effects; essentially, answers are reliable and allow easier comparison between samples. These types of interviews may be used as a qualitative research method within focus groups to compare and contrast participant responses and reactions (see SBa_M0006 for more information).
