It is made up of classic course materials such as videos, readings and tests. Alongside this, there are interactive forums for users to build communities for collaboration, support and Q&A. A MOOC can last from 6 to 12 weeks and is accessible 24/7; there may be some live features such as ‘webinars’ (analogous to SBa_M1002 ) that would have to be synchronised by time. Videos that are 5-10 minutes long are used to teach a topic, the learning is then assessed by follow up, multiple choice questions. Students must also produce assignments that are evaluated by peer review or by whoever is managing the system.
