This theory analyses the society structure by interpreting the subjective meanings that individuals impose on objects, events and behaviours. This way, society is thought to be constructed through human interpretation. Since most of the interactionist research uses qualitative research methods, this SocioBrick may be coupled with SBa_A1001 .
