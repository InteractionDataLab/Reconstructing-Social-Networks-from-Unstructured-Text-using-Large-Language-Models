Having evidence of this is also something to fall back on in case something goes wrong or accusations are made of force since the participant signs the agreement. Limitations As there is the option of withdrawal of consent from participating in the research, you cannot always guarantee reliability that you will be able to gain information from your research. However, mostly, this is quite unlikely.
