Meaningful results take time to collate so there needs to be a balance of good marketing research as well as quick marketing research. A broad range of people should be targeted for the research to minimise any bias. The number of people involved is also a highly important factor; the sample size must be large enough to justify the meaningfulness of results. Further information Bradley, Nigel Marketing Research.
