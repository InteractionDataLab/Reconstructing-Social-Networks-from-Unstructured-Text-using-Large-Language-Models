    SBa_E0005: DBS Check

    Contents

    • 1SBa_E0005: DBS Check
    • 1.1Strengths
    • 1.2Limitations
    • 1.3Further information

    Strengths

    It is a legal requirement therefore it allows you to conduct your outreach with the piece of mind that you will not get into trouble by having legal action taken towards you.

    A DBS check for a volunteer is free of charge.

    Limitations

    It takes 13-16 working days in the UK for a DBS check to come through so it must be applied for in advance.

    Further information
