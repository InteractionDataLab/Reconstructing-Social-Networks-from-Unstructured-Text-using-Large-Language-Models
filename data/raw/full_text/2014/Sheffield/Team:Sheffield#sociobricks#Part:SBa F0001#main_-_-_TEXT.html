As a result of this, society is in conflict due to competition for limited resources and thus powerful groups use their power to exploit groups with less power. This domination and power maintains order by any means necessary, even if that means suppressing the poor and powerless. The theory is also heavily used to describe democracy and civil rights, as well as capitalism to control people.
