Time Prep: 5 minutes Run: 2 hours Procedure Weigh out 3.5g of LB agar mix in a weighing boat and add into a conical flask Measure 100ml sterile water into a measuring cylinder and dispense into the same conical flask. Swirl. Stopper the flask with cotton wool and foil. Label on autoclave tape. Format 'iGEM, Room Number'. Move the flask(s) to the autoclave to be sterilised.
