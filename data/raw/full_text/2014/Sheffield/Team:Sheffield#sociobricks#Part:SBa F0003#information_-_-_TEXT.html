Because of this it is an empirical method as such to gain insight into general sociological inquiry. Limitations The incorporation of nonhumans with the capacity to act within networks is a very controversial aspect of the theory. All actors are treated as equal, even if this may not be the case and so social class or power imbalance is not accounted for.
