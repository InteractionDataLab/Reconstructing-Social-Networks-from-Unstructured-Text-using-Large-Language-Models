This means mitigating circumstances can be identified early in order to prevent and limit their impact. Limitations Some of the weaknesses identified at the start may be forgotten or ignored during the remainder of the project; it is important to keep in mind that it is not possible to completely limit risks and there may be unforeseeable risks as the project is running.
