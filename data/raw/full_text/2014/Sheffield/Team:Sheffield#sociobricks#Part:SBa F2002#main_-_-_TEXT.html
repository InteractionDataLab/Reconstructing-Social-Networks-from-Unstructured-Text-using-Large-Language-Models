It should be an effective portrait of the ideas that have lead to the generation of this new product. Product design synthesis should be done in the following sequence: Analysis of the problem the product aims to tackle Introducing the concept, which consists in defining the product based on the analysis Synthesis of the final product. Considerations for the Design of your product: Final price Competitors Efficiency How easy it is to use Maintenance cost/labour/time Risks associated with the product in case of failure. There are many outputs of the product design research carried out; these can be found under SBa_O0007 .
