It may also cause modifications to the scale and scope of the research if, once stated in a concise manner, seem unrealistic. Limitations One limitation of carrying out research is your own position as a researcher which may lead to influence and bias over research subjects, for example, within your own organisation. Therefore, methods, analysis and discussion of the final results from the research on the approval form must be done carefully.
