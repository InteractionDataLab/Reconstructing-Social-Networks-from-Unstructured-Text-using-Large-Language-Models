For all of our experiments, the content of template of 0.5μL DNA lower than 500ng. PCR protocol Temperature Times 95℃ 5min. 30-35 Times 95℃ 30sec. 55-65℃ 30sec. 72℃ 1-2min. 72℃ 10min. Note: for finding the optimal annealing temperature, we have done a series of gradient of annealing temperature first. And as for the elongation time, Taq DNA polymerase could synthesize DNA as the speed of 1000-2000 nucleotides per minute.
