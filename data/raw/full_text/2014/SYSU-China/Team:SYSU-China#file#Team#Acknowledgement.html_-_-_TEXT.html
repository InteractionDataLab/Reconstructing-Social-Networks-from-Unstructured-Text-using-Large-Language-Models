All their generous help lays the foundation of our success. Project & idea support Idea is the spirit of iGEM work, and a good idea should be polished by precious advice. Additionally, undergrad student Cancheng Liu from School of Software, Sun Yat-sen University took the work of writing codes for Wiki website, which saved us from lacking such knowledge. Thanks for your generous help and participation.
