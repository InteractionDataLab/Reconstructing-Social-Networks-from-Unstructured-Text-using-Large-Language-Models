Begin culture the strains 3mL three antibiotic LB culture per tube. Overnight，then add Arabinose to induce 2. Prepare to test Take 200μL from original culture to test OD Discard the culture Resuspend: OD=0.n add n*200 μL Yat-sen spring. (Because LB have very strong signal of green fluorescent) 3. Test fluorescent using the Hybrid reader in the fifth floor Take 200μL from each tube to test OD and fluorescent. (3 wells repeat) Test 3 times in the machine. The parameters: OD=600 Fluorescent: 485/528; 480/507; 510/550; 580/607 Repeat the whole experiment 3 times.
