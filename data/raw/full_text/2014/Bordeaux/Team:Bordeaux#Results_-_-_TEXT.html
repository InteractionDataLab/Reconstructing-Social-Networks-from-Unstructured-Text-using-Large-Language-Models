Different medium and production time were tested and a special BL21 strain was used. Purification (click for more info) The special properties of the ELP were used to purify the produced proteins by inverse thermal cycling (ITC), which is a quick, easy and column-free method for purification of this polymer Characterization (click for more info) Characterization was performed according to different physical and chemical methods. As the protein produced is a polymer, it was transformed into a fiber and its resistance and elasticity have been tested. References [1] Doreen M. Floss et al.
