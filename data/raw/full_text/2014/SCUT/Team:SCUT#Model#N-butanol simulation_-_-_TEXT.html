The model shows that, with high concentrations of NADH in mitochondria, the production of n-butanol will be greatly improved. Introduction We firstly constructed the biochemical reactions of n-butanol producing pathway by Michealis-Menton kinetics and then modeled them by kinetics at the beginning of the reactions when no products have been accumulated. Finally, considering the special environment of mitochondria, we did the work about the effects of the concentrations of NADH and NADPH, which is abundant in mitochondria.
