However, the general public are not the ones who own the knowledge of skills to turn Synthetic Biology into real products. Those who do are gathered in research labs of universities and companies. And they should be our target audience. We hope our efforts can stimulate these researchers' interest in Synthetic Biology, motivating more research work. This is the way we can make Synthetic Biology to change the world. Enterprise Outreach During this past summer, we had in-depth discussion with 10 companies. After shown to our drug resistance research work, they grew great interest in related fields. At last, four companies, namely Genewiz, Dongsheng Biotech, TransGen Biotech and Generay Biotechnology became collaborators or sponsors of our project. Antimicrobial resistance (AMR) occurs when microorganisms such as bacteria, viruses, fungi and parasites change in ways that render the medications used to cure the infections they cause ineffective. In order to deal with this situation, the applications of Synthetic Biology are used. We established a programmed antibiotic synthesis system, which can produce new antibiotics for meeting human’s needs to solve the problem of antimicrobial resistance. Now, We are holding an exhibition at the Guangdong Science Center. The topic of this exhibition is “Toward the Synthetic Biology, Toward a better life”. This exhibition will last for one month, and is free of charge. Exhibition Address: Guangdong Science Center This exhibition is planned and organized by SCUT-China.
