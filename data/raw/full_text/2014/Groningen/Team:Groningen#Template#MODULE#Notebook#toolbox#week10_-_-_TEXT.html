The pSB1C3 plasmids containing the new BioBricks were again isolated. For each transformation, two seperate cultures were grown in LB with 10 μg/ml chloramphenicol. A miniprep was done on the cultures and the insert size was checked with colony PCR, using VF2 and VR as primers. In addition, a restriction analysis was done using EcoRI and PstI.
