In this study three specific devices expressing GFP were measured in labs around the globe. All the devices express GFP but under promoters with variable strengths and with plasmids which have different copy numbers. All these measurements should result in a set with a lot of data. This study will grant all teams a greater insight in the difference of a standard measurement.
