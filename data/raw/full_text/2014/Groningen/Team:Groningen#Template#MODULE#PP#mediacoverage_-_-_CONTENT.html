June 12, 2014
 
The university newpaper placed an article on this year's iGEM team on their website: iGEM team wil superverband maken.

September 17, 2014
 
Our team member Andries won the national preliminaries of Falling Walls Lab: RUG-student Andries de Vries wint Nederlandse voorronde Falling Walls.
 
 
Figure 2: Andries (second from the right) wins the national preliminaries of falling walls lab.

September 26, 2014
 
 
Figure 4: The deadline is approaching for the iGEM team from Groningen.
 
 
The university paper reported on our project again: Deadline approaches for iGEM team.
