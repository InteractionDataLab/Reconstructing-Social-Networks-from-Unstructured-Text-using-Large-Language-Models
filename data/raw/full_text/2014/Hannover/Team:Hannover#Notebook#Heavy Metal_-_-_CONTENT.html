Notebook / Heavy metal

Here you can find a list of lab work concerning our main project "heavy metals". This includes the stable transformation of Arabidopsis thaliana (A. thaliana), the heterologous expression and the quantitative analysis of our T4MBP.ONC = overnight culture ; CBD = Cellulose binding domain ; IPG = Institute for plant genetics ; OD = optical density ; PCR = Polymerase chain reaction ; RT = room temperature ; T4MBP = Top 4 metal binding protein ; ICP-OES = inductively coupled plasma optical emission spectrometry ; ICP-MS = inductively coupled plasma mass spectrometry ; MS = mass spectrometry
