The enzymes used here identify their recognition site, cut within it, and leave behind single-stranded overhangs (sticky ends). In case no recognition site is present, attachment can be achieved by adding them to the 5´ end of PCR´s oligonucleotides. To have orientated genetic recombination, two endonucleases generating different overhangs were chosen for each cloning step. The reaction mix used here is shown in the following table.
