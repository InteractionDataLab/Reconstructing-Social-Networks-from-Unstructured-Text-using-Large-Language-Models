Therefor we performed a DNA isolation from bananas with housekeeping articles to educate interested passerby. Material: Bananas Tap water Table salt Dishwashing liquid as detergent Washing agent with proteases Hot water (60 °C) Ice Teaspoon Tablespoon Fork Isopropyl (alcohol) (cooled) Coffee filter Funnel 50 mL vessels Test execution: preparation of extraction solution: 1 teaspoon table salt 1 tablespoon dishwashing liquid 10 drops washing agent Fill up to 100 mL with tap water Mixing by shaking Homogenization of a banana with the fork Transfer of 3 teaspoons homogenized banana into a 50mL reaction vessel with 30 mL extraction solution. 15 min incubation at 60 °C 5 min chilling on ice Filter the mixture into a new 50 mL vessel.
