    • Construction of 5X-UAS -mCherry-pX330 & 7X -UAS-mCherry-pX330

    2014/8/25

    Materials

    Enzyme: EcoRI, MfeI Buffer: Buffer2.1, Cutsmart Buffer T4 DNA ligase, T4 DNA ligase buffer The usual things needed for transformation

    Methods

    Restriction digestion of mCherry-pX330 plasmid & GAL4-mCherry-pX330 plasmid with EcoRI

    Digestion system (unit:ul)

    DNA EcoRI(V)/MfeI(I) Buffer Total
    Vector mCherry-pX330(454ng/ul) 1 1 1 10
    Vector GAL4-mCherry-pX330(644ng/ul) 1 1 1 10
    Insert 5XUAS(25ng/ul) 8.5 0.5 1 10

    Incubate at 37C for 4 hours

    Ligation for 5X-UAS fragment with mCherry-pX330 plasmid & GAL4-mCherry-pX330 plasmid

    Ligation system: [unit:ul]

    System I System I
    5XUAS+GAL4-mCherry-pX330 5XUAS+Cherry-pX330
    Vector 1 1.5
    Insert 0.2 0.2
    Buffer 2 2
    T4 ligase 1 1
    H20 15.8 15.3
    Total 20 20

    Protocol： a. incubate at RT for 15min b. heat-inactivation at 65C for 10min

    Digestion of ligation reaction with EcoRI

    Digestion system: [unit:ul]

    Ligation reaction II EcoRI Buffer Total
    13.5 0.5 1.6 15.6

    Incubate at 37C for 4 hours

    Transformation

    Till now, Ligation reaction I without digestion: 6.5ul Ligation reaction II with digestion: 15.6ul

    We transform bacteria with Ligation reaction I & II, respectively. [5ul reaction to 50ul competent cell]

    Results

    There were no colonies growing on the plate transformed with Ligation reaction II (digested). There were decades of colonies growing on the plate transformed with Ligation reaction I.

    So, we suspected that the failure of ligation reaction II transformation was caused by the still activated digestion enzyme EcoRI which interfered with the transformation process, since we didn’t perform heat-inactivation before transformation.
