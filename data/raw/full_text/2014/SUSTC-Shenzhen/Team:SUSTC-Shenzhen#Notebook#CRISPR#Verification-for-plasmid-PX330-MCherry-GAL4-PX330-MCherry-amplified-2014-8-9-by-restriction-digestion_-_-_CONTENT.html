    Verification for plasmid PX330-MCherry-GAL4,PX330-MCherry (amplified on 2014.8.9) by restriction digestion

    2014/8/09 Failure, as it is.

    Materials

    • Enzyme & Buffer: EcoRI & AgeI Buffer2.1

    Methods

    1) Digestion system：（unit: ul）

    DNA EcoR I Age I Buffer ddH2O
    PX330-MCherry-GAL4\PX330-MCherry 0.5 1 1 1 6.5

    2) Loading system：（unit: ul）

    DNA DYE TAE Total volume
    PX330-MCherry-GAL4\PX330-MCherry 0.5 2 9.5 12

    Running conditions：130V, 30min

    Results

    Supposed results: Two bands: 4200, 800bp Actual results:

    There was no band appearing on the position that aligned to 800bp pointed by marker, and the bands of 4200bp were also very indistinct, all of which might be caused by the low concentration of our DNA sample loaded for electrophoresis.
