To test whether the failures of those digested groups were caused by the interferences of activate EcoRI or NEBuffer 2.1 which had partly reduced the transformation efficiency of our competent cells, we repeated the transformation experiments. However, this time we performed heat inactivation before transformation. And luckily, we still preserved 5ul DNA enzyme-digested system yesterday… Materials: The usual things that bacteria transformation needed Methods: a. Heat inactivation, 70°C, 10min b. Chill on ice c.
