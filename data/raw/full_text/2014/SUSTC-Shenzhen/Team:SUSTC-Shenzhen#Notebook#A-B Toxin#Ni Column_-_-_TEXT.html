Resuspend the bead s and add 15ml ddH2O to per-wash 2. Add 15ml binding buffer to balance the column and flow out all the binding buffer 3. Close the flow valve , add 15ml supernatant to the column, incubate for 30min, stir the beads when the beads precipitate. 4. Wash with 5ml binding buffer 5. Wash with 10ml wash buffer 6. Close the flow valve, add 1ml elution buffer, stir by pipetting, incubate for 2min, collect the eluent 7. Repeat step 6 three times 8. Wash with 15ml cleasing buffer 9. Wash with 20ml 22% ethanol 10. Store in 3ml 22% ethanol Maintained by the iGEM team SUSTC-Shenzhen.
