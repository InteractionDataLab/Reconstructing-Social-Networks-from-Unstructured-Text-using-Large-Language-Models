Plasmid purification for Struggle I (Part A) & Struggle II We performed plasmid purification for the very one monoclone of the newly transformed group in Struggle I (part A) [named BIG], and the very monoclone growing out in Struggle II [named SMALL]. Materials TIANprep Plasmid Kit Methods Performed as the usual protocol provided… Results BIG 461.4ng/ul SMALL 291.3ng/ul B. Verification with AgeI, EcoRI and BbsI for M+U2, BIG and SMALL A, E test for the results of ligation A, B test for the results of Point-mutation done before by point-mutation PCR Materials Enzyme: BbsI, AgeI, EcoRI Buffer: Buffer2.1, Cutsmart Buffer Methods Digestion system: [unit: ul] DNA A/E/A+E/A+B/B Buffer H2O Total BIG/SAMLL 0.5 0.5/0.5/0.5+0.5/0.5+0.5/0.5 1 8(single)/7.5(double) 10 M+U2 (130.5ng/ul) 2 0.5/0.5/0.5+0.5/0.5+0.5/0.5 1 6.5(single)/6(double) 10 Incubate at 37C for 4 hours Results Assumptions of Ligation: Successful situations: EcoRI site was eliminated. [filled with MfeI] Plasmid has no change when digested with E only. [the same as original plasmid] Plasmid became linearized when digested with A+E. [the same as digested with A only] Failed situation: EcoRI site still exists. Plasmid became linearized when digested with E only. [the same as digested with A only] Plasmid was cut into two fragments by A & E. [the small one is around 800bp] Assumptions of Point-mutation: Successful situation: BbsI site on the 5XUAS was eliminated. [There is only one BbsI site on the plasmid now] Plasmid became linearized when digested with B only. [the same as digested with A only] Plasmid was cut into two fragments by A & B. [the small one is around 800bp] Failed situation: BbsI site on the 5XUAS still exists Plasmid was cut into two fragments when digested with B only. [the small one is around 1300bp] Plasmid was cut into three fragments by A & B. [one is around 800bp, the other is about 500bp] Actual results Obviously, both M+U 2 and BIG & SMALL matched all of the successful items above. So we can now say that the construction of 5X-UAS -mCherry-pX330 plasmid (M+U2) & 7X -UAS-mCherry-pX330 plasmid (BIG/SMALL) has finally succeeded! Maintained by the iGEM team SUSTC-Shenzhen.
