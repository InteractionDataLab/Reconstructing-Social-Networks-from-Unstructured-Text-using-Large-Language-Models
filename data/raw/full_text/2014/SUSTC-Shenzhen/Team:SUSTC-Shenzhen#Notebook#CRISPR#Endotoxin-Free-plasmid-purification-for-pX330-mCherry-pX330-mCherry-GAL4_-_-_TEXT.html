Contents 1 Materials 2 Methods 3 Results Endotoxin Free plasmid purification for pX330+mCherry, pX330+mCherry+GAL4 2014/8/21~2014/8/22 Materials EndoFree Plasmid Kit (TIANGEN) Methods 1. Picked up 2 monoclones from each plate (pX330+mCherry12ml&pX330+mCherry+GAL4) 2. Incubate: Incubate in 18ml ampicillin medium for pX330+mCherry plasmid, and 22ml for pX330+mCherry+GAL4 plasmid. Put in shaker at 37C for 14h [8.12 6:45pm~8.22 8:45 am] 3. Endotoxin Free plasmid purification for pX330+mCherry, pX330+mCherry+GAL4 Performed as the usual protocol provided by TIANGEN plasmid Kit, except that we eluted for three times at the last step with 100ul EB solution.
