We also included attributions to all experts and stakeholders consulted. Question 1: What constitutes suitable and unsuitable use of organisms by humans? Should organisms be ever used in research? Do organisms have "rights" as we have human rights? Question 2:Going from genetic manipulation in microorganism to plants, animals and humans. Should we mark a line cautioning genetic manipulation and put a bound at some point ? Question 3: When we create a GMO, what motive we have in our mind? Commercial, Humanitarian, inquisitiveness, professional honor, national interest, to improve mankind etc. ! Question 4: Is there any feasibility of homologous recombination of our biobricks into other organisms if it gets released into biosphere ?
