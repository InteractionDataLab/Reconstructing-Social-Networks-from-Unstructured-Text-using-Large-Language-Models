The teams officially presented their projects for each other and participated in an Ethics and Collaboration Workshop. The workshop was divided into three parts: A discussion about Collaboration and how the current Swedish iGEM Teams can jointly create a support network to aid in the establishment of new Swedish iGEM teams A discussion about what direction the field of Synthetic Biology will take in the next 10 and 20 years, and how to foster an interest for Synthetic Biology amongst young people and the general public A discussion about the more problematic sides of Synthetic Biology, namely accidental release into the environment, patenting of synthetic organisms, and how these issues should be approached. The Meet-Up Weekend was deemed successful and all participating teams look forward to many years of future collaboration!
