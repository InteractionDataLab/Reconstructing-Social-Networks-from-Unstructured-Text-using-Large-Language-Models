To this mixture, add: 1 µL of forward primer. 1 µL of reverse primer. 1 µL of template. Total volume = 20 µL PCR settings: A general program for PCR is as follows: Initial denaturation at 98oC for 30seconds. Followed by 30 repeats of: Denaturation – 98oC for 10seconds. Annealing – 60oC for 10seconds. Extension – 72oC for 2minutes. Final extension – 72oC for 10minutes.
