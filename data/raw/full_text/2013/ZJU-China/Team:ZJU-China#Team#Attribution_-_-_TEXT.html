We also paid special attention to the biosafety issues with biomineral shell, holin-anitiholin system and ccdB circuit. Besides, standardized riboswitches have been characterized to invest their performance. Human practice is also an important part in our project and we devoted ourselves to the promotion of synthetic biology.
