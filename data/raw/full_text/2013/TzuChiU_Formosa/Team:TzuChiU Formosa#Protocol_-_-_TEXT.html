Ligation Pepare cocktail Insert DNA 5µl plasmid 1µl 10X buffer 1µl ligase 1µl 10mM ATP 1µl ddH2O 1µl total 10µl Place it in 22℃ dry bath for 1hr/overnight * Insert DNA : Plasmid = M ole ratio 5:1 or 3:1 Gel Extraction Kit Excise the gel slice containing the DNA band with a clean, sharp scalpel. Weigh the gel slice. Add 1–2 volumes of diffusion buffer to 1 volume of gel. Incubate at 65°C for 10 min. Centrifuge the sample for 1 min. Carefully remove the supernatant using a pipet or a drawn-out Pasteur pipet. Pass the supernatant through a disposable plastic column or a syringe containing either a Whatman GF/C filter or packed, siliconized glass wool to remove any residual polyacrylamide. Determine the volume of the recovered supernatant. Add 3 volumes of Buffer QG to 1 volume of supernatant and mix. Check that the color of the mixture is yellow. Place a QIAquick Spin Column in a provided 2 ml collection tube. To bind DNA, apply the sample to the QIAquick Spin Column and centrifuge for 1 min. Discard flow-through and place QIAquick Spin Column back into the same collection tube. To wash, add 0.75 ml Buffer PE to column and centrifuge for 1 min. Discard flow-through and place QIAquick Spin Column back in the same tube.Centrifuge column for an additional 1 min at maximum speed. Place QIAquick Spin Column into a clean 1.5 ml microcentrifuge tube. To elute DNA, add 50 μl TE Buffer or water to the center of the QIAquick Spin Column and centrifuge for 1 min. Plasmid Purification Pellet of the cells by centrifugation for 10 min at 12000rpm. Pour off the supernatant and remove excess media. Completely resuspend the cell pellet in 100 μl of Solution I by pipetting or vortex. Add 200 μl freshly prepared Solution II(2% SDS:0.4N NaOH = 1:1)and mix by inverting the tube 5-7 times, then let stand for 5 min. Add 150 μl Solution III and mix by inverting the tube ten times, put on ice for 5 min, then centrifuge at 12000rpm, 10 min at 4℃. Transfer the supernatant into a new eppendorf. Add 3 μl RNaseA, and put in 37℃ water bath for 30 min. Add 1 volume phenol/chloroform, inverting gently. Centrifuge at 12000rpm, 10 min at 4℃. Transfer the supernatant into a new eppendorf. Add 1 volume chloroform, inverting gently. Centrifuge at 12000rpm, 10 min at 4℃. Transfer the supernatant into a new eppendorf. Add 1 volume 95% ethanol, and put it on room temperature for 20 min. Centrifuge at 12000rpm, 10 min at 4℃. Air dry the pellet for 30 min or overnight. Add 30 μl TE buffer to elute.
