    About us

    We are University of Tuebingen's iGEM-Team. Our team mainly consists of undergraduate students of biology, biochemistry, and bioinformatics.

    Our official team roster can be found on the igem.org team page.

    Instructors

    First of all, we want to thank our instructors who have kindly supported us financially throughout the project and with their knowledge and ideas when we were devising our project or struggling with various problems:

    Professor Dr. Oliver Kohlbacher

    Professor Dr. Alfred Nordheim

    Dr. Kristian Apel

    We especially want to thank Professor Dr. Ralf-Peter Jansen and his team for supplying us with a laboratory and lab-equipment. Special thanks also go to Christian Genz (PhD Student), Wolf Hirschmann (PhD Student), Ulrike Thieß (TA) and Ingrid Fetka (TA) for helping us on a day-to-day basis!
