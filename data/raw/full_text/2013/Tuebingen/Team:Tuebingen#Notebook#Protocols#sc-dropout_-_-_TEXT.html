Adjust pH to 5.6. Add agar and autoclave mixture. After autoclaving, let cool down a bit and add antibiotics if required. Perform next step at laminar flow cabinet! Cast plates in petri dishes (approx. 20 mL per plate). Store plates at 4 °C.
