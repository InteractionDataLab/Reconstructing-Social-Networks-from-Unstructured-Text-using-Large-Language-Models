Mix all reagents in an autoclavable flask and fill up to 1000 mL with Aqua dest. Autoclave, let cool down a bit, and add antibiotics if medium is required for selective purposes. Perform next steps at laminar flow cabinet! Add glucose solution. Store medium at 4 °C. If agar was added: cast plates in petri dishes (approx. 20 mL per plate) while medium is still warm. Store plates at 4 °C.
