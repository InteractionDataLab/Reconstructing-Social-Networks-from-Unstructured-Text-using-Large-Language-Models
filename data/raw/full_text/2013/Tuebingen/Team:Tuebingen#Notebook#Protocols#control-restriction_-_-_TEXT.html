Heat inactivate restriction enzymes at 80°C for 20 min. Add 1/10 volume of 10X Antarctic Phosphatase and mix. Incubate at 37 °C for 15 min for 5' extensions. Heat inactivate phosphatase at 70 °C for 5 min. Run control-gel in order to check a ligation / transformation.
