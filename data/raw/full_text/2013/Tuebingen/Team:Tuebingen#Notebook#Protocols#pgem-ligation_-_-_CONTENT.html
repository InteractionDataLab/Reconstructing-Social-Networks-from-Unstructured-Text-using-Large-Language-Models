    pGEM Ligation

    Reagents

    5 µL2x Rapid Ligation Buffer
    0.5 µL (25 ng)pGEM vector
    3.5 µLPCR
    10 mLKCl (c = 250 mM)

     

    Procedure

    Dissolve all reagents in 1000 mL Aqua dest., adjust pH to 7.0 (with NaOH). Sterilize by autoclaving. After autoclaving add 5 mL MgCL2 (c = 2 M).
