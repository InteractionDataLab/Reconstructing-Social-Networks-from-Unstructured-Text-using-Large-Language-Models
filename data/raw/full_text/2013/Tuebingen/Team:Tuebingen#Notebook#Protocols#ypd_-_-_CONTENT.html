    YPD Medium

    Reagents

    10.0 gYeast extract
    20.0 gPeptone
    92.0 mgAdenine-sulfate
    33 ngAdenine
    (16.6 gAgar)
    50.0 mLSterile glucose (40 %)
    to 1000 mLAqua dest.

     

    Procedure

    For plates add agar, for liquid medium leave out agar.Mix all reagents in an autoclavable flask and fill up to 1000 mL with Aqua dest. Autoclave, let cool down a bit, and add antibiotics if medium is required for selective purposes. Perform next steps at laminar flow cabinet! Add glucose solution. Store medium at 4 °C. If agar was added: cast plates in petri dishes (approx. 20 mL per plate) while medium is still warm. Store plates at 4 °C.
