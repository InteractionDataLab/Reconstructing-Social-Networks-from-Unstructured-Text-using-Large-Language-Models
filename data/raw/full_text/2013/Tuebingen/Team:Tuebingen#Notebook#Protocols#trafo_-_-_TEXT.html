Home Project Overview Motivation Receptor Inverter Reporter Plasmids Application Collaboration Safety Team About us Attributions Notebook Protocols Overview: Journal Weekly-Journal Activities SynBio-Day BMBF-Conference School-Project Results Modeling Overview Shipped Parts Sponsors Contact us E. coli Transformation Procedure Add desired volume of (plasmid) DNA (not more than 1/10 vol.) to 100 µL E. coli culture (i.e. to aliquot ) in Eppendorf-tube. Incubate on ice for 30 min. Heat shock at 42 °C for 90 s. Incubate 10 min on ice. Add 0.9 mL of LB-medium and let bacteria grow at 37°C. Plate on LB plates + required amount of antibiotic and incubate at 37 °C over night. If necessary, store plates at 4 °C.
