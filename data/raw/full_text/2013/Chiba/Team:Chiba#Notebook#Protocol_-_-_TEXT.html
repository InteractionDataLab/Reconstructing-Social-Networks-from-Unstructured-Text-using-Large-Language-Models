Day 1 morning 100 ml SOB medium in 1L or 500 mL flask and sterilize E.coli culture grown in 2 mL of fresh LB medium. Day 1 night Inoculate preculture (100 μL-1 mL) to sterile SOB medium.Shake culture vigoruoussly at 20-25 ℃ until OD is 0.4-0.6. Day 2 Transfer the culture to ice 10min. Prepare Wash buffer and Competent buffer by adding 3 mL Dilution buffer to 3 mL of Wash buffer(x2) and to 2.5 mL of Competent buffer(2x), respectively.(on ice) Pellet the cells by centrifugarion at 2500rpm for 6 min. Remove supernatant and genlly resupend the cells in 6 mL ice-cold Wash buffer(1x). Pellet the cells by centrifugarion at 2500rpm for 6 min. Completely remove the supernatant and gently resuspend the cells in 6 mL ice-cold Competent buffer(1x). Aliquot (on ice) 100μL of cell suspension into sterile 1.5 mL microtube and store in deep freezer.
