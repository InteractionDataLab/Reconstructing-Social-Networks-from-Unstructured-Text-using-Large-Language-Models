    The LZU-china iGEM 2013 team consists of 7 dedicated undergraduate students forming a large family. This year, it is our first year to attend iGEM.There is no doubt that we own amazing enthusiasms for researches and experiments.All of us always have been looking forwards to understand more about life science.
Jinbo Yang is our instructor, he offers guide for our research directions.
