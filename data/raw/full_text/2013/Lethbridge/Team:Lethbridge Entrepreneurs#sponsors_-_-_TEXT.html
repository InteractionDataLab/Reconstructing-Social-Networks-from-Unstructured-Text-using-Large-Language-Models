Without your help we would not have been able to achieve rising levels of success that we have seen to this point. As we set out on our journey towards developing our business, the Synbiologica group has seen nothing but a willingness to help and educate. Whether it has been within the university, out in the local community of Lethbridge, across the province and even beyond we have been presented with willing support and advice where ever it was needed. The people and groups below have provided us with advice, guidance, and feedback, training and skills development, In-kind gifts and services, and financial support.
