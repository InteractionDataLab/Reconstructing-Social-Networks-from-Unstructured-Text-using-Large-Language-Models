IGEM UANL team is convinced that the best way to teach is through dynamic and interactive activities, which is why we performed two exhibitions museum-type schedules of games, activities, explanations and colors for all ages. Come and take a look at these great activities! Synthetic Biology Tunnel Last year, we reached about 7,000 people in the exhibition we called Synthetic Biology Tunnel 2012 . This year we continued to grow as we went to a small town named Cuatrociénegas, Coahuila, a place known for its great metagenomic diversity, whose people we believe should be aware of what the treasure they possess. Synthetic Biology Tunnel 2013 was a great experience both for local people and for us. More Genomics Biotechnology Roundabout This year we also designed a new exhibition for all ages to explain the application of Synthetic Biology in the main Biotechnological areas: medical, industrial, genomics, environmental, agriculture and marine. The Genomics Biotechnology Roundabout was exposed in two editions where we caught the attention of more than 4,000 people of all ages.
