    • 1st Requirements Set: Bronze medal.

  • Register the team, have a great summer, and plan to have fun at the Regional Jamboree. Easy.
  • Fully complete the Judging form.
  • Create and share a Description of the team's project using the iGEM wiki and the team's parts using the Registry of Standard Biological Parts.
  • Plan to present a Poster and Talk at the iGEM Jamboree. Count on it!
  • Document at least one new standard BioBrick Part or Device used in your project/central to your project and submit this part to the iGEM Registry.

    • 2nd Requirements Set: Silver Medal.

  • Experimentally validate that at least one new BioBrick Part or Device of your own design and construction works as expected. Document the characterization of this part in the 'Main Page' of that Part's/Device's Registry entry and submit it.
  • Describe one or more ways in which safety implications have been taken into consideration in the design and execution of your project.

    • 3rd Requirements Set: Gold Medal!

  • Help any registered iGEM team from another school or institution.
  • Outline and detail a new approach to an issue of Human Practice in synthetic biology.
