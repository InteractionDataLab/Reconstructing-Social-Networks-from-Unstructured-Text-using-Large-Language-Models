It was founded by Don Isaac Garza on November 8, 1890, the same year the company released its first product to the domestic market: Carta Blanca Beer and this receives recognition gold medal at the world fair in Chicago. The dedication of these workers, which has allowed the development of a wide range of products make it today the largest of the market; It signed an agreement with the Heineken Beer Company positioning itself as a world power in its area. The company offers an extensive portfolio of beverages on the market and meets the taste of different consumers in select countries around the world. Each year, Cervecería Cuauhtémoc Moctezuma invests in research to improve fermentation.
