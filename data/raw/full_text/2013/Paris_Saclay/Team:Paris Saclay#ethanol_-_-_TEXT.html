Protocol : 1. Measure the volume of the DNA sample. 2. Add 3x volumes of ethanol 100% 3. Add 1/10x volume of sodium acetate (3 M, pH 5.2) 4. Add 1µL of glycogen 5. Incubate the solution at -20°C for 30min or more 6. Centrifuge at > 14,000 x g for 30min at 4°C 7. Discard supernatant being careful not to throw out DNA pellet which may or may not be visible. 8. Wash with 1mL of ethanol 70% 9. Centrifuge at > 14,000 x g for 15min at 4°C 10.
