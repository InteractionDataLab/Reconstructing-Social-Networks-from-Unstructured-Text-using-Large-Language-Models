  • Personal Statement of Ma Xin

Ma Xin is the captain of this team in 2013, who has been majoring in biomedical engineering in Beijing Institute of Technology since 2011. Debate, speech, board games, writing and composing are his five favorites. He’d like to take advantage of the new discovery related to human health to create a better and more beautiful life for all the mankind. For him, iGEM provides an excellent platform to take a step forward to achieve his ideal.
