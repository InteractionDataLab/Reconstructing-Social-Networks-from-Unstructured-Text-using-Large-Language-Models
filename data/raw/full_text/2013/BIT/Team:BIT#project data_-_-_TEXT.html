B) Sample Test     We conducted a sample test, in which we detected the concentration of antibiotics in pure water. In the test, we had seven samples: 1) Five SAFE samples, with only pure water; 2) One HARMFUL sample, with a low concentration of antibiotics; 3) One TOXIC sample, with a high concentration of antibiotics. Before the test, we did not know which sample was safe and which sample was harmful. Both of the conventional method (VarioskaFlash) and our new method were adopted to detect the concentration of antibiotics in the sample and here is the result.
