    Example of the method of touch up PCR

    Touch up PCR is a method of amplifying a PCR-product that have bad primers or are troublesome in other ways. This is an example:

    1. 98°C for 2:00 - Denaturing and warm up
    2. 98°C for 0:10 - Denaturing after each cycle
    3. 50°C for 0:30 - Annealing
    4. Temperature increment of 1°C/cycle
    5. 72°C for 2:00 - Elongation
    6. Go to number 2 - repeat 10 times
    7. Go to number 2 - repeat 5 times
    8. 72°C for 5:00 - Final extension
    9. End
