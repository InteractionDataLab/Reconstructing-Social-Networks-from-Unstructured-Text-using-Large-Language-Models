Centrifuge for 1h at 4C and 20000 g. Carefully transfer the supernatant to a new Eppendorf. Be careful not to take any solid parts (this is the cell debris). To the sample add 3 volumes of ice-cold 70% ethanol. If needed divide into several samples. Put samples to -20 for 0.5h to 1 h to precipitate DNA. Centrifuge at 4C for 30 mins at 20000 g. Remove the supernatant. Add 300 uL of 90% ice-cold ethanol. Do not pipette up and down. If the pellet is detached centrifuge for 5 to 10 mins at 4C and 20000 g. Remove the ethanol with a pipette. Let the sample stand open to dry for up to 5 mins. Resuspend in 50 - 100 uL water.
