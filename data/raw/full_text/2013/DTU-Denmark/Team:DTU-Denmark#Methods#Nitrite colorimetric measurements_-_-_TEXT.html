Add 1 level spoon of reagent 1 (NO2-1). Shake vigorously to dissolve. Check that p.H. is between 2.0 – 2.5. If required adjust p.H. by adding sodium hydroxide or sulfuric acid. Reaction time: 10 mins. Pour sample into cuvette. Select method in the spectrometer by entering the AutoSelector (little black tube) Note the result If the read is above the linear range then put the sample back to the tube test, dilute it and try again.
