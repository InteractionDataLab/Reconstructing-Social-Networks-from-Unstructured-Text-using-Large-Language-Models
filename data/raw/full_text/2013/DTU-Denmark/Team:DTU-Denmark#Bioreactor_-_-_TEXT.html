Specifically, we measure nitrous oxide production over a period of 10 hours. The experiment was performed on September 17th . Methods Protocol Bioreactor For reasons that are not understood, the experiment became aerobic in each chamber just after 12:00, and the lab technician began sparging the reactors at a low rate to keep the reaction anaerobic. Since the chambers were sparged with nitrogen, any nitrous oxide we would have produced would have been sparged off as well. Ideally, we would repeat this experiment to have an instance that did not become aerobic, but we did not have time to complete this work prior to the wiki freeze.
