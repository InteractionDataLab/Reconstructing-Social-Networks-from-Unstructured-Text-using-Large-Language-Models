Results Mg of Nitrogen (as Nitrite or Ammonia) per liter measured for varying concentrations of Nitrite added at t=0. All the experiments were performed anaerobically. Samples were measured every 20 min for 3 hours. Conclusions and Discussion Under anaerobic conditions, nitrite is consumed and converted to ammonium. Since we want to convert the nitrite to nitrous oxide, a functional prototype will require either that the pathway to nitrous oxide be designed to outcompete this pathway, or that this pathway that consumes nitrite be knocked out (and the transformants grown in a medium containing plentiful ammonium so they do not need to synthesize it).
