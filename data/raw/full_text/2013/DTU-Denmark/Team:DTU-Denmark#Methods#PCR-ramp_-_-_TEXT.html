For instance: 98°C for 2:00 - Denaturing and warm up 98°C for 0:10 - Denaturing after each cycle 65°C for 0:05 - Annealing ramp 0.1°C/s until 60°C 72°C for 2:00 - Elongation Go to number 2 - repeat 35 72°C for 5:00 - Final extension End A program like that will typically help you if your primers have annealing temperatures in the area between 65°C and 60°C but you don't know the best single step temperature.
