Without their help the project wouldn’t have been such a success. Dr. Achim Dickmanns Achim Dickmanns is a member Prof. Dr. R. Ficners group in the Structural Biochemical Department of the Göttingen University. He performed the high-throughput crystallization assays, which was a really great help for us. Moreover, he refined the crystallization conditions in order to get crystals that are suitable for elucidating the 3D structure of our protein. To find more information about him, please click HERE Dr. Piotr Neumann Piotr Neumann also works in the group of Prof. Dr. R. Ficner. Based on the diffraction pattern, Dr. Neumann calculated the 3D structure of the Listeria DacA protein. He did an excellent job! To find more information about him, please click HERE Annette Garbe Annette Garbe works in the group of Prof. Dr. V. Kaever at the Hannover Medical School. She determined the c-di-AMP concentrations in our samples. To find more information about her, please click HERE Prof. Ivo Feussner Ivo Feussner was really interested in our project from the beginning on. As the head of the Department for Plant Biochemistry of the Göttingen University he helped us with funding our project. Prof. Feussner is also the representative of the GZMB. To find more information about him, please click HERE Prof. Herbert Jäckle We met Herbert Jäckle at the BMBF congress in Berlin this summer, where he was really interested in our project. After discussing our project, Prof. Jäckle offered to help us in attracting more sponsors. To find more information about him, please click HERE Dr. Wilhelm Krull Wilhelm Krull is the Secretary General of the Volkswagen Stiftung. He was mainly involved in the final funding of this project.
