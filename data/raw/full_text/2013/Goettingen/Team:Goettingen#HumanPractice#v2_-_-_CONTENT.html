    • Interview with Prof. Dr. Uwe Groß

    Prof. Dr. Uwe Groß is a professor at Universitätsmedizin Göttingen(medical school of University Goettingen). He and his team mainly focus in the field clinic microbiology, specificly the cross-talk between the parasite and its host cell on a molecular level. In this interview, he talked about the severe situation of antitiobic resistance, and the urgence to promote public awareness about antibiotic resistance.
