    Art & Synthetic Biology

    Synthetic biology is based on artificial systems. Systems that are able to equip chassis with new features. These features have been defined to resolve problems our society is dealing with. Wether environmental, medical or nutritional issues are concerned; the first step will be to recognize such a problem. You really have to deal with that issue before you can start finding a solution. On the other hand stands art. A form of expression that allows to criticize in a nonverbal way and that can encourage thinking processes. Art is a medium for social reflexion and offers the possibility to address the general public.

    The Photo Project - DNA Bodypaint

    The idea behind the body paint was to combine art and synthetic biology. At first sight these two categories seem to be pretty different, but on second sight we realized that they have more in common than we thought. The work in both fields requires creativity, technical skills and a lot of commitment! Especially in concerncs of safety and ethics it's important to explain challenges and benefits of Synthetic Bilogy. Art is a medium for social reflexion and offers the possibility to address the general public.

    The Video Weekend

    When you’re making a video you realize just how much work you can put into things other than labwork. For a whole weekend we transformed our lab into a film studio! Max put great effort into writing our lyrics and planning film and audio recordings. We were greatly supported by the Medienzentrum Freiburg who provided us with excellent equipment and our fabulous camera & post production team who knew how to use it. The team was kept busy with hair & makeup, rehearsing the lyrics, serving tea in cooking pots and making the lab look like a real film studio. In shooting breaks we found time to play music, do (some) experiments and discover several acting talents among our teammates! Especially we had great fun shooting the time-lapse scenes explaining the CRISPR/Cas mechanism of our toolkit in a rather unorthodox way.

    The Soundtrack

    For professionally recording the CRISPR Song soundtrack we spent one extremely entertaining afternoon at the Medienzentrum. From opera singing to rapping everything was covered. We are eagerly waiting for the final cut that will include all video footage and sound files. Until then please enjoy the current 1.0 version by following the links atop of this page!
