Gradient PCR of inserts -As we were not sure about the annealing temperatures of the primers for the MMP inserts, we ran a gradient PCR for the MMP2 and MMP9 inserts with 8 different temperatures between 56°C and 65°C. This Gradient PCR worked very well, the inserts were amplified at almost all of the temperatures.
