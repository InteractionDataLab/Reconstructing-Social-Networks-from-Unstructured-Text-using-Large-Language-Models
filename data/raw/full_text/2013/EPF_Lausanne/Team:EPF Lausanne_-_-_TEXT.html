Taxi.Coli is a highly flexible and adaptable smart drug delivery system. We want to use E.coli as a Taxi, that expresses streptavidin on its surface as Seats for the Passengers, which are the biotinylated nanoparticles that contain a drug. Once Taxi.Coli arrives at the Address, a specific environment, it will produce an enzyme that degrades the nanoparticles, releasing the incorporated drug the passenger gets out of the car. Our effort will be limited to a proof of principle, but one can imagine a wide range of future applications.
