    Protocols

    Plasmid Backbones

    This protocol was developed by Tom Knight. Samples of standard Registry plasmid backbones prepared using this method are sent out in the DNA Distribution kits.

    Why Linearized Plasmid Backbones? Short single stranded DNA fragments will not ligate to 4 bp overhangs. By creating a very short overhang on a PCR of a plasmid backbone, the remnant, when cut with EcoRI and PstI is sufficiently short that it will not anneal at ligation temperature, and will therefore not ligate. This allows us to build high quality construction plasmid backbone without purifying away the cut fragments remaining after PCR.
