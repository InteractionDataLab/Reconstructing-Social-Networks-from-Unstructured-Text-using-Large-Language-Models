We use those buffers for extraction: Buffer 1 Buffer 2 Buffer 3 TE 10:1 (mM) pH8 NaOH 0.2M SDS 1% AcOOK 3M AcOOH 15% (V/V) · Centrifugate of 2mL eppendorf (vortexing cells) · +200µL TE 8.0 (mix become homogeneous) · +400µL Buffer2 (wait 5 min in ice, invert tighltly) · +300µL Buffer3 (no vortex) · Centri. (10000 or + RPM) 4°C, 15’ · Keep the supernatant, on a 1.5mL eppendorf, add 600µL isopropanol · Centri.
