II) Expected Outcome Viable liquid culture of E. coli, W3110 III) Reagants Used W3110 plated colonies LB IV) Actual Procedure We put a colony from our W3110 plate of E.coli into 1mL LB broth and left in shaker at 37◦ overnight. Repeated 4 times, leaving a total of 5 1 ml colonies. V) Results Growth of W3110 liquid cultures of bacteria to create glycerol stocks from. The control tube showed no contamination.
