II) Expected Outcome Plaques from the bands in the CsCl gradient where phage were purified. Expecting plaques down to at least -5 concentration III) Reagants Used BL21 freezer stock LB 1.5x top agar T7 phage from CsCl gradient IV) Actual Procedure Infect 500 µL of LB with 1 loop-full of BL21 freezer stock and put on shaker at 37◦ C for 5 hours. Plate BL21 on an LB plate with 4.5 mL top agar. Dilute T7 using a dilution series. Spot 2 µL of phage from dilution series onto plate using the concentrations 0, -2, -3, -4, -5, and -6. Place in 37◦ C incubator overnight. V) Results The titer of T7 phage showed plaques down to a -5 concentration. This concentration will allow us to perform and EM and examine how pure the phage solution is.
