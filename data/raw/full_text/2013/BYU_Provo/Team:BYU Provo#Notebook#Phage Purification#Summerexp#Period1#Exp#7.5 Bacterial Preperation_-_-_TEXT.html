II) Expected Outcome Viable colonies of BL21 and E. coli B. III) Reagants Used E. coli B and BL21 colonies from plates. LB plates (2) IV) Actual Procedure Streak colonies of bacteria in a 4 quadrant pattern and leave in incubator at 37◦ overnight. Parafilm and store in fridge. V) Results Growth of BL21 and E. coli B. The colonies will remain alive for at least one week.
