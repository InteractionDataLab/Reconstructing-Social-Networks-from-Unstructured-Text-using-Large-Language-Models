II) Expected Outcome Plaques will appear on the plate proving the presence of viable phage. III) Reagants Used T4 purified from 2x top agar LB W3110 freezer stock IV) Actual Procedure Place two loop fulls of W3110 freezer stock in 25 mL LB. Place in 37° C for 1 hour. Prepare top agar by using one part 2x top agar and one part LB to create a 1x top agar solution. Add 4.5 mL 1x top agar to .5 mL W3110 and plate. Peform a dilution series on T4 phage diluting it down to -5 concentration. Spot dilution series T4 phage on plate. Leave overnight in 37° C. V) Results Our titer once again showed contamination on the plate.
