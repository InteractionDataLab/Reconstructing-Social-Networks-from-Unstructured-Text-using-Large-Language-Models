II) Expected Outcome Viable liquid cultures of both W3110 and BL21 III) Reagants Used W3110 and BL21 freezer stock LB IV) Actual Procedure Infect 45 mL of LB with 2 loop-fulls of bacterial freezer stock and let sit at 37◦ C for 1 hour. Infect 4 mL of LB with 1 mL of liquid culture prepared in previous step. Let both liquid cultures sit overnight at 37◦ C. V) Results We were able to grow viable cultures of W3110 and BL21.
