II) Expected Outcome Bacteria that can be retrieved from the freezer and used at any time. III) Reagants Used W3110 liquid culture BL21 Liquid culture 40% Glycerol IV) Actual Procedure Placed .5 mL cultures of liquid bacteria from the cultures on 4.3 W3110 Liquid Culture into cryo vials. Added 500 mL 40% glycerol. Vortexed for 10 seconds. Put in the -80◦ C freezer for storage. V) Results Bacteria will be stored and available for use until no longer viable or contaminated.
