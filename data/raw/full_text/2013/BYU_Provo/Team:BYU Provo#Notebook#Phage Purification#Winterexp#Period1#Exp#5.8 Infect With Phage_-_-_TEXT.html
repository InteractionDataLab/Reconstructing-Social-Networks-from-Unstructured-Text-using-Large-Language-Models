II) Expected Outcome Phage available to purify. III) Materials Used Cultures of bacteria to infect Phage from Lysate (Experiment 5.6) IV) Actual Procedure add 100 microL of phage from lysate to bacterial culture. Allow proper infection time before using in purification protocol. For BL 21: infect with T7 phage for 24 - 48 hours For W3110: infect with T4 phage for 7-8 hours Place in 37◦ C for infection V) Results The phage infected the cultures, as proven by visible clearing when compared to the noninfected control cultures of bacteria.
