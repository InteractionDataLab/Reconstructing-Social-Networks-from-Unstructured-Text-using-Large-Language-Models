II) Expected Outcome Clean, usable stock of phage suspension buffer. III) Reagants Used NaCl MgCl2 Tris-HCl IV) Actual Procedure Put 12.4 g Tris-HCl in a 1 L flask. Added 1 L H2O. Titrated to 7.2 pH using 50% NaOH. (.1 M Tris-HCl) Added 58.44 g NaCl (1M) Added 9.52 g MgCl2 (.1M) Autoclaved and stored in a sealed bottle for use.
