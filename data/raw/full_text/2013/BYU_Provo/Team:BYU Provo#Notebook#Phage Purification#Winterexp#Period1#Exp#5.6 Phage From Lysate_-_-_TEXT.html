II) Expected Outcome Viable phage, easily stored. III) Materials Used 2 mL eppendorf tubes (Qty 20) 5 mL cultures of BL 21 (infected with T7 phage for 24 hours) and W3110 (infected with T4 phage for 7 hours) IV) Actual Procedure Separated the 5 mL cultures into 5 eppendorfs, 1 mL in each. Centrifuged at 3050 rpm for 5 min Gently extracted the supernatant and placed into clean eppendorf tubes. V) Results The phage were still viable and are stable when stored in the fridge.
