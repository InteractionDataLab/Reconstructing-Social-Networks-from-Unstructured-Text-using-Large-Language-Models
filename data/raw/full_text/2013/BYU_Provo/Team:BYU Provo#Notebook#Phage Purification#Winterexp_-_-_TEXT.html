However, our instructor Dr. Grose so wisely informed us that it is essential to begin any research by seeing what others have done first. The first few weeks of lab we focused on researching in the library. We reviewed the work done by others before us in order to understand where we should begin. The research provide a variety of avenues that we could follow and even a few procedures of purification that were explained step by step.
