II) Expected Outcome PCR products of the T4 wild type and mutant capsid protein genes. III) Reagants Used 2 microL T4 wild type or mutant phages in each. TAQ PCR 2.5 microL Thermo Pol. buffer (for high fidelity TAQ) 1 microL primers (B1 297/B1 298) (B1 302/B1 303) 17 microL ddH20 1 microL 10 mM dNTP's .5 microL Polymerase (TAQ) TAQ was run to amplify and sequence the capsid proteins using two sets of different primers. (4 samples) IV) Actual Procedure Put 2 microL phage in an eppendorf tube. Incubate at 99 C for 5 min. Add the above reagents in the order listed to each tube. Run PCR using the TAQ protocol Run in 1% gel 100 mL 1x TAE buffer 1 g regular agarose heat until dissolved completely cool and add 2 drops ethidium bromide pour with 14 well mold and allow to solidify add 2 microliters of dye to 4 new tubes extract 5 microliters of each phage and put them into each of the tubes.
