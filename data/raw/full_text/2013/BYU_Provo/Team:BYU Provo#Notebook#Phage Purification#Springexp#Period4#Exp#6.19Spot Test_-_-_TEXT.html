III) Reagants Used W3110 and BL21 freezer stocks LB T4 and T7 phage from CsCl gradient .2% Agarose Top Agar IV) Actual Procedure Infect 500 µL of LB with 1 loop-full of bacterial freezer stock and put on shaker at 37◦ C for 30 min. Plate on an LB plate with 4.5 mL top agar. Spot 5 µL of phage from CsCl gradient onto plate. Do not invert or move. Leave on desktop for one hour. Place in 37◦ C incubator overnight.
