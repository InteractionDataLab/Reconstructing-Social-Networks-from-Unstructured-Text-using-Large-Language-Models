II) Expected Outcome Viable stock of RNase. III) Reagants Used Sigma-Aldrich Ribonuclease A from bovine pancreas (powder form) 10 mM sodium acetate buffer, pH 5.2 1 M Tris-HCl, pH 7.4 IV) Actual Procedure Add 10 mL of 10 mM sodium acetate buffer to the RNase powder. Heat to 100◦ C for 15 minutes. Allow solution to cool to room temperature. Adjust to pH 7.4 using 1 M Tris-HCl. Aliquot and store in -20◦ C. V) Results We created an RNase A solution that can be used in the phage purification procedure.
