II) Expected Outcome Plaques will appear on the plates proving the presence of viable phage. III) Reagants Used T4 purified from T7 purified from 2x top agar LB W3110 freezer stock BL21 freezer stock IV) Actual Procedure Place two loop fulls of W3110 and BL21 freezer stocks in separate tubes with 25 mL LB. Place in 37° C for 1 hour. Prepare top agar by using one part 2x top agar and one part LB to create a 1x top agar solution. Add 4.5 mL 1x top agar to .5 mL W3110 and .5 mL BL21 and plate. Peform a dilution series on T4 and T7 phage diluting it down to -5 concentration. Spot dilution series T4 and T7 phage on plates. Leave overnight in 37° C. V) Results The BL21 plate with the T7 phage showed plaques. Unfortunately, these plaques were smeared and we were unable to differentiate the concentration. However, the plaques showed that our purification was successful. The W3110 plate with T4 phage showed no plaques. It did have signs of possible contamination so we will rerun the test again.
