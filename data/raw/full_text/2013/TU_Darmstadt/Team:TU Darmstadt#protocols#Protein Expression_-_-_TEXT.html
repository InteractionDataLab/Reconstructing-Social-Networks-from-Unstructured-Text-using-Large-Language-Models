Incubation at 180 repulsion per minute (rpm) at 30°C to an OD600= 4 Transferation of the starter culture into 1 L DYT medium in a 3 L flask resulting in an OD600= 0.2 Incubation to an OD600= 0.6 at 180 rpm and 30°C. Incubation for 15 minutes on ice. Induction of the proteinexpression with 20 mL of IPTG (stock conentration 1M). Incubation of the cell suspension over night at 180 rpm at 30°C.
