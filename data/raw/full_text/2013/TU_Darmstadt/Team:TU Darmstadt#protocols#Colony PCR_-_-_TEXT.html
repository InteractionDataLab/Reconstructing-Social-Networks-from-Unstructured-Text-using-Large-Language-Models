This is necessary due to the fact, that a transformation with the empty vector may lead to antibiotic resistance. Pick one colony with a sterile tip and suspend in 10 µL of ddH2O. Inoculate tip with colony into tube. Pipet up and down to ensure all cells are transferred to tube. Start the PCR using the following programm and 1X mix.
