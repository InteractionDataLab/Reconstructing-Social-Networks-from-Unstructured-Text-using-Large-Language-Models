Add the DNA and incubate the suspension for 5 minutes on ice. Heat shock is done by incubating the cells for 1 minute at 42°C. Add 1 mL of DYT medium and incubate for 1 hour at 37°C in order to obtain antibiotic resistance. Finally the cells are ready to be crossed out.
