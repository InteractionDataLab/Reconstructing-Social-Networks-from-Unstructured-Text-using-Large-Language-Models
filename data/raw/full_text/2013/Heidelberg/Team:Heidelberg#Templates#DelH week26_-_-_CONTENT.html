    Restriction Digest of Preps with PvuI

    Results: --> only clone A1 was positive --> A1 was send to sequencing with primer DN07

    Screening colonies of pSB4K5+DelH (without promotor)

    46 colonies 0.5 Primer (FS73 and FS95) 5 µl Dream-Taq 4 µl ddH2O Results:

    • Colonies B4, B9, B10 and C4 were positive
    • Those colonies were inoculated in 6 ml LB Kan

    Test if electrocompetent BL21-DE3 with DelRest and pIK8.6 contain DelRest

    Screening of plasmid pFSN-D8w (positive control), Triple clone and electrocompetent BL21-DE3 with DelRest and pIK8.6 0.5 µl FS_14 0.5 µl VR 4 µl ddH2O 5 µl Dream Taq Results:

    • All three screenings were positive. Consequently our electrocompetent BL21-DE3 with DelRest and pIK8.6 really contain DelRest.
