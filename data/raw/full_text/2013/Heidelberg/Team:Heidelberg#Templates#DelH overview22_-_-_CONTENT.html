Strategy B: ccdB Construct Selection for mutated and thus truncated DelH sequences will be minimized by using a ccdB strategy. DelH will be integrated in a pSB4K5 backbone flanked by KpnI and BamHI sites (pFS02). The pSB6A1 backbone will be assembled with a ccdB cassette flanked by KpnI and BamHI sites (pFS03). The final DelH plasmid will be assembled by restriction - ligation of pFS02 and pFS03 to pFS05.
