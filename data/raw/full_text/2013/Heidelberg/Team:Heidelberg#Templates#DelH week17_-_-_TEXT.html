New Gibson Strategy Gibson Strategy without mRFP Try a new construct without mRFP, so that we can exclude the red clones from the screening. Therefore we are going to use a new reverse primer for the Backbone which includes only the terminator of the mRFP, but not the mRFP. The primers for the Backbone are HM11 & HM17. The construct is named pHM04 and shown in week 16 were the idea was specified. For first approach pHM04, we will amplify the backbone without mRFP, avoiding backbone reassembly due to ribosome binding site homology. In the second strategy pHM05, we will additionally introduce a tetracycline resistance, to ensure integration of the insert.
