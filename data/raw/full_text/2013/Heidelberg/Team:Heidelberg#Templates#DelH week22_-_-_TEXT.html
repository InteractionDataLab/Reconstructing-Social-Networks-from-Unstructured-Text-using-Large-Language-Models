The following day, the LB Chlor plate was covered with colonies. Since BL21 are in contrast to BL21 pLys not ntaurally resistant to Chloramphenicol, they are obviously contaminated. In order to check whether this is also caused by an Indigoidine plasmid, extensive evaluation of stocks and transformed cells will be undertaken. Test for Chloramphenicol Resistance All stocks of competent cells were streaked on LB Chlor plates and incubated at 37°C ON.
