Achievements. Introducing NRPS to Synthetic Biology.

Automated Design of Custom Non-Ribosomal Peptide Synthetases Production of Synthetic Peptides in different engineered E. coli Hosts Invention of a Universal Tag for Non-Ribosomal Peptide labeling in vivo Using the Tag for peptide characterization and quantification of production Application of delftibactin NRP for sustainable Gold Recycling Introducing our standardized Framework for Production of Synthetic Peptides to the Synbio Community: RFC 99 and RFC 100Having lots of fun and very much looking forward to the Jamboree in Boston
