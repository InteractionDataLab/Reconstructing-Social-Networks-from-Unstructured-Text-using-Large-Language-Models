Step 2: Precipitation of Gold Nanoparticles D. acidovorans culture in ACM was divided in 50 ml aliquots and centrifuges at 3,750 rpm for 30 min. Supernatant was transferred to fresh tube. 300 µl gold chloride stock solution [µg/ml] was added to D. acidovorans supernatant, suspention mixed and incubated at RT for 20 min.
