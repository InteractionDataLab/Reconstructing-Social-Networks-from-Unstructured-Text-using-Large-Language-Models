These plasmids were obtained from iGEM DNA distributions kit (listed below). We added 10ul of water into the samples and left them for 5 minutes before using the parts. (1)2012KitPlate2,6O(pSB1A3) (2)2012KitPlate2,8A(pSB1A2) (5)2013KitPlate5,15J(pSB1A2) (6)2013KitPlate5,24E(pSB1A2) We obtained colonies of sample (1), (2), and (6), but did not "5". We prepared sample (5) again and got it. We cultured each transformant in 3 ml LB liquid medium containing ampicillin (37°C, 125rpm overnight).
