Remove competent cells (100uL aliquots) from -80°C and thaw on ice. Note, aliquots can be thawed GENTLY by hand if time is an issue* 2. Add 1µL ligation mix to thawed cells and incubate for 30 minutes on ice. 3. Heat shock in water bath/heat block for 60 seconds and put back on ice for two minutes. Remember to turn off the water bath* The following steps must be done near a flame* Don’t forget a growth control, especially if you aren’t screening using antibiotics* 4. Add 400uL LB Broth and incubate at 37C for 2 hours. Note, 1 hour is also sufficient* 5. Spread plate entire 500µL. Turn off the flame* 6. Incubate overnight (<18hrs) at 37°C.
