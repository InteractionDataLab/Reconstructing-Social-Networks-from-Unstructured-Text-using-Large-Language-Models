The narrative follows the life of synthetic biologist Mr. Cool, as he attempts to save the microscopic residents of YeastVille from the dire situations of an oil spill and a few unwelcome guests, among other disasters. The book is targeted to an audience of children from the ages of 6-10, and explains the basics of synthetic biology in language that the children can understand. The book will be published in hard copy, as an eBook, and also as an audiobook.
