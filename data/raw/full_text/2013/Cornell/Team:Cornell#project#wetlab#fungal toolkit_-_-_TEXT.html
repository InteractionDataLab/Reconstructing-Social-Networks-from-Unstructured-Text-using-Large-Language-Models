Regulatory Elements Provide modular protein expression controls for genetic constructs. Selectable Markers Allow for the selection of desired fungal transformants. Characterization Quantifies gene expression levels using fluorescent proteins. Homologous Constructs Enable integration of genetic constructs into specific regions of fungal genomes. Biosafety Implements measures to prevent horizontal gene transfer and induce a kill switch.
