It is derived from Saccharomyces cerevisiae and it catalyzes the conversion of isoamyl alcohol to isoamyl acetate, a compound that gives off a banana scent. This is the final step of our pathway, completing the neutralization of isovaleric acid. Select "Lab Book" to view our procedure in detail!
