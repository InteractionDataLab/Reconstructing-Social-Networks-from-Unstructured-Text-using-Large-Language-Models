We documented and submitted a new part, yjgB, to the Biobrick Registry. Silver We experimentally validated that our new yjgB part works as expected. We documented the characterization of our part yjgB on the “Main Page” of its Entry in the Biobrick Registry. We documented and submitted a new part, yjgB, to the Biobrick Registry. We worked to increase awareness to the issues affecting the security, safety and ethical issues affecting synthetic biology through several means. We developed internet video presentations, conducted a radio interview with CBC Radio One, were featured in a news article in a local newspaper and organized debates for upper-year high school students regarding the ethics of synthetic biology through participation in the Shad Valley program.
