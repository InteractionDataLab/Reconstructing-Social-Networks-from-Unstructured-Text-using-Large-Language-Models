Our team created a collection of software tools which address specific technical synthetic biology challenges while simultaneously advancing the way in which users interact with computing environments. Our software will support the workflow of the scientific process by supporting various steps: research, brainstorming, building, testing, troubleshooting, iteration, and analysis. The combination of human computer interaction, bio-design automation, and experimental design makes our effort unique in the iGEM experience and closes the loop on the design-build-test methodology.
