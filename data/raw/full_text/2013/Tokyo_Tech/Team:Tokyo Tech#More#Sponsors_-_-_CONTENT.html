    • Sponsors

    Thanks to our sponsors, we were able to carry out our experiments and join the iGEM Jamboree. We really appreciate their kind help.

    Tokyo Institute of Technology Found project

    The Foundation supports students' projects financially, especially the projects of those who belong to the Department of Bioscience and Biotechnology.

    Aizawa Foundation

    Aizawa Foundation was established by Masuo Aizawa, the ex-President of Tokyo Tech. The Foundation supports students' projects financially, especially, the projects of those who belong to the Department of Bioscience and Biotechnology.

    Mr. Isao Ono

    He graduated from Tokyo Institute of Technology in 1963. He is now working as a consultant for Hitachi Solutions and a director of the Tokyo Institute of Technology Alumni Association. He supports our team personally through the Tokyo Institute of Technology Fund project.

    Mr. Fumio Hombo

    He graduated from Tokyo Institute of Technology in 1963. He is working as director-general of Tokyo Institute of Technology Alumni Association now. He supports our team personally through the Tokyo Institute of Technology Fund project.

    Promega KK.

    This company gave us the part of restriction enzyme we used at no charge.

    Leave a Nest Co., ltd.

    This company introduced our team to Promega KK. and gave us the opportunity to be given material support.

    COSMO BIO corporation

    COSMO BIO Incorporated company provides with the donation to the organization which participates in iGEM for the purpose of support of the young researcher who bears progress development of life science.

    Integrate DNA Technology

    The Integrate DNA Technology (IDT) is a company that supports DNA composition, especially, the transformation of ideas into real products.

    MEDICAL & BIOLOGICAL LABORATORIES CO., LTD

    The MEDICAL & BIOLOGICAL LABORATORIES (MBL) CO., LTD is a company that supports DNA composition, especially, the transformation of ideas into real products.

    Japan Student Services Organization

    This organization aims to do the assistance of student scholastic and prepare the appropriate environment for students to study in Japan. By doing so, they want to develop human resources creative for the next generation society. Furthermore, they want to develop human resources who can play an active role in international.

    IKEDA SCIENTIFIC Co., Ltd.

    IKEDA SCIENTIFIC is a specialized trading company that provides the most advanced scientific and biotechnological equipments. In addition, they provide their original attractive items to scientists.
