    • Bleed-Through Test
    1. Follow the general protocol to obtain inoculated culture. 2. Follow the protocol for a 96-Well Plate setup with these settings: - Highlight the first three wells in a row and set all [Color] Wait to 0 except for Green Wait. - Select every other wells that are not being tested and set all [Color] Wait to 0 except for the Black Wait.

    Note: Addidtional lit wells on the columns serves as replicas. 3. Align the 96-well Plate to the wells on the app and then tape them down securely.
