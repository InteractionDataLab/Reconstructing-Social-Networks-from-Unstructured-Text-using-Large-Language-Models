There are four restriction endonucleases we used in our experiments: EcoRI, XbaI, SpeI, PstI. Single Digestion Volume (μl) Plasmid DNA Up to 1μg 10×FastDigest Buffer 2 Enzyme 1 ddH2O To 20 Total 20 2. Mix gently and spin down. 3. Incubate at 37°C in a heat block or water thermostat for 30 min. 4.
