Excise gel slice containing DNA fragment of interest. 2. Add 3×sample volume of Buffer DE-A. Incubate at 75° C for 15-20 min or until gel melts completely. Add 0.5 × Buffer DE-A volume of Buffer DE-B. 3. Binding sample DNA(Centrifuge at 5,000 - 6,000×g for 5 min). 4. Add 800 μl of Buffer W2 (5,000 - 6,000×g for 1 min). Repeat wash with Buffer W2. Centrifuge empty plate for 5 min at 5,000 - 6,000×g to remove residue W2. 5. Elute with 50 μl of Eluent or Deionized Water.(5,000 - 6,000×g, 5min).
