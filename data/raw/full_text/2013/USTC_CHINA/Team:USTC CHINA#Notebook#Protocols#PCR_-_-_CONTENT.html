    PCR

    Performed with TaKaRa PrimeSTAR® GXL DNA Polymerase
    Volume (μl)
    5×PrimeSTAR GXL Buffer10
    dNTP Mixture（2.5 mM each）4
    Forward primer10~15 pmol
    Reverse primer10~15 pmol
    Template DNA10pg~10ng
    PrimeSTAR GXL DNA Polymerase1
    ddH2OTo 50
    Total50
