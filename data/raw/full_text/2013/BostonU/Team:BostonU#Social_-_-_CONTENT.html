    Bowling at Jillian's

    On June 4th and 5th, the Wellesley Desyne iGEM team visited for two days of collaboration and learning. We spent the time on Monday sharing knowledge of basic biology and practicing with the Clotho app Eugene. On the second day, we learned about one another's projects while providing constructive feedback. To end the two day event, we went to Jillian's for a friendly round of bowling.

    Museum of Fine Arts

    The BU iGEM team met up with Wellesley Desyne at the Museum of Fine Arts, Boston.

    Team Dinner

    We had a team dinner at Border Cafe in Harvard Square!

    Smoothies

    During a week long heat wave, we met up with Wellesley Desyne at Life Alive, a smoothie shop in Central Square, Cambridge for some cold drinks.
