This enzyme directs the degradation down a pathway that prevents the formation of trimethylamine. As previously mentioned trimethylamine has been known to cause cardiovascular disease. This new pathway ultimately leads to glycine which is a nature substance in the body. This enzyme is the centerpiece of our experiment.
