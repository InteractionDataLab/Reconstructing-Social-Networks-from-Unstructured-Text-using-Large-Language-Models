Future work All parts are still not perfect, needed to be optimized. 1.It may be necessary to mutate PalkM to reduce the leakage of the system. 2.Different reporters needed to be tried to get different relationships between input and output. 3.Mutating AlkR will probably promote the interaction between AlkR and alkanes, and improve the function of AlkR-PalkM system. 4.Addition of genes related to biosufactant biosynthesis may increase the sensing range of Alk-Sensor to alkanes. 5.For biofuel synthesis, Alk-Selector provides more opportunities to engineer the strains. Many methods related to building a library can be used in this field with the help of Alk-Selector.
