Tuesday 6th August Results from 5th August transformation: Vial Ampicillin Plate Plasmid Insertion Colony Count 1 (Main) Yes Yes 100+ 2 (Positive Control) No Yes 100+ 3 (Negative Control) Yes No 15 This indicates that there is still an issue with the ampicillin , possibly a problem with the stock powder used. A final test with both old and new ampicillin was carried out and compared without plasmid insertion. Wednesday 7th August Vial Ampicillin Plate Plasmid Insertion Colony Count 1 Old Ampicillin Yes Yes 100+ 2 New Ampicillin v2 No Yes 100+ 3 Positive Control Yes No 15 Results indicated that the ampicillin source may not have been fully functional, therefore a new source of ampicillin powder was located and ampicillin was remade. Thursday 8th August Preparation of 4x ampicillin plates and 4x no drug plates for storage in the fridge.
