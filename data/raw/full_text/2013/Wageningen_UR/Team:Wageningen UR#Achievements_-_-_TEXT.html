Please justify its novelty and how this approach might be adapted and scaled for others to use info Last year, Paulien Poelarends studied the communication of IGEM teams with the public. The knowledge she obtained was combined into a workshop What can we, as IGEM members, learn from our own conversations with the public to improve the dialogue between the science and society. This is an introspective approach to human practice that will let us learn how to communicate with public instead of having a one-way conversation about synthetic biology. The knowledge we obtained was also applied to the Science cafe, an evening where the public was invited to discuss with experts about synthetic biology.
