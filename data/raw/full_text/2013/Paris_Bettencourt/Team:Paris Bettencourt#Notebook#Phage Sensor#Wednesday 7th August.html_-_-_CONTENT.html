Transformation Biobricks BBa_K649304 (with pSB1C3 backbone) and BBa_J04450 (with pSB1A3 backbone) into NEB Turbo (heat shock trafo) 1) Thaw competent cells on ice. 2) Place 20 ul of cells in a pre-chilled Eppendorf tube. 3) Add 2.5 ul of plasmid (from Biobrick stock) 4) Mix gently by flicking the tube. 5) Chill on ice for 10 minutes. 4) Heat shock at 42 °C for 30 seconds 5) Return to ice for 2 minutes. 6) Add 200 ul LB medium and recover the cells by shaking at 37 °C. Ampicillin: 15-30 minutes Chloramphenicol: 60-120 minutes 7) Plate out the cells on selective LB (10ul) 8) Incubate at 37 °C. Transformants should appear within 12 hrs.Result: BBa_J04450: many colonies, BBa_K649304 no colonies, plate rest
