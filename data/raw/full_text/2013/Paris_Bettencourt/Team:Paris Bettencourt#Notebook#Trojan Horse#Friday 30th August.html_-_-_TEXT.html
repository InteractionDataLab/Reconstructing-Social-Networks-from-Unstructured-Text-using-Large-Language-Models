Put 1 mL of cells in each tube. Add in tubes respectively : nothing, 100uL phages of litmusGFP-LacZ1 & litmusGFP-LacZ2, litmusGFP-Cm1 & litmusGFP-Cm2 litmusGFP-Cm1 & litmusGFP-Cm2 are used as negative control. - Incubate 45 minutes at 37°C - Serial Dilute every tube until 10-5. Plate for each tube 100ul of 10-5 and 10-3 dilutions on IPTG+Xgal and LB and 100uL on AMP+IPTG+Xgal and LB.
