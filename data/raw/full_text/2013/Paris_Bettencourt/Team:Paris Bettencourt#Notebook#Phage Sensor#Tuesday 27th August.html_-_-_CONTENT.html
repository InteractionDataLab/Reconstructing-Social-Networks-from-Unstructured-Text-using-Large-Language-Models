Tuesday 27th August

Starting liquid cultures and Miniprep of pS.005 and pS.006.

Starting liquid cultures sSP001, sSP002, sSP010, sSP011, sSP012, sSP020Miniprep of pS.005 and pS.006 1) Pellet 2x 9ml of liquid culture (4000rpm, 10 min) 2) Discard supernatant 3) resuspend the cells in 250ul of resuspension solution 4) add 250ul of lysis solution, mix by inverting 4-6 times 5) add 350ul of neutralization solution 4) centrifuge for 5 min 5) transfer supernatant to spin column 6) centrifuge for 1 min 7) discard flow through 8) add 500 ul wash solution and centrifuge for 1 min , discard flow through(repeat this step) 9) centrifuge for 1 min to remove left over liquid 10) transfer the column on a 1.5ml tube 11) add 50ul of elution buffer and incubate for 2 min 12) centrifuge for 2 min 13) Nanodrop the concentration and freeze at -20°Colony PCR
