Bottles were autoclaved. Additionally 1 flask of 500ml of LB broth was made in the same fashion. Glycerol Stocks Single colonies from agar plates were picked and used to innoculate 5ml LB broth overnight. 750ml of overnight culture was added to 250ml of 60% glycerol in a cryotube. Two sets of Glycerol stocks were used for each of sD001, sD002, sD003, sD004; one set was frozen at -20ºC and the other set was frozen at -80ºC.
