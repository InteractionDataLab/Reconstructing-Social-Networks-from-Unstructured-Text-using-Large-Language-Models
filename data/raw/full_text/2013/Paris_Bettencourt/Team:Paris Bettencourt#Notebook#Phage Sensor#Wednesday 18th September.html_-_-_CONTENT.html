at 16° for 1hTransformation 1) Thaw competent cells on ice (Gibson Kit competent cells) 2) Place 20 ul of cells in a pre-chilled Eppendorf tube. 3) Add 2.5 ul of plasmid (from Biobrick stock) 4) Mix gently by flicking the tube. 5) Chill on ice for 10 minutes. 4) Heat shock at 42 °C for 30 seconds 5) Return to ice for 2 minutes. 6) Add 200 ul LB medium and recover the cells by shaking at 37 °C. Ampicillin: 15-30 minutes Chloramphenicol: 60-120 minutes 7) Plate out the cells on selective LB (10ul) 8. Incubate at 37 °C.
