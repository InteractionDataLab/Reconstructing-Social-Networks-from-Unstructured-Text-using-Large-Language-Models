Now we can also follow the number of E. coli because it has RFP. In 20 mL of LB we added 100 µL of the M. smegmatis liquid culture (O.D. = 1.2) and 100 µL of the E. coli liquid culture (O.D. = 1) so we get 1:1 ratio. We measured the O.D. and the CFU for time 0, 1, 2, 3 and 6 h. To do the CFU for E. coli we plate the dilution on LBA + Kan + Amp, and for M. smegmatis we plate on LBA + 30 µL.
