Incubate at 37 °C. Transformants should appear within 12 hrs. Result: Colonies (around 10) Vincent and Aude In silico cloning: sRNA gBlocks into pUC18 and litmus28-GFP Successful with both Gibson assembly and regular cloning. See file: “in silico cloning trojan horse.geneious” Vincent streaking Ortiz’s strain ELS-41 and ELS-13 containing Litmus28i_J23115-B0032-GFP and M13K07, respectively.
