Cells were incubated on ice for 30 minutes. Cells were transfered to heating block at 42C for 45s. Cells were returned to ice for 2 minutes. 200ul of LB Broth was added to the cells and they were incubated at 37C for 1h. After incubations cells of each transformation were plated on two plates: one with 5 ul , and the other with 100 ul of the post recovery cells culture.
