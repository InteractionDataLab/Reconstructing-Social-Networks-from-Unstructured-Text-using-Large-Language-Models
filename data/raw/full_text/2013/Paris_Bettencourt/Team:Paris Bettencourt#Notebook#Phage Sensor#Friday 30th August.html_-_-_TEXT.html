DpnI digest (SPCR5,6,7) add 8ul DpnI per tube and digest for 1h at 37°, shaking Gel SPCR5,6,7 1%, 100V, 20min PCR purification of DpnI digested SPCR5, SPCR6, SPCR7 Add 5 volumes of Buffer PB to 1 volume of the PCR sample and mix. To bind DNA, apply the sample to the QIAquick column and centrifuge for 30–60 s. Discard flow-through. Place the QIAquick column back into the same tube To wash, add 0.75 ml Buffer PE to the QIAquick column and centrifuge for 30–60 s. Discard flow-through and place the QIAquick column back in the same tube. Centrifuge the column for an additional 1 min. Place QIAquick column in a clean 1.5 ml microcentrifuge tube. To elute DNA, add 30 μl Buffer EB to the center of the QIAquick membrane and centrifuge the column for 1 min.
