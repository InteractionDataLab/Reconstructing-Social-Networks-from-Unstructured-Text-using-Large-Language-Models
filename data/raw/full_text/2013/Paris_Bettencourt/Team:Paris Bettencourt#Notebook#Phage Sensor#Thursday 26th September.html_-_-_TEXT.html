Discard flow-through and place the QIAquick column back in the same tube. Centrifuge the column for an additional 1 min. Place QIAquick column in a clean 1.5 ml microcentrifuge tube. To elute DNA, add 50 μl Buffer EB to the center of the QIAquick membrane and centrifuge the column for 1 min.
