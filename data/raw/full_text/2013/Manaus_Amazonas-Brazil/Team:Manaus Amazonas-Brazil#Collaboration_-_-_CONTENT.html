    • Collaboration

    At first, Marcelo Boareto (team of the USP) came to Manaus to give us a course about Math modeling. In this course we knew a lit bit more about the iGEM’s competition spirit and about the history of the USP’s team and the project that was in competition on 2012. He also told us about the project of this year (Detecthol). While Marcelo Boareto was in Manaus, we changed experiences about synthetic biology and we showed the university and laboratories In August, there were the Jamboré event, where each Brazilian team presented about their projects, this event counted with a deep discussion about synthetic biology, and also each team could help another, be on the laboratory experiments wiki development and math modeling.
