This collaborative effort taught new and verified many important facets in making data standards: how to communicate in a common vocabulary in a global environment, to weave in standardization with the already strict timetables of iGEM teams, and to understand what components of the characterization process can be standardized. Most importantly, however, this process showed iGEM-er's eagerness to share. Check out the results of this collaborative efforts at The Depot.
