We also wrote “Ten tales on Synthetic Biology” , a book of short stories showing five negative and five positive dream visions on SB. • We collaborated with the Norwich , Purdue ("Characterization collaboration"), Copenhaguen ("Bricks of knowledge") and UC Chile (promoting "Research" the game) teams. • We performed a successful pre-field validation test . • And we did all this with one of the pillars of Responsible Research and Innovation: transparency.
