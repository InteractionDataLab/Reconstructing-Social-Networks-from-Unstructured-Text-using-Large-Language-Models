Different protocols used during the project are described below. Reaction for one sample: Component Sample DNA x μL (up to 1,0 μg) Buffer (10x) x μL (for 1×) Restriction enzymes x μL (10 units/μg DNA = 1 µL) H2O x μL 20-25 μL Buffer H (Roche): 50 mM Tris-HCl, 1 M NaCl, 100 mM MgCl2, 10 mM DTE, pH 7.5 at 37 °C. Incubate for (at least) one hour at 37 °C. Inactivate the restriction endonucleases by heat, incubation at 80°C for 10 minutes and centrifuge shortly.
