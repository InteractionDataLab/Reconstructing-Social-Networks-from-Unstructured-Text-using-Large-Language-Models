    6th of June

    Finaly Chosen Peptides

    Today, we chose our final peptides after a long time research. In particular, we went through the literature in order to find peptides that satisfy the aforementioned criteria:

    1. No postranslational modification
    2. Do not affect E.Coli
    3. Affect S. Aureus
    4. No possibility to be dangerous for human (based on MIC concentration)
    The list with the final peptides is:
    1. Magainin
    2. Brevenin
    3. Maxim
    4. Signiferin
