Make a 10X stock solution of IPTG by adding 10µL of the 1000X IPTG (1M) in 990 µL of LB with the right antibiotic. Make 1/50 times dilutions of the overnight grown culture. Wait untill the OD at 600nm reaches 0.4-0.6.
