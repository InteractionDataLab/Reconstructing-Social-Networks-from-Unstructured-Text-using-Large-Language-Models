Wiki Interviews Meeting with RIVM Human Outreach Extras Acknowledgements Collaborations PR iGEM Netherlands Meet with Christopher Voigt Photo Gallery Brainstorm Astrid Moerman Because of my interest in biology, especially related to the human body, I studied Biomedical Sciences in Leiden. After I obtained my bachelor’s degree, I also wanted to explore the field of technology and chose the master track Biomedical Engineering at the TU Delft. Although I have always focused on the (dis)functioning of human cells and tissues, this iGEM experience also makes me aware of the importance and, of course, the fun of working with micro-organisms. I have discovered that the combination of biological systems and technological applications is very interesting! Being responsible for the human practice and the PR of our project, I hope that, besides having a cool project, we can also make more people aware of the impact of synthetic biology.
