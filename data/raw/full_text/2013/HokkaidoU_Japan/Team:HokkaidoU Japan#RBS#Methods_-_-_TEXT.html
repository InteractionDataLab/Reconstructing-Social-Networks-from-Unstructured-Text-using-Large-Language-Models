These RBSs have enhancer sequence (GCTCTTTAACAATTTATCA) and SD sequence (SD2:GG, SD4:GAGG, SD6:AGGAGG, SD8:TAAGGAGG). We constructed SD8 from synthetic oligos (forward:SD8-f, reverse:SD8-r). We constructed SD2, SD4, SD6 by PCR (forward:EX-f, reverse:SD2-r, SD4-r, SD6-r, template:SD8). fig.1: oligos. RED: enhancer sequence, BLUE: SD sequence. fig.2: RBS construction. fig.3: our parts. Assay We ligated TetR repressible promoter (pTet), each of the new RBSs', LacZα and double terminator. Using this construct we performed β-Galactosidase assay.
