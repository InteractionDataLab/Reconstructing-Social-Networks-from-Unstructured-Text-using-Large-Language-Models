At high school At high school, we conducted seminars for students having interests for science. After our presentations and brain storming with students, we took questionnaires from them. fig. 8 We found that more than half of students had positive view on gene recombination, but one third of students viewed it negatively. fig. 9 o our delight, when we visited high schools, nevertheless they knew little about iGEM, all of the students showed interests for synthetic biology! And some students seemed to be attracted to research activities and iGEM. Observation From our questionnaires, we got images for gene recombination from public. Positive Gene recombination is used for plant breeding. This technology might have both merit and demerit. It is useful for our livelihood. Gene recombination is more useful than selective breeding because we can get improved phenotype immediately when we use this technology. Negative Released recombinant species could negatively affect the environment. Genetically modified food may have bad effect on our health because snack food package often says “Non- recombinant”. By results of our survey, we found some important facts that a lot of people still have prejudice for gene recombination and little correct knowledge for DNA or our field. We should improve such a present state. By our human practice activity, we might give people a chance to know what gene recombination is actually. Next year, iGEM will be 10 years old. By making use of this good opportunity, we would like a lot of people to come to know the gene recombination technology itself must not wrong or bad. In the same way as other common technologies, it’s the matter of how to use it. Succeeded in our endeavor, our activity and project was broadcasted in local TV program. This was so a big step for us to know our activity and iGEM broadly. Our planning We believe that to let a lot of citizen realize our view, we must continue activities like this year; such as getting popularity about iGEM, educating people for our field, discussing how to use technology with them and to appeal to the mass media.
