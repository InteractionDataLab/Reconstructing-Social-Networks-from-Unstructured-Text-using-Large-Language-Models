    iGEM Japan

    There was an iGEM-Japan meeting at Tokyo University Festival on May 19th. We couldn’t go meeting however our posters were displayed.

    iGEM-Japan exchanging meeting was held in Tokyo Metropolitan University on Aug 25th. The teams that took part in are TMU-Tokyo, Tokyo_Tech, UT-Tokyo, Tokyo-NoKoGen, Kyoto and HokkaidoU_Japan. We participated in it through skype. At there, each of teams introduced their own project and its state of progress. We exchanged ideas and promoted friendship. We contributed in developing of iGEM as education and competition for undergraduate students.
