Project is developed considering the BSL of our facility which is 1. Member of laboratory crew has been selected on experience. Holding at least one month laboratory experience was criteria for selection. MSDS files of chemicals were studied before using. All experiment carried in a convenient place (laminar hood, regular hood etc.). Waste both biological and organic materials were handled properly. Offical Safety Form 1.
