On Friday, 16th of August, we organised a workshop with architecture students and our team. We held a discussion on topics such as what we believe the possibilities from this relationship are and if it is a good thing. The main aim was to identify and explore ideas and concerns raised by these topics. The students expressed a variety of views, such as concern and excitement.
