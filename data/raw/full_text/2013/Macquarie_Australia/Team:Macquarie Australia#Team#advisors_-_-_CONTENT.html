    • Our Advisors

    The 2013 Macquarie iGEM team has received wonderful advice throughout this project from students that have previously competed in iGEM at Macquarie University. Additionally, they have also helped to supervise the team in the lab for tireless hours out of their own time.

    Joanna Hare

    Jo was a member of the original 2010 team. Now a Ph. D student with Dr. Louise Brown, her advice and guidance in the lab has been invaluable.

    Elizabeth Daniel

    Liz is currently completing MPhil in Biophysics with Dr. Louise Brown. Liz as an adviser has provided us with supportive encouragement and advice. We are grateful for her assistance throughout the project.

    Elle Worgan

    Elle was a member of the original 2012 team. Elle assisted and guided us with publications and her previous iGEM experience.

    Silas Vick

    Silas was a member of the original 2012 team.

    Yagiz Alp Aksoy

    Yagiz provided the team with his expertise when he was available. He was also a member of the original 2010 team.
