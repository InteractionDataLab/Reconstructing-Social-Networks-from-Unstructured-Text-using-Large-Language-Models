We aim to introduce ITB_Indonesia team and the device we built as a cheap, fast, and easy-to-use aflatoxin-sensing cellular systems. This activity was held during ITB Class of '93 Reunion to maximize the exposure and to gain more audience. We discussed with the alumni about our project and give them souvenirs as our thank-you note for supporting ITB_Indonesia team. According to the data we gathered, 32% of the respondents feel that developing a cheap and reliable aflatoxin-sensing device is an urgent need.
