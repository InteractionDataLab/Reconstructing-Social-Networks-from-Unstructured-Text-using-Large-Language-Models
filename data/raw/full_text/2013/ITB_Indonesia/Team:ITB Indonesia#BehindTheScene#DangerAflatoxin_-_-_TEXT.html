Aflatoxins are carcinogenic and mutagenic, heat stable (237-289oC), and can accumulate through the food chain. The presence of aflatoxins especially aflatoxin B1 on  food such as peanuts, milk, and corn  can increase a person's risk of  liver cancer. According to data from the International Agency for Research of Cancer, 1 in 4 people who have liver cancer caused by aflatoxin exposure [1]. From that fact we know that aflatoxin exposure can become  serious problems to our health. Reference [1] International Agency for Research of Cancer (IARC).
