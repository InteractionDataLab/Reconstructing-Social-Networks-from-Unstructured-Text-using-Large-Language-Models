We gave questions to randomly chosen respondents, representing diverse background such as high school students, college students, and working people. The responses gathered illustrated that synthetic biology was fairly known by college student but not many of them are aware about its potential and applications. Through this activity, the students become interested to join in this field, more aware of future prospects for developing synthetic biology in Indonesia, and of course willing to support the ITB_Indonesia team for iGEM 2013.
