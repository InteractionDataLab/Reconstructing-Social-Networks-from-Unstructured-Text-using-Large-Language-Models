    Outreach

    Synthetic biology isn’t well-known to the general public and we would like to increase awareness concerning its applications and safety. We decided to work on different areas. We organized a symposium, gave workshops in secondary schools, and went to farmers to talk about the project and about synthetic biology in general. We also sent press texts to publicise both synthetic biology and iGEM.
