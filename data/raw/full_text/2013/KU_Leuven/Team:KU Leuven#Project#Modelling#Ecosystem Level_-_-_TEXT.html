Because of practical reasons and difficulties with regulations, we were unable to conduct a field experiment for our BanAphids this summer. Instead we turned to our best friend again: the computer. We created a model, based on diffusion and convection equations, to simulate the effect of our pheromones on the ecosystem. Ecological impact of the BanAphids On this page you will get a thorough explanation of the steps taken, the software used and the results. This data can then be used to better plan field tests and provides useful information for the end user.
