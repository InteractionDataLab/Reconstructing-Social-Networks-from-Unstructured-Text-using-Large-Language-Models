Team:Tokyo-NoKoGen - 2013.igem.org

When we talk about iGEM and synthetic biology, we talk about dealing with E. coli and DNA and all that... but hey! Isn't that dangerous? Don't you get infected from E. coli? Isn't genetic modification a bad thing? People might wonder. This page provides you an insight to how safe synthetic biology is, and how fun it can be.
