Colony B from pSB1C3-S1-S5 and colony D from pSB1C3-S2-S5 seem positive candidates. Those two colonies were streaked out on LB + Cm and incubated overnight at 37°C. PCR was performed on S3 and S9 using primer pair S34910. PCR was performed on S6, S13 and S14 using primer pair S16 (Sander). PCR was performed on S16 using primer pair true-S16.
