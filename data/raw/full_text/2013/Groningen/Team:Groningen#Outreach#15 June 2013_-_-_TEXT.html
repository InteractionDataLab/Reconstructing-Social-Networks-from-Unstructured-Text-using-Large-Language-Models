The festival is centred around 5 big stages which play different types of music. In the middle of the was a big science dome, were a lot of universities and companies presented their cutting edge research. Mostly centred around sustainability. We from the RuG made bacterial paintings. This was done by taking big square petri dishes and plating bacteria on LB Agar, for the colours the following bacteria were used: Colour Bacteria White Bascillus subitilus Yellow micrococcus Red Serratia marcescens The paintings displayed various things Many people were interested and it was a lot of fun. iGEM 2013 Groningen Retrieved from " http://2013.igem.org/Team:Groningen/Outreach/15_June_2013 " Recent changes What links here Related changes Special pages My preferences Printable version Permanent link Privacy policy Disclaimers
