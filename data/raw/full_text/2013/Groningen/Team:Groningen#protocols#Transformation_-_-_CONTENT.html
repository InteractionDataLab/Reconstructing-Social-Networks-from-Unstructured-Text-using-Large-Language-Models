    • B. subtilis transformation

    The Losick protocol is used
    5 Day 0: Streak out the Bacillus strain of use and plate this on an LB agar plate o/n at 37°C.

    Transformation (D-Day): 1. Pick up a nice big colony and drop it in 2 ml of completed MC (1x) (see sub1). 2. Grow at 37°C for 5 hours (longer if the culture is not really turbid). 3. Mix 400 µl of culture with DNA* in a fresh tube (i.e. 15 ml tubes loosely closed – aeration) 4. Grow the cells at 37°C for an additional 2 hours . 5. Spread the complete 400 µl reaction mix on selective antibiotic plates, and incubate at 37°C overnight.

    (*usually 1 µl. Then 10 µl of Qiagen plasmid miniprep or <1 µl of chromosomal prep)
