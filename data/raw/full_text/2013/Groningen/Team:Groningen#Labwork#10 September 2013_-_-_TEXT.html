A new PCR reaction is made, using the genomic DNA of wild type B. subtilis 168 as control. Claudio pSB1C3-S1-S5-S13 and pSB1C3-S2-S5-S14 plates showed colonies. ColonyPCR on 5 colonies per plate was performed using VF2 and VR primers (annealing temperature 58°C). The samples were checked on agarose gel 0.8% beside the positive control (pSB1C3-S1-S5), which is not shown in the picture. All the colonies screened were positive candidates. The bands were all around the expected height: 1075 bps ('I am gonna get lucky' cit.). Colonies C and D from pSB1C3-S1-S5-S13 were inoculated in LB + Cm and incubated over night (Sander).
