As the polyphosphate is too large to pass out of the pores of the MCP, it accumulates in itm preventing phosphate return to the system/ environment. We have two suitable strains for ppk1 gene (Tanneralla forsythia ATCC 43037. 2.Kingella oralis ATCC 51147) in Rory’s lab. We will clone both native genes and signal-fused gene to compare their enzyme activities. Also, the signal-fused ppk1 will be co-expressed with native MCP and tagged MCP (developed from Surface team) to test the localization of enzyme and Pi clearing efficiency.
