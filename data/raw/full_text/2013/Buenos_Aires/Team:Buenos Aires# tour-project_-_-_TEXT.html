Thereby you could easily afford multiple measurements with the very same device. The project will focus on the measurement of a primary pollutant: arsenic. However, its design provides an easy way to incorporate various contaminants already identified, such as nitrate / nitrite, lead and hydrocarbons. We aim to build a home kit, so people could use it without any previus knowledge or science background.
