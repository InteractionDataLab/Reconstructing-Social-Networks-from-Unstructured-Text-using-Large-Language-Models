Using the lux operon and circadian promoters, we designed two plasmids: the Luciferase generator is to produce bioluminiscence, and the substrate generator recovers substainably the substrates necessary for the reactions. We achieved to transform Synechocystis with the luciferase generator plasmid, and verificated light emission in a luminometer. Al being lit by bioluminescent bacteria!
