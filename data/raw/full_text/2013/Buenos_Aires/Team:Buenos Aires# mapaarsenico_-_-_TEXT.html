To accomplish that, we are trying to make a map indicating the level of arsenite pollution in each location. However, we must know that due to the strange distribution of arsenic in groundwater,the detection of arsenic in a sample doesn't mean that the whole location is contamianted. We have collected sample water from different towns around the country, specialy from the nearby of Buenos Aires, and we have measured the arsenic concentration of each sample. After that we have made an interactive google map in which you will be able to see the concentration of arsenic in each location. Please, click on any picture if you want to see the interactive map.
