Used RcoM_F and RcoM_R with a Tm of 52 and 56, an elongation time of 1:15 minutes and 32 cycles. This is the Tm established by our analysis with APE when creating the primers. The Tm from the sides of the primer tubes would suggest a Tm of 60 C but this is taking into account the full length of the primer (including the overhang). RcoM is 495 bp and the master mix has an extension of 30 seconds / kb. 30-35 cycles is recommended for gDNA amplification. I used 2 uL of template DNA (~50 ng).
