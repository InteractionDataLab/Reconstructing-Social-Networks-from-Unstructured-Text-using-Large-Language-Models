Project:AbstractHay fever curE.coli

We are making E.coli which has ability to cure hay fever.
We will give following functions to E.coli.

1. Expression of IL-10 receptor to E.coli.
2. Phosphorylation of STAT3.
3. Preparation of gene array with HlyA and L-12 promoter and receiving the STAT3.
4. Preparation of gene array with TolC and HlyB and HlyD promoter and to receive the STAT3.
