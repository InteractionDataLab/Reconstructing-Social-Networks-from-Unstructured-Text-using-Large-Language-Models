Our aim is to submit a composite biobrick for E. coli which will allow control three genes of your choice using Red, Green and Blue ( RGB ) light. We will also submit the three constituent bio-bricks for each light input. See our project section for details about our bio-bricks. We plan to extensively characterise our system and provide a mathematical model of our system for quantative control of outputs. See our modelling section .
