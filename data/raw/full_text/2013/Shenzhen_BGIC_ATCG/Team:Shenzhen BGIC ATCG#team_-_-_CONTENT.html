    Consortium

    Shenzhen_BGIC_ATCG is a special consortium. Team members are from all over the country. In the 3rd or 4th year in university, some undergraduate students came to Shenzhen and involved in a joint program called Innovation Program in BGI College. After lectures introducing synthetic biology and iGEM given by our instructor K2, 29 students from ten schools have joined the consortium BGIC_ATCG, which include: - Huazhong University of Science and Technology, China - Sichuan University, China - South China University of Technology, China - Wuhan University, China - University of Electronic Science and Technology of China, China - Southeast University, China - South China Normal University, China - Anhui Medical University - China University of Geosciences - Jinan University
