We specifically focused on synthetic biology as for many in our audience this was a term which they’d never heard of before. The presentation was also given with the aim to share with the audience the experiences which we gained while working on a scientific project. We received an enthusiastic response from our audience along with messages from inquisitive freshers who were interested in learning more about synthetic biology. We hope our presentation ignited an initial spark towards research in synthetic biology in some members of our audience. Below are some pictures from our presentation.
