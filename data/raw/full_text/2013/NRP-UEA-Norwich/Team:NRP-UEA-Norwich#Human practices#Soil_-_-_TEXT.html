Building up a library of antimycin gene clusters will broaden known pathway (biosynthetic) diversity with the foresight of facilitating genetic engineering. In the long term, pathways could be engineered to create new variants of antimycin with unique or enhanced properties. Soil Samples To aid our project we need numerous soil samples and sediments from various locations so we can find as many different Antimycin producing streptomycetes as possible..
