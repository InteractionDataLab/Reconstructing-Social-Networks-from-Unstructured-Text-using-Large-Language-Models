Claudia Ismania Samogy CostaUndergraduate in Biology - IB/USP

Claudia is fascinated by molecular biology. She began her academic study in the field of chemistry, but his passion and fascination for the microscopic part of life was stronger, which led her to change course.

She is interested in education and dissemination of science education in Brazil, she participated in events of the Human Genome Project for the dissemination of science and accessibility of knowledge, particularly in public schools. Began to participate in the iGEM project because she believes that she has much to learn from it, and is an amazing experience. In his spare time enjoys spending time with family and friends.
