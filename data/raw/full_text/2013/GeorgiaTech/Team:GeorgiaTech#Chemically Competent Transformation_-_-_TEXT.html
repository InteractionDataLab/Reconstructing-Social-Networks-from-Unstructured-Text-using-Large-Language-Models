Estimated Time: 1 hr 11 mins Be sure to sterilize the bottle with EtOH, and not contaminate the media when loading it into the heat cycler Plate 20 µL and 200 µL from each tube using the spreaders on LB /agar plates with the appropriate antibiotic Leave plates at 37°C overnight.
