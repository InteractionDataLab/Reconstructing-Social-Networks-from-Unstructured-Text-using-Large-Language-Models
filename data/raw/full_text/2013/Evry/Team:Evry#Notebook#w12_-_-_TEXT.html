We pre-cultured a remaining glycerol stock at 37°C overnight: 10 mL LB 10 µL Carbenicillin 1000X 50 µL cells from glycerol stock 03/09/13 The transformations succeeded and made liquid cultures for glycerol conservation. Also, we isolated the plasmid AceB-GFP from the pre-culture of the 03/09/13.
