    Acknowledgement

    We wanted to thanks people that support us during the project and make it possible !

    • First of all, Jean-Loup Faulon, director of the Institute of Systems & Synthetic Biology and Alfonso Jaramillo, head of Synth-Bio team who open their labs for us !
    • All the member of iSSB and of Genopole for their support during our project, give us advices, tips and solution when we needed !
    • Doctor Gaël Nicolas and Professor Pierre Brissot for the help and the expertise they provide about hemochromatosis and iron overload diseases.
    • Hervé Segalen (President of the Association Hémochromatose Paris), Brigitte Pineau (President of the Fédération Française des Associations de Malades de l'Hémochromatose ) and Professor Henri Michel (President of the Association Hémochromatose France) for their help for seminary organisation and with the sending out of the survey .
    • The Industrial galenic department of Faculty of Pharmacy of Lyon (ISPB) for their help with pill design.
    • Last but not least, all our sponsors that make our project achievable !
