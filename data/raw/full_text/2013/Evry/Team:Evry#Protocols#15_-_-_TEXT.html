Smaller the fragment is, the more it migrates on the gel. Using a DNA ladder, we can know the size of DNA sequence and then check if we have the sequence we wanted. Preparation Agarose gel 1% preparation: Add 0,5 g of Agarose in 50 mL of TAE 1X (See protocole for TAE 1X preparation there ) Put the solution into microwave until Agarose is disolved. When until the cool down and add 3 mL of Ethidium Bromide (EtBr). EtBR is a carcinogen substance, use it with caution ! Put the solution in the cuve and let it cool down. Mix gel Put 5 μL of PCR product and 1 μL of loading dye 6X in each well. Do not forget your positive and negative controles, and the kB ladder. Put in the electrophoresis cell at 100 V during 40 minutes.
