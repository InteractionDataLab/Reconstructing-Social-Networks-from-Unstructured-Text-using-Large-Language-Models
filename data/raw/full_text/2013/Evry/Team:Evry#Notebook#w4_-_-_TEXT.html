Preparation of the stock solution (1 M, pH 7.5) for a volume of 50 mL: Disolve 4,6g of Trisbase in 30 mL of distilled water. Adjust pH with concentrated HCl(~4 mL) until pH=7,5. Add distilled water until 50 mL then put in autoclave. Note: If the solution has a yellow coloration, do the preparation again with better Trisbase. Preparation of the solution (10mM, pH 7.5) for a volume of 50 mL. Take 50µL of the stock solution at 1M and add 49.5 mL of water for the dilution.
