However, our sequences of natural promoter give us good results. Then we supposed that the problem of our Golden Gate come from the hybridation of our synthetic promoters. We made new hybridations of: Fur Binding Site 1 to 15 (5 µM) Andersen promoter J23100 (5 µM) RBS 0034 (5 µM) Golden Gate A new Golden Gate of our 2nd construction are made with: Fur Binding Site 1, 2 or 3 + sfGFP Fur Binding Site 1, 2 or 3 + LacI-LVA 5 µL of each Golden Gate product are transformed into Top10 competent cells. To controlled the quality of the transformation, a negative controle (transformation procedure without plasmid) and a positive controle (transformation procedure with plasmid 1A3) are made. Sequencing preparation Preculture with LB medium (with carbenicillin) have been launched for Top10 transformed with our 2nd construction, then minipreps have been realized using the miniprep kit from Machrey Nagel.
