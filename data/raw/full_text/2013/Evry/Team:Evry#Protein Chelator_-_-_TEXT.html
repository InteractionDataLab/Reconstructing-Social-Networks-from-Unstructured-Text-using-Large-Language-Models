This protein from Neisseria gonorrhoeae is able to chelate various ion in solution as Fe 3+ , Ti 4+ ,Cu 2+ , Zr 4+ (Zirconium ion) or Hf 4+ (Hafnium ion). Its affinity for ferrous iron is 10 18 , less than our enterobactins system, but it remains a good alternative for our treatment.
