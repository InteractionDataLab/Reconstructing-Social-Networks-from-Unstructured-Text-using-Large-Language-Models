We first had to model the behaviour of the duodenum regarding iron absorption to determine if a flush treatment strategy was viable. Then we wanted to model the flush treatment by simulating a flush of iron-chelating bacteria. Disease model We first modeled the iron absorption of a hemochromatosic patient using ODEs. Here is the link to the disease model . Treatment model We then used the disease model as a base to develop our flush treatment model. It aims to answer the following question: "Is it possible to chelate a significant amount of iron with a flush strategy?" Here is the link to the treatment model .
