Human Practice

 

We decided to propagate the idea of synthetic biology in a way that would reach everyone – by internet.

First it was a local project, involving some webpages of our city. After a while, and thanks to persistency of everyone, we managed to have some longer articles published on popular news webs – our dream was slowly starting to happen, we could reach even more people!

But the highest peak of surfers' interest in synthetic biology and our project was after publishing our movie with short description on the most popular entertainment page in Poland. Thanks to it we were able to show the idea of synthetic biology and our project to more than 32 000 people! We have never dreamed than we could achieve this number but we hope that our movie was a good way to inform people about synthetic biology and its capabilities.
