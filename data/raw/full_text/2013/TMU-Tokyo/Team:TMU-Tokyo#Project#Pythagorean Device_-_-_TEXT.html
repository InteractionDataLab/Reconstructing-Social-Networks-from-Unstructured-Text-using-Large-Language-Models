They are known as "Rube Goldberg machines" in the US. Pythagorean Devices are deliberately over-engineered or overdone systems that perform very simple task in very complicated process and they usually include some chain reactions. We can enjoy this complicated process. In this year, we tried to design and make the device which is like “Pythagorean Device” in a genome of E.coli. We are very interested in site specific recombination systems in bacteria, so we used lots kind of site specific recombination enzymes in our device. They were useful to make the process of this device more complicated. Therefore, we succeeded in making a complicated chain reaction course in a genome of E.coli. We inserted them in a genome by lambda bacteriophage recombination system, “RED”. Then, how does this device work? A large quantity of lacI is expressed by lacI gene. LacI represses plac. By adding arabinose, pBad is activated and site specific recombination enzyme, HbiF comes to be expressed. Because of HbiF, inversion happens between the site of IRL and IRR. And then site specific recombination enzyme named Flp comes to be expressed. Site specific recombination happens and the sequence between the 2 site of FRT is left off by Flp. Repression of plac becomes off by deleting of lacIq gene and then β-gal comes to be expressed.
