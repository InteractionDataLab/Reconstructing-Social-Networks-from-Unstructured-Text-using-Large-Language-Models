Discard supernatant Resuspend cells in 2mL of lysis buffer In a 1.5mL tube, add ~0.5mL of glass beads and 1mL of cells resuspended in lysis buffer Leave on ice for 5 min. Leave on BeadBeater or Vortex for 5 min.
