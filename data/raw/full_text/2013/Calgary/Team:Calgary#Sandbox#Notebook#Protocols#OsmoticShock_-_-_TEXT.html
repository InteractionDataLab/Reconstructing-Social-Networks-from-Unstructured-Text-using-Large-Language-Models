Incubate for 10 minutes at room temperature Pellet cells at 4C for 10 minutes at 4,000 rpm Re-suspend pellet in 200µL of ice-cold 5mM MgSO4 and transfer into an eppendorf tube and let sit on ice bath for 30 minutes Spin at >13,000 rpm for 10 minutes.
