As such they must be rehydrated before they can be used. Reagents and Reagents Biobrick parts ddH2O (deionized water) Protocol Puncture a hole through the foil with a pipette tip into the well that corresponds to the Biobrick - standard part that you want Add 10 μL of ddH2O (deionized water) Let the water incubate at room temperature for 10 minutes Take 1 μL DNA and transform into your desired competent cells, plate out onto a plate with the correct antibiotic and grow overnight.
