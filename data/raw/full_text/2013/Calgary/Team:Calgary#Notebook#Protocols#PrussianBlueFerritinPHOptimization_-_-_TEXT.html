Set the spectrophotomer to read the appropriate absorbance (650nm for TMB, 415nm for ABTS) for 10 minutes with 10 second intervals. Add the appropriate amount of hydrogen peroxide, and IMMEDIATELY begin taking readings. Repeat the experiment multiple times for each pH point (2, 2.73, 3.6, 4, 4.4, 5, 5.6, 8, 9, and 11). Slopes of each experiment was determined and plotted against pH to determine optimum pH.
