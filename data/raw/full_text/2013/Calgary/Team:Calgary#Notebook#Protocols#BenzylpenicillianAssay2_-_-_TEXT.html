Add this to the wells. Add an additional 50 µL of pH 8 water to the wells. Add 10 µL of bromothymol blue pH indicator to these wells. Add 100 µL of 60 mg/mL benzylpenicillin to the wells. Measure the change from blue to yellow using a spectrophotometer at an absorbance wavelength of 616 nm.
