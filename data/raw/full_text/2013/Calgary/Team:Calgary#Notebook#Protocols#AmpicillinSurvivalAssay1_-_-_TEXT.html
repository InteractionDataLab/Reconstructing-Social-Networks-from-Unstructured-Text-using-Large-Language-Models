Reagents and Materials LB Broth Ampicillin (100mg/mL) Chloramphenicol (30mg/mL) Falcon tubes E. coli transformed with pLacI resistant to chloramphenicol but not ampicillin E. coli transformed with RFP resistant to chloramphenicol but not ampicillin E. coli transformed with β-latacmase ( pLacI +beta-lactamase+His) resistant to chloramphenicol Protocol Add 5mL of LB Broth to 9 Falcon tubes and 3µL of chloramphenicol to 6 Falcon tubes only In 3 of the tubes (2 of them with chloramphenicol and 1 with LB Broth only), add colonies of E. coli transformed with pLacI In 3 of the tubes, add colonies of E. coli transformed with RFP In 3 of the tubes, add colonies of E. coli transformed with β-latacmase ( pLacI +beta-lactamase+His) Incubate the tubes overnight in a shaker at 37°C Spin down the tubes and resuspend cells in 5mL of fresh LB Broth Add ampicillin to the tubes that had no antibiotic on it and to one of the tubes that had chloramphenicol on it.
