Enzyme kinetics of horseradish peroxidase The sliders above the plot are parameters you can adjust to this kinetic model of horseradish peroxidase. By moving to the right you increase the corresponding parameter and immediately you should see the response on the plot, colored blue. The red line is plotted at the initial conditions when you run the model. It is included so that by comparing against it you see the effect of the change in the parameters. For a quick feel of the model, click on the auto-run button on the upper-right corner.
