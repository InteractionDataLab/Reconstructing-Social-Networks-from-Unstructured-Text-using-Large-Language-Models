Centrifuge cells at max for 10 minutes. Discard supernatant. Resuspend cells in 1:10 dilution of lysis buffer (Lysis buffer composed of: 50mM NaH2PO4, 300mM NaCl, 10mM imidazole, pH 8.0). In a 1.5mL tube, add ~0.5mL of glass beads and 1mL of cells resuspended in lysis buffer. Leave on ice for 5 min. Leave on BeadBeater or Vortex for 5 min. Repeat this cycle 2 times. 6. Centrifuge at 14,000rpm for 20 minutes. Transfer supernatant (crude lysate) to a new tube and discard the glass beads.
