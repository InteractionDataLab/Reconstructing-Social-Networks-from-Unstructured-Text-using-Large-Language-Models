    Clinical Strains

    After using AHL molecules as our testing targets, we start trying to use clinical species. We incubate the bacteria resistant strains overnight, centrifuge the bacteria medium, and use the supernatant(which is possible to contain AHL) as our testing sample. We use ELISA plate reader to read the fluorescent signal after 4 hours of reaction of the supernatant and the biosensors. We hope the results of clinical species provide better data for these biosensors in order to reach the market.

    These strains include:

    (1) Acinetobacter baumannii ATCC baa-747

    (2) Pseudomonas aeruginosa PAO 1

    (3) Pseudomonas aeruginosa 1009373

    (4) Pseudomonas aeruginosa 2000671

    (5) Pseudomonas aeruginosa 0000765-1

    (6) Pseudomonas aeruginosa 0000963

    (7) Pseudomonas aeruginosa 0001596

    (8) Pseudomonas aeruginosa 0001819

    (9) Acinetobacter baumannii 8070903

    (10) Acinetobacter baumannii 0008115

    (11) Escherichia coli 0000424

    (12) Escherichia coli 0000703
