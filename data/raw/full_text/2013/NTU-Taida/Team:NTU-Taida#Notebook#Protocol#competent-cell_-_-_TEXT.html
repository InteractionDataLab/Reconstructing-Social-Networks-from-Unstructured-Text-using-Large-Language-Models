Shake for a further 2-3 hrs until Klett=40. (OD550 =0.5) Spin cells down in four 50 ml polypropylene tubes, in SS34 rotor at 3K rpm for 10-12 min, just enough to pellet the cells. Drain cells and re-suspend in 30-40 ml per 100 ml starting culture of TfbI. Leave on ice. For DH1 the optimal time is 5-10 min; good results are obtained for MC1061 when it is left 5 min on ice.For HB101 the time is 90-120 min. For DH5α the time is 90-120 min. For BL21 the time is 25-30 min. Other strains should be assayed for optimal time. Spin cells down, 3K rpm for 8 min at 4℃. Resuspend in 4 ml per 100ml starting culture of TfbII on ice. Be gentle. Aliquot 0.1 ml into eppendorf tubes for DH5α,on ice. Aliquot 0.05 ml into eppendorf tubes for BL21,on ice,store at-80℃.
