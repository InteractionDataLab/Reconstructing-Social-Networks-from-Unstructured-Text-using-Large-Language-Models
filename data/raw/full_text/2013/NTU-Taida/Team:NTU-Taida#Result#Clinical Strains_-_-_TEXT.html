We incubate the bacteria resistant strains overnight, centrifuge the bacteria medium, and use the supernatant(which is possible to contain AHL) as our testing sample. We use ELISA plate reader to read the fluorescent signal after 4 hours of reaction of the supernatant and the biosensors. We hope the results of clinical species provide better data for these biosensors in order to reach the market.
