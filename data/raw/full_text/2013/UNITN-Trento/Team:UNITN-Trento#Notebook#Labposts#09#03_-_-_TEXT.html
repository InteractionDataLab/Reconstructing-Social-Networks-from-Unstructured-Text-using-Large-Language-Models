In this test the fruit is subjected to a cruching loat to measure the resistence of a sample to compression strength. The test allows to measure the strength necessary to break the material analized, as an indication of maturation stage. The results were then plotted. Plots It's well clear that fruit threated with cells tranformed with AraC-pBAD EFE were softer than the control fruit indicating a more advance state in the ripening process. We did then a colorimetric assay in order to quantify the difference between the color of ripe and unripe kiwi's pulp.
