If our system become widely used as a commercial/industrial product there doesn't arise new risks. It is only for producing electricity so this scenario has just positive consequences. 6. All the members of our team received instructions by Mr. Dr. Jörn Kalinowski according to German lab safety regulations including working with biological genetically engineered materials. 8.
