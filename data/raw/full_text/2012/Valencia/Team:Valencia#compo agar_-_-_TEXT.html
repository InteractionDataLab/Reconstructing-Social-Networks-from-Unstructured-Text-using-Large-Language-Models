Reagents and Materials 1’125 μl COMPO/mL distilled water 3% Agar. Protocol Pick up with a micropipette 1’125 μl COMPO in 25mL volume for each plate. Add 3g of agar and set pH at 7’4. Autoclave at 121ºC during 20 minutes. Fill the plates with COMPO agar and wait until it solidifies.
