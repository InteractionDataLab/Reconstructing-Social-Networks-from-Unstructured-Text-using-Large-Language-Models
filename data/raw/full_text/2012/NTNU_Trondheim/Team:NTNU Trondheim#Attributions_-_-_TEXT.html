Laboratory work All biological work, including cloning, measurements, etc. was performed by the students on the team. Clone Manager Suite 6.0 and the online tools j5 and Primer3 were used for in silico work. Various providers were used for synthesis of oligos for PCR. Sequencing was performed by Eurofins DNA at a generous discount as well as GATC Biotech. Modelling All the modeling has been done by team member Rolf using the stochastic simulations program Cain . Wiki-design The wiki was designed by team member Ove using the default MediaWiki framework, Twitter Bootstrap and jQuery . Matchmaker The Matchmaker was designed and built with PHP, jQuery and Twitter Bootstrap and Icons by Font Awesome and famfamfam.com by team member Ove. Poster and presentation The poster and presentation was designed by the students on the team using InkScape, with help and tips from our advisor Rahmi Lale.
