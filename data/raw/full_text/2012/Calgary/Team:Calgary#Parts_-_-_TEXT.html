Details of each part can be found on their respective pages on both the Registry and this wiki. Parts of FRED In terms of FRED, we submitted, two novel hydrolase enzymes as well as generators and composite parts for each. Parts of OSCAR In terms of OSCAR, we submitted two novel genes for use in decarboxylation, previously submitted xylE and catalase genes with new promoters, novel desulfurization and denitrogenation enzymes and composite parts and finally, a functional oxidoreductase enzyme expression construct as well as a functional amidase expression construct.
