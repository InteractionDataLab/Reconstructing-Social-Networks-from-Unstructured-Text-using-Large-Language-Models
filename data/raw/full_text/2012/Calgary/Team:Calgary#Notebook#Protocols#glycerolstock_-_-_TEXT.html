Preparing Glycerol Stocks Reagents and Materials Overnight bacterial growth Screw Cap Tubes Glycerol Protocol Pipet 0.5mL of 50% glycerol into 3 1.5mL screw cap tubes Add 0.5mL of overnight culture to each tube. Pipet up and down to gently mix. Flash freeze in liquid N2 or dry ice/ethanol bath. Place in -80°C freezer when frozen.
