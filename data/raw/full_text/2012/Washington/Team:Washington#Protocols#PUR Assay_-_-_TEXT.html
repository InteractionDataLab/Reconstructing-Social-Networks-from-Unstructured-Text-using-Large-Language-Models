Using a sonicator, sonicated the aliquots at an amplitude of 20 with 1 second pulses on and off for 30 seconds. Applied lysate to pre weighed samples of foam within 25mL culture tubes Pipetted 2 mL of LB into a 25 mL culture tube with a pre weighed foam sample (negative control) Repeat steps 7-10 three more times.
