Grow overnight culture (1 mL) of your cells in selective media 2. Mix 500 uL of your culture with 500 uL of 50% glycerol. Immediately place at -80 C (high concentration of glycerol is toxic to cells and prolonged exposure to it kills them at RT). If you need to take from the stocks, do not thaw - scrape ice off of the top with a sterile loop and streak across plate.
