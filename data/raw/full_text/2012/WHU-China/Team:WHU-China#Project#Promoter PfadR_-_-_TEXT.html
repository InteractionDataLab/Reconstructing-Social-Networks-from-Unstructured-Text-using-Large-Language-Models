To design promoter that can under the sole regulation of fatty acid concentration. Double FadR binding sites from promoter of FadL are place and overlap downstream of constitutive promoter J23110 (TTTACGGCTAGCTCAGTCCTAGGTACAATGCTAGCTGGTCCGACCTATACTCTCGCCACTGGTCTGATTTCTAAGA). Experiment We test the function of PfadR in low copy number plasmid pSB6A1. Also, FadR was overexpressed to prevent leaky expression of the promoter. Specifically, bacteria with BBa_K861061 in pSB6A1 was vortex in M9 medium with oleic acid as sole carbon source for 24 hours. Oleic acid was emulsified with 10% Triton X100 with volume 1:1. Then various volume of mixture is added to M9 medium with 0.2% triton X100. We vortex E.coli Dh5 &alpha in the medium for 24h and using plate reader to see its OD600 and fluorescence. For more details, please see our Protocol website.
