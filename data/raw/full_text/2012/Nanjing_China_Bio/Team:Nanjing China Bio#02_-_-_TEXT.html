She obtained Bachelor Degree in 2002, and Ph.D. Degree in 2008 from Nanjing University. Her works focus on: the exploration of non-apoptotic function of FADD by proteomics and genomics, tumor gene therapy. She is an expert in two-dimensional electrophoresis (2D) and LC/MS/MS, which are the core technologies of proteomics. Some of her works have been published in Molecular & Cellular Proteomics, Lung Cancer, et al.
