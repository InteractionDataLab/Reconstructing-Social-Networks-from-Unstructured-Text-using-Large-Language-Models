In order to remove this obstacle on the application prospects of our Luminesensor, we used LexA408 instead of wild-type LexA DNA Binding domain. LexA408 and LexA are bio-orthogonal with each other since the sequence of the binding sites have variations (See Project Luminesensor Characterization ).
