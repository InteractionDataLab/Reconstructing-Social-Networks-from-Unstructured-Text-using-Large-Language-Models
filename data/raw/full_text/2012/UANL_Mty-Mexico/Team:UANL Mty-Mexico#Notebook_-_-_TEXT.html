Briefly, a scar is obtained which translation results in the benefical amino acids glicine and serine (Figure 1). In contrast with the Standar Assembly 21, the XhoI site was replaced with a SpeI site, which allows us to use the Standard Assembly 10 backbones. Figure 1. Cloning strategy for contructions of fusion proteins. For the rest of the constructs Standard Assembly 10 was used.
