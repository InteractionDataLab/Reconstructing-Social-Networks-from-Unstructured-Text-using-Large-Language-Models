It is a monthly event organized by the University of Trieste, for a wide audience. It is more than a speaking tour; the Science Café nights are in-depth meetings to discuss, in a simple way, about the latest scientific advances, in a relaxed environment sipping a cup of coffe. In this comfy atmosphere, we had the opportunity to talk and explain what Synthetic Biology is, its application in the common life and especially give voice to 'The Jolly JoCare' project!
