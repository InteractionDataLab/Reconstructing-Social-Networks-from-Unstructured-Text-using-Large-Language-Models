    Members

    Synthetic biology is an interdiscipline which combines science and engineering. In order to solve the problems that might be encountered in our project, team members majoring in different disciplines were recruited. Sixteen of us are undergraduate students from 7 different departments, and the other 3 come from Bioinformatics Division & Center of Synthetic and Systems Biology, TNLIST in Tsinghua University as advisors A brief introduction is presented below. readmore
