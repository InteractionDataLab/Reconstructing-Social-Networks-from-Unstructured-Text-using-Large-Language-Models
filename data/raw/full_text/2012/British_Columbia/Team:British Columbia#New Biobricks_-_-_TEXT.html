A restriction digest was set up to test the efficiency of our EcoR1-HF (we do not have plain EcoR1) and Pst1 enzymes, because cloning has been unsuccessful thus far. Two digest mixes were made: (1) the EcoR1 and Pst1 with later expiry dates, and (2) the EcoR1 and Pst1 with earlier or passed expiry dates. The DNA template used was a biobrick, 3I. After being run through the thermocycler, the completed digests were stored in the -20°C freezer. They will be run through a gel by Joe tomorrow, and the size of the products will indicate whether or not the digest was successful.
