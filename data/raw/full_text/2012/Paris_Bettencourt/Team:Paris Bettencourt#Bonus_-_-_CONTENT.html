    Two gradients diverged on a bacteria lawn

    And sorry I could not swim towards both But be one bacteria amongst many spawn And feeling with out glucose tired and drawn I looked at the path that would foster my growth

    Then took the other one, just as fair And having perhaps the better claim For it was nutrient rich and wanted wear Though as for that, the colonizing there Had worn them really about the same

    And both that morning equally lay In LBA no contaminants had debased Oh, I kept the first for another day! Yet knowing how way leads on to way I doubt my steps I will retrace

    I shall be telling this with a ligand Somewhere minutes and minutes hence, Two gradients in a lawn did bend And I, I sought the one that would amend

    And that has made all the difference
