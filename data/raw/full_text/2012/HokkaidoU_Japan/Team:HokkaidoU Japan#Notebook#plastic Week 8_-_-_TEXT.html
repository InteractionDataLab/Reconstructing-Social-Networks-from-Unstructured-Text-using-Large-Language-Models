Transformation The ligated DNA were transformed into E. coli (strain:DH5α). E. coli solution was spread on LBC. August 23rd Colony PCR result Colony PCR result Colony PCR We confirmed whether the ligation at August 22nd went well by colony PCR result. August 25th Colony PCR result Colony PCR result Colony PCR We confirmed the length of PhaA on pSB1C3 and PhaB on pSB1C3 by colony PCR. The result showed only PhaA and pSB1C3 were ligated correctly. Liquid culture We started to incubate bacteria holds RBS (B0034). Digestion We digested PhaC(BBa_K342001) by XbaI and SpeI.
