The colonies were isolated to another plate. September 25th Liquid culture We got colonies on the plate, and started liquid culture. PCR Did PCR for promoter of R.eutropha from pGEM to ligate with RBS-PhaC-RBS-PhaA-RBS-PhaB-dT on pSB1A2. Digestion Digested the promoter done PCR and RBS-PhaA on pSB1A2 with XbaI and SpeI, and plasmid of RBS-PhaC-RBS-PhaA-RBS-PhaB-dT on pSB1A2 with XbaI. Gel extraction We confirmed succession of digestion by electrophoresis. And then DNA were extracted from TBE gel. Ethanol precipitation The digested DNAs were condensed by Ethanol precipitation. Ligation Ligated the promoter with RBS-PhaC-RBS-PhaA-RBS-PhaB-dT and with pSB1A2. Transformation Transformed each ligation product into JM109, and incubated. September 26th Colony PCR Colony PCR for colonies transformed at 25th.
