Attribution

All manifested work for our project, including submission and characterization of BioBricks, Human Practice and Team Wiki, were done by HKUST iGEM 2012 team members.

We conducted all experiments by ourselves, under the supervision of our Laboratory Technician and Instructor, Dr. Jessica Ce Mun Tang.
