Human Practice

1. Highschool - From July 30 to Aug. 1, 2012

Center for the Advancement of Science is founded in 2005. It has attracted teenagers’ interest of science and encouraged the development of science and technology throughout popularization of science by supporting the Korea Foundation for the Advancement of Science and Creativity, Chungbuk National University, and Cheong-Ju Cityhall. Center for the Advancement of Science gives an attraction about natural science though inquiry institution of basic science for all of highschool students, and then is held the Nation high school students camp nature of scientific inquiry every year that is aim to training natural philosophers by constructing foundation of natural science fields. We instructed how we found and analyzed genes and introduced about Synthetic Biology and iGem. Approximately 200 highschool students were participated in camp.

2. College & University - October 16th

We were held for promoting about iGem in October 16th, 2012. We sent invitation card to all professors of the Science college and informed students to seminar. In this seminar, we published the results of our research work concerning essential genes analysis, bio-brick software. About 50 students and 10 professors were participated in seminar.

3. Society

We did publicity activities to let them know about importance of iGem competition and the result of it through the number of 7 newspaper companies.
