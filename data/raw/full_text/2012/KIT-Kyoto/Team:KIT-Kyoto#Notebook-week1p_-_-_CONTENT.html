    • First

    The first number 1 indicates experiments about UAS. The first number 2 indicates experiments about GAL4. Second number 1 indicates making two experiments at the same time when we carried out ligation. Second number 2 indicates making three experiments at the same time when we carried out ligation. The parts we are going to submit are these seven, pSB1C3-UAS, pSB1C3-UAS-LacZ, pSB1C3-UAS-EGFP, pSB1C3-UAS-TNFAIP3,pSB1C3-GAL4, pSB1C3-HS-GAL4, and pSB1C3-Act5c-GAL4.

    August 23rd

    1-1-1 and 2-1-1 Transformation of E. coli with pEGFP-C2 DNA and pAct5C-GAL4 DNA E. coli transformed with pEGFP-C2 was spread on the LB Kanamycin(＋) plate and that transformed with pAct5C GAL4 was spread on the LB ampicillin(＋) plate.

    August 24th

    1-1-2 and 2-1-2 The appropriate colonies were picked up and cultured in the LB medium containing the appropriate antibiotics.
