Ampicillin works on most gram-positive and some gram-negative bacteria. Stock Mix: 4 g ampicillin (100 mg/mL) ad. 40 mL dist. water sterile filtration aliquot in 1 mL stocks and store at -20°C Stability Ampicillin is not autoclavable, it has to be added before pouring plates (keep in mind that the agar is not too hot when adding it) Culture plates can be stored for about 2 month at 4°C (as a batch in a bag or individually sealed with Parafilm ) References http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ampicillin Retrieved from " http://2012.igem.org/Team:TU_Darmstadt/Materials/Ampicillin " Recent changes What links here Related changes Special pages My preferences Printable version Permanent link Privacy policy Disclaimers
