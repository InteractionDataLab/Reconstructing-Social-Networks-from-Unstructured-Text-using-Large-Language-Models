Procedure For 1x PBS Combine: 8.18 g NaCl (140 mM) 0.2 g KCl (2.7 mM) 1.77 g Na2HPO4 (10 mM) 0.24 g KH2PO4 (1.8 mM) Add. 1L dH2O Adjust to pH 7.4 For 10x PBS Combine the following: 80 g NaCl 2 g KCl 14.4 g Na2HPO4 (dibasic anhydrous) OR 18.1 g Na2HPO4 · 2H2O (dibasic dihydrate) OR 27.2 g Na2HPO4 · 7H2O (dibasic heptahydrate) 2.4 g KH2PO4 (monobasic anhydrous) 800 mL distilled H2O Adjust pH to 7.4 with HCl Add H2O to 1L Autoclave for 20 minutes on liquid cycle.
