    • SOB

    The SOB medium is used for growth of bacteria in preparation of chemically competent cells.

    Ingredients

    • 0.5% (w/v) yeast extract
    • 2% (w/v) tryptone
    • 10 mM NaCl
    • 2.5 mM KCl
    • 20 mM MgSO4

    For 1 L:

    • 0.186 g KCl
    • 2.4 g MgSO4
    • 0.584 g NaCl
    • 20 g tryptone
    • 5 g yeast extract

    Adjust to pH 7.5 prior to use. This requires approximately 5 mL of 5M NaOH per liter.
