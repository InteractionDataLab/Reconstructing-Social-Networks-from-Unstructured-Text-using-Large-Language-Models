    GFP

    About

    The green fluorescent protein (GFP) is a protein composed of 238 amino acid residues (26.9kDa) that exhibits bright green fluorescence when exposed to light in the blue to ultraviolet range.[1]

    Application

    • GFP can be used to determine an origin of expression via flourescence microscopy
