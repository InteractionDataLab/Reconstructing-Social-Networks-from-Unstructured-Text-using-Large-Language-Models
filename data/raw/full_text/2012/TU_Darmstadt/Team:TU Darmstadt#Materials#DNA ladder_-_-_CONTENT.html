    DNA ladder

    About

    DNA ladders are used as size markers in agarose gel electrophoresis. They contain a mix of DNA fragments or chemicals that run at a specific bp.
    GeneRuler 1kb Plus

    Application

    Before first use dilute the ladder with deionized water to the specified concentration and add loading dye. Store at 4°C.
