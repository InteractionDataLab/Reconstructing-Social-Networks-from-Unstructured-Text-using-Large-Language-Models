Procedure Defrost stocks of competent cells (100 µL in 1.5 ml Eppendorf tube) on ice Add the DNA and incubate the suspension for 5 minutes on ice Heat shock is done by incubating the cells for 1 minute at 42°C Add 1 mL of DYT medium and incubate for 1 hour at 37°C in order to obtain antibiotic resistance Finally the cells are ready to be crossed out Retrieved from " http://2012.igem.org/Team:TU_Darmstadt/Protocols/Heat_Shock_Transformation " Recent changes What links here Related changes Special pages My preferences Printable version Permanent link Privacy policy Disclaimers
