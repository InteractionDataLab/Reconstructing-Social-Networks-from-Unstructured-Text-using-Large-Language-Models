    • PBS

    About

    PBS stands for phosphate buffered saline and is a isotonic buffer solution.

    Procedure

    For 1x PBS

    1. Combine:
    2. 8.18 g NaCl (140 mM)
    3. 0.2 g KCl (2.7 mM)
    4. 1.77 g Na2HPO4 (10 mM)
    5. 0.24 g KH2PO4 (1.8 mM)
    6. Add. 1L dH2O
    7. Adjust to pH 7.4

    For 10x PBS

    1. Combine the following:
    2. 80 g NaCl
    3. 2 g KCl
    4. 14.4 g Na2HPO4 (dibasic anhydrous) OR 18.1 g Na2HPO4 · 2H2O (dibasic dihydrate) OR 27.2 g Na2HPO4 · 7H2O (dibasic heptahydrate)
    5. 2.4 g KH2PO4 (monobasic anhydrous)
    6. 800 mL distilled H2O
    7. Adjust pH to 7.4 with HCl
    8. Add H2O to 1L
    9. Autoclave for 20 minutes on liquid cycle. Store at RT.[1]
