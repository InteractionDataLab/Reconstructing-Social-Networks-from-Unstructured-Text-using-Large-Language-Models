    • Enhancing a thermo cyclers cooling capabilities

    Sometimes thermo cyclers (those we used) tend to be extremly slow in terms of cooling. The following method reduces the waiting time to a minimum.

    Required tools

    • glove
    • ice
    • thermo cycler

    Instruction

    1. Fill a glove with ice
    2. Put it on the thermo cycler
