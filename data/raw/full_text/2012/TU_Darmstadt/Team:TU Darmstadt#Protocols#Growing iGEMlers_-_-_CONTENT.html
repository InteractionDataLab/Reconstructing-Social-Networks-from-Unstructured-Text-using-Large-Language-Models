    iGEMler growth / Requirements

    Induction / lag phase

    • One person to come up with the iGEM topic
    • Some [in our case at least 7] people to get started
    • A brilliant idea
    • Darwins will to survive
    • Find a volluntary professor to temporary lend you his workspace / manpower (just don't tell the manpower part jet)

    Early log Phase

    • Putting posters everywhere
    • Telling everyone how cool iGEM is and that it was initiated by the MIT (the TU Darmstadt rocks!)

    log Phase

    • Getting the first feedback!
    • Friends bringing friends effect
    • ...

    stationary phase

    • The actuall work is to be dealt with
    • Start of the 40h weeks
    • Labwork
    • A LOT of coffee

    Early death phase

    • Upcoming jamboree
    • Cells won't grow
    • Only days left to the wiki freezdown
    • Everything needs to be done
    • Running low on coffee
    • No sleep...
    • ...

    Death phase

    • Work is dealt with
    • iGEM is over ...
    • Returning home and getting to know the own parents and/or friends again
