    NEB buffers

    Composition of NEBuffers (1x) as provided by their website [1]:

    NEBuffer 1 (yellow)

    • 10 mM Bis Tris Propane-HCl
    • 10 mM MgCl2
    • 1 mM DTT (pH 7.0 at 25°C).

    NEBuffer 2 (blue)

    • 10 mM Tris-HCl
    • 10 mM MgCl2
    • 50 mM NaCl
    • 1 mM DTT (pH 7.9 at 25°C)

    NEBuffer 3 (red)

    • 50 mM Tris-HCl
    • 10 mM MgCl2
    • 100 mM NaCl
    • 1 mM DTT (pH 7.9 at 25°C)

    NEBuffer 4 (green)

    • 20 mM Tris-acetate
    • 10 mM magnesium acetate
    • 50 mM potassium acetate
    • 1 mM DTT (pH 7.9 at 25°C)

    T4 DNA Ligase Buffer

    • 50 mM Tris-HCl
    • 10 mM MgCl2
    • 1 mM ATP
    • 10 mM Dithiothreitol (pH 7.5 at 25°C)

    NEBuffer EcoRI

    • 100 mM Tris-HCl
    • 50 mM NaCl
    • 10 mM MgCl2
    • 0.025 % Triton X-100 (pH 7.5 at 25°C)
