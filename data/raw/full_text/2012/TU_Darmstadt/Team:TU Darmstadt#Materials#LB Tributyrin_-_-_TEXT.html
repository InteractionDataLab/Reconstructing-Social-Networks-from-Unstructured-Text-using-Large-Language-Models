Tributyrin serves as a model substrate and is a good indicator for hydrolytic activity. Ingredients 5 g tryptone 10 g yeast extract 5 g NaCl 15 g agar-agar 15 mL Tributyrin medium 5 g gum arabic 5 mL Tributyrin ad. 15 mL dist. water sonification for 45 min. ad. 1 L dist. water autoclave for 20 min. at 121°C wait until the temperature decreased under 60°C and add the prefered antibiotic pour the medium into petri plates (1 L LB-Agar is equal to 40 plates) store the plates at 4°C Retrieved from " http://2012.igem.org/Team:TU_Darmstadt/Materials/LB_Tributyrin " Recent changes What links here Related changes Special pages My preferences Printable version Permanent link Privacy policy Disclaimers
