    XbaI

    About

    XbaI is a endonucleasic restriction enzyme isolated from Xanthomonas badrii. It creates stick ends with 5' end overhang.

    Working conditions

    The recognition motive of XbaI is:[1]

    5' TCTAGA --> 5'---T     CTAGA---3'
3' AGATCT --> 3'---AGATC     T---5'

    Buffer: NEBuffer 2 + BSA

    SpeI requires at least 1bp overhang on its cutting site (99% cleavage efficiency).[2]
