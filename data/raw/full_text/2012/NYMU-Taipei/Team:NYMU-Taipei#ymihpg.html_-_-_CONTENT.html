iGEM Demonstration in NYMU Club Exibition

In the middle of September, National Yang Ming University will hold an orientation for the freshmen about two days. In the afternoon of second day, September 13th , there will be an exhibition for many clubs to introduce themselves to the freshmen from 14:00 to 19:30. Our iGEM team got a booth to show our poster and let people extract the plasmid of E. coli in person according to our lab’s protocol we will also spread our project’s propaganda material to attract people from our freshman, other students, and the masses to come to our stall. Just like the northern welcome party for the freshmen of medical department, we want to attract more potential enthusiasts to join iGEM and synthetic biology!
