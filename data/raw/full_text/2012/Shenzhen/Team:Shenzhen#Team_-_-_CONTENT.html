            About the Consortium

              Shenzhen is a special consortium named after a city. Team members are not from one school, but eight. In their 3rd or 4th year in university, some undergraduate students came to Shenzhen and joined a co-cultured program called Innovation Classes on Genomics, in BGI College. After lectures introducing synthetic biology and iGEM given by our instructor K2, 22 students from eight schools, including HUST, SCU, SCUT, UESTC, WHU, SCNU, SEU and CUHK, have decided to set up a united team for iGEM 2012.
