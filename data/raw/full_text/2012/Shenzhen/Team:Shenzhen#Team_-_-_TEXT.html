About the Consortium Shenzhen is a special consortium named after a city. Team members are not from one school, but eight. In their 3rd or 4th year in university, some undergraduate students came to Shenzhen and joined a co-cultured program called Innovation Classes on Genomics, in BGI College. After lectures introducing synthetic biology and iGEM given by our instructor K2, 22 students from eight schools, including HUST, SCU, SCUT, UESTC, WHU, SCNU, SEU and CUHK, have decided to set up a united team for iGEM 2012. Students from eight different universities have contributed to this project including:  - Huazhong University of Science and Technology, China  - Sichuan University, China  - South China University of Technology, China  - Wuhan University, China  - University of Electronic Science and Technology of China, China  - Southeast University, China  - South China Normal University, China  - The Chinese University of Hong Kong Name List Advisors  - Prof. Dr. Huanming YANG, Director at BGI Research Institute  - Dr. Junguang XU, Research Scientist at BGI Research Institute  - Dr. Ming NI, Research Scientist at BGI Research Institute Instructors  - Mr. Kang KANG, B.Sc., Research Scientist at BGI Research Institute - Mr. Yu CHEN, M.Sc., Research Scientist at BGI Research Institute & Ph.D. candidate at ZMBH Non-student Team Member  - Mr. Qinsi LIANG, B.Sc., Research Scientist at BGI Research Institute Postgraduate  - Ms. Qiong WU, B.Sc., M.Sc. candidate at CUHK Undergraduates  - Ms. Min OU, SCUT  - Mr. Junjie ZHANG, SCUT  - Mr. Jianhui GONG, SCUT  - Mr. Qiwu XU, HUST  - Mr. Chao FENG, HUST  - Mr. Xiaopeng XU, HUST  - Mr. Denghui LIU, HUST  - Ms. Chengran ZHOU, SCU  - Ms. Wenjing LI, SCU  - Mr. Mingyu GAN, SCU  - Mr. Senwei TANG, SCU  - Mr. Jie WANG, SCU  - Ms. Yan HUANG, WHU  - Mr. Luohao XU, WHU  - Ms. Haojun JIANG, SEU  - Ms. Zhikun ZHAO, SEU  - Ms. Yang DENG, UESTC  - Mr. Junjie BU, UESTC  - Mr. Xuefeng LUO, SCNU  - Mr. Haosen CHEN, SCNU  - Mr. Chusheng ZHAO, SCNU Retrieved from " http://2012.igem.org/Team:Shenzhen/Team " Recent changes What links here Related changes Special pages My preferences Printable version Permanent link Privacy policy Disclaimers
