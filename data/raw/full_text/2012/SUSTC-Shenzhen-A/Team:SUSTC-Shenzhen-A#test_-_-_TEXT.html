Team abstract This iGEM team is set up by South University of Science and Technology of China, which is a public institution funded by the municipality of Shenzhen, a special economic zone city in southern China. The team is multi-disciplinary with sophomore undergrads. project The website "Registry of Standard Biological Parts" promotes the development of synthetic biology through providing genetic parts. To expand its influence in data exchange, we found it necessary to build an app client for it. In order to save time in searching BioBrick, simplify the process of selecting classifications, and facilitate changing selected category, we will dedicate ourselves to adjust database structure so that we can change the interface of BioBrick searching into spinner-formed .(need a tour)   Recent News   16 Aug 2012 a line to devide the title and content is needed. main page work remains fixing the size of the img frame adding details... 20 Aug 2012 The first project has been updated.
