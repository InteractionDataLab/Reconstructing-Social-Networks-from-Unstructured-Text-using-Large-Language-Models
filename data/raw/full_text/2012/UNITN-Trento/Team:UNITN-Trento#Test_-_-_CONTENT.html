    We will save the World, one statue at a time

    Project Codename: Crust Away

    Statues and monuments all over the world are not enjoyed to the extent that they should be because they are covered in a disfiguring black crust. A practical solution would be if a cheap mechanism were available to continually remove the pollutant molecules that adhere to the surface of statues. To build such a system, we are engineering E. coli to dissolve the disfiguring black crust, which consists mainly of CaSO4.
