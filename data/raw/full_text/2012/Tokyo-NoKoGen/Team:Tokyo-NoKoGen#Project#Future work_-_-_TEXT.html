But our constructed blue light sensor switches off the system under light condition which will stop us from constructing “Coli. Express” that can respond to light signal. To solve this problem, we will regulate the system using a repressor. Our idea is to use lacI repressor and construct this expression system. Under dark condition, blue light sensor switches on the lacI transcription. lacI repress the transcription of mcherry.
