The surveys were conducted to understand different perspectives of students who may have the chance to join iGEM. Our target does not only include university fellows but also high school juniors in Hong Kong as iGEM high school has been introduced. In details, they can be separated into two age groups: 15-17, and 18-25 years old. We hoped to find out the suitable approaches of human practice toward our targets.
