I. biobrick DNA length verification In order to check the inserted biobrick DNA length, PCR was performed with standard biobrick vector sequencing primers (BBa_G00100 and BBa_G00101) / restriction digestion by EcoRI and PstI, targeting on biobrick standard RFC10 site. 1. BBa_K786001 2. BBa_K786002 3. BBa_K786003 5. BBa_K786010 II. Protein expression verification Negative Phototactic Construct for Blue Light Detection (BBa_K786001) We verified whether BBa_K786001 was expressed in E. coli. After transformation of BBa_K786002 into 3 different E. coli strains (BL21, TOP10 and DH5α), cells were collected and lyzed. Cell lysates were resolved on SDS-PAGE. Western blot with anti-His antibody was conducted. Bands were detected in the cell lysates, indicating the fusion protein was expressed in all 3 bacterial strains. Since the fusion proteins were also put under the same constitutive promoter in other two constructs BBa_K786002 and BBa_K786003, it is believed that these constructs can also express the corresponding fusion proteins in above 3 bacterial strains.
