    NOTEBOOK - JULY

    1. Miniprep of picked clones from transformed NpSRII-NpHtrII-Ectar-backbone cells.
    2. Plasmids containing NpSRII-NpHtrII-Ectar -backbone gene were sent to sequencing.
    3. Make plate with 0.5% soft agar with antibiotics- chloramphenicol.
    4. Make plate with antibiotics –Ampiclin.
    5. Overlapping PCR of NpSRII-NpHtrII-Ectsr-backbone which the plasmid is responsible in repelling from blue light.
    6. Direct transformation of NpSRII-NpHtrII-Ectsr-backbone gene, follow by spread plate.
    7. Miniprep of picked clones from transformed HsSRI-HsHtrI-Ectsr-backbone cells.
    8. Sequencing results shown that the sequence of plasmids containing NpSRII-NpHtrII-Ectar-backbone gene are incorrect.
    9. Overlapping PCR of NpSRII-NpHtrII-Ectar -backbone which the plasmid is responsible in attracting by blue light.
    10. Make LB agar solution
    11. Direct transformation of NpSRII-NpHtrII-Ectar-backbone gene, follow by spread plate.
    12. No clone was being found on the plate containing transformed NpSRII-NpHtrII-Ectar-backbone cells.
    13. Overlapping PCR of NpSRII-NpHtrII-Ectar-backbone which the plasmid is responsible in attracting by blue light.
    14. Direct transformation of NpSRII-NpHtrII-Ectar-backbone gene, follow by spread plate.
    15. Pick clones from transformed NpSRII-NpHtrII-Ectar-backbone cells.
    16. Miniprep of picked clones from transformed NpSRII-NpHtrII-Ectar-backbone cells.
    17. Miniprep of picked clones from transformed NpSRII-NpHtrII-Ectar-backbone cells and was sent for sequencing.
    18. Sequencing results shown that the sequence of plasmids containing NpSRII-NpHtrII-Ectar -backbone gene is correct.
    19. The sequenced cells which contain the NpSRII-NpHtrII-Ectar-backbone gene are being cultured in 0.5% soft agar plate for testing using normal light bulb with its corresponding antibiotics.
    20. Repeat in testing cells that contain the NpSRII-NpHtrII-Ectar-backbone gene are being cultured in 0.5% soft agar     plate for testing using normal light bulb with its corresponding antibiotics.
    21. Pick clones from transformed NpSRII-NpHtrII-Ectsr-backbone cells.
    22. Plasmids containing HsSRI-HsHtrI-Ectsr -backbone gene were sent to sequencing.
    23. Miniprep of picked clones from transformed NpSRII-NpHtrII-Ectsr-backbone cells.
