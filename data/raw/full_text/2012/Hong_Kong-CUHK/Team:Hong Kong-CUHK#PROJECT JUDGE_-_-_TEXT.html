To help the judges, please indicate if you feel you should be evaluated for any of the following special prizes: ✓Best Human Practice Advance Please explain briefly why you should receive any of these three special prizes: We did comprehensive survey to find out their response on iGEM and synthetic biology of high school and university students in Hong Kong. We analyzed the potential iGEMers among students of various majors besides science in Hong Kong, which can help iGEM to build up a better reputation and higher impact on synthetic biology in Asia region. Also, we proposed a new biosafety approach (exploiting the natural bacterial immune defend mechanism on digestion of target engineered DNA), which can further help in higher standard of safety in synthetic biology.
