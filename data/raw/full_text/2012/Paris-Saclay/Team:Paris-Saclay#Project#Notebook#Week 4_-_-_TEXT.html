Follow @iGEMParisSaclay Week 4 We placed an order for the primers. DNA (biobricks) Resuspension using iGEM protocol. Thermal shock treatment of each biobrick to have a large quantity of DNA. Staggering of the transformed bacteria on a selective medium to select bacteria which recieved the plasmid. We started some colonies, one per biobrick, on a liquid culture, in order to make a Miniprep. Miniprep to purify each biobrick (ready to use). Groove for two colonies because they did not grow on the liquid culture.
