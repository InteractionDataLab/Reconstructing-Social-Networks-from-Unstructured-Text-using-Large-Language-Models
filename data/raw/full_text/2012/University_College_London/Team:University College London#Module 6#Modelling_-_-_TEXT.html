This would allow predictions to be made on how large a cell colony will be required to generate a halo diameter (indicating a DNA-free zone where the nuclease has digested the DNA in the agar) of desired size. Methods From the DNase agar test, the colony diameter,d, halo diameter,d_h, and absorbance at 600 OD were measured. From the absorbance values, we work out the cell concentration in cell per litres. This is done using a third party calculator generated by Aligent Technologies .
