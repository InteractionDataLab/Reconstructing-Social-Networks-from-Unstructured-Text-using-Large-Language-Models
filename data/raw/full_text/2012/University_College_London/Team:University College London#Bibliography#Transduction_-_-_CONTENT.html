"Gene transfer by transduction in the marine environment" (Jiang, S. C., & Paul, J. H., et al. Applied and environmental microbiology. 1998; 64(8):2780-2787 This paper highlights the importance of transduction as a mechanism for horizontal gene transfer in the marine environment. It suggest that this is due the the high population of bacteriophages in the ocean that can mediate transduction.<a href="http://aem.asm.org/content/64/8/2780.abstract" title="Reference Link" target="_blank">Paper on AEM</a>
