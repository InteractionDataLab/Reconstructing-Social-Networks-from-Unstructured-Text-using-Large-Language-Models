Measure absorbance on a spectrometer until A6000 is approximately 0.3. Step 3 – Incubation: Transfer to a chilled sterile 50ml Falcon tube and incubate on ice for 10 minutes Step 4 – Centrifuge: -Time: 5 mins -G – 3300 -Temperature: 180C Step 5 – Incubate: Resus in 10ml of ice cold 0.1M CaCl2 in 15% glycerol and incubate on ice for 30 minutes. Step 6 – Centrifuge: -Time: 5 mins -G – 3300 -Temperature: 180C Step 7 – Transfer: Resus in 1ml of ice cold 0.1M CaCl2 in 15% glycerol. Transfer 100ul aliquots to pre-chilled, pre-labelled eppendorf tubes.
