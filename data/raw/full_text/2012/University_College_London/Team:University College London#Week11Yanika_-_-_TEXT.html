Ligate the Upstream and Downstream Parts into the digested Destination Plasmid, using the following: Ingredient Amount Upstream Part digestion 2 µl Destination Plasmid digestion 2 µl 10X T4 DNA Ligase Buffer 2 µl T4 DNA Ligase 2 µl H2O 11 µl After this is prepared, incubate at room temperature for 10 minutes and then heat inactivate at 80°C for 20 minutes.
