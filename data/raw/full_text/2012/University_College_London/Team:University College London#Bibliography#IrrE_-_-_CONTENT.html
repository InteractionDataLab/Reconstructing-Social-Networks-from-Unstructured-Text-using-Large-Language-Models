"The IrrE Protein of Deinococcus radiodurans R1 Is a Novel Regulator of recA Expression" (Earl, A. M., Mohundro, M. M., Mian, I. S., & Battista, J. R., et al. Journal of Bacteriology. 2002; 184(22):6216-6224) This paper outlines the discovery of IrrE, a regulator in Deinococcus Radiodurans, that is apparently one of the gene responsible for the high environmental tolerance of Deinococcus Radiodurans. <a href="http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC151961/" title="Reference Link" target="_blank">Paper on NCBI</a>
