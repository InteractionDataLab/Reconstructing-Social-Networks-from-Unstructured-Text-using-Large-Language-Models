From 2012.igem.org

"Laboratory-evolved mutants of an exogenous global regulator, IrrE from Deinococcus radiodurans, enhance stress tolerances of Escherichia coli" (Chen, T., Wang, J., Yang, R., Li, J., Lin, M., & Lin, Z., et al. PloS one. 2011; 6(1):e16228) This paper suggests that the similarities between the regulatory systems of Deinococcus Radiodurans and E. Coli allow the IrrE global regulator of Deinococcus Radiodurans to be utilised similarly in E. Coli, conferring resistance to radiation, heat, oxidative, and osmotic stress to the transformed strain.<a href="http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC3022760/?tool=pmcentrez&rendertype=abstract" title="Reference Link" target="_blank">Paper on NCBI</a>
