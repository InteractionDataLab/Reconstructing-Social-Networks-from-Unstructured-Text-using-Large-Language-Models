F., et al. Journal of Bacteriology. 1989; 171(2):837-846) This paper indicates that the NahR protein is found bound to the promoter region sal. In the absence of salicylate or a salicylate-analogue, expression is at basal level. However, in the presence of salicylate or a salicylate-analogue, a conformational change is induced in NahR, activating downstream expression.
