Step 3 - Sampling: Mix and take 1ml sample from each flask – return the flask to shaker. Take Optical Density readings of each sample – record time of inoculation and Optical Density in a spreadsheet. (In order to allow for the time taken measuring Optical Density, the inoculations for each type of media should be staggered by approx. 10-15 mins).
