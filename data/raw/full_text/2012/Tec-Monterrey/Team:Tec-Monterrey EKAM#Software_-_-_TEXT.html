Enzyme compatibility, including digestion reaction conditions and cohesive end affinity, as well as their availability and other characteristics, are taken into account as the user points out the limits they wish to define for each module, so a future manipulation of the fragment can be carried out with as little enzyme as possible. To aid in the generation of modular restriction sites in the master molecule. Multiple factors can be considered toward enzyme compatibility, including: • Digestion reaction conditions • Cohesive end affinity • Availability • Other characteristics The user points out the limits they wish to define for each module, so future manipulation is possible with optimal enzyme use.
