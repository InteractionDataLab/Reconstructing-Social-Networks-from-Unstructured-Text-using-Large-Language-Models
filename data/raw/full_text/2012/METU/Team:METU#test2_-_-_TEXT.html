Kill Switch We needed a kill switch system to keep our genetically modified bacteria under control which are found in CO filter. With the help of this system these modified bacteria would die in the case of its release to the environment. Cell Limiter In order to make our biological CO filter’s activity well characterized we designed a quorum sensing system.
