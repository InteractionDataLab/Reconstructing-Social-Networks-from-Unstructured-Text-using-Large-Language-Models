Killing Device The construct is designed to eradicate S.aureus through the production of lysostaphin, which would be released till a certain concentration. Biofilm Formation Device In order to increase bactericidal effect, we enhance biofilm formation by over-expression of yddV, a di-guanylate cyclase, which catalyzes GTP into c-di-GMP. Application The system we have designed can be applied to the pools which contain flowing water with existence of S.aureus. For further application, this device can developed for medical use.
