    Brain storming and discussing:

        All the students have read lots of literature and taken part in the brain storms offering new ideas. The brain storms began in 2012 Feb. and last a long time. What's more, we have meetings on Saturdays to discuss our project and share achievements with the leading by Qiao Lei.

    Experiments:

        The experiments are leading by Qiao Lei, Cui Shuang Yang Yubo, Li Ang, Zhao kunming and Zeng Xiangmiao. Besides, the sophomores often comes to give a hand and learn some basic skills of experiments as well.

    Modeling:

        Zhang Ying, Xie Fangping, Yu Yue and Cheng Borong do the modeling. They learn a lot themselves and Pro. Xu train them sometimes. Model 1: Detecting is edited by XIE Fangping, Model 2: Killing is by ZHANG Ying. Finally, thanks for MATLAB.

    Designer:

        The logo, the T-shirt and the background of wiki are designed by Zhao Kunming, who has a talent of designing.The brochure for our sponsors and one of our human practice, the song, are made by Wu Yifan and Pro. Xu. And thanks for the help and advice provided by the other students.

    Wiki:

        All of us joined the process of making wiki this summer, with the coding work by XIE Fangping. Gathering materials, designing our own style and making it, everyone is enjoying it. Besides, big thanks for the technical assistance provided by ZHou Letao.
