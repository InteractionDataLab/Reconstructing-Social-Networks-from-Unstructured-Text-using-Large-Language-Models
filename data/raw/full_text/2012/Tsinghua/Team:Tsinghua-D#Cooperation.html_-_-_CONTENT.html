Team:Tsinghua-D/Cooperation.html

From 2012.igem.org

iGEM Tsinghua-D Team values efficiency, thus collaborated with undergraduates from Department of Industrial Engineering, Tsinghua University to optimize schedule of the project. Gantt chart was made and schedule of the project was made according to it.

iGEM Tsinghua-D Team takes social responsibilities, thus collaborated with Laboratory of Microorganism, School of Life Science, Tsinghua University to hold freshmen workshop and iGEM Tsinghua-D open house. These activities provided undergraduates from various departments an opportunity to experience laboratory research.

iGEM Tsinghua-D Team seeks opportunities to communicate with other iGEM teams, thus co-organized meetups and workshops between iGEM teams with Students’ Sci. & Tech. Association, SLS., Tsinghua Univ.
