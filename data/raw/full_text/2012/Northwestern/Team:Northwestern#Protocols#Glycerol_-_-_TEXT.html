Overnight Cultures 60% Glycerol by volume (Autoclaved) 2mL microcentrifuge tubes Procedure Create the Glycerol Stock Create the Glycerol Stock Add 1.5mL of the overnight stock into the microcentrifuge tube Add .5mL of the sterile 60% glycerol stock to the microcentrifuge tube Gently shake and mix to ensure even distribution of the glycerol Label the tube, and put it into the -80C freezer Recovering the Cells Scrape the cells from the ice on the glycerol stock with a sterile loop Plate the cells with the loop on a plate with antibiotics Place the glycerol stock back in the -80C freezer Incubate the plate at 37C overnight for individual colonies Retrieved from " http://2012.igem.org/Team:Northwestern/Protocols/Glycerol " Recent changes What links here Related changes Special pages My preferences Printable version Permanent link Privacy policy Disclaimers
