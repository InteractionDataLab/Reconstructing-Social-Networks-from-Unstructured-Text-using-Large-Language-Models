The iGEM Team of University of Southern Denmark 2012 igem.sdu.2012@gmail.com Laboratory Notebook 1st week 2nd week 3rd week 4th week 5th week 6th week 7th week 8th week 9th week 10th week 11th week 12th week 27-08-2012 to 02-09-2012 After 48 hours in the incubator, the 1-FFT colonies finaly reached a high enough concentration for sequencing. Meanwhile, all the SST colonies had died out. To avoid unnecesary excess work, we simply ran PCR with all mutagenesis primers on an older 1-SST containing pJET plasmid. Miraculous, both gel band length and check digest on the resulting sequence yielded perfect results. Why had we not done this before? In the following days 1-SST ligated into pJET, grown on ampicillin plates, transformed into Top10, transfered to liquid medium and miniprepped for sequencing.
