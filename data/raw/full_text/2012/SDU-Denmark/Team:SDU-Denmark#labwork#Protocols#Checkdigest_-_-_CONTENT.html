Check Digest

Digest mix, using Fastdigest enzymes

Digest

1) Cast an agarose gel for purification of the cut product 2) Mix the digestion mixture in an eppendorf tube 3) Leave for 5 min. at 37°C (no shaking!) 4) Immidiately load the restriction mixture in the gel 5) Run the gel, cut out and purify bands of correct size.Digestion Mix

4 µL H2O 
0,5 µL enzyme A
0,5 µL enzyme B
2 µL Fast Digest green buffer
3 µL dna product
