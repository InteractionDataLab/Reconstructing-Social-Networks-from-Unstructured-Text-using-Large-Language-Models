    Modeling

    The enzyme production and activity was modeled using a Michaelis-Menten model along with a Flux Balance Analysis to gauge an expected level of protein concentration and metabolism.

    References

    1. J. D. Orth, I. Thiele, and B. Ø. Palsson, "What is flux balance analysis?," Nature biotechnology, vol. 28, no. 3, pp. 245-248, 2010. 2. J. Reed, T. Vo, C. Schilling, and B. Ø. Palsson, "An expanded genome-scale model of escherichia coli k-12 (ijr904 gsm/gpr)," Genome Biology, vol. 4, no. 9, pp. R54+, 2003.
