They can differentiate into different cells which own special functions. That`s why we call them “omnipotent”. How to achieve this shift(differentiation)? Is there a switch which can adjust this mechanism? Then we come up with a hypothesis: There should exist a kind of “stem switch” during the differentiation, which can be affected by the cell themselves (such as cell density) and the surroundings. This magic switch can change the multidirectional cells into the particular function cells. We are happy to know that this hypothesis is proved to be true recently----There really has a special switch to regulate differentiation. Figure 1.
