It encompasses a variety of different approaches, methodologies, and disciplines with a variety of definitions. The common goal is the design and construction of new biological functions and systems not found in nature.    Actually, people often fail to adopt new ideas timely and tend to dodge the revolutions of new ideas. The hesitation results from lack of relevant knowledge and thus lack of control of unknown areas. We are committed to spread relevant knowledge, communicate with people and motivate innovation.
