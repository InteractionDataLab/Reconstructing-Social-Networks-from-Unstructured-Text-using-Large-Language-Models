We hope to engage the general public in a thoughtful conversation on this hot topic by discussing the history, ethics, and future of several GMO areas, all of which have been effected by the innovations of synthetic biology. Over the past few weeks all of us have been hard at work doing preliminary research, reaching out to the synbio community, and writing shooting scripts.
