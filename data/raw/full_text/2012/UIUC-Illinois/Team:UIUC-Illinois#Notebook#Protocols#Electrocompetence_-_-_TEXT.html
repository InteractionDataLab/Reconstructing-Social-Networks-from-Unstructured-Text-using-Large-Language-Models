Divide liquid cell subculture into 50mL conical tubes and centrifuge for 10 minute at 4000rpm 2. Discard supernatant and add 25mL cold 10% glycerol 3. Stir the mixture by mixing and centrifuge for an additional 10 minutes 4. Repeat steps 2-3 two more times 5. Discard the supernatant and resuspend the cells in 1mL 10% glycerol 6. Transfer to 1.5mL centrifuge tubes and centrifuge for 2 minutes 7. Discard the supernantant and resuspend the cells in 2 volumes (cell volumes) 10% glycerol 8.
