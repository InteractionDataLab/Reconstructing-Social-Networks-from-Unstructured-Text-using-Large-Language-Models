Here you can see our successes, failures, and alterations in our plans as we continue to gain hands-on experience in the cutting-edge field of synthetic biology. The journey was long an arduous, but there is a light at the end of the tunnel, and that feeling when months of work pay off is worth every minute of disappointment.
