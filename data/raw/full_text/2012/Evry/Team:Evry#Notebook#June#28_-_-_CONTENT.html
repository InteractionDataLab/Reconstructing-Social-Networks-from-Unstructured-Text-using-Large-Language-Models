Plasmid purification 1) Pellet 3mL bacterial culture by centrifugation at 8000 rpm for 3 min at room temperature 2) Resuspend pelleted bacterial cells in 250 uL Buffer P1 3) Add 250 uL Buffer P2 and mix 4-6 times. Let it for no more than 5 min 4) Add 350 uL Buffer N3 and mix 4-6 times 5) Centrifugation at 13 000 rpm for 10 min 6) Take the supernatant and apply to the QIAprep spin column 7) Centrifugation at 13 000 rpm for 60 sec 8) Discard flow-through 9) Centrifugation at 13 000 rpm for 60 sec 10) Discard flow-through 11) Place column in a clean 1.5 mL microcentrifuge tube 12) Add 50 uL Buffer EB to the column for elution and let stand for 1 min 13) Centrifugation at 13 000 rpm for 1 minDNA Concentrations
