Gregoire LejayContact

Freelance designer After a master in bioinformatics and modeling, I started a research project in which I use mathematical models and computer simulations to study vision in Drosophila. At the same time, I'm involved in many different projects that bring in several aspects of design, art and science. I enjoy the self-taught approach of science and technology thus I joined groups of DIY enthusiasts such as La Paillasse with which I participate for the biotic games project. My goal is to find mechanisms that will enable us to involve tadpoles in a gameplay environment for the biotic game project.
