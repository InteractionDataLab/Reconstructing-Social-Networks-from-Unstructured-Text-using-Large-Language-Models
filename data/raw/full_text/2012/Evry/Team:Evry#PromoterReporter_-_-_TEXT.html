A reporter gene, to be introduced into an organism, is placed with the gene of interest in the same DNA construct. A good reporter confers easily identifiable characteristics as selectable markers. Our interest was to perform promoter’s assays to visualize where our promoters allow the gene expression in the tadpoles with fluorescent proteins.
