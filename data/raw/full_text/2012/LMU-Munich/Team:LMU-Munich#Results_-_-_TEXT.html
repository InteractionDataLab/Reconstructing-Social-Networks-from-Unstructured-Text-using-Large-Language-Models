Result of fluorescence evaluation of the three strains: W168, B53 and B70. Erase* germination ability! Knockout strains The germination rates of B. subtilis germination mutants. All germination rates are relative to wild type 168 (W168), our positive control. W168 showed near 100% germination rates. The mutant spo0A::tet is unable to form spores, and should therefore show no germination (our negative control). Our results were consistent with this expectation.
