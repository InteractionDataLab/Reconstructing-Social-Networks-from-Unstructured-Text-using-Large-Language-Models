Research interest Cell Biology, Theoretical Biology, System and Synthetic Biology Personal statement “Hello World!” This is the first time I participate in the iGEM, albeit I have known it from my friend (who took part in iGEM twice) when I was young…..uh, I mean, younger (though at that time I was still always thought to be a married man), and have started to acquire stuffs from the works of other teams before I came to this college. I Have a Dream. Because of the interest in those works which demonstrate the fundamental rules of the natural world, I always want to do some research to show the basic principles of living things………………..Nevertheless, I join in the iGEM team just for fun, … at very beginning; indeed I started to understand how synthetic biology could help us to figure out those essential mechanism now, and that is why I gradually transform into an avid iGEM “sophomore” from a “freshman” in this summer.
