We proceeded to standardize our own growth conditions by building a custom made cyanobacterial incubator and setting the growth conditions based on what the majority of the literature specified () Here we show our growth curve for Synechocystis PCC 6803 using the conditions set in our DIY shaking incubator (40uE/s/m²), 90 RPM, 30°C.
