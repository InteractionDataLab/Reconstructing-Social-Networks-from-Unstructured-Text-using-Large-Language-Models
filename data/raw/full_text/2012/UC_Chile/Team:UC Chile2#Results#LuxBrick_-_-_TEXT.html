As we are using various elements from this Biobrick, we believe that is cornersome to know how we may increase its output by modifying the conditions that affect its behaviour. We set out to understand diverse factors that could be influencing the total bioluminescence yield of the brick. Some of them have direct relation with some of the aspects of our project. These factors are: Glucose concentration Growth state at time of induction Cell mass Temperature Here you'll find detailed the characterization methods we used in this experiment.
