    Modeling

    1. Introduction Ordinary differential equation(s) (ODE) is one of the most popular methods in modeling. Frank R. Giordano and other scientists have introduced it exhaustively. [1] In many computational biological researching, researchers often use it to simulate the dynamics part of biological process. The concentrations of RNA, proteins, and other molecules are represented by time-dependent variables. [2] We used the same method to construct our model.
