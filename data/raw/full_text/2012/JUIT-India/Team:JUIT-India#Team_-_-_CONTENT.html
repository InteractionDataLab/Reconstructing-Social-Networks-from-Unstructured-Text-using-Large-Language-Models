    • Meet the team

    Hi! We are the JUIT-India iGEM Team, a collection of Biotehnology and Bioinformatics all focused on increase in yield of the rice and reducing greenhouse effect.

    The team consists of seven Biotechnologist and two Bio-Informatics. The whole team have been inducted into the Department Of Biotechnology and Bio-Informatics at the Jaypee University This means everybody is cleared to work in the lab and all of us can contribute to the hands-on experiments.
