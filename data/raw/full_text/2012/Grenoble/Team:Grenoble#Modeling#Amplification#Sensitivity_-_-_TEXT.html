Explanation 3 Activations by (CRP-cAMP): To set the value of this parameter, we plotted the concentration of (CRP-cAMP) at t=2000 min as we varied the initial concentration of cAMP: Evolution of (CRP-cAMP) concentration at t=2000 minutes in function of the initial concentration of cAMP. We could notice that the maximum value of (CRP-cAMP) varied between 0 and 10-6 mol.L-1. The value of the threshold had to be in this range of concentration. Not too low, else it would have meant that the proteins are always produced, and not to high, else the genes would never be expressed. In addition we assumed that AraC had to be activated first and without amplification of cAMP, seing that it is only when arac is expressed the adenylate cyclase is produced.
