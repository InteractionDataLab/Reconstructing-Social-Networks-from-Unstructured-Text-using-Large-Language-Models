In order to fulfill this general aim, we focused our study on three axes: How useful can our sensor be in the medical field? We contacted a medical expert in the field of infectious agents' detection and identification. We were informed about the most used pathogen detection methods, their limits and the researchers' needs and expectations. We had then gathered all the essential constraints that our device had to meet.
