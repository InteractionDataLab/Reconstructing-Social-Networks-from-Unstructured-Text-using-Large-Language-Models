Carbon Flux

In addition to measuring the OD730 of our Synechocystis, we also measured the dry weight in grams of the culture between the third and eighth day. We also measured the dry weight of an equal volume of BG-11 to obtain the dry weight of the Synechocystis alone. Our measurements were made by weighing 10mL of culture in pre-weighed aluminum weigh boats that had been dried for 24 hours in a 90°C oven. In order to calculate the approximate carbon flux from our data, several other plots were created. First, we selected a linear portion of our growth curve, and fitted the curve to a line in order to determine the rate of change of OD730 per day.

Second, a graph of gDW vs OD730 and fitted to a line to determine the rate of change of gDW per OD730.

Finally, a graph of the mass of culture vs day was fitted to a line to determine the rate of change of gDW per day.
