Each staff member has helped to support our project with their vast research experience and have also helped financially to send our team to the Hong Kong iGEM Regionals. Dr. Louise Brown Louise Brown oversaw all of our groundwork, especially the gBlocks. She helped us to keep up our morale throughout the project. Louise had the important role in developing communication between the team members so that our project proceeded efficiently. A/Prof. Rob Willows Rob helped, alongside Louise, interpreting our gels and our sequences. We turned to Rob when an experiment didn't proceed as planned for advice and ways to improve the experiment. Prof. Nicki Packer Nicki helped the team with finances, finding sources of funding previously unobtainable.
