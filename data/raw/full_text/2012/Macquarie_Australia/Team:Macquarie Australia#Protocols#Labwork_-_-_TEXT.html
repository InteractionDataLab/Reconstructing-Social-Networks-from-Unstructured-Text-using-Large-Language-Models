From the red & green fluorescent bacteria that grew, we inoculated SOC broth with a loopful of each transformant - i.e. green or red fluorescent. Using this inoculated broth, we drew various pictures with a sterilized loop onto LB (ampicillin) plates which resulted in 12 plates for incubation. These plates are incubated overnight at 37C.
