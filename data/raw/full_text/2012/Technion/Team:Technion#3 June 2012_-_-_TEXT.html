We managed to get some competent cells from another lab. The cells were DH5-alpha competent for electroporation. I did the transformations of the pUC19+12.1-luciferase (1ul), BBa_R0062 (2 ul) and BBa_J06702 (2ul). The cells recovered in SOB (instead of SOC which was suggested by the lab we took the cells from) for an hour. Then they were plated on LB+Amp plates. For each transformation, 1 and 10 ul of transformed cells was diluted in 50 ul of LB and plated. The cells were incubated at 37 degrees overnight. Tomorrow we will check the transformation results.
