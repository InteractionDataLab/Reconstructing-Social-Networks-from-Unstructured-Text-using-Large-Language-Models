These controversial results have just been released two days ago! Click on the image below or in this link to read the complete version of their report! After analyzing it, we realized that these equations and the parameters used belong to previous studies done in 2010 (1). Since we wanted to test whether these results were true, we have also carried out our own experiments (Table 1) to determine the best data to use and check the possible outcomes.
