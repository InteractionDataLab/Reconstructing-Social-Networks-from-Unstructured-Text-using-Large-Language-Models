We considered that production of the CCMV VLP with the negative inside had worked well in the first batch which produced with the well acknowledged production strain BL-21, and not in the later batch from the JM-109 strain. Because our last attempt to produce PLRV VLPs was done in JM-109 cells as well, we figure it may be possible that the production strain was the problem. Therefore, we will try the same procedure again but now in BL-21 E. coli. We transformed electrocompetent BL-21 cells with Bba_K883401, which contains a PLRV Coat Protein encoding gene under the IPTG inducible promotor.
