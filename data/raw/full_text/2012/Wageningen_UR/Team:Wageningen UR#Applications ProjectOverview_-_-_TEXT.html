They can also be used as standardized vaccine platforms, nanoreactors, or novel building blocks. The goal of our project is to create standardized self-assembling particles with a simple and versatile attachment system for either packaging molecules, presenting ligands/epitopes, or both. By combining the PnA (Plug ‘n Apply) System and Virus-Like Particles (VLPs), we create a tool that can be applied in numerous applications.
