Deutsch  /  English  #1 Selection / Swimming - 2nd Week Back to overview V05_11 V05_11_1: Comparison of Yeast- and Beef-agar for Chemotaxis experiments Experiment: In this experiment it will be tested, if Yeast-agar (1%) or beef-agar (0.3%) is better for chemotaxis experiments. Observations & Results: Booth strains show swarming on Yeast- (1%) and beef (0.3%) agar. Swimming in general very small. Maybe lower nutrition concentrations. V05_11_2: Finding the right cell-concentrations for chemotaxis experiments Experiment: We will test the cell concentrations between 10^6 and 10^9 cells/ml. We drop 10 µL on the agar plate. Observations & Results: All cell-concentrations between 10^5 and 10^9 showed good swarming. All tested cell-concentrations are usable. V05_11_3: Finding the right volumina for chemotaxis experiments Experiment: Due to the problems with blured colonies we test different volumina for the colony-drop. Here are tested 1; 5 and 10 µL. Observations & Results: The volumina 5 and 10 µL show swarming. The volume of 1 µL does not show swarming. 5 µL will be used for the further experiments Back to overview ↑ Back to top!
