References Attributions Scottish iGEM Team Meet-up: 27th June, Edinburgh University, Edinburgh Dundee iGEM Team joined the other Scottish teams from Edinburgh and St Andrews universities at a collaboration day hosted by The University of Edinburgh. We discussed strategies, shared ideas and discussed potential problems. We also participated in workshops together before giving a small presentation on our plans for devlopment of our project. The feedback was really helpful. UK iGEM Team Meet-up: 17th August, Google Campus, London Wuhan University iGEM 2012 collaboration Dundee iGEM Team helped the Wuhan University 2012 in China by supplying Salmonella LT2 DNA for use in their project. You can find out more details about this by clicking on the "Acknowledgements & Attribution" tab of this webpage: WHU-China 2012 Characterisation and debugging existing biobricks The Dundee 2012 iGEM team sequenced, improved and debugged two existing biobricks from the registry. The details are listed on our Biobricks page.
