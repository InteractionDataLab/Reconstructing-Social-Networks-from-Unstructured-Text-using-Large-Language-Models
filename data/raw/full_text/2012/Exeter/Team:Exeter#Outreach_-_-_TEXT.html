Using iGEM as our springboard, we invited local A-Level students to participate in the project as short term members of the team, and held a synthetic biology workshop with a wider audience of local sixth form students (age 16 to 18). To advertise our experience to an older audience, we presented our work at the Exeter Café Scientifique meeting where we discussed, and were challenged, on the wider effects of our project and of synthetic biology as a whole.
