Digest for 37C/30 min and then heat kill at 80C/20 min. Once heat killed, a ligation mix is made-up containing: 2µl of digested plasmid backbone (25ng) Equimolar amount of EcoRI-HF SpeI digested fragment (< 3 µL) Equimolar amount of XbaI PstI digested fragment (< 3 µL) 1 µL T4 DNA ligase buffer. 0.5 µL T4 DNA ligase Top up solution to 10µL with water Ligate at 16C/30 min and then heat kill at 80C for 20 min. Transform with 1-2µL of product.
