This takes about 1h in the autoclave. Prepare the plates by labeling and ethanol cleaning a big enough surface. Close the bottle and allow to cool down to 50-55ºC (colder will start to solidify, warmer might degrade the antibiotic). Add the antibiotic to the bottle: 100 µg/ml for ampicillin, 50-100 µg/ml for spectinomycin. Add 25-30 ml of the medium with antibiotic to each plate. Leave half open for about 2 h for cooling. Close hte plates. Wrap the in alu foil (antibiotics are sensitive to light) and store the at 4ºC.
