It is useful if you want to quantify your fluorescent readout relatively to a baseline. To control it, use the provided Excel utility. 1. Take ~1 million cells in 750 µL PBS. 2. Centrifuge at 450g for 5 min. 3. Remove the supernatant. 4. Resuspend in 200µL of 1% TritonX in PBS. 5. Load onto a black well plate and leave in the shaker for 1h. 6. Read fluorescence on the machine using the appropriate Excel program.
