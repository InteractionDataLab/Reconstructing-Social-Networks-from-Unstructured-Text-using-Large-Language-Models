Preparing a Maxiprep for 12th July Two maxipreps to be done: LovTAP + Ampicillin, transformed with our cells the 28 June. RO + Spectinomycin 100 µg/ml, transformed with our cells the 10 July. We use 2 big erlenmeyers (like 1l), to allow the liquid to be shaken (bacteria like it). We want a volume of 200 ml for each, with 100 µg/ml of antibiotic. This means 200 µl of antibiotic per maxiprep (antibiotics have both a concentration of 100 mg/ml). Steps: Take the 2 plates from the fridge and let warm in oven for 5 minutes, 37ºC. Take the antibiotics from the freezer and let thaw. Fill the 2 erlenmeyers with 200 ml of LB each. Add 200 µl of antibiotic to each, spect to RO and amp to LovTAP. Pick a colony from each plate with a pipete tip and add it to the resp. erlenmeyer. Leave overnight in the incubator, turning, at 37ºC. Protocol: None Forgot to insert protocol.
