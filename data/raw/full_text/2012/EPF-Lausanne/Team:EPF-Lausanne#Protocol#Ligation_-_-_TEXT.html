This is often the step following a PCR (and a PCR cleanup ) or a gel extraction . You can also do a "dirty" ligation, where you follow a certain number of digestions directly by a ligation. Download the following spreadsheet : File:Team-EPF-Lausanne Ligation.xls Fill in the pink areas with the vector and fragment concentration, their size and the ratio. Add all the suggested ingredients order in a microcentrifuge tube, in the order they appear. Ligate for 2 hours at 14ºC. Immediately transform competent bacteria with the ligation product. Note: This protocol hasn't been optimized for blunt-end ligation (though it might still work).
