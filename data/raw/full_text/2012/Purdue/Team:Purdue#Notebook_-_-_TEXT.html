Had a team meeting, discussed goals for the following week: Write out protocols and email them to the entire team for revisions Plan a meeting to go through and troubleshoot the protocols together Find a way to determine the optimized concentration of curli Purchase all materials needed for future protocols Monday, July 16 Researched how Membrane Aerated Bioreactor work and how we will use one for testing. There are multiple types of Membrane Aerated Biofilms: MABR (the type we will be using in our experiments), Membrane biofilter, and extractive membrane bioreactor (EMB). These reactors can be in shell and tube or plate and frame configurations. We will be using the shell and tube configuration. The two limiting substrates are introduced at opposite ends of the bioreactor. The limiting substrates are C-substrate (carbon substrate) and/or oxygen. The biofilm thickness must be less than the oxygen penetration (50-200 micrometers) but stay less than 150 micrometers for best performance.
