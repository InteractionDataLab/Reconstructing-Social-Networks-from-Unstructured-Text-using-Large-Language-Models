Web site : www.iecb.u-bordeaux.fr University of Bordeaux 1 Bordeaux I About : Bordeaux 1's focus is on sciences and technologies. Its motto is "Science and Technologies for the service of Man and the Environment.[3] Among its topics are: biological sciences, chemistry, computer sciences, earth sciences, mathematics, physics, and sea sciences. It is located in the Bordeaux suburb of Talence. Web site : www.u-bordeaux1.fr University of Bordeaux Segalen Bordeaux II About : It principal focus on medical, life, and human sciences makes Bordeaux Segalen the biology and health hub of the rapidly-developing Bordeaux University. In December 2010, Bordeaux Segalen, a founder member of the Bordeaux University PRES (Pôle Recherche et Enseignement Supérieur - Research and Higher Education Cluster), joined Bordeaux 1 and Bordeaux 4 universities, Sciences Po Bordeaux, and IPB (Institut Polytechnique de Bordeaux - Bordeaux Polytechnic Institute) in making the commitment to set up a single establishment. Web site : www.univ-bordeauxsegalen.fr École Nationale Supérieure de Technologie des Biomolécules de Bordeaux ENSTBB About : ENSTBB is a graduate School of Biotechnology and Bioprocessing funded by the French Ministry in charge of higher education and research. Its goal is to educate and train advanced students and to provide professional training and resources for industry and conduct innovative research.
