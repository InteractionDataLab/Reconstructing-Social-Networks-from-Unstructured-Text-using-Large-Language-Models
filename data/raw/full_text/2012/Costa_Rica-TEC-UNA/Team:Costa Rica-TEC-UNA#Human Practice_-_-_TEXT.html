Also we set out to promote for other students in high schools and other universities for them to participate with new projects for iGEM in representation of our country. To accomplish this objective we made expositions, classes and conferences of these subjects for 10th grade high school students of the Scientific High School of Cartago, for the teachers of the faculty of biology, for pharmacy students of the University of Costa Rica and for different career students, teachers and investigators in the Biology Week organized by the faculty of biology in the National University (UNA).
