E.coli which has ability to kill a cancer cell

Project:Abstract

We are making E.coli which has ability to kill a cancer cell.
We will give following functions to E.coli.

  1. a function to E.coli with the chemotaxis to cancer cell.
  2. a function to E.coli to combine with a cancer cell.
  3. a function to E.coli to release azurin to cancer cell.
The azurin make cancer cell death with apoptosis.
