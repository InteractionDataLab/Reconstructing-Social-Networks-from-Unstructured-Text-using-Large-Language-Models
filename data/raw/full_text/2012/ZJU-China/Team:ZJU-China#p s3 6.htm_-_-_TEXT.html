IAM pathway (Group: iGEM2011_Imperial)   Assessment:   Five pathways described above all have some drawbacks, finally, only one pathway left, IAM pathway. The two-step IAM pathway generates indole-3-acetic acid (IAA) from the precursor tryptophan.
