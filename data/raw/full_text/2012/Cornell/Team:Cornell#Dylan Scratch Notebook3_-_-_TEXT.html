Dylan connected second reactor to SP-200, which just arrived back from field use in Alaska. Current was steady at ≈ 20 µA. However, flow through the reactor was paused because the media tank we depleted. Dylan made new media, and will wait overnight to ensure that the reactor reaches steady state. If steady state is indeed reached, we will perturb the reactors with salicylate to start getting more definitive data.
