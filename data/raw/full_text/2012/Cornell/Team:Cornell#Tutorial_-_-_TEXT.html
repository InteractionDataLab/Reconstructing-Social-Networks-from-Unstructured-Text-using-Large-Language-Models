It gets processed into text, layout, images, and so on when the page is viewed. The most basic system for organizing information on a wiki page is the section. The headers above are themselves sections. In order to make a section, you simply write the title of the section, then surround it by a certain number of equals signs. Below are some sections of different levels, and to the right is how they would appear in the table of contents.
