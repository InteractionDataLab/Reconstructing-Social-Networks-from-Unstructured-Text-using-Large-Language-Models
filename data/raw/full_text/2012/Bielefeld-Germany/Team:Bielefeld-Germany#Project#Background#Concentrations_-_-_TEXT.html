These data show that our water is contaminated with several micro-contaminants. Some of them are found in a terrifying concentrations in treated wastewater. Micro-contaminants concentration of sewage effluent and surface water These assay results were collected by the State Agency for Environment, Nature Conservation and Geology Mecklenburg-Vorpommern from 2008 to 2010. Measured data from watersamples of sewage treatment plants and surface waters are shown as part of a study ordered by the Federal Environment Agency (UBA-Germany).
