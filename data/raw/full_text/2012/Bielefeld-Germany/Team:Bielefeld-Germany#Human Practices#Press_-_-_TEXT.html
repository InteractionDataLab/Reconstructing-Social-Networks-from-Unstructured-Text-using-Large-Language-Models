In following weeks and months a lot of newspapers, journals and online magazines reported on our project. We are very proud to have raised interest not only in Germany but in several other countries, too. Not only did we have much fun but we are sure that the film will help to communicate SynBio and iGEM and thus to lower prejudices and anxiety of the broader public towards SynBio.
