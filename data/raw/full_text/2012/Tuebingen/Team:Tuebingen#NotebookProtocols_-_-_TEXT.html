Cells Pick an E. coli colony and inoculate 25 ml SOB. Let bacteria grow for 8 hours at 37 °C and 250 rpm. Inoculate three 100 ml SOB volumes with 1 ml, 2 ml and 4 ml of the prepared pre-culture. Incubate over night at 18 - 22 °C and 200 rpm. At OD600 = 0.55, put culture for 10 min on ice. Centrifuge cells at 2500 g for 10 min at 4 °C. Discard supernatant completely. Resuspend cell pellet in 30 ml 0 °C Inoue buffer. Centrifuge cells at 2500 g for 10 min at 4 °C. Discard supernatant completely. Repeat the previous two steps. Resuspend cells in 8 ml 0 °C Inoue buffer. Add 1.5 ml DMSO and incubate on ice for 10 min. Aliquot cells à 100 µl and freeze in liquid nitrogen. Incubate reaction at 4 °C over night. Chemotransformation Component Volume chemo-competent E. coli 100 µl plasmid DNA up to 10 µl (max. 1/10 of volume) Add plasmid DNA to cell culture. Incubate for 30 min on ice. Heat shock for 90 sec at 42 °C. Add 900 µl LB. Let the bacteria grow at 37 °C for at least 1 hour.
