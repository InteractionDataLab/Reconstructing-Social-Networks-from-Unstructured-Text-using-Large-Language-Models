Excised the DNA fragment from an agarose gel. For each 100 mg of agarose gel added 200 μl Buffer NT. 2. Incubated sample for 5 – 10 minutes at 50 °C. Vortexed the sample briefly every 2 – 3 minuets until the gel slice was completely dissolved. 3. Placed a NucleoSpin Extract II Columu into a Collection Tube and loaded the sample. Centrifuged for 1 minute at 11,000 x g. Discarded flow-through and placed the column back into the collection tube. 4. Added 700 μl Buffer NT3 to the NucleoSpin Extact II Column. Centrifuged for 1 minute at 11,000 x g. Discarded flow-through and placed the column back into the collection tube. 5. Centrifuged for 2 minutes at 11,000 x g to remove Buffer NT3 completely. 6. Placed the NucleoSpin Extract II Column into a new 1.5 ml microcentrifuge tube. Added 50 μl Buffer NE and incubated at room temperature for 1 minute.
