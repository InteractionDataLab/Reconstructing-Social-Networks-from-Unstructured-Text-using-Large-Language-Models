Historical Basis Developing Tools Support iGEM HQ Detail Fund Database Historical Basis Our project, Biohub, is an enhanced work of Biopano, which is the product of the same team in 2014. When we started our project, we received much information about Biopano from our easy-going seniors. Therefore we decided to make a superior artwork in order to pay tribute to our university. We give them our most genuine gratitude to the team members of the team USTC-Software of 2014, and their project as well. Developing Tools & Libraries Backend: MySQL, Docker, Python Flask framework, SQLAlchemy, Biopython, PyCharm Professional, Gitlab. Frontend: Materialize, d3.js, jQuery.
