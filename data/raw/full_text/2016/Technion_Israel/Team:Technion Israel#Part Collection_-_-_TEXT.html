This collection contains our designed hybrid receptors, reporters and devices regarding E. coli chemotaxis abilities. The devices in this collection are versatile, expressing different receptors including versions fused with reporters and with different RBS. We also included the receptors and reporters as stand alone parts, allowing other teams to construct their own chemotaxis devices. Each part in this collection is well documented and tested in the lab, ensuring easy usage for future iGEM teams. As our goal is to construct a large library containing various receptors and devices for chemotaxis, we believe that this collection is a great start! Hopefully more teams will contribute to this growing collection in the future to make chemotaxis more universal and accessible.
