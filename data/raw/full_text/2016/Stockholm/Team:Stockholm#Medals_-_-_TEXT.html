It was an amazing and enriching experience for all of us and we had the opportunity to present our poster to many other science and engineering enthusiasts as well as give a presentation on our project. Our hard work was rewarded - we met all the criteria listed below and received a gold medal. We were also nominated for awards given for 'Best Presentation' and 'Best Education and Public Engagement'! iGEM has been a fascinating, eye-opening journey for our team and we'll always treasure the memories.
