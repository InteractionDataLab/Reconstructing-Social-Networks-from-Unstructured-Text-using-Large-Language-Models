This includes: Expressing biofilm-degrading 'combat proteins’ and quantifying their potency Assembling combat proteins with a linker and tag needed for purification and attachment to spider silk Attaching the enzymes to spider silk using Sortase A, a transpeptidase Isolating Sortase A from the S.aureus genome and creating a ‘Sortase Package’ including basic parts needed for other iGEM teams to use Sortase in their own projects Testing the efficacy of the fully assembled wound dressing with attached enzymes on biofilms iGEM Stockholm is sponsored by
