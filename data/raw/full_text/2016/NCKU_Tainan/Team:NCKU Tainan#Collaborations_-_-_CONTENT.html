    Protocols & Materials

    To collaborate with TCU_Taiwan, we shared the protocol and sent the material for 1000ml of Glucose-free modified M9 medium for cell culturing and functional experiments.

    Protocol for modified M9:

    For preparing 10X M9 stock

    • Formulation
    • Disodium Phosphate Heptahydrate 128 g/L
    • Monopotassium Phosphate 30 g/L
    • Sodium Chloride 5 g/L
    • Ammonium Chloride 10 g/L
    • Final pH (2X solution): 6.6–7.0 at 25°C

    After diluting to 1X and autoclave, for every 20 ml, add

    • 20% D-Glucose solution 400ul
    • 1.0 M MgSO4 solution 40ul
    • 1.0 M CaCl2 solution 2ul
    • 20% Casamino acid 200ul

    Modified M9 Sample sent to TCU_Taiwan
