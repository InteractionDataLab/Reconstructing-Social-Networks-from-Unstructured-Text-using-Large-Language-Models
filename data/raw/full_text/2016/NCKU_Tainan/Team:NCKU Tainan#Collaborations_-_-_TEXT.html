Protocol for modified M9: For preparing 10X M9 stock Formulation Disodium Phosphate Heptahydrate 128 g/L Monopotassium Phosphate 30 g/L Sodium Chloride 5 g/L Ammonium Chloride 10 g/L Final pH (2X solution): 6.6–7.0 at 25°C After diluting to 1X and autoclave, for every 20 ml, add 20% D-Glucose solution 400ul 1.0 M MgSO4 solution 40ul 1.0 M CaCl2 solution 2ul 20% Casamino acid 200ul Modified M9 Sample sent to TCU_Taiwan Dry Lab Collaboration with TCU_Taiwan Both of us are doing the project about glucose detection. We discuss some issues about how E. coli reacts when glucose is added and shares the information and difficulties. Furthermore, we also provide some mathematical support to derive the formula of production rates when system reaches its equilibrium. Protocols & Materials Dry Lab
