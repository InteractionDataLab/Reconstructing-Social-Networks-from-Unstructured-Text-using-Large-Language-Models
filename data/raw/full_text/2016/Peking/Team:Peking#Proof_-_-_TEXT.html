Uranyl Adsorption Super Uranyl-binding Protein (SUP) is stable and highly selective for uranyl which may cause negative impact on the environment. Triple SpyTag-SUP (3A-SUP) was constructed to adsorb uranyl, and the cross-linked product of 3A-SUP and 3B showed an even better adsorption capacity. Clearance The monomeric streptavidin constructed in the polymer network could realize the clearance of Spy Crosslinking Network in the environment by interacting with biotinylated magnetic beads. Secretion To make the polymer network reproductive and economical, a signal peptide library was constructed. We carried out a series of experiments to find an optimal secretion strategy.
