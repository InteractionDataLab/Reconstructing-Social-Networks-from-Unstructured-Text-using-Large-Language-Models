    • tolR: Available both with (BBa_K2022003) and without (BBa_K2022002) a his-tag

    Our favourite part, however, and that which we submit for the Best Part prize is BBa_K2022000. We managed to confirm its role as an inducer of hypervesiculation, by increasing both vesicle size and number.
