    Hello, We are Korea_U_Seoul team from Korea University! :)

    Korea_U_Seoul is Korea University iGEM Team since we started participating in iGEM in 2009.Korea_U_Seoul has achieved various awards from 2010 to 2015. In Korea, Korea_U_Seoul is known as KUAS (Korea Univ. Association of Synthetic biology).
