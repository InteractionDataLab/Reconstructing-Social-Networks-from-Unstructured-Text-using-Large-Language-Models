    Cleantech Challenge is a competition of technologies aimed at reducing environmental impact, participate in this contest, he gave us a great opportunity to show how it can influence a project as we develop in society. We reached the penultimate stage of the competition and although we did not get first place, the team received very good reviews about their performance, for example, was described as innovative, different, with a great future and great members. It is important to mention that we were the youngest in participating team and also the only one to present a proposal for biotechnology. Winning the competition represent the best opportunity to bring society the use of genetically modified organisms and to advance the search for new, better and cleaner technologies. This meeting gave us the opportunity to show what young people are doing as to make the change from three different ways, how we impact from political, sports and scientific fields? and what are we doing to achieve this?
