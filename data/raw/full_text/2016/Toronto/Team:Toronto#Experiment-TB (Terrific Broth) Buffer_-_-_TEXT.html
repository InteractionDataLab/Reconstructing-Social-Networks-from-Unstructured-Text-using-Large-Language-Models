TB Mix tryptone, yeast extract and glycerol, and add distilled water up to 900mL. Pour into 2 L flask (or greater) Autoclave (liquid cycle) both the potassium phosphate solution and TB buffer. Allow liquid to cool to less than 60 degrees Centrigrade. Add both the potassium phosphate solution and TB buffer together.
