    PureLink ® PCR Purification protocol

    Introduction

    Minor changes were made to the protocol supplied with the Invitrogen PureLink® PCR Purification Kit

    Materials

    • Reagents
    • PureLink ® Binding Buffer (B2)
    • PureLink ® Spin Column
    • Wash Buffer (supplied with kit)
    • Elution Buffer (10 mM Tris-HCl, pH 8.5)
    • Disstilled water >7.0 pH
  • Materials
  • PureLink ® Spin Column
  • Collection Tube
  • centrifuge

    • Procedure

    • Protocol
    1. Add 4 volumes of PureLink ® Binding Buffer (B2) with isopropanol to 1 volume of the PCR product (50–100 μL). Mix well.
    1. Remove a PureLink ® Spin Column in a Collection Tube from the package.
    1. Add the sample with the appropriate Binding Buffer (from step 1 of this procedure) to the PureLink ® Spin Column.
    1. Centrifuge the column at room temperature at 13,000 × g for 1 minute.
    1. Discard the flow through and place the spin column into the collection tube.
    1. Add 650 μL of Wash Buffer with ethanol to the column.
    1. Centrifuge th e column at room temperature at 13,000 × g for 1 minute. Discard the flow through from the collection tube and place the column into the tube.
    1. Centrifuge the column at maximum speed (15, 000 x g)at room temperature for 2–3 minutes to remove any residual Wash Buffer. Discard the collection tube.
    1. Place the spin column in a clean 1.7-mL PureLink ® Elution Tube supplied with the kit.
    1. Add 50 μL of Elution Buffer (10 mM Tris-HCl, pH 8.5) or sterile, distilled water (pH >7.0) to the center of the column.
    1. Incubate the column at room temperature for 1 minute. Centrifuge the column at maximum speed for 2 minutes.
    1. The elution tube contains the purified PCR product. Remove and discard the column. Store the purified PCR product at –20°C or use the PCR product for the desired downstream application.
