To further evolve binding proteins with an innate ability to interact with our target we want to mutagenize them and thereby enable an ongoing adaptation to the target. We successfully cloned two different approaches to in vivo mutagenesis, demonstrated and quantified mutagenesis by reversion assays and determined the mutation spectrum by next-generation sequencing .
