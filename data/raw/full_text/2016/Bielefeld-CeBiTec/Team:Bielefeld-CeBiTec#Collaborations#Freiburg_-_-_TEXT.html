Protocol from 2016-09-15 Participants Freiburg: Wlad Paricipants Bielefeld: Judith , Mika The projects were once again resumed and innovations were stated. We then came back to the topics we could help each other out. Freiburg: The principle of the OCTET RED measurement and the conditions the proteins have to fulfill were mentioned once again.
