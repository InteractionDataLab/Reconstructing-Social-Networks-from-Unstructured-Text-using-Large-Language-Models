Two plasmids will be constructed, each carrying a module of our two part system. Metarhizium anisopliae ARSEF 549 will be transformed with both plasmids and inoculated on live specimen of Bactrocera dorsalis and Spodoptera litura to test the viability and efficacy of the system. If the system is viable, we think that it could be adapted for other organism for similar purposes.
