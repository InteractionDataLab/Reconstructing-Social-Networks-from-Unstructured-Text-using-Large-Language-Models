Attributions

[2016-Attribution]

　We are proud to say that all the ideas we worked on this year were proposed by undergraduate members of iGEM 2016 team Tokyo_Tech. We also want to thank all those advisors and instructors who helped us discuss these ideas and guided us through doing the experiments. The whole experimental data produced by our team comes from experiments all carried out by undergraduates.

The project "Snow White" was proposed by Ayako Tamaki.
