    Medal Criteria

    Here, we want to summarize how we fulfilled the criteria required to win medals in the iGEM competition in the course of our project.

    Bronze (4/4)

    Register & Attend We successfully registered for the competition and are very excited to finally fly to Boston for the Giant Jamboree.

    Deliverables: (8/8)Team wiki As you can see here, we were able to create an awesome wiki to proudly present our results. Poster We would like to invite everyone to come to our booth and check out our poster. Presentation We will give a presentation about our whole project at the Giant Jamboree. Project attribution All of the work done for our project has been attributed properly.Registry part pages To document the parts that we created in the course of our project, we prepared pages on the Registry which should help everyone to use our parts in the most efficient way.Sample submission The DNA of our new parts is submitted to the Registry.Safety forms All safety forms were completed with great care.Judging form We completed the judging form to help judges evaluate us for prizes and awards.

    Attribution On our attribution page you can find a detailed description of who contributed to our project and how.

    Part / Contribution To our great satisfaction, we successfully documented multiple new standard BioBrick parts central to our project and submitted them to the Registry.

    Silver (3/3)

    Validated Part/Contribution In addition to the creation of new standard BioBrick parts, we also experimentally validated the functionality of one of our parts and documented its characterization in the Registry.BBa_K2020042BBa_K2020002

    Collaboration We gladly worked together with other teams to establish a mutual connection that would benefit both of our projects.

    Human Practices To examine our project from a different angle, we identified, investigated, and addressed important issues outside of the lab.

    Gold (3/4)

    Integrated Human Practices In due consideration of the results of our human practice work, we improved the final design of our project.

    Improvement of a previous part or project We continued the work of the iGEM team Texas Austin 2014 and improved their measurement kit to determine the incorporation-efficiency of a tRNA-synthetases-pair. We successfully used the improved kit in the course of our project and made it available for everyone by submitting it to the Registry in the BioBrick standard.BBa_K1416004BBa_K555009

    Proof of concept With one of our newly created BioBricks, we were able to prove that the principle of our project is functional.
