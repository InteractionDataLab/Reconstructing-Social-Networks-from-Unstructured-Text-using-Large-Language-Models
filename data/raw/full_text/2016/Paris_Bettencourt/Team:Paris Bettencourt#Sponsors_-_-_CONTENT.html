    Fondation Bettencourt Schueller

    We are very grateful for the Fondation Bettencourt Schueller that made possible our participation through their extremely generous support, they backed the team since its first appearance in the competition in 2007.

    Inserm and CRI (Centre de recherche interdisciplinary)

    Our iGEM team is kindly hosted in the CRI (Inderdisciplinary Research Center) located in the Faculté de médecine Paris Descartes at the center of Paris. For the Laboratory's facilities, we are kindly hosted by the CRI/INSERM U1001 lab, that lent us benches and others facilities. We thank all the members of the INSERM U1001 for their kind help and that they shared not only their equipment but also their experience with us.

    Université Paris Descartes and Université Sorbonne Paris Cité

    We are very grateful for Université Paris Descartes and Université Sorbonne Paris Cité's financial support for our project.

    IDT

    IDT was the iGEM official sponsors, we would like to thank them for meeting all our synthesis needs.

    GATC

    Thank you GATC for their great support for genome sequencing.

    New England Biolabs

    New England Biolabs was one of the iGEM official sponsors, we would like to thank them for providing us enzymes.

    Geneious

    We would like to thank Geneious for providing us a licence for their software.

    MathWorks

    We would like to thank MathWorks for providing us a licence for MathLab.

    Petit Bateau

    We are very grateful for the marinières, these typical fancy French jumpers, provided by Petit Bateau so we look very elegant to all our events.
