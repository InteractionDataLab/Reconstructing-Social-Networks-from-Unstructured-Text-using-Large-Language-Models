    • SynBioThon 2016Projects

    Here you can read about the 15 projects that were generated at SBT16.

    Design of a modified Pseudomonas capable of bioremediating aluminium saturated soils.

    Design of a genetic circuit that allows the bioavailability of caffeic acid so it can be used as a nutritional supplement in drinks.

    Genetic modification of a yeast so it can produce Betaine from tyrosine.

    Over-production of n-acetyl polysaccharides to generate a natural glue inspired in the polysaccharides that bacteria produce to anchor different surfaces.

    Production of Ectoine from orange peels (agroindustrial waste). Ectoine can be used as additive in sunblock formulations in order to diminish skin cancer incidence.

    Solve the loss of climateric fruit due to ripening provoked by ethylene synthesis, stopping it by the suppression of essential genes at the adequate time.

    Peptide secretion as a treatment for gastrointestinal infections generated by Helicobacter pylori.

    Heparin (anticoagulant) synthesis in microalgae to provide a lower cost product.

    Synthesis of a new antibiotic.

    Biological synthesis of Melanin to supplant the chemical synthesis.

    Production of a fosfatase with higher analgesic effect than morphine and no side effects.

    Probiotics production with a modified Lactobacillus with the capacity of synthesising an enzyme for gluten degradation.

    Nepidermin (Human Epidermal Growth Factor) production in yeast, for cicatrization.

    Synthesis of Phosphatidylcholine (main component of egg yolk) with the objective of producing vegan products.

    Biosensor of volatile compounds generated in the body as indicators of Cirrhosis.
