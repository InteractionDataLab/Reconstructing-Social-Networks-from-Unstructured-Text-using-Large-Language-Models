    • SynBioThon 2016Evaluation & Winners

    The evaluation consisted of two different rounds.

    First Round

    During first round the 15 teams were divided in three groups (five teams per group) and one judge was assigned per group. Every team gave an exposition of five minutes while the respective judge scored from 1 to 10 the following aspects:

    Presentation

    Impact and viability

    Chassis

    Bioinformatic design

    Once every judge listened the five teams that corresponded, they checked quickly the scores assigned and selected the two teams with highest scores. In this way, every judge selected two finalists, having a total of six teams for the second round:

    AlumiGen3+

    GastroLac

    Hemoalgae

    PAP aleve

    MelAro

    BetPro

    Second Round

    At the second round the six finalists were asked to give a presentation of 10 minutes, with two minutes extra for questions from the judges. In this round the four judges (including Silver, who qualified Benchling during the first round) listened the six presentations, but every judge evaluated a different aspect:

    • 30% - Bioinformatic design, by Silver Ceballos.

    • 30% - Technical viability, by Raúl Trejos.

    • 10% - Presentation, by the four judges.

    • 20% - Impact by Karla Valerín.

    • 10% - Chassis by Karol Jiménez.

    The winners

    Hemoalgae First Place

    Myrka Rojas, Luis Barboza and Diana Mendoza.

    Bet PRO Second Place

    Alex Araya, Adrián Salas, Thomas Prieto and Bradd Mendoza.

    PAP-aleve Third Place

    Lil Gutiérrez, Andrés Vargas, Marian Morera and Maite Sainz.
