Human Practices

Human Practices is an important part of LiU iGEM’s project objective. The purpose of the Human Practices was to reach a broad target audience via different types of information channels and activities. The audience varied between all the different segments of the society and the activities consisted of everything from movie production to lectures about gene technology for University students.
