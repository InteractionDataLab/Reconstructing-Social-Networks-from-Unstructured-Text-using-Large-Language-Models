Team Leicester iGEM 2016

             

(Calcitonin Hyper Expression using E.Coli in Space Exploration)

Throughout our project we received much support and help from many people. Firstly we’d like to thank Dr Badge and Professor Dalgleish for offering us the opportunity to participate in iGEM and for helping us get to the Giant Jamboree in Boston. We would also like to thank Liam Crawford for being our advisor and helping us get started in the lab and with our project.

  • University of Leicester Biochemistry Department for giving us financial support when applying for the iGEM registration

  • Harinder Matharu for helping us with promoting our team to the public
  • Anisha Bhogaita for giving us the opportunity to fundraise and promote ourselves at various open days
