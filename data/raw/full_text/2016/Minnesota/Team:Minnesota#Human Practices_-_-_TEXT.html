This event allows the community to learn about the latest concepts in synthetic biology, as well as have an open discussion of these topics. We were able to use this opportunity to run activities aimed at teaching people of all ages about biological science and also gauge public opinion on new technologies such as gene drives and genetically modified food. Learn more about Building With Biology at their website: http://buildingwithbiology.org/ Photo Gallery Gene Drives and Society Video To understand the concept of gene drives, as well as gauge the opinion of scientists who work in fields related to this advancement, we created a documentary from interviews we had with local researchers.
