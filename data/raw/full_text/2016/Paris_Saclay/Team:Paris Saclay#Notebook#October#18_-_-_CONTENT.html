    Electro transformation of DH5a cells with K2039002

    By Sylvie

    4 samples of DH5a cells were transformed using electroporation protocol using:

    • 1µL of K2039002 preparation, diluted 10 times
    • 1 µL of K2039002 preparation, no dilution
    • 5 µL of K2039002 preparation, no dilution
    • 1 control (without DNA)

    Transformed cells were plated on LB solid media with Chloramphenicol and Ampicilin.
