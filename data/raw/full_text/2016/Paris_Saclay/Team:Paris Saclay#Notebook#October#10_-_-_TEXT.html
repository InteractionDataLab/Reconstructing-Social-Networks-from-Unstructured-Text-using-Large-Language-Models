Digestion of extracted plasmids by EcoRI By Maxence & Caroline Extracted pPS16_019, pPS16_018, pPS16_009 and BbaB0015 from the 10th October were digested by restriction enzymes EcoRI in order to verify the concentration as following: 2 µL of plasmid 2 µL of buffer FD 2 µL of restriction enzyme EcoRI 14 µL of water The mix were incubated for 1 hour at 37°C. Then, 20 µL of digestion products and 15 µL of DNA ladder were placed in wells and migrated at 100V during 30 min. Result of the migration The digestions were good for pPS16_019, pPS16_018 and BbaB0015, but we did not obtained the expecting band size for pPS16_009.
