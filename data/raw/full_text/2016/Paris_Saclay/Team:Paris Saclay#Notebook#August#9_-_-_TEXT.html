Result of the PCR pUC19 digestion with HincII enzyme By Alice 5 µL of pUC19 plasmids were digested with 5µL of tango buffer 10X, 38µL of sterile water, and 1 µL of HincII enzyme. The mix was incubated at 37°C for 1 hour. After incubation, 1µL of HincII enzyme was added again, and the mix was incubated 1 hour again.
