High fidelity Q5 PCR on pPS16_004, pPS16_006 and pPS16_007 By Caroline Q5 PCR was carried out following the usual protocol adapted to 50µL at a Tm of 72°C. The primers used were specific to amplify only the sequences of interest. The products were put for migration on a 0.8%agarose gel with BET. Only, PCR form pPS16_004 and pPS16_007 showed a positive result.
