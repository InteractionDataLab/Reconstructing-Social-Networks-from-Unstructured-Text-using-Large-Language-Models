Segment DNA quantity (ng/µL) 260/230 260/280 1 84,20 1,25 1,84 2 169,88 1,29 1,83 Enough DNA quantity was obtained to perform a Gibson assembly on segments 1 and 2. Gibson Assembly of segments 1 and 2 By mathilde Two preparations were made : the 1-2 Gibson assembly product tube : 1,12µL of purified segment 1 and 0,56µL of purified segment 2 + 0,85µL of digested pSB1C3 + 7,47 µL of sterile water + 10µL of NEB Builder Hifi DNA Assembler Master Mix the control tube : 0,85µL of digested pSB1C3 + 17,47 µL of sterile water Gibson products were incubated 1h at 52°c. Transformation in DH5α with the 1-2 Gibson assembly product By Mathilde The transformation was performed following the usual protocol with 2µL of plasmid. 50, 150 and 350µL of the transformed DH5α with the 1-2 Gibson assembly product were plated on 3 plates of LB + chloramphenicol (30µg/mL) 350 µL of each Gibson assembly control and transformation control were plated on 2 plates of B + chloramphenicol (30µg/mL) Petri cultures were incubated at 37°c overnight.
