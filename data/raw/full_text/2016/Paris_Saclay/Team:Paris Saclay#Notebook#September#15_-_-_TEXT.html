Q5 PCR of NM sgRNA in pJET By Maxence As we did not achieve to obtain PCR products of NM sgRNA with Phusion, another strategy was tried with Q5. Q5 PCR was performed on plasmids with the following protocol: For each 50μl of reaction, mix the following reagents : 1 µL of plasmid 1 µL of dNTPs (10mM) 1 µL of each primer mix (10µM) 10 µL of Q5 buffer (5X) 0,5 µL of Q5 high fidelity polymerase 35,5 µL of nuclease free water Furthermore, 3% DMSO was also used: instead of 35,5 µL of nuclease free water : 1,5 µL of DMSO + 34 µL of nuclease free water. Mix gently and aliquot 50 μl of the mix into the PCR tubes on ice.
