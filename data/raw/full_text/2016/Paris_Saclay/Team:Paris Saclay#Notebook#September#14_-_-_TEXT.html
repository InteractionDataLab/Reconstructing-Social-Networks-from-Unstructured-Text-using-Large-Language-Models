Furthermore, ligations products from the 13th September were also amplified. For each 50μl of reaction, mix the following reagents : 1 µL of matrix 1 µL of dNTPs (10mM) 2.5 µL of each primer mix (10µM) 10 µL of buffer (5X) 0,5 µL of Phusion polymerase 32.5 µL of nuclease free water Mix gently and aliquot 50 μl of the mix into the PCR tubes on ice.
