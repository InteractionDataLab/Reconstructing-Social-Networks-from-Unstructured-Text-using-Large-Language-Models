For that purpose, PCR was performed with the following protocol. For each 50μl of reaction, mix the following reagents : 1 µL of matrix 1 µL of dNTPs (10mM) 2.5 µL of each primer mix (10µM) 10 µL of buffer (5X) 0,5 µL of Phusion polymerase 31 µL of nuclease free water 1.5 µL of DMSO Mix gently and aliquot 50 μl of the mix into the PCR tubes on ice.
