For that purpose, plasmids extracted today (clones 4, 7, 10 & 11 for 2 fragments Gibson and clones 2, 4, 4 & 6 for 3 fragments Gibson) were used. For each 50μl of reaction, mix the following reagents : 1 µL of matrix 1 µL of dNTPs (10mM) 2.5 µL of each primer mix (10µM) 10 µL of buffer (5X) 0,5 µL of Phusion polymerase 32.5 µL of nuclease free water Mix gently and aliquot 50 μl of the mix into the PCR tubes on ice.
