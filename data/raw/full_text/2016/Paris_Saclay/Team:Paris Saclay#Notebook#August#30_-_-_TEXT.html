Transformation in DH5α with the 1-2 Gibson assembly product By Mahnaz The transformation was performed following the usual protocol with 2µL of plasmid. 50, 150 and 350µL of the transformed DH5α with the 1-2 Gibson assembly product were plated on 3 plates of LB + chloramphenicol (30µg/mL) 350 µL of each Gibson assembly control and transformation control were plated on 2 plates of B + chloramphenicol (30µg/mL) Petri cultures were incubated at 37°c overnight.
