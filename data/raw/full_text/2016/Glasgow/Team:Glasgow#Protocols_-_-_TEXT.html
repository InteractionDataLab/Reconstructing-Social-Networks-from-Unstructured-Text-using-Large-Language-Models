Shortbread A good supply of shortbread is essential to any productive lab environment. Recipe courtesy of BBC Food . 125g/4oz butter 55g/2oz caster sugar, plus extra to finish 180g/6oz plain flour Heat the oven to 190⁰C/375F/Gas 5 Beat the butter and the sugar together until smooth. Stir in the flour to get a smooth paste. Turn on to a work surface and gently roll out until the paste is 1cm/½in thick. Cut into rounds or fingers and place onto a baking tray. Sprinkle with caster sugar and chill in the fridge for 20 minutes. Bake in the oven for 15-20 minutes, or until pale golden-brown. Set aside to cool on a wire rack.
