LB Agar plates preparation (500mL) Mix the following component in 500mL of water: 5g Tryptone 2.5g Yeast extract 5g NaCl 7.5g Agar powder Add a magnetic stirrer bar and mix the solution until no there is lump left Autoclave at 121 Celsius degrees for 15 to 30 minutes to sterilize Allow the autoclaved medium to cool to 55ºC (until you can hold it in your hand) and transfer it into a 50mL Falcon tube* Add 50µL antibiotics (Chloramphenicol at a final concentration of 25µg/mL) and mix* Poor 25mL of the liquid into an identified petri plate* Let the plate dry* *Steps to realize under the PSM class II Reagents Distilled water Tryptone (VWR, 84610.0500) Yeast extract (Biokar,A1202HA) NaCl (Merck,7647-14-5) Agar powder (Sigma-Aldrich, 05040) Chloramphenicol (Sigma-Aldrich C0378) Materials Weighing scale Petri dish (Thermo scientific, 101RT/IRR) Autoclave pH-meter Magnetic stirrer Sources Sigma-Aldrich - Instructions to microbial media Facebook Twitter youtube LinkedIn iGEM IONIS We're a group of six different schools from the IONIS Education Group.
