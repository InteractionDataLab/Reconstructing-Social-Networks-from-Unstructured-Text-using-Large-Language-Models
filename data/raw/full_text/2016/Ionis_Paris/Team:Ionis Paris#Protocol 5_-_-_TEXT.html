Shake at 37°C until the DO is around 0,6 NB : all the following steps must be perform in ice Transfer cells into sterile ice-cold Falcon tubes Incubate on ice for 30 min Spin cells at 4000 rpm for 10 min at 6°C Discard supernatant and try to drain all remaining media Gently resuspend in 10mL cold 0,1M CaCl2 Incubate on ice 20 min Centrifuge 10 min at 4000 rpm at 4°C Discard supernatant and vortex, gently resuspend on 5mL cold 0,1M CaCl2/15% Glycerol Transfer in 2mL cryotubes Store at -80°C for subsequent transformation Reagents E.coli DH5α or BL21 LB medium (see Protocol 1 : Culture medium preparation) CaCl2 (Panreac, 131232) Glycerol (Sigma-Aldrich - G5516) Materials Sterile 1.5mL tubes 50mL falcon tube (VWR, 525-0156) Cryotube (Dutscher 055003) Spectrophotometer Incubator Centrifuge Ice bucket Sources NYU Protocol for competent cell preparation FSU Protocol for competent cell preparation Facebook Twitter youtube LinkedIn iGEM IONIS We're a group of six different schools from the IONIS Education Group.
