    Protocol 2 : TAE preparation

    Aim: Preparation of the buffer used for electrophoresis

    Put 800mL of deionized water in the bottle along with the magnetic bar Dissolve 48.5g of Tris base Add 11.4 mL of glacial acetic acid Add 20 mL of 0.5M EDTA or 3,7g of EDTA disodium salt Add deionized water up to 1L Shake the solution until it becomes homogeneous Autoclave at 121 Celsius degrees for 15 to 30 minutes to sterilize Store at room temperature Be sure to dilute the buffer to 1:10 before us

    NB : 1X buffer will contain approximatively 40 mM Tris, 20 mM acetic acid and 1 mM EDTA

    Reagents

    • Tris base (Sigma-Aldrich)
    • Glacial acetic acid (Sigma, ARK2183-1L)
    • 0.5M EDTA (pH = 8.0) (Sigma-Aldrich, EDS-100G)
    • Deionized water

    Materials

    • 1L glass bottle
    • Magnetic bar and stirrer
    • Weighing scale
    • Autoclave
