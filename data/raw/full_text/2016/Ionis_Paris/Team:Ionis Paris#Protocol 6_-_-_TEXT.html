Mix gently and carefully pipette 50 µL of cells into a transformation tube on ice Add 1-5µL plasmid DNA to the cell mixture (one tube for each ratio) Carefully flick the tube 4-5 times to mix cells and DNA. Do not vortex. Place the mixture on ice for 30 min. Do not mix. Heat shock at exactly 42°C for 45 s. Do not mix. Place on ice for 5 min. Do not mix. Pipette 250µL of room temperature SOC into the mixture. Place at 37°C for 60 min at 250 rpm. Warm selection plates to 25°C. Mix the cells by flicking the tube and inverting. Spread 100µL of cells onto selection plates. Incubate all the plates O/N at 37°C.
