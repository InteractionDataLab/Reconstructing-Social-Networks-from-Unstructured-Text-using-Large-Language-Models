The first collection is a magnetosome toolkit that contains genes involved in magnetite formation. The other collection the amyloid collection with amyloid nano-fibril forming Sup35-NM fusion proteins. The magnetosome collection forms the start of a new biobrick-compatible magnetosome toolkit and the amyloid collection improves on the set created by Kent 2015.
