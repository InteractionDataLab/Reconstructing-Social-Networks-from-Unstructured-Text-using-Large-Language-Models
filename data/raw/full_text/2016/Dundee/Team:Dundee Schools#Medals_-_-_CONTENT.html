    Medals

    We had a great time at the Giant Jamboree, and are hugely appreciative of the opportunity! We came away with several awards/nominations, which made the experience even better.

    Overall project medal

    We are very proud to say that our project fulfilled all the criteria for a Gold medal.

    Nominations

    We were nominated for 4 awards: best poster, best presentation, best composite part and best part collection. We are extremely happy with this!

    Thanks again to all of our advisors and supervisors - and the iGEM foundation - without whom, this amazing experience would not have been possible.
