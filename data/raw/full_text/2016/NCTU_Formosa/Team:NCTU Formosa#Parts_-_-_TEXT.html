We strive to express PANTIDE that can be used to reduce appetite of the major pests in the farmland. So far, we have already designed two types of BioBricks that three spider toxic peptides are included with and without lectin respectively. We can then do several experiments to test the function of BioBricks and prove that PANTIDE truly works. We submitted a collection of spider toxins, lectin, and GS linker as basic parts, and the composite parts with T7 promoter and RBS. All in all we have provided twelve new BioBricks to the Registry in total this year. All BioBricks were sequence-confirmed.
