Its history is an embodiment and a continuation of centuries of cultural and educational tradition in this country.  Public teaching activities in Bulgaria date back to the second half of the 9th century. Year by year the Sofia University has developed into an important academic and scientific centre on the Balkans, a well developed academic institution with European dimensions. Today Sofia University St. Kliment Ohridski is the largest and most prestigious educational and scientific centre in the country.
