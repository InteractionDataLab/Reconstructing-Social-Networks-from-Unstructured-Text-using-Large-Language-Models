PI&&Advisor

PI:  Zongyun Li

Research Interests:

  1. Plant genomics and molecular cytogenetics
  2. Environmental genetic toxicology

Secondary PI:  Jun Lu

Research Interests:

  1. Study on the molecular regulation network of cognitive deficits in type 2 diabetes mellitus
  2. Signal transduction in the neuroinflammatory network mediated by bacterial lipopolysaccharide (LPS)
  3. Molecular mechanism of Parkinson's syndrome
  4. Molecular pathology of Alzheimer's disease (AD)

Advisor:  Zhengkun Zhou

Research Interests:

  1. Nanotechnology in food processing applications
  2. Food safety risk identification and control technology during fruit and vegetable processing
  3. Reasearch & Development of functional food with the homology of medicine and food

Advisor:  Qin Ye

Research Interests:

    Qin Ye received the B.S. degree in Biology from Jiangsu Normal University, China, in 1998, and the Ph.D. degree in Medical School of Southeast University, China in 2011. Since 1998, she has been a teacher with the school of biology, Jiangsu Normal University, China. From 2013 to 2014，Dr.YE worked in Dr.Zengquan Yang's lab in Karmanos Cancer Centre as visiting scholar, MI,USA. She focus on aberrant epigenetic modification molecular mechanism.
