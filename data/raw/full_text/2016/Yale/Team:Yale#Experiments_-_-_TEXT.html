What This Page Contains Protocols Experiments Documentation of the development of our project Quick Links 2014 Colombia 2014 Imperial 2014 Caltech Nodulation Assay To ensure that rhizobia do not lose the nod plasmid, necessary for nodule formation on legume roots, after being subjected to the heat-shock prior to performing MAGE, we conducted a nodulation assay. This assay involves inoculating cowpea roots with both untransformed (control) and pORTMAGE-3 transformed (experimental) R. tropici and S. meliloti and observing the presence or absence of nodule formation.
