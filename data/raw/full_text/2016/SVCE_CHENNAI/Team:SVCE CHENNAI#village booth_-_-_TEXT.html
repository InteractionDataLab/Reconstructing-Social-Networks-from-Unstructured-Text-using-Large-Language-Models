Members from our team first visited large scale milking industries to observe the methodologies they use to maintain sanitary conditions, with the acquired knowledge we visited a vilage headquater in vilapakkam and implemented the acquired knowledge to the small scale milking vendors there so that they could minimise spoilage by eliminating contamination.
