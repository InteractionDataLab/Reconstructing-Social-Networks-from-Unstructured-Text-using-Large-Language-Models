A variant with extra glycine residues is used to increase the half life of the peptide, consistent with the N-end rule.    To read more about our basic parts click here COMPOSITE PARTS:    Parts BBa_K2113001 is a composite part coding for a RNA thermometer followed by expression of chromoprotein cj blue,this can be used for the characterization of RNA thermometer under different promoters.    Parts BBa_K2113002 is a composite part that serves as a temperature sensitive system with a lambda pr promoter under cI regulation. BBa_K2113003 similarly is a temperature sensitive system with a RNA thermometer.    Parts BBa_K2113007 and BBa_K2113008 are composite parts regulating the expression of basic parts BBa_K2113004 and BBa_K2113005 under a lac promoter, so that the system gets activated in the presence of milk.    Part BBa_K2113006 contains a strong promoter regulating part BBa_k211301 downstream of which a double terminator is present. BBa_K2113009 and BBa_K2113010 are composite parts regulating the expression of basic parts BBa_K2113004 and BBa_K2113005 under a RNA thermometer.   To read more about our composite parts click here PARTS COLLECTION: Part Name Part Number RNA thermometer + cjBlue BBa_K2113001 temperature sensitive system with lambda pr promoter under cI regulation BBa_K2113002 temperature sensitive system with RNA thermometer BBa_K2113003 AMP without glycine BBa_K2113004 AMP with glycine BBa_K2113005 temperature sensitive system with RNA thermometer with cj blue BBa_K2113006 lac inducible AMP without glycine BBa_K2113007 lac inducible AMP with glycine BBa_K2113008 RNA thermometer and AMP without glycine BBa_K2113009 RNA thermometer and AMP with glycine BBa_K2113010 Lactoshield - SVCE_CHENNAI Lactoshield - SVCE_CHENNAI Toggle navigation LACTOSHIELD Project Overview Anti-Microbial Peptide Temperature sensitive activation Spores Delivery System Interlab Study Notebook Protocols Competent cells preparation Transformation Restriction Ligation Agarose gel electrophoresis Human Practices Overview Analysis Global Analysis Urban Rural Education and Engagement Overview Schools College Workshops Urban Super Market Symposium Village awareness camp Integrated Human Practices   Overview Scholar Industries Entrepreunership Team Members Attributions Results Wet Lab Insilico Parts Overview Part collection Collaboration Safety Lactoshield - SVCE_CHENNAI Sponsors igemsvce@gmail.com Sri Venkateswara College of Engineering Tamil Nadu, India      Find us here
