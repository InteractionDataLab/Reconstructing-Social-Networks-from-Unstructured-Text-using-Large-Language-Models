The most important challenge we ran into was the problem of planetary protection: making sure that our balloon would not contaminate the areas we explored. We met with several experts in space exploration at NASA who helped us think about how we might design our balloon to be most useful to planetary scientists while also taking precautions against contamination. These conversations framed our project design.
