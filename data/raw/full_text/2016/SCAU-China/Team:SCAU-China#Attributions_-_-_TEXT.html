Lab support   Our project was mainly conducted in State Key Laboratory for Conservation and Utilization of Subtropical Agro-bioresources (SKL-CUSA). This laboratory is a leading research unit focusing on molecular mechanisms of rice sterility and rice genetic engineering. The SKL-CUSA is a P1 laboratory with advanced machines, mature methods and strict safety regulations. Therefore, we can access good facilities for molecular manipulation, rice transformation and finish the experiments smoothly.   3.
