Mosquitos are the most deadly animal, accounting for more than one million annual deaths. Mos(quit)o is a long-lasting, environmentally-friendly, safe alternative to currently available mosquito repellents. Current mosquito repellents are harmful to the environment. Since there is no mass chemical manufacturing, Mos(quit)o is a more environmentally-friendly alternative. Current mosquito repellents last a maximum of three hours.
