    How to upload a protocol

    1. Click on the "upload" button on the home page.

    2. Enter a title, a description, and optionally a category.

    3. For each step add a title and description.

    4. If a warning for a step is applicable, enter the warning information in the warning area below the step.

    How to find a protocol

    1. Use the search bar on the home page.

    2. If the protocol you are looking for does not appear in the results, click on the advanced search bar.

    3. Fill out any applicable information into the advanced search area. Then press "apply filters".

    4. View your filtered results.
