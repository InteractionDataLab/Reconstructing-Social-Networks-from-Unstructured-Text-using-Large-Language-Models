    • TV show

    Nippon Hoso Kyokai Osaka broadcasting station “School re-discovery Variety AHOYANEN SUKIYANEN”

    This program was a program for students. It was broadcasted in the Kansai area. We had a chance to appearance on TV and to spread our project and synthetic biology. We discussed our project, synthetic biology, safety of genetically modified foods with the cast.　It was very valuable time for us to have a discussion with them.

    Nippon Television Network Corporation　”NEWS ZERO”

    This program is a Japanese evening news. It has a small segment called of ZERO human, which ZERO human focuses bright people.We were introduced as the key people in the future of synthetic biology. Our project was introduced as innovative to preserve of foods. The program also introduced synthetic biology in general.

    TV TOKYO Corporation「CHARGE 730！」

    This program was a Japanese morning news Our project was introduced as the latest technology for food preservation method. Through trial and error, we transferred the gene that produces antimicrobial volatile gaseous substances into E.coli. Our “FLAVORATOR” can preserve food without consuming power or electric energy.

    Nippon Television Network Corporation　”THE MOST USEFUL SCHOOL IN THE WORLD”

    This is a Japanese entertainment TV. And the program imitates experts give lectures classroom environments, and in this program to a group of show business people. One day the program picked up how to prevent mold as a topic partly. Our project was introduced as a method of food preservation using fragrance that was made through synthetic biology techniques.
