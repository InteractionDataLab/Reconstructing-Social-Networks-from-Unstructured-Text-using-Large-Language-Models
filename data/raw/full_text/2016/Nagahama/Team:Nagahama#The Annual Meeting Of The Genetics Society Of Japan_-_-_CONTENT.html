    • We helped other iGEM teams in JAPAN. The 88th Annual meeting of The Genetics Society of Japan was held from September 6 to September 9.

    fig.１Venue photos fig.２Venue photos fig.3 Hall

    Because we wanted to achieve the following two goals in this meeting, we contacted the society headquarter. ⓵First, we hope to obtain opinions of experts who are participating in the meeting. ⓶Second, we hope to spread synthetic biology in the meeting. As a result of contacting with the society headquarter, we allowed to present in the meeting.

    fig.4 Explanation of synthetic biology and our project fig.5 iGEM Nagahama presentations

    In order to achieve the two goals by other iGEM teams of Japan, we had been contacted to the society headquarter. And, iGEM Kyoto , iGEM Gifu , iGEM Tokyo and iGEM Hokkaido was able to present in this meeting by iGEM Nagahama.

    fig.6 iGEM Kyotofig.7 iGEM Gifufig.8 iGEM Tokyofig.9 iGEM Hokkaido

    We helped iGEM Kyoto , iGEM Gifu , iGEM Tokyo and iGEM Hokkaido. Specifically, we offered the following two goals. ①Opportunity for other iGEM teams of Japan to present in the society meeting. ②Opportunity for iGEM teams of Japan to discuss the projects.
