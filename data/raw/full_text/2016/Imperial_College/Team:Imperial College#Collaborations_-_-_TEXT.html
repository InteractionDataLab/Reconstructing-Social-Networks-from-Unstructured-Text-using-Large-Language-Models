Evry The Evry iGEM team has conducted growth experiments with Pseudomonas putida KT2440. Their data has then been added to our A.L.I.C.E. software. Their experiment involved tracking the growth rate of their bacteria at 30°C. They also sent us some data, which they gathered during their project on the growth of Pseudomonas putida in minimal media with different carbon sources: glucose, glycerol and benzoic acid. CGU_Taiwan The CGU Taiwan iGEM team has kindly sent us some growth data, which has now been uploaded on our A.L.I.C.E. software. The experiments were conducted for two concentrations of LB medium, and with two different strains of Escherichia coli: BL21 and DH5α. Columbia_NYU The Columbia-NYU iGEM team has performed some growth experiments for us with two different strains of Pseudomonas putida.
