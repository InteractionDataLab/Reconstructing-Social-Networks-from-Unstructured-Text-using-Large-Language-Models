    Cyclebow

    A Colourful Family Tree Of Stem Cells.

    Shedding light into stem cell therapy

    Project

    Using recombinases and fluorescences, we made cells change color when undergoing different numbers of cell cycles. As our cells can shine like a rainbow, we named it cyclebow.
