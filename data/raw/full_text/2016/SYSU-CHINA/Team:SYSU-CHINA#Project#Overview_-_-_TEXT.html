Therefore, by referring to the color chart, we can determine how many cell-cycles the cells have undergone.      This can help confirm whether stem-cells used in stem cell therapy have successfully renewed and differentiated, and how many cell cycles they experienced. In fundamental stem-cell researches, our project can help distinguish actively self-renewing cells from quiescent cells which hardly proliferate. Apart from these, our project has many other potential applications in both fundamental and applied science.
