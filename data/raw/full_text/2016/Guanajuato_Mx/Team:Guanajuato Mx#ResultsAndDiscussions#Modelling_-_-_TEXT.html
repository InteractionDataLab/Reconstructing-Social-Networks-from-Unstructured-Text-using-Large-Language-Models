It will then analyze how P. aeuroginosa dies with the effect of an antimicrobial protein (lysozyme or alginate lyase protein). Our second model will describe the concentration profile on the nitrocellulose membrane under the analysis of a single flux of homoserine lactone and antimicrobial agent (lysozyme or alginate lyase protein), on the other hand the analysis of a non-equimolar opposite diffusion of the two fluxes under study.
