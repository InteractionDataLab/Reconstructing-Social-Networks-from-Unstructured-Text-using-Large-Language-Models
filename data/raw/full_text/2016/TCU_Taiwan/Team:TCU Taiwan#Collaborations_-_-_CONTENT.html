    1.Protocols & Matericals
                                                                                                        Imaging use fron NCKU-Collaborations
    They shared the protocol and sent the material for 1000ml of Glucose-free modified M9 medium for cell culturing and functional experiments for us. Protocol for modified M9: For preparing 10X M9 stock Formulation Disodium Phosphate Heptahydrate 128 g/L Monopotassium Phosphate 30 g/L Sodium Chloride 5 g/L Ammonium Chloride 10 g/L Final pH (2X solution): 6.6–7.0 at 25°C After dilution to 1X and autoclave, for every 20 ml, add 20% D-Glucose solution 400ul 1.0 M MgSO4 solution 40ul 1.0 M CaCl2 solution 2ul 20% Casamino acid 200ul
