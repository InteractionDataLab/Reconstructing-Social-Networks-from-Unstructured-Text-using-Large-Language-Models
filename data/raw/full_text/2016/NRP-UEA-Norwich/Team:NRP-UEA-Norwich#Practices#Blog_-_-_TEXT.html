The lessons ranged from synthetic biology techniques, to applications and myths surrounding synthetic biology. The aim of this project was to get the public interested in synthetic biology and to increase understanding biology as a whole amongst the public. This project received interest and is currently being developed for use in secondary schools across the country. Sponsors Copyright UEA 2016 . All rights reserved.
