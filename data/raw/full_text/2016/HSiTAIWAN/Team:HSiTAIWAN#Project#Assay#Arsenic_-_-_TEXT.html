As Experiment Functional Assay Chinese Medicine As Experiment Functional Assay "Click here to see Copper" Functional assay is to ensure that our E.coli can survive and produce green fluorescence under arsenic. We measure the absorbance value every 30 minutes in order to know whether it can survive under arsenic. Then, we measure the fluorescence to test it’s function of producing green fluorescent protein. The excitation and emission we measure the fluorescent intensity is 485nm and 538nm. Growth Rate Results: Figure 1: In this figure, we find that when arsenic concentration is under 100ppm, it doesn’t affect the growth of E. coli. GFP in Arsenic Solution Results: Figure 2: Fluorescence intensity in arsenic solution. Figure 3: Fluorescence intensity / Optical density in arsenic solution. We can see that our E.coli produces more green fluorescence in higher arsenic concentration and as well as stronger fluorescence.
