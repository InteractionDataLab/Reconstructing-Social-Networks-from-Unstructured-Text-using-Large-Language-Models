About modeling Here are some equations we write with NKU to model the uptake of exogenously added AI-2 by each plasmid in AI-2 Consumer Device pLsrACDBFGK interrelated to their project. To ‘quench’ AI-2 signal in the nature or artificial environment, NKU_China constructed six AI-2 Consumer Devices by overexpression the components responsible for AI-2 uptake (lsrACDB), phosphorylation (lsrK) and degradation (lsrFG). Matlab Simulations of AI-2 Consumer Devices were given for a better understanding of how the devices work when exogenously AI-2 was added.
