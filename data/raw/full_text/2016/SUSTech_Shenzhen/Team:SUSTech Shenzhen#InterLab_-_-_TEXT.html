Load QC(Lot: 45065) falcon tube to do pre-tests. Load Rainbow beads, set FSC-A-SSA, FSC-A-FSC-H, FITC-A-Count. Mix 100ul overnight culture with PBS, load samples and examine the fluorescence. Close experiment and perform daily clean with ddH2O. Exit the software.
