Heat shock transformation

Introduction

This protocol is from Addgene (https://www.addgene.org/plasmid-protocols/bacterial-transformation/)

Materials

Procedure

  1. Take comp cells out of -80 C freezer and thaw on ice 20-30 minutes
  1. Mix 1-5 uL DNA into 20-50 uL comp cells in a microcentrifuge tube. Mix gently.
  1. Incubate in ice 20-30 minutes
  1. Heat shock at 42 C for 5 seconds
  1. Quickly return cells to ice. incubate 2 minutes
  1. Add 250-1,000 uL LB broth
  1. Incubate 45 minutes at 37 C
  1. Plate 50 uL on agar and/or innoculate 1-100 uL in liquid culture
