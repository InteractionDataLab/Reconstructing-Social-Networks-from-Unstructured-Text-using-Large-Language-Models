    Historical Basis

    Our project, Biohub, is an enhanced work of Biopano, which is the product of the same team in 2014. When we started our project, we received much information about Biopano from our easy-going seniors. Therefore we decided to make a superior artwork in order to pay tribute to our university.

    We give them our most genuine gratitude to the team members of the team USTC-Software of 2014, and their project as well.

    Developing Tools & Libraries

    Backend: MySQL, Docker, Python Flask framework, SQLAlchemy, Biopython, PyCharm Professional, Gitlab.

    Frontend: Materialize, d3.js, jQuery.

    Fund

    USTCIF

    We sincerely thank USTCIF for its efforts of arranging the fund.

    USTCIF, The University of Science and Technology of China Initiative Foundation, is an educational NGO serves to help with the development of USTC. As one of USTC's alumni foundations, its purpose is to enhance the alumni foundations to make USTC better. USTCIF is the first college alumni foundation independent from the university.
