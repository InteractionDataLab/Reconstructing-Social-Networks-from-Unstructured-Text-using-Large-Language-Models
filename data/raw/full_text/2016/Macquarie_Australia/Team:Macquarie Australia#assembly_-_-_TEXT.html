Water was added to bring the total to 10µl. Ligation was performed for 30 minutes at 16oC followed by a further period of 20 minutes at 80oC. Transformation Obtain competent cells from -80oC. Defrost gently on ice. 100µl is sufficient for 2 transformations. Add 1-10µl of plasmid DNA/ ligation mix to each tube. Incubate on ice for 30 minutes. Heat shock in 42oC water bath for 30 seconds, then back on ice for 2 minutes. Add 200µl of SOC media to each tube, and incubate in the 37oC shaker for 1 hour. For each tube of cells, spread 50µl onto one LB plate with appropriate antibiotic, and 100µl onto a second plate, using aseptic technique. Place your plate upside-down in the 37oC incubator.
