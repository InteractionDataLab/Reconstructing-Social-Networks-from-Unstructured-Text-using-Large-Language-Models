    • Macquarie University

    The Macquarie University 2016 iGEM team would like to thank Macquarie University for the continued support, resources, laboratories and personnel that have been made available to our team. We would also like to thank the Department of Chemistry and Biomolecular Sciences for their support and encouragement towards our Project.

    Biomolecular Frontiers Research Centre

    We would also like to thank the Biomolecular Frontiers Research Centre at Macquarie University for contributing to the funding of our project.

    Bioplatforms Australia

    The Macquarie University 2016 iGEM team would like to thank Bioplatforms Australia for generously contributing to the funding of our project, in particular, General Manager Andrew Gilbert for organising the funding.

    Resources and EnergyNSW Government

    Our team would also like to thank the Resources and Energy division of the NSW Government Department of Industry for contributing to the funding of our project.

    Integrated DNA Technologies

    Additionally, our team would like to acknowledge IDT for providing our team with 20 kb of DNA as gBlocks free of charge.
    All funding and resources donated allowed the Macquarie University iGEM project to progress and provided our team with the opportunity to present our research at the 2016 iGEM Jamboree in Boston.
