    Ligation

    2µl of digested Plasmid Backbone an equimolar amount of digested Part A (< 3µl) and an equimolar amount of digested Part B (< 3µl) were added to 1µl T4 DNA ligase buffer and 0.5µl of T4 DNA ligase. Water was added to bring the total to 10µl. Ligation was performed for 30 minutes at 16oC followed by a further period of 20 minutes at 80oC.

    Transformation

    1. Obtain competent cells from -80oC.
    2. Defrost gently on ice. 100µl is sufficient for 2 transformations.
    3. Add 1-10µl of plasmid DNA/ ligation mix to each tube. Incubate on ice for 30 minutes.
    4. Heat shock in 42oC water bath for 30 seconds, then back on ice for 2 minutes.
    5. Add 200µl of SOC media to each tube, and incubate in the 37oC shaker for 1 hour.
    6. For each tube of cells, spread 50µl onto one LB plate with appropriate antibiotic, and 100µl onto a second plate, using aseptic technique. Place your plate upside-down in the 37oC incubator.
