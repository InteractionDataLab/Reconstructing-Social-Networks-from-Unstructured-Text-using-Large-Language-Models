BBa_K2163000 This is a composite part, comprising of Cyan Fluorescent Protein (CFP), HIV Aspartyl Protease Specific Cleavage Site, and Yellow Fluorescent Protein (YFP). This part functions as the core of FRET detection system. As long as the cleavage site is intact, the FRET pair is active, while cleavage of the site leads to inactivation of the FRET pair. When attached with OmpA at the C terminal, the entire system is presented on the cell surface. 2. BBa_S05365 This is one of the two submitted intermediate parts. It consists of Cyan Fluorescent Protein (CFP) and HIV Aspartyl Protease Specific Cleavage Site. 3. BBa_S05366 This is the other intermediate part.
