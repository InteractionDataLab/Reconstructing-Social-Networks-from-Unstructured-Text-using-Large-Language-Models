PARTS

♦K2007000♦ Constitutive promote expressing GFP and Lac promoter expressing FadR gene

This is a circuit which expresses GFP under a constitutive promoter. It also expresses FadR under the Lac promoter.

♦K2007001♦ pLR promoter expressing GFP and Lac promoter expressing FadR gene

This is a circuit which expresses GFP in a fatty acid concentration dependent manner. It also expresses FadR under the Lac promoter.

♦K2007005♦ Constitutive promoter expressing DmJHAMT and GFP, Lac promoter expressing FadR

This circuit expresses GFP and a Drosophila melanogaster Juvenile Hormone Acid O-methyltransferase (DmJHAMT) under a constitutive promoter. It also expresses FadR under the Lac promoter.

♦K2007006♦ pLR promoter expressing DmJHAMT and GFP, Lac promoter expressing FadR

This circuit expresses GFP and a Drosophila melanogaster Juvenile Hormone Acid O-methyltransferase (DmJHAMT) under a fatty acid concentration dependent manner. It also expresses FadR under the Lac promoter.

ABOUT US

We are an iGEM team from the University of La Verne and La Canada High School. We are an interdisciplinary team with members from biology, math, computer science, art, and library science. We are a community-engaged team that hopes to partner with other high schools, non-profits, and local businesses to create innovative solutions to global issues!
