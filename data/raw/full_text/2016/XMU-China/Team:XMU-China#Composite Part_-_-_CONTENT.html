    This year we've designed more than 20 parts, including 2 basic parts. All of the parts are designed ourselves and most of them have been tested to insure their reliability. To see their specific function, you can click here to learn about our project, or browse the specific page on parts.igem.org by clicking on the part numbers.

    Basic Parts

    This year we've designed two basic parts. BBa_K1960102 is the sRNA which can silence chloramphenicol resistance, synthesized and cloned into pSB1C3 plasmid, and BBa_K1960103 codes formaldehyde dismutase from Halomonas sp. JC-5B, the sequence with BioBrick prefix and suffix is obtained by PCR.
