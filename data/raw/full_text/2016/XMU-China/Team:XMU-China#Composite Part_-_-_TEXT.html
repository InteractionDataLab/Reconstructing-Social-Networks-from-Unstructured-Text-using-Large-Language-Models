Basic Parts This year we've designed two basic parts. BBa_K1960102 is the sRNA which can silence chloramphenicol resistance, synthesized and cloned into pSB1C3 plasmid, and BBa_K1960103 codes formaldehyde dismutase from Halomonas sp. JC-5B, the sequence with BioBrick prefix and suffix is obtained by PCR.
