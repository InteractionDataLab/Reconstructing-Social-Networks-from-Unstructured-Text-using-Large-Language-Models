    Summary of the Class in the primary school

    For our human practice, we gave a lecture to pupils in a primary school. The theme of the lecture is “growing with health”. The content of this lecture mainly included: some simple introduction about bacterial, the strong immune system of humans, the abuse of antibiotic inducing the emergence of superbugs and some healthy lifestyle. During our lecture, we prepared an interesting short play and a video to make the lecture more vivid.

    As a speaker, I was concerned that the knowledge is too difficult for them to understand. However, connecting knowledge with real life can solve this problem. So, during the lecture, I used many vivid examples to make them have a better understanding. At the same time, I also helped them to have a correct understanding about some conception. For example, many pupils thought that all the bacterial were harmful to us. I took lactobacillus for an example to tell them that some bacterial are beneficial to us.

    When we were talking about the abuse of antibiotic, we concerned that this section is boring for them, so we decided to prepare an interesting short play and we carefully prepared for two weeks. At last, we gave a wonderful performance with the help of some cute props. The pupils immersed themselves in our performance and learnt much knowledge at the same time.

    The last section is prize-giving guess contest game. I asked some questions about my lecture. Many pupils were putting up their hands to answer the questions. This can be proved that they’ve really learnt a lot.

    In a word, this activity is successful. It is believed that this activity brought them knowledge and happiness. We make them know how to live healthier in the future. This is what we want to achieve.
