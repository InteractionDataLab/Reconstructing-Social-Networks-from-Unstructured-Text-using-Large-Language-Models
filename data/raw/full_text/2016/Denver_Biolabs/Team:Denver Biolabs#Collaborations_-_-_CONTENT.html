    • Collaborations

    Denver Biolabs collaborated with both the Colorado State University and University of Colorado Boulder by providing a space and community audience to present the iGEM work being done in Colorado to a broad audience. In addition to being an opportunity for all of our teams to practice our Jamboree presentations, it facilitated networking between our institutions, served as a community outreach and educational event, and helped the University teams identify lab space and project mentorship resources through Denver Biolabs that can facilitate their ongoing involvement in synthetic biology post-graduation.
