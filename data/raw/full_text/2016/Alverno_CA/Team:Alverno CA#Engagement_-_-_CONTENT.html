    Engagement

    To stay engaged with our community we created a variety of social media accounts (TwitterYouTubeFacebookand Instagram) as a means to reach out to other teams, and to keep interested people informed about our progress.
    On those accounts we would post about exciting things occurring in the lab such as autoclaving, micropipetting, and wiki coding!

    To see more about our Engagement, look at our Human Practices where we demonstrate ourengagement through Outreach.
