As well we would like to highlight certain social events   which helped us to further improve our team spirit. Our iGEM year started with weekly team meetings on tuesdays in January. On February 27 and 28 we officially iniated the project with our Kickoff-Event in which teams have been formed and weekly teamleader meetings were set. To teach the new members all the needed basics for working in the lab and genetic engineering in general, the experienced students offered classes like a snapgene workshop (March 4), how to use scifinder (March 12) and a cloning strategies workshop (March 17).
