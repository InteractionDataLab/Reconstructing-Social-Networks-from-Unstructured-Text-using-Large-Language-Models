    • Composite Parts:

    In this section we present our composite parts. Using our Basic Parts we made a regulatory system and a detection system easily editable using a couple of restriction enzymes.

    1. ➟ PFur+LacI-LVA+Terminator is used as an Iron sensitive regulatory system.
    2. ➟ PLacO+PiBlue+Terminator is used as a reporter. It is possible to switch between operator Lac I and the other operators
    3. ➟ Combination of PFur+LacI-LVA+Terminator and PLacO+PiBlue+Terminator : It allows us to detect the presence of iron in the bacteria. The aim of this system is, by adding another system to it, to detect bees' infection by a specific pathogen,Paenibacillus larvae.
