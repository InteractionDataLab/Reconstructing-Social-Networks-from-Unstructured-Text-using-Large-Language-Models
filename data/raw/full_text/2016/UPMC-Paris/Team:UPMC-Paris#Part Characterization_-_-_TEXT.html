Our parts collection : iGEM2016 UPMC-Paris We designed a biobricks collection that can be used in B. subtilis as biosensors. In our collection, promoters and operators can be easily changed depending on the detection requirements. Basic parts: Here we present LacI-LVA and PiBlue for B. subtilis a high sensitive regulator and a blue pigment, respectively. In addition we designed different promoters/operators, based on Registred parts or using literature. Composite parts : Here we present our parts composed by our basic parts.
