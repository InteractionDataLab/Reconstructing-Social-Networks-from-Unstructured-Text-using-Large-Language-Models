As a control DH5alfa strain was used. The schema of the 96 wells plate can be observed below. Procedure Both strains, DH5α and PACO, were harvested in a liquid culture with optimal conditions until saturation. Optical density of both cultures was mesaured and the concentration was readjusted to be 0.1 A. Growth mediums were cleaned twice in order to ensure a perfect control over the growing conditions during the test. A M9-polyamines Mix (x2) was prepared. M9-polyamines Mix (x2) 22.12 μL Putrescine (1000x) 22.12 μL Spermidine (1000x) 2.5 mL Last column of the 96 wells plate was filled with 100μL of M9-polyamines Mix (x2) and 100 μL of M9. 100μL of M9 was placed at each well except for the last column. 100μL of M9-polyamines Mix (x2) was placed at well A-11 and resuspended. 100μL were transferred from well A-11 to well A-10. 1:2 serial dilutions were performed all along the row until well A-1, discarding the 100μL remaining in the last step. This procedure was repeated for all the remaining rows of the 98 wells plate. Finally, 100μL of cells with the convenient antibiotic were placed at each well; form A-D with DH5α strain and from E-H with PACO strain. Characterization of absorbance peak of polyamines The aim of this characterization was to be able to determine the amount of polyamines in a medium just by using an spectrophotometry assay. Several concentrations of Putrescine were used in the assay being 10mM and 0.016mM the highest and the lowest concentration respectively. The schema of the dilustions can be observed below.
