Having characterized a toolkit of mammalian parts that could be applied to a diagnostic circuit for endometriosis, we designed a full circuit and tested its logic step-by-step in both hormone phases and disease vs. healthy state. We've also considered an ideal clinical situation, which we see as a possibility after more thorough testing of our work, and finally a broader view on the applications of mammalian synbio in medicine.
