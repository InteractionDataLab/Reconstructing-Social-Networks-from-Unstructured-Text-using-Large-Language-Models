Midiprep

Introduction

Purifying large amount of DNA to tranfect mammalian cells

Materials

  • Qiagen MidiPrep

Procedure

  • Overnight Culture 50mL (06/20/16)
  1. Prepare the overnight culture 1 day before the midiprep
  1. Choose 1 sample for each plasmid constructs from the diagnostic digest result
  1. For each sample, preapre a 500mL breathable conical tube
  • Add 50mL media
  • 5uL of antibiotic
  • 10uL of culture from the miniprep
  1. Incubate at 37C for 16-20 hours
  • Midiprep preparation (06/21/16)
  1. Transfer 50mL overnight culture into 50mL Falcon tube
  1. Centrifuge at max speed for 15 mins at 4C
  1. Following the Qiagen midiprep protocol
