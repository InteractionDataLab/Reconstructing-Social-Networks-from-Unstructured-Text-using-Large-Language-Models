The project encompasses detecting the harmful gases (CO and oxides of NO) from vehicle exhausts using a portable, cheap, and easy–to–use strip test. Because vehicles contribute a major portion to air pollution, the project can help eliminate such vehicles form the society thus giving us all a chance to breathe cleaner air.
