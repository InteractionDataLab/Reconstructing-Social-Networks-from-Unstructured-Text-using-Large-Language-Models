BBa_K1987000 GFP with BsaI sites for promoter insertion Composite This testing construct consists of a degradation-tagged GFP orf preceded by the lac promoter and RBS. The promoter and RBS are flanked by BsaI sites, allowing for different promoter+RBS combinations to be easily swapped into the construct.
