Fill the chamber with 0.5x TBE until the gel is covered Use 5 µl HyperLadderI (BIOLINE) For colony PCR, put 5 µl of each reaction in the well Close the chamber with the lid (black pole to black=negatively charged and red to red=positively charged…) Run the gel with 80-100 V The Sample Loading Buffer contains two dyes: Bromophenol blue runs at ~300 bp and Xylene cyanol FF runs at ~4 kb.
