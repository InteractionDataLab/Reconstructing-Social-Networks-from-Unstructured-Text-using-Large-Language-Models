Time Bombers

Team Time Bomb is one of two University of Minnesota teams participating in the upcoming 2008 IGEM competition. Ultimately, the team's goal is to cheaply and safely design bio-engineered machines which can potentially be used in bioremediation or cell cycle specific research.
