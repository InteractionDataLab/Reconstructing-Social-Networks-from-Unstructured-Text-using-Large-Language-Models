Therefore, you can see how much of certain size units are present in the sample. WEAR GLOVES!!! Weigh 0.25g Agarose into flask with 25ml 1xTAE Buffer, stirring Weigh, remember weight Microwave to boil several times allow to cool until just cool enough to hold in hand. Reweigh, adding ddH2O until original weight Add 5ul EtBr Pour in gel tray allow gel to set, with template in place remove template pour 1xTAE until full on parafilm, put 1ul drop of dye down for every sample. load 1ul DNA Ladder into first well Get 2ul of sample, mix with dye on parafilm Pipette mix into well Hook up Electrophoresis: Red(-) on right, black(+) on left, 80V voltage, for 60min When gel is done, turn off power, remove gel, wrap in Saran Wrap, take picture next door.
