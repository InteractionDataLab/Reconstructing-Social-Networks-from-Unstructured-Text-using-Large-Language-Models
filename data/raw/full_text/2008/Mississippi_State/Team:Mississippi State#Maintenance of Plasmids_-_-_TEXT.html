To propogate and maintain the pPIC6alpha plasmid, use the following procedure: Resuspend each vector in 20ul sterile water to prepare a 1ug/ul stock solution. Store the stock solution at -20C Use the stock solution to transform a recA, endA E.coli strain like TOP10, DH5alpha, JM109, or equivalent. Select transformants on Low Salt LB plates containing 100ug/ml blasticidin. Prepare a glycerol stock from each transformant containing plasmid for long-term storage .
