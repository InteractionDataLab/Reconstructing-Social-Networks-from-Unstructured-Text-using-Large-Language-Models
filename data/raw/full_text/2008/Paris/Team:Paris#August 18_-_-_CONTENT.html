    Minipreps & Stocks

    We wanted to analyse the transformants of the ligations L133 (OmpR* in pSB1A2) and L134 (EnvZ* in pSB1A2) from the experience of august 9th/11th. 3 clones of L133 and 3 clones of L134 were analysed. Each clone tested was then cultured in 7,5 mL LB + ampicilline.
