    PCR

    PCR screening programm elongation time: 1 min 30 total volume reaction (25 µL)

    • 12,5 µL Quick load PCR Mix 2X
    • 0,5 µL O18
    • 0,5 µL O19
    • 1 µL DNA
    • 10,5 µL water

    Digestion

    total volume reaction (30 µL)

    • 2 µL DNA
    • 3 µL buffer 2 10X
    • 0,3 µL BSA 100X
    • 1 µL XbaI
    • 1 µL SpeI
    • 22,7 µL water

    Incubation 2h55 at 37°C and then 20 min at 65°C.
