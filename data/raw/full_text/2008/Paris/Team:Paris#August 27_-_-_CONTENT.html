    Protocol of digestion

    • D 188 : 3 µL of PCR 148
    • D 189 : 3 µL of MP 101.2
    • 3µL Buffer (n°2) 10X
    • 0.3µL BSA 100X
    • 22.7 µL of pure Water
    • 1 µL of each enzyme
    • Incubate during about 2h30 at 37°C
    • 20 minutes at 65°C
