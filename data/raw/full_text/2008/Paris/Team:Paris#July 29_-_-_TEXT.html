Excise the bands of interest from the gel and the DNA was purified using the QIAquick DNA Gel Extraction kit (QIAGEN). The elution of DNA was performed using 50 µL of water (after 10 minutes of incubation at 37°C). ==> Unfortunately, there were not enough columms, so we took some columms from the QIAGEN MiniPrep kit, hoping that it will work with the QIAquick DNA Gel Extraction kit.
