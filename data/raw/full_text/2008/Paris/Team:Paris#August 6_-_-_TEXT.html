Remarks : We forgot the negative control The temperature should be 60°C +- 5°C For a promoter, 45s of elongation is enough (~1min per kb) We could have used a taq polymerase instead of the phusion one We will do it again The return of PCRs for amplification of promoters As our results were not very encouraging, we started again a PCR to amplify: pflhDC ( 12 times with 12 different temperatures between 55°C and 65°C) pflgA (60°C) pflgB (60°C) pflhB (60°C) Negative control Protocol We used the taq polymerase, following a typical protocol.
