Autoclave the solution. Let it cool to ~40-45°C Add the proper antibiotic if needed. ONLY FOR PLATES: Pour an homogenous layer of agar LB into Petri plates under the hood. Let agar LB polymerase. Cover and store at +4°C. Always check for contaminations before using!
