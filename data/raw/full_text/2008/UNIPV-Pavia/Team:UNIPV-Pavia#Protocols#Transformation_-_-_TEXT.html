Incubate on ice for 30-45 min. Heat shock: 42°C for 1 min. Put transformed TOP10 tube on ice and then add 250 µl SOC medium. Incubate 2 hours at 37°C, 220 rpm. Centrifuge 10 min, 1200 rpm. Remove 150 ul of bacteria-free supernatant. Plate the remaining part of solution (resuspending the bacteria) on a proper agar plate. Incubate overnight at 37°C.
