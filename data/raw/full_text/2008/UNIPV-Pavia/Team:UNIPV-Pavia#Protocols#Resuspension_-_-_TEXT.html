Cut paper spots using scalpel and tweezers (or punch tool, following the instructions provided with the IGEM kit); if you use scalpel and tweezers, try to cut pieces of about the same dimension of the punch tool. Put the cut paper into the 0.5 ml tube. Clean scalpel and tweezers (or punch tool) with water and ethanol every time you cut a spot; be careful to dry your tools correctly, especially if you use punch tool, which needs much more time to dry than scalpel/tweezers. Incubate at 42°C for 20 min. Vortex and spin down.
