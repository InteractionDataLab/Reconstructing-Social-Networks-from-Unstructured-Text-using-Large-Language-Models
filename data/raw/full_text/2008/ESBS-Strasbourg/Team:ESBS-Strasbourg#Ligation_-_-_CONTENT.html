2)Protocol:

  • For insert/vector ligation:

see iGEM protocol

  • For intra vector ligation:

5 T4 DNA ligase unit, for 25-50ng of DNA

  • 2c) Protocol according to Fermantas
  • 50-400 ng vector
  • 1 to 3 times the molar amount of the insert
  • 2 µl ligation buffer
  • 1 µl ligase
  • to 20 µl water

-> vortex and spin down -> 1h at 22°C water bath (in 4°C room) -> enzyme inactivation at 65°C for 10 min -> directly usable for the transformation
