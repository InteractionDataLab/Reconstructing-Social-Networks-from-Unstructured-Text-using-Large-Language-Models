From 2008.igem.org

Tris-HCl 10 mM pH8 EDTA 1 mM pH8 NaOH 0.1 M SDS 0.5%

Make a Sterilfiltration of the solution or use steril stock solutions for its preparation For 40 mL of TENS:

  • 0.8 mL of 0.5M Tris-HCL pH8
  • 0.1 mL of 0.4M EDTA
  • 0.4 mL of 10 M NaOH
  • 1 mL of 20% SDS
  • fill up to 40 mL with milliQ water
