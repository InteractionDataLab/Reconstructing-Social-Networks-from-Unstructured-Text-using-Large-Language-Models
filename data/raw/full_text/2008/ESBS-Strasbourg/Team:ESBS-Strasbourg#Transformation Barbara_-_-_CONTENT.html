Team:ESBS-Strasbourg/Transformation Barbara

From 2008.igem.org

DNA in small volume (maximal 10µl) + 20µl of KCM 5X qsp 100µl H2O

Add 100µl of competent bacteria

Leave 20 min on ice, then 5 min at room temperature It is possible to let them grow again for 1 hour at 37°C, or plate them immediately No thermic shock
