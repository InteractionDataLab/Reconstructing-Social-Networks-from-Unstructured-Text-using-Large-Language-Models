    Saturday 5 July

    • Among the plates collected, only the plate of LacI-RFP has colonies. Plates with E7-empty plasmid and LacI-GFP do not have colony.

    Lu Chao

    minipreps for LacI-RBS 34.

    Hung, Lu Chao

    • Nanodrop to determine concentrations and purities of LacI-RBS 34 plasmids, which show good results.
    • Prepare digestion mixtures for gel confirmation.

    Darius

    Gel electrophoresis to check if there are LacI-RBS 34 plasmids.Result: All have the 2000 kb band, the empty vector plasmid (expected). Besides that band:
    • There is 1 sample that only has 250 bp band, which is LacI-RBS 34. (Good!!)
    • There is 2 samples that have both 250 bp band and less than 50 bp band, which can be interpreted as RBS (due to self-ligation of RBS).
    • The rest only have the RBS band.
