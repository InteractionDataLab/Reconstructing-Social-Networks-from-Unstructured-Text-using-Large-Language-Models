Remove the supernantant and store the cell pellets at -20 deg C Repeat this for the next 4 hours, collecting 8 sets of data One eppendorh tube of each type of cells from the above experiment after the 4 hrs duration, were used as samples to be seen under the fluorecemce microscope Cells fluorescene intensity was directly linked to the strenght of the promoters.
