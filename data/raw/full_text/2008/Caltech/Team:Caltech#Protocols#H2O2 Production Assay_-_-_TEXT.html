In the morning, dilute culture 1:100 into 5 ml SOC +Amp +IPTG in triplicate. Grow cultures to an OD600 of ~0.8, then assay supernatant for hydrogen peroxide approximately every 30 minutes. 1hr after reaching an OD600 of ~0.8, induce cultures with 10 nM AHL.
