Take 50µl of cells and add 5µl of DNA (samples 9 and 11). Incubate on Ice 20 min. Heat shock of 40-50 seconds in termoblock at 42°C. DO NOT AGITATE. Thaw on ice for 2 minutes. Add 950µl of sterile LB media. Incubate for 1.5hrs at 37°C at 15rpm. Plate in petri dishes with LB/amp, 100µl of media. First centrifugate (7min, 3500rpm) and disolve the pellet in 100µl of media, plate. Incubate at 37°C overnight.
