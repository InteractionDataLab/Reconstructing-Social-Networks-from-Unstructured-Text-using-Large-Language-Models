Where we're from

Hong Kong

Hong Kong

Hong Kong (Chinese: 香港) is a territory located on China's south coast on the Pearl River Delta, and borders Guangdong province in the north and faces the South China Sea in the east, west and south. It has a population of 6.9 million people, and is one of the most densely populated areas in the world.

(http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hong_Kong)

HKUST

The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) is a public university located in Hong Kong. Established in 1991, it is one of the best universities in Hong Kong.
