We are loosely affiliated with the University of Lethbridge team. Our focus for iGEM 2008 is on ligase-independent cloning (LIC). In existing, commonly-used cloning protocols, the ligation step is often problematic and inefficient. We believe that LIC can simplify and speed up the cloning procedure, and we plan to try out a LIC procedure which we believe will be of interest for general use by iGEM teams and others.
