Dry lab General We discovered that the LVA tagging of T7 RNA polymerase is a no go, as the C-terminus is part of the active site of the enzyme. We'll have to do N-terminal tagging. This should be able to work, see the crystal structure of T7. We could use the Lon-mediated degradation of lambda excisionase (Xis) and E.coli UmuD gene product. A 30-40 amino terminal AA of UmuD should do the trick. So we will have to construct a T7 polymerase with UmuD tag to make it degrade faster. For more information on this tag, check out Literature .
