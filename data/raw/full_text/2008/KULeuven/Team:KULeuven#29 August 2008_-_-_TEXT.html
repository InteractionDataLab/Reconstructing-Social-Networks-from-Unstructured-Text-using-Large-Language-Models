Dry Lab Meeting Had another good meeting today. Possibility was raised that adenosine methylation by the dam methylase is inhibiting our XbaI GATC restrictions. Checking it out. JM109 is probably dam+ so XbaI shouldn't cut, while for example JM110 and DM1 (Invitrogen) are dam-. If we want to use XbaI we'll need dam- cells. Edit: Dam methylation seems not to be responsible for the failure of BBa_B0015. Probably the big issue is the vector around BBa_B0015 which causes troubles. See this page.
