The Team (from top left to bottom right): John, Sara, Derek, Andrew, Asad, Allin, and Prathna
This year's MIT iGEM team is comprised of 6 undergraduate students (Prarthna Desai, Derek Ju, John Kucharczyk, Asad Moten, Sara Mouradian, Allin Resposo) from MIT as well as one high school student (Andrew Ang) from Phillips Andover. In addition, there are 11 Graduate School advisors (Robbie Barbero, Scott Carlson, Brian Cook, Rachel Hillmer, Lav Varshney, Laure-Anne Ventouras, Chia-Yung Wu, Vikramaditya Yadav, Felix Moser, Jyoti Goda, Woo Chung) and two Faculty Advisors (Tom Knight and Drew Endy).
