To create your iGEM wiki account:

  1. Go to https://2008.igem.org
  2. Set up a user account for yourself by clicking on the "iGEM 2008 Registration" link on the right and clicking on the "iGEM registration is now closed" link at the bottom of the page.
  3. From the registration page click on the "Apply for user account" link to create your account.
  4. Now that you have an account, you can join Illinois team by clicking on "Join an exisitng iGEM 2008 team" link from the registration page (https://igem.org/IGEM_2008_Registration)
  5. Welcome aboard :o)
