GROUP 7: Microscopy/Yeast 

 Milestones 
 * GFP-expressing yeast have been visualized by microscopy and photographed. The pictures of these can
   be found on our poster.
 * We have the resources to photograph green, various red, yellow, blue, and violet fluorophores. 
 * STE3-GFP "sex detector" has been transformed into MATa, MATalpha, and MATa/alpha. Permanent cultures
   of these will be banked in the Boeke lab early in the week of November 3rd.
 * For micrographs of STE3-GFP "sex detector" cells/colonies, see the poster.
