Possible silent changes gag-ttc or gaa-ttt. We opted for gaa-ttt. Forward: tgttgaaaaacctgctaacc - 54°C Reverse: aattcgataattttatcgttc -52°C Site 2: codons 356 to 358, gtg-aat-tca, VNS. Possible silent changes gtc-aat-tca, or gtg-aac-tca, no good possibility changing base 1 of the serine codon only. We went for g-aac-tca.
