Team:Rice University/Notebook/21 May 2008

From 2008.igem.org

Wednesday 21 May

  • Taylor Stevenson
  • Transformation From New Registry (Paper Punches)-Electroporation of BBa_I13602 located on pSB1A2 into TetR electrocompetent cells.
  1. Added 2uL of DNA solution obtained by soaking DNA punch in (10:1) Tris:EDTA buffer incubated @ 42*C for 20min.
  2. Electropulse parameters: 1.8 kV & 5.4 ms
  3. Plated 100uL and 20uL of transformed cells as well as 20uL of 1/100 and 1/10,000 dilutions in sterile 50% glycerol. Plates were cultured O/N.
  • Result-no colonies were observed at any dilution
