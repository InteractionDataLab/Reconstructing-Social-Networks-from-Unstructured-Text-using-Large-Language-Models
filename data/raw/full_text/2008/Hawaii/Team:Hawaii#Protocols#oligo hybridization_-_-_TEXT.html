Add 4 μl 0.5 M NaCl. Heated mixture in PCR machine to 97C for 2 min. Cooled mixture at each degree between 80 and 96 for 2 min (Tm range of oligos). Cooled mixture at each degree between 25 and 79 for 1 min. Reference: Pam Silver Lab. Oligonucleotide Inserts.
