Heat at 97C for 10 min. Vortex again briefly. Use 1 μl in PCR reaction. PCR reaction Combine: 1 μl colony sample (above) 0.5 μl 10mM forward primer 0.5 μl 10mM reverse primer 3 μl nanopure water 5 μl Taq (we used EconoTaq Green Taq) Run for 30 cycles of denaturing, annealing, extension Initial denature @ 94C for 2 min. Denature @ 94C for 30 sec. Anneal @ 62C for 30 sec. Extend @ 72C for 90 sec. Final extension @ 72C for 10 min. Hold @ 4C inifinitly. Run PCR products on a gel to verify desired DNAs.
