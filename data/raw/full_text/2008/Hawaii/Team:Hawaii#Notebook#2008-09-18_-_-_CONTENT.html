Sequencing Analysis

Grace
  • Correct:
  • C0012V (sequence to see what it was): EcoRI/XbaI/SpeI/PstI sites present. Insert aligns to sequence for lacI (C0012 insert, vector was uncut)
  • C0012V-SAP'd (sequence to see what it was): EcoRI/XbaI/SpeI/PstI sites present. Insert aligns to sequence for lacI (C0012 insert, vector was uncut)
  • slr1+GFPf
  • Incorrect:
  • 3A:nrp: EcoRI/PstI sites present. 82bp insert aligns to sequence for E. coli flagellar biosynthesis RNA polymerase sigma factor
  • 3A:nrpg12-2: EcoRI/PstI sites present. 759bp insert aligns to sequence for E. coli fumarate reductase (anaerobic) catalytic and NAD/flavoprotein subunit
  • 3A:nrpg12-6: EcoRI/PstI sites present. 899bp insert aligns to sequence for E. coli protein C5 component of RNAse P
  • 3A:nrs2: EcoRI/PstI sites present. 85bp insert aligns to sequence for E. coli diaminopimelate decarboxylase, PLP-binding
  • 3A:nrs3: SpeI/PstI sites present. Insert aligns to pilA sequence and pSB1A3 transcriptional termination stem loop
  • 3A:nrsg10: SpeI/PstI sites present. Insert aligns to pilA + GFPf
  • pilA+GFPf12: GFPf only
  • plac+rbs1 (sequence to see what it was): EcoRI/XbaI/SpeI/PstI sites present. rbs (B0034) only
  • plac+rbs2 (sequence to see what it was): EcoRI/XbaI/SpeI sites present. Insert showed 97% similarity to RP4 origin of replication
  • plac+rbs12 (sequence to see what it was): EcoRI/XbaI/SpeI/PstI sites present. Insert is GFP only.
