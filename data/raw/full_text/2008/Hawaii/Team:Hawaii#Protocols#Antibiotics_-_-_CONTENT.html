Ampicillin

1000X ampicillin stock is a 100 mg/ml solution.

  1. Combine 1 g ampicillin with 10 ml nanopure H2O.
  2. Sterile filter using a 0.2 μm Corning filter.
  3. Aliquot into eppendorf tubes and store at -20C.

Kanamycin
