" Page Discussion View source History teams Log in   Team:Hawaii/Protocols/Transformation E coli From 2008.igem.org Protocol Thawed cells on ice. Transformed 50 μl cells w/ 1 μl DNA Incubated on ice 30 min. Heat shocked 60 sec. in 42C water bath Added 250 μl SOC Incubated @ 37C for 1 hour with shaking (150 rpm) Plated 20-250 μl onto LB+antibiotic plates Plate more if needed. Notes Adjust DNA amount as necessary. Transformation is saturated at >10ng DNA. DNA should not exceed 10% of cell volume (Re: SC). Incubating 10 min. on ice after adding DNA and returning cells to ice for 2 min. after heat shock seems to improve transformation efficiency. -GK Retrieved from " http://2008.igem.org/Team:Hawaii/Protocols/Transformation_E_coli " Recent changes What links here Related changes Special pages My preferences Printable version Permanent link Privacy policy Disclaimers
