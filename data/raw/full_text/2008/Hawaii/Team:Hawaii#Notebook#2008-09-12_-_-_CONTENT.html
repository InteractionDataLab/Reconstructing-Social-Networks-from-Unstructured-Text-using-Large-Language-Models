Competent Cells

Margaret, Krystle, & Grace
  • Several attempts were made to make more DH5-alpha competent cells.
  • 5ml culture grown over-night in TB (terrific broth), in the morning 125ml SOB was inoculated with 12.5mL(1), and 2 flasks with 50ml (2&3) SOB were inoculated with 5ml of the ON culture.
Flask # OD550 (1hr) OD550 (1.5hr)
1 0.2056 0.3432
2 0.2006 0.3312
3 0.2547 0.4184
  • The cells were spun down at 2147 x g, 4°C, 30 minutes. The yield was low (1/4 of the tip of a 50ml centrifuge tube), so the culturing was repeated, except that the starting OD was 0.14 and the final OD was more than 0.3 but less than 0.6 (with the same amount of cells yielded).
  • The cells are being cultured again tonight to try again tomorrow. IF we get the same amount of cells, we will continue with the competent cell making procedure.
