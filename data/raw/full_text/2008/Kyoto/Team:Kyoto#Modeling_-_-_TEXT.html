The inside of the hull is jam-packed with E.coli. The lower surface of the hull is totally covered with E.coli. Individual thrusts do not interfere each other. The weight of the Titanic in water = 4.0 × 108 N Volume of the Titanic = 9.7 × 104 m3 Thrust by the total flagellum (thrust per one flagella = 0.29 pN) 2.5 × 103 N Buoyancy by the total gas vesicle (buoyancy per one gas vesicle = fgas pN) fgas × Vtitanic / VE.coli = fgas × 9.7 × 1022 N The Titanic floats when fgas is more than 4.1 × 10-3 pN !! Further simplification It is assumed that the ship floats when filled with the same volume water as that of its underwater part.
