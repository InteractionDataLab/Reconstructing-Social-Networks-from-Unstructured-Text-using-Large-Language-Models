LacI is a repressor that is constitutively expressed. It binds to the promoter preventing the promoter from expressing an output, in this case GFP. In the absence of IPTG, LacI is constitutively expressed. Hence the constitutive expression model from the previous characterisation step can be assumed. At the same time, it can bind to the promoter to repress it.
