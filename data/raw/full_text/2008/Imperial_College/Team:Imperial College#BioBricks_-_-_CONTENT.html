BioBricks Submitted

This year Imperial College has added 45 new parts to the Registry - 45 of those designed specifically for use in B. subtilis. In addition we sought to characterise some of these parts, including the constitutive promoter and ribosome binding site pveg-spoVG; you can find information about the characterisation of some of our parts in the Major Results page.
