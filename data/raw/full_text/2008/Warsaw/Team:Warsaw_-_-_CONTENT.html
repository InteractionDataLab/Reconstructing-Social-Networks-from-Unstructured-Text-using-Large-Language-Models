Team:Warsaw

From 2008.igem.org

A novel system for generation and screening for new interactors targeted against any bait

  • maximization of interaction between given bait and randomly generated protein by mutating sequence of the latter
  • interactor presentation on bacterial surface
  • selection of best bait interactor
