Cloning of alpha to pACYC177+OmpA_A_omega and pACYC177+OmpA_Z_omega

Michał K.

  1. PCR on pUC19 plasmid using AlphaL+SacI and AlphaPlinkSac primers (gradient of annealing temperature from 55°C to 75°C; elongation length 60s; 35 cycles).
  2. Gel electrophoresis and chioce of proper PCR conditions.
  3. PCR on pUC19 plasmid using AlphaL+SacI and AlphaPlinkSac primers (annealing temperature - 65°C; elongation length 60s; 20 and 25 cycles (Fig. 1.)).
  4. Gel electrophoresis and gel-out of 550 bp band.
  5. Overnight digest of isolated PCR product with SacI (SacI buffer).
Fig. 1.PCR to obtain alpha with various number of cycles. 1. Marker 2. Alpha PCR, 25 cycles 3. Alpha PCR, 20 cycles
