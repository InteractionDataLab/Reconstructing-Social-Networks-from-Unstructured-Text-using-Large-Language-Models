Home Project Overview: pH Sensor Attachment Time Regulation Waste Removal Experiments and Parts About Us

Waste removal is function we want to perform when our BacToKidney enters the small intestine. Waste removal is split into three parts:

  • Urea -- the removal of urea.
  • Guanidine -- the removal of guanidine.
  • Phosphate -- the balancing of phosphate.

The removal efficiency of Urea, Guanidine and Phosphate can be quantitatively characterized by promoter of LacI (pLac, R0010)
