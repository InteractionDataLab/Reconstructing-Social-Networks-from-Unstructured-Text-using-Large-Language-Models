    ppk mutant (270 A→G, 273 C→T)

    mutation

    • to avoid undesired EcoRI cutting site
    • GAA TTC (268-273) at +1 reading frame; it only affects 2 amino acids
    • GAA TTC will be translated into EF (glu, phe)
    • to keep amino acids content, there are several way to mutate
    • glu: GAA → GAG
    • phe: TTC → TTT

    biobrick cutting sites scanning report

    • EcoRI cutting site is eliminated
    • No new cutting site is produced
