In a few short months he recruited four fellow undergraduates and gathered support from various departments and offices at the University. Last year's team successfully competed in iGEM2007 and earned a silver medal! The team has been and will continue to be a very much student-driven endeavour with all-original project ideas. This makes the team very independent but it also poses challenges which other teams that are under the umbrella of an existing research effort often do not face.
