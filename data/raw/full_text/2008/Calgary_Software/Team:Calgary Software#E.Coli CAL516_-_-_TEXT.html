Coli bacteria Escherichia coli CAL516, informally known as E. Coli Calgarius, is a strain of the bacterium Escherichia coli that is commonly found in Calgary, Alberta . It is usually detected by the high concentration of awesome , measured at levels up to 49.9 ppm in a CAL516 colony. [1] Due to this property, a body of mythology has been created about confirmed and unconfirmed properties of E. Coli Calgarius.
