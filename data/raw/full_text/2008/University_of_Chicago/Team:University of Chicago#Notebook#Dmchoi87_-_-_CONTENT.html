Teh Book of Theorie

Daniel M. Choi is head of the Theory Division, which entails mathematical modeling and articulation of general findings. He is therefore more interested in numbers, graphs, and words than with materials, equipments, and procedures, so very little practical information will be found here. However, he hopes that whatever is in these dismal pages will be of some use to clever experimentalists.
