Varying bacterial numbers-results and discussion results No Dilution 0h 0.5h 1.0h 100-fold dilution 0h 0.5h 1.0h 1.5h 1000-fold dilution 0h 0.5h 1.0h 1.5h 2.0h discussion We demonstrated that the GFP expression switch is delayed by the ratio of sender to receiver. The result indicates that the amount of AHL from one bacterium per time is constant and independent of bacteria number density. This is probaly because the sender has no feedback circuit of AHL production. Although this strategy can not change the time interval, we can manage the switch timing by changing the ratio of sender to receiver. It is probably because of circuit working, since the AHL is provided enough for the receiver.
