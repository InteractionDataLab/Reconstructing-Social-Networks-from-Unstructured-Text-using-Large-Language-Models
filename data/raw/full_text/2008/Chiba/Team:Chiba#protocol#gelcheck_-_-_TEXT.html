Visualizing agarose gels Remove gel from gel box Soak the gel in ethidium bromide solution Let it 30 min. Place the gel in Trans-Illuminator and turn on UV light after make sure the door closing. Print the picture. Remove gel and throw in trash Wipe down Trans-Illuminator if necessary.
