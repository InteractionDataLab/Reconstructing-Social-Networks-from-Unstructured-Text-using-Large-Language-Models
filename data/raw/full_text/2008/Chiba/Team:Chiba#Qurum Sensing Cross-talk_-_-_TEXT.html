These are the protein synthesizing signaling molecule AHL (called "I protein"), the protein receiving the signaling molecule (called "R protein") and endogeneous promotors. Interestingly, these sets can cross-talk beyond the species. Each speices of bacteria has unique LuxI proteins and they synthesize AHL that has acyl chains with different length and/or different functional group at c-3 position.
