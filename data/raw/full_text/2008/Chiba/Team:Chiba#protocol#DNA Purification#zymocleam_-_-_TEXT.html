Load mixture silica column and place column into a 2 ml collection tube Centrifuge at full speed for 30 sec. Discard the flow-through. Add 200μL of wash buffer and spin 30 sec. Place siliva volumn into a new 1.5 ml tube. Add water directly to the column matrix and spin to elute the DNA.
