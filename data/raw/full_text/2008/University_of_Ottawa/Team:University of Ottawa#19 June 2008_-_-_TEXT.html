Integration of 597/8 plasmids The BY4741 which was an old strain and was supposed to grow slower actually grew way faster than expected in liquid media. Therefore we almost doubled our target OD. Mila suggested that we should do transformation tomorrow. This time we will measure the OD after 3 hours instead of after 4 hours. It actually turns out that we should be using the BY4742(MAT alpha) instead of BY4741(MAT a). This will prevent any reproduction between this strain and the YPH500 which would be a disaster for the oscillatory dynamics.
