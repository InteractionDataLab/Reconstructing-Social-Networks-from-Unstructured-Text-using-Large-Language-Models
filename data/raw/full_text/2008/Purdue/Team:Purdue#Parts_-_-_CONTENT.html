Part BBa_K154000: recA promoter (J22106) + LacZ gene (I732017): DNA damage sensor

We have submitted this part to the Registry with all of its details. Click the link above to learn more! We are currently working on preparing a sample to send to Biobricks. The part, as the project suggests, is a linkage between the recA promoter (J22106) and the lacZ gene (I732017). It is filed as a Measurement part with the nickname "UV Sensor".
